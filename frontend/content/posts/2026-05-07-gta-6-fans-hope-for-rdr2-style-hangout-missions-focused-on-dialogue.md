# GTA 6 Fans Hope for RDR2-Style 'Hangout' Missions Focused on Dialogue

A user on the r/GTA6 subreddit, u/Getawaydrive, has shared a popular sentiment among the community: the hope for 'hangout' missions in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. Drawing a direct parallel to Red Dead Redemption 2's beloved 'A Quiet Time' mission, the user expressed a desire for segments focused purely on character interaction and humorous dialogue, rather than action. They further speculated that an early GTA 6 screenshot showing protagonists Jason and Lucia (referred to as 'Cal' in the original post) in a bar could potentially hint at such a mission being included in the game.
