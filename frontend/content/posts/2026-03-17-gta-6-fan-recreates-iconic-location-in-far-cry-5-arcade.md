# GTA 6 Fan Recreates Iconic Location in Far Cry 5 Arcade

A passionate gamer, u/Fresh-War5908, has managed to bring a section of a highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6 location to life within Far Cry 5's Arcade mode. Despite facing map size limitations, the creator ingeniously shortened a prominent bridge by 20-30% to accurately recreate a near island and a portion of a far island. The primary goal was to perfectly replicate the memorable jump onto an old railway bridge, which the creator is thrilled to have achieved. The custom map, titled "Bridge from GTA 6," is currently available for free to PS5 players, with a gameplay review also featured on the creator's YouTube channel.
