# GTA 6 on Xbox: Is it Already 'Half-Released' on PC?

A thought-provoking discussion has emerged from the GTA 6 subreddit, where a user posits that a release on Xbox effectively means the game is 'half-released' on PC. The argument hinges on the deep technical similarities between Xbox consoles and modern PCs, citing shared x86 architecture, analogous AMD hardware, and Microsoft's integrated ecosystem. This perspective suggests that the decision to withhold a PC version concurrently with console releases is often a strategic platform choice rather than a genuine technical hurdle, implying that the game's core code is largely PC-ready from the start.

## Key Takeaways
- Xbox consoles and Windows PCs share the same x86 architecture.
- Both platforms utilize very similar AMD hardware components.
- Microsoft's ecosystem is tightly integrated between Xbox and Windows.
- The absence of a simultaneous PC release is often attributed to platform strategy rather than technical limitations.
