# GTA 6: Inflation-Adjusted Price Positions It as the Most Affordable 3D Universe Launch

Contrary to some fan complaints regarding the potential launch price of Grand Theft Auto 6, a recent analysis shared on Reddit's r/GTA6 community offers a surprising perspective. User 'GiveMeWaterPls' posits that when the prices of previous 3D universe GTA titles are adjusted for inflation, GTA 6 is projected to be the most affordable entry in that lineage at its launch. This finding aims to address and potentially alleviate concerns among the fanbase who have expressed apprehension about the game's cost relative to its predecessors, suggesting a more budget-friendly proposition than initially perceived.
