# GTA 6: Rockstar's Eight Officially Named Characters Hint at a Deliberate Narrative Divide

A recent analysis of officially named characters for Grand Theft Auto 6 reveals that Rockstar Games has only confirmed eight individuals: protagonists Jason and Lucia, and six supporting characters. This is a surprisingly small number for a game of its anticipated scale, especially considering many other characters seen in trailers are known only by fan-coined names like 'Hammer Lady' and 'Selfie Guy'.The named characters appear to fall into two distinct thematic groups or 'circles'. The 'Keys circle' includes Jason, Brian Heder, and Cal Hampton, hinting at a narrative involving drug running, coastal paranoia, and the sun-bleached underbelly. The 'Vice City circle' comprises Boobie Ike, Dre'Quan Priest, and Real Dimez, suggesting storylines focused on music, clubs, clout, and the entertainment industry.Intriguingly, Raul Bautista, a professional bank robber with a 'big score' in his official bio, doesn't fit neatly into either group. He is speculated to be a 'bridge' character, potentially pulling Jason and Lucia from the Keys environment into the broader, overarching plot. Notably, there is still no officially confirmed villain for GTA 6, with the 'corrupt detective' being pure trailer theory at this point. This deliberate naming strategy by Rockstar suggests a carefully constructed narrative, though the full plot implications remain speculative.
