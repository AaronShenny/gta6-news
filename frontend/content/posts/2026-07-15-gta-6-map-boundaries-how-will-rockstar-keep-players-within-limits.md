# GTA 6 Map Boundaries: How Will Rockstar Keep Players Within Limits?

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked debate among fans concerning how Rockstar Games will manage map boundaries in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. The original poster ([/u/chud-69](https://www.reddit.com/user/chud-69)) brought up the question of how players would be prevented from venturing beyond the playable area, particularly if the map isn't an island and features a northern 'border'.

The user referenced Red Dead Redemption 2, where environmental obstructions and sniper zones were utilized to keep players within the designated game world. This led to speculation about what methods Rockstar might employ in GTA 6. Given the potential for aerial travel with planes, the question was raised: 'would there just be random SAMs scattered across the forest?' to deter players from flying into unrendered or restricted zones, hypothetically named 'Gloriana'.

The community is now pondering whether the map will indeed be an island, simplifying boundary management, or if Rockstar will introduce new, perhaps more creative or lore-friendly, methods to enforce the map's edges without breaking immersion.

## Key Takeaways
