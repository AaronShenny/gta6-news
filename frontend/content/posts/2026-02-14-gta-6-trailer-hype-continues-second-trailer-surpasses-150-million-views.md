# GTA 6 Trailer Hype Continues: Second Trailer Surpasses 150 Million Views

The excitement surrounding Grand Theft Auto 6 remains sky-high, as its second official trailer has now eclipsed 150 million views within nine months of its release. This significant milestone, when combined with the first trailer's impressive 273 million views, brings the total viewership for GTA 6's promotional videos to a staggering 423 million. This demonstrates the immense global interest and anticipation for Rockstar Games' upcoming title, highlighting its incredible progress and impact.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 Trailer 2 has reached 150 million views.
- This milestone was achieved within nine months of the trailer's release.
- The first GTA 6 trailer currently stands at 273 million views.
- Combined, both GTA 6 trailers have accumulated 423 million views.
- The high viewership underscores the massive anticipation for Rockstar Games' Grand Theft Auto 6.
