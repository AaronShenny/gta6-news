# GTA 6 Online: Will Progress Carry Over or Will It Be a Fresh Start?

A recent Reddit discussion in the r/GTA6 community has ignited a passionate debate among fans regarding the future of GTA 6 Online. The central question revolves around whether the highly anticipated online component will offer a completely fresh start, erasing all progress, vehicles, and businesses from GTA 5 Online, or if certain elements will carry over.
