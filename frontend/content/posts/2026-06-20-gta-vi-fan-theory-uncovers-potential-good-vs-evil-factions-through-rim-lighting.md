# GTA VI Fan Theory Uncovers Potential "Good vs. Evil" Factions Through Rim Lighting

A dedicated German YouTuber has proposed a compelling fan theory, dubbed "The Rimlight Theory," suggesting that Rockstar Games is subtly categorizing characters into opposing factions within the official Grand Theft Auto VI artwork. By meticulously analyzing the consistent use of dual rim lighting—a warm yellow and a distinct magenta pink—across various promotional materials, the theorist posits that the unchanging direction of these lights for specific characters isn't coincidental. Characters like protagonists Jason and Lucia consistently feature magenta light on their left and yellow on their right, while others, including Raul Bautista and Boobie Ike, display the exact opposite configuration. This highly consistent pattern, even appearing on the latest cover art, leads to the intriguing conclusion that Rockstar may be using this visual language to hint at deeper narrative alignments, possibly distinguishing between "good" and "bad" characters or rival groups within the game's world.
