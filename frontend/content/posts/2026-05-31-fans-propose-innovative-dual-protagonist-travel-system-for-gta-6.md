# Fans Propose Innovative Dual-Protagonist Travel System for GTA 6

A recent discussion sparked by Reddit user /u/subHOU on the r/GTA6 subreddit proposes a revolutionary take on travel within Grand Theft Auto 6's anticipated dual-protagonist system. The idea centers on allowing players to set a waypoint and have one character, such as Jason, take the wheel, while the player instantly switches to control the other character, Lucia. This would transform traditionally passive long-distance drives into active gameplay opportunities. Instead of merely holding down an accelerator, players could engage in various in-game activities like browsing social media, managing side businesses, texting contacts, playing mini-games, or planning future missions, all while on the move. The suggestion emphasizes that this feature would not only make travel more immersive and productive but also strengthen the 'Bonnie-and-Clyde' dynamic between the protagonists. The concept includes the ability to instantly switch back to the driver if an event on the road demands attention, sparking debate among fans about its potential to enhance or detract from the classic GTA driving experience.
