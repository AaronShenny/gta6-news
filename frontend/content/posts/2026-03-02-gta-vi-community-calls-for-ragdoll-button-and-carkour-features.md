# GTA VI Community Calls for Ragdoll Button and 'Carkour' Features

The Grand Theft Auto VI community has expressed a passionate desire for specific gameplay mechanics, prominently featuring a 'ragdoll button' and 'carkour.' Originating from a Reddit post, players are advocating for the ability to instantly trigger their character's ragdoll physics and perform parkour-like stunts with vehicles. The sentiment, while playful, conveys a strong expectation for these features, suggesting potential disappointment if Rockstar Games does not incorporate them into GTA VI.

## Key Takeaways
- Players are demanding a dedicated 'ragdoll button' in GTA VI.
- The community also strongly desires 'carkour' mechanics (vehicle-based parkour).
- The sentiment highlights a significant player expectation for these specific physics-based interactions.
- These demands emerged from active discussions within the GTA VI subreddit.

## FAQ
**What is a 'ragdoll button'?**
A requested in-game feature allowing players to instantly activate their character's ragdoll physics, causing them to go limp and react to environmental forces.

**What is 'carkour'?**
A community-coined term referring to the desire for parkour-like movement and stunts performed using in-game vehicles, emphasizing creative and dynamic vehicle interactions.

**Why are players demanding these features?**
Players often enjoy the emergent gameplay, comedic potential, and creative freedom offered by robust ragdoll physics and dynamic vehicle interactions in open-world games like Grand Theft Auto.
