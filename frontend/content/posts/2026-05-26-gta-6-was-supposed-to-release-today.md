# GTA 6 Was Supposed To Release Today

Today, May 26, 2026, marked a significant date for many fans anticipating Grand Theft Auto 6, as it was the previously announced release day for Rockstar's highly anticipated title. However, the game is not available, having been delayed again to November 19, 2026. This latest push follows an earlier delay from an initial Fall 2025 target. Rockstar explained the necessity for more time to "deliver at the level of quality you expect and deserve," a common practice for the developer known for delaying its games. Take-Two boss Strauss Zelnick hinted that the current November 2026 date is approximately 18 months behind an even earlier internal target of May 2025. GTA 6 is confirmed for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S on November 19, with a PC version expected later, following Rockstar's historical release patterns.

- GTA 6 will launch on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S in November; a PC version is expected later.
