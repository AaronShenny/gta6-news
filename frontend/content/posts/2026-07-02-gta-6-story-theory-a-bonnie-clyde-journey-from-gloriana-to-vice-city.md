# GTA 6 Story Theory: A 'Bonnie & Clyde' Journey from Gloriana to Vice City?

A compelling fan theory circulating on Reddit proposes a fascinating narrative structure for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/ChevUser4207 suggests that the game's story could commence in a previously unseen location, dubbed 'Gloriana,' drawing parallels to Grand Theft Auto V's opening sequence in North Yankton. The core premise involves the protagonists fleeing Gloriana and embarking on a journey towards Vice City, aiming to establish a new life and rise to prominence. Along their route to Vice City, the theory posits that the characters might engage in 'Bonnie and Clyde' style criminal activities in various other cities, such as Port Gellhorn, indicating a grind towards their ultimate goal. A key observation supporting this theory is the perceived difference in the characters' appearances in the trailers: they appear more affluent in the Vice City scenes compared to their 'on the grind' look in other locations, hinting at a rags-to-riches progression within the game's storyline.
