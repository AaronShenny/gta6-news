# GTA 6 Rumored to Explore User-Generated Content Following Industry Trends

Amidst discussions on the struggles of live-service games and PUBG Studios' future ambitions for their franchise, a notable detail has emerged regarding GTA 6. A recently spotted job advertisement from Rockstar Games, the developer behind the highly anticipated open-world crime title, has sparked speculation that GTA 6 could be set to lean into user-generated content (UGC). This potential move would align GTA 6 with a growing industry trend, seen in successful games like Fortnite and Roblox, and is also an area PUBG Studios plans to explore as it aims to evolve its battle royale into a 'global cultural icon.' While concrete details are scarce, the job ad suggests Rockstar may be considering ways for players to contribute to and shape the GTA 6 experience.

## Key Takeaways
- A job posting at Rockstar Games has led to speculation that GTA 6 may feature user-generated content (UGC).
- This potential strategy for GTA 6 mirrors approaches taken by successful titles such as Fortnite and Roblox.
- The discussion arose in the context of PUBG Studios' plans to experiment with UGC as part of its long-term franchise vision for PUBG.
