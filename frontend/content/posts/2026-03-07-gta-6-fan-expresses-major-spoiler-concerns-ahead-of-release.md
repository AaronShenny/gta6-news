# GTA 6 Fan Expresses Major Spoiler Concerns Ahead of Release

A passionate Grand Theft Auto 6 enthusiast, known as /u/TheLastOneStanding01 on Reddit, has voiced significant apprehension regarding the upcoming title. Anticipating GTA 6 to be a cornerstone of their gaming for at least the next 15 years, the user plans to meticulously explore all side missions and activities. However, their primary fear revolves around other players rushing through the main story and subsequently flooding the internet with spoilers, potentially forcing them to avoid online communities until they complete the game.

## Key Takeaways
- User anticipates GTA 6 will be a long-term gaming commitment (15+ years).
- Player intends to take their time, focusing on side content and exploration.
- Major concern about spoilers from players rushing the main story.
- Fear of needing to avoid the internet to prevent spoilers from ruining their experience.
