# GTA 6 Fan Hopes for Enhanced Interiors and Realistic Police Encounters

A dedicated fan on Reddit has shared their wishlist for Grand Theft Auto 6, expressing frustration with the lack of accessible interiors and simplistic NPC and police reactions in GTA 5. They hope GTA 6 will feature realistic interiors, dynamic crowd panic during chaotic events, and a significantly more advanced police response. Specifically, the user envisions SWAT units coordinating with shields, rather than the 'one-by-one' approach seen in GTA 4, to create more challenging and immersive gameplay scenarios.

## Key Takeaways
- Desire for more accessible and realistic interiors in GTA 6.
- Expectation of panicked and dynamic crowd movement during player-initiated chaos indoors.
- Hope for sophisticated police AI, including perimeter lockdown and coordinated SWAT unit entry with shields.
- Critique of previous GTA titles' police AI, specifically GTA 4's 'one-by-one' enemy approach.
