# Fan Theory: GTA 6 Prologue Could Take Us Back to Liberty City's Snowy Past

A compelling fan theory from Reddit user /u/gulagsurvivor2019 suggests Grand Theft Auto VI's prologue could continue Rockstar's tradition of snowy openings, but this time in Liberty City. The theory posits that the prologue would focus on Lucia's backstory, portraying her in her late teens or early twenties. Drawing from Lucia's known character details – that she's from Liberty City and 'fighting for her family landed her in the Leonida Penitentiary' – the theory speculates her father was a significant drug dealer, possibly cartel-affiliated, who was killed. The prologue would then depict Lucia seeking revenge for her father's death, leading to her arrest and transfer to Leonida Penitentiary, where she'd eventually become eligible for parole in her late twenties or early thirties. Furthermore, the theory proposes a way Lucia and Jason could have known each other before her incarceration: Lucia's father might have taken her on business trips to the Leonida Keys as a child, where she met a young Jason, establishing a connection prior to the main narrative.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's prologue might be set in a snowy Liberty City, following Rockstar's tradition.
- The prologue could reveal Lucia's origin story, detailing the crime that led to her imprisonment.
- The theory suggests Lucia's father was a major drug dealer, and she sought revenge for his death.
- It's speculated Lucia met Jason as a child in the Leonida Keys during her father's business trips.
