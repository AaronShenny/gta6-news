# GTA 6 Television: Fans Eagerly Speculate on In-Game Programming

A recent Reddit post has sparked discussion among Grand Theft Auto fans, focusing on the highly anticipated in-game television experience in GTA 6. Speculations include the return of fan-favorite animated series such as *Impotent Rage* and *Princess Robot Bubblegum*, with hopes for expanded storylines or even archival episodes. Additionally, the community is looking forward to new channels that could feature significant events or prominent characters from Leonida, the game's setting, akin to talk shows featuring personalities like Steve Hanes or Jack Howitzer. Many, like the original poster, anticipate making in-game TV watching a priority upon GTA 6's release, highlighting the feature's potential to deepen immersion and world-building.
