# GTA 6 Completion: Fans Debate Between GTA V's Simplicity and RDR2's Epic Grind

A recent discussion sparked on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited a debate among fans regarding the optimal design for 100% game completion in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. The core of the conversation revolves around whether Rockstar Games should adopt the more straightforward and accessible completion criteria seen in Grand Theft Auto V, or embrace the notoriously exhaustive and detailed checklist approach characteristic of Red Dead Redemption 2. Many are also suggesting a hybrid model that balances depth with player accessibility, aiming for a system that provides a satisfying challenge without becoming overly tedious.

## Key Takeaways
- Community debate on GTA 6's 100% completion structure.
- Comparison between GTA V's simpler completion model and Red Dead Redemption 2's extensive checklist.
- Player desire for a balanced approach, potentially blending elements from both titles.
- Discussion originated from the r/GTA6 subreddit, highlighting fan engagement.
