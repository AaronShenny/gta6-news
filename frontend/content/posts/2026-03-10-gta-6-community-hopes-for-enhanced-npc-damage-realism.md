# GTA 6 Community Hopes for Enhanced NPC Damage Realism

Amidst the fervent anticipation for Grand Theft Auto VI, a recent discussion on Reddit's r/GTA6 community highlights a specific player expectation: significantly more realistic damage modeling, especially when NPCs are impacted by gunfire. User /u/Kasm001 expressed excitement and curiosity regarding the potential for unprecedented realism in how non-player characters react to and display damage in the upcoming title, suggesting a significant leap from previous installments like GTA V.

## Key Takeaways
- Community expectation for highly realistic NPC damage in GTA 6.
- Specific interest in how NPCs will visibly react to being shot.
- Speculation points to a potential significant graphical and physics upgrade from GTA V.
