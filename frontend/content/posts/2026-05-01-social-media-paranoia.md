# SOCIAL MEDIA PARANOIA

A user on the r/GTA6 subreddit shared an imaginative, albeit slightly unsettling, concept for an in-game social media app in Grand Theft Auto 6. The idea suggests players could watch random NPC livestreams and then realize they are physically present in the background of that very stream. The scenario escalates, with the possibility of another NPC filming the player watching the stream, creating a 'strange loop' where player actions are constantly being observed and potentially broadcast within the game world. This mechanic aims to make the city feel hyper-alive and interconnected, fostering a subtle sense of digital paranoia, described as 'weird but funny' and 'very GTA' by the creator.

## Key Takeaways
- Players could watch NPC livestreams directly within GTA 6's social media app.
- The player might discover they are an unscripted background character in an NPC's live stream.
- Another NPC could film the player watching the stream, potentially uploading that content.
- This creates a 'strange loop' effect, implying constant digital observation and blurring the lines between player and in-game content.
- The concept aims for a 'creepy in a subtle way' yet 'funny' and 'very GTA' experience, enhancing city immersion.
- The post reflects community anticipation for GTA 6's upcoming trailers.
