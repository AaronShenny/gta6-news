# GTA 6 Combat Rumors Point to Grounded Melee and Small Arms Focus

A recent discussion among the Grand Theft Auto community speculates on a significant shift in GTA 6's combat mechanics, moving towards a more grounded and less arcade-like experience. The primary rumor fueling this theory is the potential for weapons to be stored in the back of vehicles rather than being carried on the player's person at all times. This change, if true, would necessitate a greater reliance on small arms and weapons acquired from fallen enemies, such as rifles from officers, rather than the ability to instantly access a wide arsenal including heavy weaponry.

Further supporting this theory are observations from the second GTA 6 trailer, where Lucia is seen high-kicking a training bag and, in another instance, slamming someone's head into a car door. These actions suggest the possibility of dynamic melee combat, where players can interact with their environment during fights, a feature previously seen in Red Dead Redemption 2's bar brawls.
