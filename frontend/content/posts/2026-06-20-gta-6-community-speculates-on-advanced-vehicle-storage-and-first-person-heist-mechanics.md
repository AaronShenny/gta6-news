# GTA 6: Community Speculates on Advanced Vehicle Storage and First-Person Heist Mechanics

The GTA 6 community is abuzz with speculation regarding advanced vehicle mechanics, particularly the potential for weapon storage within vehicle trunks and immersive first-person perspectives during heists.
