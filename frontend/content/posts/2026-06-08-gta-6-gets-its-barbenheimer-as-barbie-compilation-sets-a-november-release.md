# GTA 6 Gets Its Barbenheimer As Barbie Compilation Sets A November Release

While many publishers are avoiding Grand Theft Auto 6's release window, Atari is boldly counter-programming by launching two titles in November, mere weeks before Rockstar's highly anticipated game. The most notable is 'Barbie Rewind,' a collection of 16 classic Barbie games from 1991-2007, including the previously unreleased 'Barbie: Vacation Adventure.' This compilation, developed by Digital Eclipse, is set to release on November 12 across PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch platforms. Additionally, Atari will release a remastered 'Godzilla: Destroy All Monsters Melee' on November 3. This strategic proximity to GTA 6's launch is drawing comparisons to the 'Barbenheimer' cultural event, suggesting a humorous, albeit low-key, challenge to the gaming behemoth's dominance.

## Key Takeaways
- Atari is releasing 'Barbie Rewind' on November 12, a week before GTA 6's anticipated launch month.
- 'Barbie Rewind' is a compilation of 16 Barbie games from 1991 to 2007, developed by Digital Eclipse.
- The compilation includes the previously unreleased game 'Barbie: Vacation Adventure'.
- It will be available on PC, PlayStation 4/5, Xbox One/Series X|S, and Nintendo Switch.
- Atari is also releasing a remastered 'Godzilla: Destroy All Monsters Melee' on November 3.
- The proximity of these releases to GTA 6 is being likened to the 'Barbenheimer' phenomenon.
- Other publishers, like Devolver Digital, are also planning releases near GTA 6, though details are unclear.
