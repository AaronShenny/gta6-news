# GTA VI Community Speculates on Cosmetic Fighting Styles to Enhance Melee Combat

A recent discussion within the GTA VI community, sparked by protagonist Lucia's high kick in the trailer and her background as a 'trained fighter,' explores the potential for customizable cosmetic fighting styles. One fan suggested that players could choose distinct movement sets, such as Boxing, Karate, Kickboxing, or Jiu-Jitsu, for their characters during melee encounters. This proposed feature would be purely aesthetic, having no impact on damage output or animation speed, but would aim to inject unique visual 'taste' and variety into the game's hand-to-hand combat.
