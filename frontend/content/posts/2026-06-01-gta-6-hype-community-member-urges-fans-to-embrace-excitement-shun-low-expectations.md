# GTA 6 Hype: Community Member Urges Fans to Embrace Excitement, Shun Low Expectations

A passionate post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked discussion, with user /u/Maleficent-Ad6734 challenging the growing trend of negativity and calls for 'low expectations' within the game's dedicated community. The user argues against hostility towards excited fans and pre-emptive criticism of an unreleased title, questioning why individuals who aren't interested or are cynical about GTA 6 would frequent its fan hub. Emphasizing that this is anticipated to be a 'biggest launch in history,' the post encourages fellow enthusiasts to fully embrace their excitement and anticipation, asserting that tempering expectations only diminishes the overall experience. The author concludes with a plea for an excited, positive environment, urging those who are bothered by such enthusiasm to leave the community.
