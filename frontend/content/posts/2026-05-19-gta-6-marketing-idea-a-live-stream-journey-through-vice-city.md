# GTA 6 Marketing Idea: A Live Stream Journey Through Vice City

A highly original marketing concept for Grand Theft Auto 6 has emerged from the community, suggesting a unique live stream approach. The proposal involves a continuous 24/7 YouTube live stream presenting a fixed camera angle, mimicking a CCTV feed from within the game world of Vice City. Initially, this feed would show a desolate, rural area, capturing sporadic occurrences like passing vehicles or wildlife. The core idea is that every two weeks leading up to the game's release, the camera perspective would shift, gradually moving from these remote locations towards increasingly urban environments, ultimately culminating in a view from the bustling heart of the city.

## Key Takeaways
- A 24/7 YouTube live stream showing a fixed camera angle from Vice City.
- Initial view from a remote, rural area (e.g., utility pole over a dirt road).
- Occasional in-game occurrences like cars or animals passing by.
- Camera angle changes every two weeks, moving closer to urban areas.
- Progression culminates in a view from the heart of Vice City.
- Proposed as an 'incredibly original and interesting' marketing strategy.
