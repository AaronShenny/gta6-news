# GTA 6 Trailer 2 Hits 150 Million Views, Combined Trailer Count Surpasses 400 Million Mark

Rockstar Games' highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6 is demonstrating immense popularity, with its second official trailer recently surpassing 150 million views within just nine months of its release. This significant milestone, combined with the colossal 273 million views garnered by the first trailer, pushes the total viewership for GTA 6's promotional content to an impressive 423 million, underscoring the massive global anticipation for the upcoming title.

## Key Takeaways

- This milestone was achieved within 9 months of its release.
- GTA 6 Trailer 1 currently holds 273 million views.
- The combined total viewership for both GTA 6 trailers now stands at 423 million.
- This substantial viewership highlights the incredible progress and strong anticipation for Rockstar Games' next major release.
