# Fan Theory: Could GTA Online's KnoWay App Become GTA 6's Uber-Style Ride Service?

A recent discussion sparked on Reddit by user /u/Benabad-2008 proposes an intriguing fan theory for Grand Theft Auto 6. This suggests a new layer of in-game utility and transportation options for players.
