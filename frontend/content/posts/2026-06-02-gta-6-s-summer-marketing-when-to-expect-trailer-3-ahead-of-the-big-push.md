# GTA 6's Summer Marketing: When to Expect Trailer 3 Ahead of the Big Push

Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick recently announced that the major marketing campaign for Grand Theft Auto VI will kick off in summer, typically around June 20th in the US. However, this statement primarily refers to large-scale, externally budgeted initiatives such as partnerships, billboards, and extensive social media campaigns. Previous trailers (1 & 2) were internally created and distributed, falling outside this major marketing scope. The article suggests that Trailer 3 will also be an internal release and is highly likely to precede any major marketing efforts by several weeks. This strategy aims to maintain an element of surprise and prevent premature leaks, as seen with past incidents. Therefore, fans could potentially see Trailer 3 released 'imminently,' ahead of the extensive summer marketing preparations.
