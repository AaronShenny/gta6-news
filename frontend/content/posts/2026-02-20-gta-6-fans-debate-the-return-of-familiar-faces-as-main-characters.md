# GTA 6: Fans Debate the Return of Familiar Faces as Main Characters

The Grand Theft Auto 6 community is buzzing with speculation and discussion regarding the potential return of a beloved protagonist from GTA IV or V to take on a significant role in the upcoming installment. Prompted by a recent Reddit thread, fans are contemplating whether Rockstar Games should follow the precedent set by characters like Ken Rosenberg, who transitioned from a main character in Vice City to a key figure in a portion of San Andreas's story, and are actively suggesting which past character they'd like to see make a comeback.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 fans are discussing the possibility of a main character from GTA IV or V returning.
- The discussion draws a parallel to Ken Rosenberg's role in both GTA Vice City and GTA San Andreas, where he was a main character in both titles.
- The community is actively being asked which past character they would want to see return in GTA VI for a portion of the story.

Yes, Ken Rosenberg served as a main character in GTA Vice City and later appeared as a main character during a portion of GTA San Andreas, setting a precedent for such returns.
