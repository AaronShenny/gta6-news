# Early Comparisons Hint at Grand Theft Auto 6's Jaw-Dropping Detail

Anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6's visual fidelity continues to grow, fueled by early community observations. A Reddit post by user u/electr0x recently highlighted what they describe as the game's 'amazing' level of detail through a series of direct comparisons. The post juxtaposes perceived graphical elements from GTA 6 against industry benchmarks like Red Dead Redemption 2 and Cyberpunk 2077, as well as its predecessor, Grand Theft Auto 5. These comparisons, spanning everything from intricate character models to environmental textures and even subtle object physics, suggest that GTA 6 aims to set a new standard for realism in the open-world genre.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 is noted for an 'amazing' level of detail based on early observations.
- Direct visual comparisons were made between GTA 6 and Red Dead Redemption 2 for character details (foot, face) and environmental elements (nature).
- Character face details in GTA 6 were also compared to those found in Cyberpunk 2077.
- Object physics and textures, specifically a towel, were compared against Grand Theft Auto 5.
- The comparisons indicate a significant leap in graphical fidelity for the upcoming title.
