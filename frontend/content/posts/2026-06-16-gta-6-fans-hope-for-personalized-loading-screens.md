# GTA 6 Fans Hope for Personalized Loading Screens

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by user /u/Classic_Adagio2930 has sparked interest in a unique personalization feature for Grand Theft Auto VI: customizable loading screens. The core idea is to allow players to tailor their loading experiences, moving beyond the traditional iconic art. Suggestions include displaying players' online characters complete with their stats, or even using cherished in-game moments captured via a potential photo mode as custom loading screen backdrops. The sentiment among fans is that while the classic GTA loading screens are indeed iconic, the ability to personalize them would offer a "sweet" new dimension to player expression and immersion in the upcoming title.

## Key Takeaways
- Proposal for customizable loading screens in GTA 6.
- Suggestions include displaying online created characters and their stats.
- Ability to use in-game photo mode screenshots as loading screens.
- Aims to enhance player personalization and immersion.
- Acknowledges the iconic nature of traditional GTA loading screens while seeking modern enhancements.
