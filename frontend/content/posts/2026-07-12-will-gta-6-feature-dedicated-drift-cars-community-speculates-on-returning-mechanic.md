# Will GTA 6 Feature Dedicated Drift Cars? Community Speculates on Returning Mechanic

A recent Reddit post by u/P4n0pticZ on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited a discussion among fans regarding the potential inclusion of dedicated drift cars in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. The user, who primarily engages in GTA RolePlay and hasn't extensively played GTA Online in recent years, expressed keen interest in the reception of drift cars introduced with GTA Online's Chop Shop DLC. They also noted an update specifically tailored for drifting, indicating a growing interest in this driving mechanic within the franchise. The core of the post revolves around the user's hope and curiosity about the likelihood of 'actual good Drift Cars' being available at the launch of GTA 6, highlighting their personal enjoyment of this feature and desire to see it return.
