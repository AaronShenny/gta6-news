# GTA 6 Vice City Graphics Ignite Fan Debate: Photo-Realism vs. GTA V Comparisons

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, sparked by a post titled 'Los Santos VS Vice City,' reveals a divided fanbase regarding the graphical fidelity of Grand Theft Auto 6's Vice City. The debate, centered around a new video showcasing a sunset scene, has elicited two distinct reactions: some players are astonished by the visuals, claiming they could be mistaken for real photographs, while others feel the graphics bear a strong resemblance to GTA V. The original poster, /u/Suspicious-Topic-495, strongly refuted the comparison to GTA V, asserting that it's 'a crime' to equate the two, underscoring the perceived generational leap in visual quality.
