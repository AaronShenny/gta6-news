# GTA 6 Release Date: Community Apprehension Mounts Amidst Rockstar Silence

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit reveals a growing sense of anxiety among fans regarding the anticipated release of Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/MostWxnt3d initiated a conversation questioning whether the community truly believes the game will launch on its rumored date of November 19, 2026. The core of the concern stems from Rockstar Games' 'complete silence for a long time' regarding development updates, which is fueling speculation about potential delays. Fans are particularly wary, recalling the 'trauma' associated with previous 'Hi Everyone' statements—a common precursor to delay announcements from the developer. The sentiment suggests a significant portion of the fanbase is bracing for the possibility of the release window shifting.
