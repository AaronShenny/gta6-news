# GTA 6 Fan Mapping Project: A Four-Year Visual Journey of Vice City's Evolution

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto VI fan has unveiled a compelling timelapse video, meticulously documenting the evolution of a community-driven mapping project from September 2022 to May 2026. The map's progression is shown through key development milestones, including initial interpretations based on the 2022 leaks, significant updates following the release of the first official trailer in December 2023, and further refinements after the second trailer and screenshots were revealed in May 2025. The project serves as a testament to the passionate community's commitment to visualizing the highly anticipated game's environment over nearly four years.
