# GTA 6: Is the Hype for Single-Player or Just GTA6Online?

A recent Reddit post by u/ozdude182 on the r/GTA6 subreddit has opened a discussion about the primary source of community anticipation for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. The user questions if the excitement is for a fresh single-player GTA experience or if the campaign is largely seen as a necessary precursor to the inevitable GTA6 Online. This query stems from the observation that GTA 5 Online remains immensely popular, while its single-player narrative is less frequently discussed outside of character anecdotes and memes.
