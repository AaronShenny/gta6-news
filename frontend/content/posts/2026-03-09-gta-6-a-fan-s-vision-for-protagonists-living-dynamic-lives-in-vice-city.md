# GTA 6: A Fan's Vision for Protagonists Living Dynamic Lives in Vice City

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked significant interest with a fan's innovative idea for Grand Theft Auto 6's dual protagonists, Jason and Lucia. The user, Electronic_Bee_6808, suggests a departure from previous GTA titles where characters only appear when switched to or called. Instead, they envision Jason and Lucia consistently existing and operating within the game world, even when the player isn't actively controlling them. This concept imagines players spontaneously encountering the other protagonist on the mini-map, engaged in various independent activities such as driving, committing crimes, or evading law enforcement. It's argued that such a feature would dramatically enhance the game's immersion, making Vice City feel substantially more vibrant and alive, and opening up opportunities for unique, unscripted interactions with the other lead character.

## Key Takeaways
- Protagonists Jason and Lucia would always be active and present in the game world.
- Players could spontaneously encounter the other character on the mini-map.
- The non-controlled protagonist would be engaged in independent activities (e.g., driving, robbing, police chases).
- Allows for unscripted interactions, such as simple dialogue or an option to hang out.
- Aims to make the game world feel significantly more alive and dynamic, enhancing immersion.

## FAQ
**What is the core idea proposed for GTA 6?**
The core idea suggests that both protagonists, Jason and Lucia, should always be active and living their own lives within the game world, even when not controlled by the player.

**How would players interact with the other protagonist?**
Players could spontaneously encounter the other character on the mini-map, observe their activities, and potentially initiate simple dialogue or an option to hang out.

**What benefit would this feature bring to GTA 6?**
It would make the game world feel significantly more alive and dynamic, offering unique, unscripted player experiences and enhancing immersion.
