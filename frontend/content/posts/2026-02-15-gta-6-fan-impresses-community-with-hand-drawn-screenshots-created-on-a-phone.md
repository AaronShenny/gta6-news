# GTA 6 Fan Impresses Community with Hand-Drawn 'Screenshots' Created on a Phone

In a refreshing display of artistic dedication within the Grand Theft Auto VI community, Reddit user u/Dragono301064 has captivated fellow fans by sharing a series of hand-drawn 'screenies' (screenshots or concept art). This personal touch highlights the community's passion and creativity while awaiting further official news on GTA VI.
