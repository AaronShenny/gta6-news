# GTA 6 Fan's PS5 Pro Monitor Dilemma: 1440p vs. 4K for Optimal Gaming

A passionate gamer, identified as u/Fancy_Row5817 on Reddit, is facing a significant hardware dilemma in anticipation of Grand Theft Auto VI. With plans to purchase a PlayStation 5 Pro specifically for Rockstar's upcoming title, the user is struggling to choose the ideal monitor that will serve both the new console and a planned 1440p gaming PC. The core conflict revolves around the perceived 'uselessness' of 1440p for a PS5 Pro, pushing them towards a 4K display, yet this would strain their PC's performance. The gamer is seeking advice from the community on whether to prioritize 1440p for PC, opt for a 4K monitor and compromise PC quality, or if their current 1080p setup might suffice, questioning if investing in a PS5 Pro without a higher resolution display would be a wasted expense.
