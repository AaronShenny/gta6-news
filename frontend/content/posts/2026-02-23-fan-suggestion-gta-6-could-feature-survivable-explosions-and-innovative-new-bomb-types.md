# Fan Suggestion: GTA 6 Could Feature Survivable Explosions and Innovative New Bomb Types

A dedicated GTA 6 fan has shared their vision for enhanced gameplay mechanics, focusing on more dynamic and impactful explosions, alongside a variety of unique bomb types. Drawing inspiration from GTA 4 and Red Dead Redemption 2, the user suggests that players should be able to survive at least one explosion, albeit with devastating and immersive effects such as shrapnel wounds, visual impairment, and auditory ringing. Beyond this, the proposal outlines several innovative bomb types, ranging from customizable improvised explosives to powerful aircraft-deployed munitions like napalm, kinetic shockwave bombs, and improved chemical gas bombs, each designed to offer distinct tactical advantages and create more varied combat scenarios.

## Key Takeaways
- Players should be able to survive at least one explosion, similar to mechanics in GTA 4 and RDR2.
- Explosions should have devastating, immersive effects, including shrapnel wounds, visual blinding, and auditory ringing.
- Introduce handmade, throwable improvised bombs that can be modified at weapon workshops with effects like proximity detonation, incendiary properties, or high explosive payloads.
- Implement 'proper napalm bombs' for aircraft, creating large fireballs and spreading long-burning gel capable of cooking occupants inside vehicles.
- Add 'kinetic bombs' that produce a massive shockwave, launching vehicles and people, with secondary impacts (e.g., hitting a wall) being fatal.
- Improve gas bombs to include disorienting chemical effects, rapid damage over time, and ragdoll physics.
