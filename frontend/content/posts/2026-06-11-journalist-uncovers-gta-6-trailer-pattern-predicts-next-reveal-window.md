# Journalist Uncovers GTA 6 Trailer Pattern, Predicts Next Reveal Window

A recent analysis, credited to Reddit user mjideau, challenges common theories regarding the release timing of GTA 6 Trailer 3. Contrary to beliefs that it would avoid the World Cup or wait until after the next GTA Online DLC, the theory highlights a consistent pattern. Both previous GTA 6 trailers were released before a GTA Online DLC, strategically leveraging the trailer's hype to funnel players back into the GTA ecosystem, boosting engagement and monetization. This pattern is further linked to the unusual timing of GTA+ benefit cycles, which sometimes shift to a Monday before a Tuesday DLC release, rather than the usual Thursday. With the current GTA+ benefits ending on Monday, July 13, pointing to a July 14 DLC, the analysis predicts that GTA 6 Trailer 3 could drop between June 21 and June 30.

- Common theories regarding GTA 6 Trailer 3 release timing (e.g., avoiding World Cup, waiting for DLC) are questioned.
- A clear pattern links GTA 6 trailer releases, GTA Online DLCs, and GTA+ benefit end dates.
