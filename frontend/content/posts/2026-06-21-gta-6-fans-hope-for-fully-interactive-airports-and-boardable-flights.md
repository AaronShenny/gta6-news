# GTA 6 Fans Hope for Fully Interactive Airports and Boardable Flights

A recent Reddit discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit reveals a strong community desire for a significantly more interactive and immersive airport experience in Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/dot_nautillus articulated the hope that Vice City International will offer fully enterable terminals, a stark contrast to the largely inaccessible Los Santos International Airport in GTA 5. The post speculates on the potential for dynamic gameplay scenarios, such as chaotic heists or escape missions, unfolding within crowded airport environments, complete with TSA checkpoints and baggage carousels. Furthermore, the idea of players being able to purchase tickets and physically board commercial flights, even if only for scenic rides or travel within the expansive map, is presented as a highly sought-after detail that would greatly enhance immersion, leveraging Rockstar's promised NPC density and open-world design.

## Key Takeaways
- Fans are expressing a strong desire for fully enterable airport interiors in GTA 6.
- The hope is for dynamic missions, such as heists or escapes, to occur within bustling airport terminals.
- Players wish to be able to purchase tickets and physically board commercial flights for travel or scenic rides.
- This level of detail is seen as a major improvement over GTA 5's largely inaccessible airport.
- The speculation is fueled by Rockstar's promises of increased immersion and NPC density in Leonida.
