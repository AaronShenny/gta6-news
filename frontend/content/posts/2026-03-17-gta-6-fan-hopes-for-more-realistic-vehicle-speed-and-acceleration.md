# GTA 6 Fan Hopes for More Realistic Vehicle Speed and Acceleration

A Reddit user, /u/HarveySpecter777, has voiced a common sentiment among gamers regarding the unrealistic feeling of speed and acceleration in many titles, including Grand Theft Auto V. The user notes that often, driving at high speeds, such as 100 km/h, feels significantly slower in-game. They highlight BeamNG.drive as a benchmark for realistic vehicle physics and express a strong desire for Grand Theft Auto 6 to deliver a more authentic and immersive sense of speed and acceleration.

## Key Takeaways
- User /u/HarveySpecter777 criticizes the unrealistic feeling of speed and acceleration in many games, including GTA V.
- They observe that high speeds (e.g., 100 km/h) often feel much slower in-game.
- BeamNG.drive is praised as the only game known to accurately simulate speed and acceleration.
- The user expresses hope for a more realistic driving experience in Grand Theft Auto 6.
