# GTA 6 Confirmed as Single-Player at Launch; Pricing and Preorder Details Revealed

Rockstar Games has officially announced that Grand Theft Auto 6 will launch on November 19 as a dedicated 'single-player experience,' with its online multiplayer component, GTA Online, not included initially. This mirrors the launch strategy of Grand Theft Auto V, which saw its online mode arrive weeks after the main game. While preorders for GTA 6 will include a free month of GTA+, hinting at a future connection to the evolving GTA Online world, the specific form that multiplayer will take for GTA 6 remains largely unconfirmed. The game will be available for $80 on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S, with a $100 Ultimate Edition offering additional story missions. Even the 'physical' editions will require an internet connection, as they will contain a download code. Preorders for GTA 6 are set to open on June 25.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 launches as a 'single-player experience' on November 19.
- GTA Online will not be included at launch, similar to GTA 5's initial release.
- The future of GTA 6's online multiplayer functionality is currently unclear.
- Standard edition priced at $80 on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S.
- Ultimate Edition priced at $100, includes extra story missions.
- Preorders for GTA 6 open on June 25.
- Physical editions will contain a download code, requiring an internet connection.
- Preorders include a free month of GTA+.
