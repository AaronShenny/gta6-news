# GTA 6 Fan Plans Medically Induced Coma to 'Time Travel' to Release Day

A Reddit user, /u/AffectDelicious8988, has captured the gaming community's attention with a unique and humorous approach to coping with the anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6. In a post titled 'I'm putting myself in a medically induced coma,' the user declared intentions to undergo the procedure next week. Their reasoning? To effectively 'time travel' to the game's release, stating that for them, due to the relative nature of time during a coma, GTA 6 will 'literally be out next week.' The post, which also included an 'AmA!' (Ask Me Anything) invitation, highlights the extreme excitement and longing within the GTA community for the upcoming title.

- Reddit user /u/AffectDelicious8988 announced plans for a medically induced coma.
