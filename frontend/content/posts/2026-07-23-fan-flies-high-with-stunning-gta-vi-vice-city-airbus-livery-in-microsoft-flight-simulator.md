# Fan Flies High with Stunning GTA VI Vice City Airbus Livery in Microsoft Flight Simulator

A talented fan, known as /u/simtom on Reddit, has brought the world of Grand Theft Auto VI and Vice City into Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 with a stunning custom livery. Designed for the Fenix A319/320/321 aircraft, this fan-made creation features an elaborate exterior design reminiscent of their previous A350 GTA VI livery, but now includes a fully customized cabin interior.

The creator invested significant time in detailing the textures, ensuring an authentic and immersive experience for virtual pilots. This custom livery allows players to 'fly' to Vice City in style, blending the excitement of the upcoming GTA title with the realism of Microsoft Flight Simulator.

Enthusiasts can download this impressive mod by searching for 'Fenix A3XX GTA VI Collection' on flightsim.to.
