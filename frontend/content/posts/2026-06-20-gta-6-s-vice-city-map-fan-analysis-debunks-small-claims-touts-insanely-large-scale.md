# GTA 6's Vice City Map: Fan Analysis Debunks 'Small' Claims, Touts 'Insanely Large' Scale

A recent fan analysis from the GTA 6 community strongly refutes claims that the upcoming Vice City map will be small. The author, using a ferris wheel as a landmark reference and comparing it to real-life Miami, argues that even if the in-game city is scaled down by a third, it remains an 'insanely large' area for a video game. The analysis highlights significant distances, such as between Downtown and Vice Beach, and anticipates an incredibly immersive open-world experience, suggesting that some fans may be underestimating the sheer scale and complexity Rockstar Games is set to deliver.
