# Community Brainstorm: Unique Gun Features Proposed for GTA 6

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit sparked discussion by proposing several unique gunplay features that could enhance the Grand Theft Auto 6 experience. The user, u/CapitalSwimming715, suggested a 'weapon jamming' mechanic where guns could jam after repeated firing, requiring a quick button press to clear. This feature, they note, could add strategic depth to online gunfights. Another novel idea was the 'bayonet attachment' for assault rifles and potentially shotguns, allowing players to perform a melee stab up close, with the caveat that it would be incompatible with suppressors. Finally, the user expressed support for the leaked rumor of GTA 6 adopting an RDR2-style weapon system, limiting players to one weapon per class for a more streamlined inventory.

## Key Takeaways
- Weapon jamming mechanic proposed, requiring a button press to clear a jammed gun.
- Bayonet attachment suggested for assault rifles and shotguns, enabling close-quarters melee attacks.
- Bayonet attachment would be mutually exclusive with suppressor attachments.
- Support expressed for an RDR2-style weapon inventory system, limiting players to one weapon per class.
