# GTA 6: Could Jason and Lucia's Fate Lead to Multiple Endings?

The highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI is already a hotbed for fan theories, and a recent discussion on Reddit has sparked interest in the possibility of multiple endings. User /u/cooleminemedit proposed an intriguing scenario centered around the game's protagonists, Jason and Lucia. The core idea revolves around a pivotal choice for Jason: either break up with Lucia or propose marriage. This decision, envisioned to mirror the branching narrative mechanics seen in Grand Theft Auto IV where players choose between two distinct mission paths, could lead to significantly different concluding missions. The speculation suggests that choosing to propose could culminate in a unique wedding mission as the game's finale, offering players a deeply personal impact on the characters' romantic future.
