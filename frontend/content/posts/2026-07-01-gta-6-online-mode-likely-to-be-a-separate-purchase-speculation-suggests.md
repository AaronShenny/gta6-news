# GTA 6 Online Mode Likely to be a Separate Purchase, Speculation Suggests

Recent speculation circulating online suggests that Grand Theft Auto VI will launch as a purely single-player experience, with its highly anticipated online component potentially being sold as a separate product or standalone game. This theory, put forth by a Reddit user, draws on several pieces of circumstantial evidence.

The core argument hinges on Rockstar's historical marketing. Past titles like Grand Theft Auto V and Red Dead Redemption 2 explicitly featured mentions of their respective online modes on their physical box art. In contrast, the PlayStation Store listing for GTA 6 currently describes the game as a "single-player experience." Further support for this idea comes from the terms of a GTA 6 pre-order bonus, which includes one month of GTA+. While GTA+ for GTA V was tied to online benefits, an FAQ section on the PlayStation support page for the GTA VI offer explicitly states, "Grand Theft Auto VI is a single-player experience" when asked about multiplayer modes.

Adding weight to the speculation is a prior statement from well-known Rockstar insider Tez2, who reportedly indicated last year that GTA Online for the upcoming title would be separate from GTA 6. While the author of the original post acknowledges that this is purely speculative and unconfirmed, the cumulative evidence presented points towards a potential shift in Rockstar's strategy for its hugely popular online offering.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's online mode is speculated to be sold separately from the base single-player game.
- Rockstar's previous titles (GTA V, RDR2) explicitly advertised their online components on base game packaging, a detail missing for GTA 6.
- The official PlayStation Store listing and a GTA+ offer FAQ for GTA 6 both describe it as a 'single-player experience'.
- Reputable Rockstar insider Tez2 previously stated that GTA Online would be separate from GTA 6.
- This information is currently unconfirmed speculation and not an official announcement from Rockstar Games.
