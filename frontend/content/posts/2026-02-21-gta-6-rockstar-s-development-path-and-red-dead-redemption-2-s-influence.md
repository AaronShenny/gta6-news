# GTA 6: Rockstar's Development Path and Red Dead Redemption 2's Influence

For fans wondering about the extensive wait for Grand Theft Auto VI, a recent Reddit post by u/siddharth1214 sheds light on Rockstar Games' development timeline. The post clarifies that Rockstar was actively engaged in the production of Red Dead Redemption 2 until its release in 2018, with GTA 6 development commencing immediately thereafter. Countering notions that Rockstar 'wasted time' on RDR2 instead of focusing solely on GTA 6, the author suggests a significant shift in Rockstar's company philosophy. Based on various comments, statements, and interviews, the post posits that GTA 6 will diverge from its predecessor, GTA V, by adopting many characteristics akin to Red Dead Redemption 2, essentially envisioning it as 'RDR3 set in modern time.'

## Key Takeaways
- Rockstar Games worked on Red Dead Redemption 2 until its 2018 release.
- GTA 6 development began immediately after RDR2's completion.
- The long wait for GTA 6 is attributed to RDR2's extensive development cycle.
- Rockstar's company philosophy has reportedly shifted.
- GTA 6 is expected to draw significant inspiration from Red Dead Redemption 2, rather than GTA V.
- The game is described as potentially being 'RDR3 set in modern time' due to its anticipated depth and style.
