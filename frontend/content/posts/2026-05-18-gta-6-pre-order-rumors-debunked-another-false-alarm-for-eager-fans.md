# GTA 6 Pre-Order Rumors Debunked: Another False Alarm for Eager Fans

Recent widespread rumors suggesting that Grand Theft Auto 6 pre-orders would begin on Monday, May 18, have been definitively debunked. These claims, reportedly stemming from emails received by content creators in Best Buy's affiliate program, had also fueled speculation about the imminent release of a third trailer for Rockstar's highly anticipated title. However, an administrator on the unofficial GTA Forums, citing a source within a European distribution company, confirmed that 'there's no pre-orders,' a report corroborated by IGN. As of the rumored date, GTA 6 had not appeared on any major online retail platforms. The constant anticipation for GTA 6 has led to a pattern of fan restlessness and a tendency to 'grasp at straws,' with this latest rumor even causing a temporary fluctuation in the stock price of Rockstar's parent company, Take-Two. With over a year having passed since the game's second trailer, official details remain scarce, beyond a few select quotes from Take-Two boss Strauss Zelnick, such as the confirmation that GTA 6 will not feature product placement. While some fans now look to Take-Two's upcoming earnings report on Thursday, May 21, for potential news, and the company has indicated a significant marketing push will commence 'this summer,' the article advises the community to temper expectations to avoid recurring disappointment.
