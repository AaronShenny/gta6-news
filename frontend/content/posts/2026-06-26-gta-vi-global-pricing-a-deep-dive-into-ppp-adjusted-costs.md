## FAQ
**What is Purchasing Power Parity (PPP)?**
PPP is an economic theory that compares different countries' currencies through a 'basket of goods' approach. It suggests that, in the long run, exchange rates should adjust so that an identical basket of goods and services would cost the same in different countries.

**Why would game publishers use PPP for pricing?**
Using PPP can help publishers set prices that are more affordable and fair for consumers in different economic regions, potentially increasing sales volume and reducing piracy in markets where a flat international price would be prohibitively expensive.
