# GTA 6 Safe House Speculation: Could Jason and Lucia Dynamically Relocate?

A recent fan theory from a Reddit user proposes an intriguing gameplay mechanic for Grand Theft Auto 6: the ability for protagonists Jason and Lucia to change their primary safe house location. The speculation suggests that while the main story unfolds in Vice City, players could travel back to the Leonida Keys, establishing it as their active 'main' safe house. This would mean that character switches and game boot-ups would consistently place the characters in the Keys area, rather than Vice City. The user draws a parallel to Michael's temporary relocation to Sandy Shores in GTA V, envisioning in-game dialogue to acknowledge these travels and suggesting such a feature would add an engaging layer of dynamic residency to the gameplay experience.

## Key Takeaways
- The theory suggests Jason and Lucia might be able to switch their main safe house location.
- Proposed scenario involves starting in the Leonida Keys, progressing to a Vice City safe house, then having the option to return to the Keys as the primary base.
- If characters relocate to the Keys, all character switches and game boot-ups would occur in that area.
- The concept is compared to Michael's temporary stay in Sandy Shores in Grand Theft Auto V.
- In-game dialogue is envisioned to enhance immersion during travels between locations (e.g., Lucia commenting on going back to the Keys).
- This is a fan-submitted speculation, not officially confirmed information about GTA 6.
