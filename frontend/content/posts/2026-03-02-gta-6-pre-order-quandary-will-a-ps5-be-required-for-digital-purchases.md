## FAQ
**Do I need to own a PS5 to digitally pre-order GTA 6?**
While official details for GTA 6 pre-orders are still unconfirmed, it's generally unlikely that you would need to own the physical console to place a digital pre-order. Major game publishers typically make digital pre-orders available through various channels, including their official websites, dedicated console storefronts accessible via web browsers or mobile apps (e.g., PlayStation Store online), and third-party digital retailers. This allows players to secure their copy well in advance, even if they plan to purchase the console closer to the game's release.
