# GTA VI: Community Hopes for Removal of Mission Completion Medals

A discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user /u/DAHbaddest, highlights a common sentiment among players regarding the mission completion medal system in previous Grand Theft Auto titles. The original poster expresses a strong hope that this feature will not return in GTA VI. They criticize the demanding and often frustrating requirements, such as strict time limits, high accuracy targets, minimal damage taken, or specific headshot counts, which they feel detracted from the gameplay experience. Describing the system as 'padding' and demotivating, especially when receiving low-tier medals despite successfully completing a mission, the sentiment points to a desire for a more streamlined and less punitive mission structure in the upcoming installment.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user /u/DAHbaddest hopes mission completion medals are not included in GTA VI.
- Criticism targets the strict and specific requirements for earning gold medals (e.g., time limits, accuracy, damage taken, headshots).
- The medal system is perceived as 'padding' and a source of frustration rather than enjoyment.
- Receiving low-tier medals despite completing a mission is seen as demotivating.
