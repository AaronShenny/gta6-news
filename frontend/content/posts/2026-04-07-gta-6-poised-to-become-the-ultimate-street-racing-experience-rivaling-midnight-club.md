# GTA 6 Poised to Become the Ultimate Street Racing Experience, Rivaling Midnight Club

A recent discussion highlights the strong possibility that Grand Theft Auto 6 could deliver the definitive street racing experience, effectively absorbing the essence of Rockstar's dormant Midnight Club series. The argument posits that GTA has long borrowed elements like street races, car meets, and extensive vehicle customization. With GTA 6's anticipated scale and Rockstar's ownership of FiveM, the game is uniquely positioned to offer features such as underground racing, high-stakes illegal activities, crew-vs-crew combat, and pervasive car culture across single-player, online, and custom server environments. This integration could make GTA 6 the largest Midnight Club-style game ever created, a strategic move that also makes considerable business sense for Rockstar.
