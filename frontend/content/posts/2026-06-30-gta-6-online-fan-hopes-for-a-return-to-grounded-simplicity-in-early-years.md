# GTA 6 Online: Fan Hopes for a Return to Grounded Simplicity in Early Years

A Grand Theft Auto fan shared their unique perspective on what they're anticipating for the first 1-2 years of GTA 6 Online. Drawing a parallel to the early days of GTA 5 Online, which they describe as 'mostly grounded' in its vehicles, weaponry, and customization, the author expressed a longing for a similar experience in the upcoming installment. They criticized GTA 5 Online's later evolution, where 'ridiculous shit' like rocket bikes and flying supercars made co-op missions trivial and 'bloated' the game. The hope is that GTA 6 Online will initially offer a more straightforward progression, where players grind to earn items like their first apartment or supercar, rather than instantly accessing high-tier content. While acknowledging the potential impact of microtransactions like 'Shark Cards' on this progression, the author still looks forward to new mechanics and a return to 'simpler' times, albeit with a lingering fear that Rockstar might introduce 'off the wall goofy shenanigans day 1' and dash these hopes.

## Key Takeaways
- The author enjoyed the early, grounded experience of GTA 5 Online.
- Criticism is directed at GTA 5 Online's later content, which became 'bloated' with unrealistic items like rocket bikes and flying cars.
- Hope for GTA 6 Online's initial 1-2 years to offer a simpler, more grounded gameplay experience.
- Desire for a progression system where players earn basic items like apartments and supercars through gameplay.
- Concern that microtransactions (Shark Cards) or immediate inclusion of 'goofy shenanigans' could undermine this desired early experience.
