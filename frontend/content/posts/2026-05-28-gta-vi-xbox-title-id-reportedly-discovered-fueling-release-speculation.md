# GTA VI Xbox Title ID Reportedly Discovered, Fueling Release Speculation

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan, known as BlackAnt02, has reportedly uncovered the specific Title ID for Grand Theft Auto VI on Xbox Series S/X through deep dives into Xbox APIs. This discovery, shared on Reddit, provides a tangible link to the upcoming highly anticipated title, offering details such as the Product ID, Decimal and Hexadecimal Title IDs, and the game's package name. While PlayStation IDs for GTA VI were already publicly known, this new finding offers a fresh piece of information for Xbox enthusiasts and further fuels speculation and excitement surrounding the game's eventual release, though Rockstar Games has not officially confirmed these details.

- The discovery was reportedly made through 'deep searching of Xbox API's.'
- Specific Xbox details include Product ID: '9NL3WWNZLZZN', Title ID Decimal: '2035456122', and Hexadecimal: '7952987A'.
