# The Mystery is Making GTA VI Even More Exciting for Me

A Reddit user, u/Lebow316, has expressed a contrarian view on Rockstar's quiet marketing strategy for Grand Theft Auto VI, suggesting that the mystery surrounding the game is actually intensifying their excitement. While acknowledging the widespread anticipation for more trailers, gameplay footage, and screenshots, the user states that the initial two trailers have already generated sufficient hype. They argue that knowing 'just enough' creates an incredible sense of anticipation, preserving the magic of discovering Vice City and its secrets for the first time upon release. Despite the difficulty of resisting future reveals, u/Lebow316 indicates a strong preference for going into the game as 'blind as possible' to maximize the exploration experience, contrasting with the common desire for more pre-release information.

## Key Takeaways
- Some players find the current lack of extensive GTA 6 marketing more exciting.
- The first two trailers provided enough information to build significant anticipation.
- A desire to experience Vice City and game elements for the first time, without spoilers, enhances the magic.
- The sentiment contrasts with the general community's demand for more marketing materials and reveals.
