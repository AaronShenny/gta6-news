# GTA 6: Inside Gaming Report Suggests Potential Digital-Only Release, No Physical Discs Planned

A recent post on Reddit's r/GTA6 subreddit has brought to light a significant report from 'Inside Gaming,' suggesting a potential shift in release strategy for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. According to the report referenced in the post's title, there are currently 'no plans' for Rockstar Games to ever produce physical disc copies of GTA 6. This unconfirmed information points towards the possibility of GTA 6 being a digital-only launch, a move that aligns with an increasing industry trend towards digital distribution but could potentially disappoint fans who prefer collecting physical media or have limited internet access.

## Key Takeaways
- Inside Gaming reportedly claims Rockstar has 'no plans' for physical GTA 6 discs.
- This suggests the possibility of Grand Theft Auto 6 being a digital-only release.
- Such a strategy would reflect broader industry shifts towards digital distribution.
- The news could be a point of contention for collectors and players preferring physical copies.

## FAQ
**Is GTA 6 officially confirmed to be digital-only?**
No, the information originates from an 'Inside Gaming' report cited in a Reddit post, not an official statement from Rockstar Games. It remains unconfirmed.

**What does this mean for players who prefer physical copies of games?**
If the report proves true, players will only be able to purchase and download Grand Theft Auto 6 digitally, potentially impacting collectors and those with specific preferences for physical media.

**Has Rockstar Games commented on this report?**
As of the Reddit post, there has been no official comment or confirmation from Rockstar Games regarding their plans for physical or digital distribution of GTA 6.
