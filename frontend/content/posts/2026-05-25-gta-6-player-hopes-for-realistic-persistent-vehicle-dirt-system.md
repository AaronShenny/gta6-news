# GTA 6 Player Hopes for Realistic, Persistent Vehicle Dirt System

A passionate Reddit user, Upbeat-Driver-4378, has shared their fervent hope for Grand Theft Auto 6 to incorporate an advanced and persistent dirt and mud system for vehicles. Criticizing the common 'magic clean' mechanic seen in many arcade racers where vehicles instantly shed grime, the user advocates for a more realistic approach. They envision a system where mud and dirt accumulate and remain until actively washed off, naturally cleaned by heavy rain, or partially removed by driving through deep water. The post also emphasizes the importance of realistic terrain interaction, such as tires digging into mud and different vehicle types handling challenging terrain appropriately, to foster a deeper sense of immersion and make exploration more rewarding. The user believes Rockstar Games is uniquely positioned to deliver the most advanced mud and dirt system ever seen in an open-world game.

## Key Takeaways
- Desire for a realistic and persistent vehicle dirt and mud system in GTA 6.
- Rejection of 'magic clean' arcade-style mechanics where vehicle dirt disappears quickly.
- Preference for dirt to remain until washed, naturally cleaned by rain, or by driving through deep water.
- Call for realistic terrain interaction, including tire deformation in mud and varied vehicle handling based on type.
- Emphasis on immersion, with vehicles visibly reflecting past offroad adventures.
- Belief that Rockstar Games can deliver the most advanced dirt and mud system in an open-world title.
