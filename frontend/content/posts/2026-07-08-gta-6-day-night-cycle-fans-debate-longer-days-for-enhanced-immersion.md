# GTA 6 Day/Night Cycle: Fans Debate Longer Days for Enhanced Immersion

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user u/No_Fun_9350, has opened up a debate about the preferred length of the day and night cycle in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto VI. The original poster questioned whether Rockstar should stick to the established 48-minute cycle seen in GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2, or if a longer duration would be more beneficial.

User u/No_Fun_9350 personally advocated for longer days, suggesting that this would allow for more gradual and realistic dawn/dusk transitions, as well as enabling weather events to unfold more naturally. The ultimate goal, as proposed, is to achieve a more convincing and immersive experience for players, potentially with days and nights lasting for several real-world hours.

## Key Takeaways
- The current day/night cycle in GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2 is 48 minutes.
- A Reddit user proposes longer day/night cycles for GTA VI.
- Longer cycles could lead to more natural dawn/dusk transitions and realistic weather events.
- The primary aim of a longer cycle is to enhance player immersion and realism.
