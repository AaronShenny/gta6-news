# GTA 6 Boss Says High Review Scores Reflect Rockstar’s “Commitment To Quality”

Take-Two Interactive CEO Strauss Zelnick is highly confident that Grand Theft Auto 6, slated for release on November 19 for Xbox Series X|S and PlayStation 5, will achieve stellar critical reviews. Zelnick highlighted Rockstar Games' consistent track record of mid-to-high 90s review scores, asserting this reflects the studio's deep commitment to quality. While acknowledging the continued importance of strong reviews, Zelnick's outlook was tempered by Future content director Dan Dawkins, who pointed out the drastically altered media landscape since GTA 5's 2013 release, where social media ensures immediate and diverse critical narratives. Despite these challenges and rising costs, Zelnick remains bullish on GTA 6's prospects, believing consumers will embrace quality entertainment, and hinted at a 'reasonable price' for the highly anticipated title.
