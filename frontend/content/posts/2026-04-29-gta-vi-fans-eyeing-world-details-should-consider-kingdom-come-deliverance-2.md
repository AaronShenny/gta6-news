# GTA VI Fans Eyeing World Details Should Consider Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2

A Reddit user on the r/GTA6 subreddit, u/Liukka123, has made an unconventional recommendation for Grand Theft Auto VI enthusiasts. Despite the stark genre differences, they suggest that players who are particularly excited about GTA VI for its potential for world exploration, discovering small details, testing game systems, observing NPCs, and generally soaking in a rich atmosphere, should consider trying Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2. The user purchased KCD2 recently and was 'blown away' by its beauty and atmosphere, rating it a perfect 5/5 for those looking for an immersive world experience before GTA VI's release.

## Key Takeaways
- Recommendation for GTA VI fans to try Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 (KCD2).
- Target audience: Players excited by GTA VI's world exploration, small details, system testing, and NPC observation, rather than just missions or chaos.
- KCD2 praised for its beauty and immersive atmosphere.
- User rating: 5/5 recommendation for those seeking a detailed and atmospheric game.
- Highlighting KCD2 as a potential game to enjoy while waiting for GTA VI.
