# Seven Years of Anticipation: Fan Recalls GTA 6 Hopes with Original Cover Art

A Reddit user, /u/90skid_, recently revisited a piece of fan-made Grand Theft Auto VI cover art they created seven years ago, offering a poignant look back at the early days of speculation. At the time, the only real clues about the game's direction were rumors of a Vice City setting and a Mexican actor's CV listing 'GTA 6.' The user expressed disbelief at the extended wait, acknowledging that they've spent over seven years in a state of anticipation. They confessed that they never imagined the development would take so long and declared an unwillingness to wait this long for any piece of media again, underscoring the immense pressure on Rockstar Games to deliver a truly spectacular title.
