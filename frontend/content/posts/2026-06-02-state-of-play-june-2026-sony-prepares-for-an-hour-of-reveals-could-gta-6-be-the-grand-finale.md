# State of Play June 2026: Sony Prepares for an Hour of Reveals, Could GTA 6 Be the Grand Finale?

PlayStation is gearing up for its next State of Play livestream on June 2, promising an extensive showcase of upcoming titles from both first-party studios and third-party partners. The broadcast, set to run for over an hour, will kick off with a look at Marvel's Wolverine. While Sony remains tight-lipped on other specific reveals, industry whispers suggest potential appearances from titles like Arc System Works' Marvel Tokon: Fighting Souls, Horizon: Hunters Gathering, and Naughty Dog's Intergalactic: The Heretic Prophet. Also rumored are a God of War Ragnarok spin-off, a re-reveal for Haven Studios' FairGame$, Persona 6, and a Rayman Origins remaster. However, the biggest speculation revolves around Rockstar Games' Grand Theft Auto 6. With a long silence since its second trailer and a known marketing deal with Sony, many are hoping for a new look at the highly anticipated open-world crime epic, especially with its rumored November release, which would undoubtedly serve as a show-stopping conclusion to the event.

## Key Takeaways
- PlayStation State of Play livestream scheduled for June 2.
- The broadcast will last over an hour, featuring updates, announcements, and gameplay reveals.
- Marvel's Wolverine is confirmed to kick off the show.
- Potential first-party reveals include Marvel Tokon: Fighting Souls, Horizon: Hunters Gathering, Naughty Dog's Intergalactic: The Heretic Prophet, a rumored God of War Ragnarok spin-off, and a re-reveal for Haven Studios' FairGame$.
- Third-party speculation points to Persona 6 and a Rayman Origins remaster.
- Grand Theft Auto 6 is a major speculative highlight, with hopes for a new look given Rockstar's marketing deal with Sony and its rumored November release date.
- The livestream will be available on Sony's official YouTube and Twitch channels.

## FAQ
**When is the PlayStation State of Play livestream?**
The PlayStation State of Play livestream is scheduled for June 2, at 2 PM PT / 5 PM ET / 10 PM BST / 11 PM CEST. For viewers in Japan, it will be at 6 AM JST on June 3.

**How long will the State of Play broadcast be?**
Sony has confirmed that the livestream will feature over an hour of updates, announcements, and gameplay reveals.

**Where can I watch the State of Play?**
The livestream will be broadcast live on Sony’s official YouTube and Twitch channels.

**What games are confirmed to appear at the State of Play?**
Officially, Marvel's Wolverine is confirmed to kick off the show. Other titles are currently speculative but highly anticipated.

**Is GTA 6 expected to be shown at the State of Play?**
While not confirmed, GTA 6 is a significant point of speculation. Its long silence since the second trailer, coupled with Rockstar's marketing deal with Sony and a rumored November release, makes it a strong candidate for a major reveal to conclude the show.
