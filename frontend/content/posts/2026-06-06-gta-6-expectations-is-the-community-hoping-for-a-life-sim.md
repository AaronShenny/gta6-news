A Reddit user, /u/ChaosTheory404, has sparked discussion by observing that some community expectations for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6 seem to lean more towards a 'real-life simulator' than a typical GTA game. While expressing personal excitement for GTA 6, the user noted a perceived disconnect between what some fans envision and the core identity of the series, drawing a humorous comparison to The Sims. The post concludes with confidence that GTA 6 will be an amazing and innovative title, but not a life simulation game.
