# Sony Confirms GTA 6 Marketing Deal in Latest Earnings Call

During its latest earnings call on May 8, Sony brought Grand Theft Auto 6 into the spotlight, officially confirming an ongoing marketing deal with Rockstar Games. The company's mention underscored its commitment to fostering an environment where players can "enjoy their favorite titles wherever they are and connect with their friends through gameplay," highlighting the strategic importance of major upcoming titles like GTA 6 for the PlayStation ecosystem.

## Key Takeaways
- Sony highlighted Grand Theft Auto 6 during its May 8 earnings call.
- The company officially confirmed a marketing deal with Rockstar Games for GTA 6.
- Sony reiterated its focus on players enjoying titles across platforms and connecting with friends through gameplay.
