# GTA 6 Fans Hope for Extensive Property and Business Ownership to Avoid Money Redundancy

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit highlights a common frustration among players: the lack of meaningful ways to spend in-game currency once basic needs are met. User u/Ultimate_Kratos11723 articulated a hope for Grand Theft Auto 6 to include extensive property and business ownership options. Drawing parallels to Red Dead Redemption 2, GTA 5, and even GTA 4, the user noted that despite accumulating vast sums of money, there was little beyond clothes and weapons to invest in. The desire is for GTA 6 to offer a wealth of money sinks, such as purchasing multiple properties, owning more diverse businesses than seen in GTA 5, or even acquiring lavish mansions, ensuring that wealth accumulation remains a rewarding and purposeful endeavor throughout the game.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user u/Ultimate_Kratos11723 wants to buy properties and businesses in GTA 6.
- Frustration stems from previous titles (RDR2, GTA 5, GTA 4) where money became redundant after basic purchases.
- Hopes for a wider variety of money sinks in GTA 6, including more diverse businesses than GTA 5 and the ability to purchase mansions.
- Aims to ensure players always have engaging ways to spend their accumulated in-game wealth.
