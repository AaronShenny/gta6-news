However, *RDR2* ultimately exceeded expectations, launching as a highly polished experience that looked even better than its trailers.

In conclusion, the author firmly believes that *GTA 6* will "blow a lot of people's minds," echoing the sentiment after *Red Dead Redemption 2*'s release. While acknowledging Rockstar's "anti-consumer" tendencies in other areas, the article emphasizes their unparalleled ability to create games with an insane level of detail, urging players not to doubt their technical prowess until given a concrete reason.
