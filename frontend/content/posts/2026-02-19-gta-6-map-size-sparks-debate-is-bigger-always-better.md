# GTA 6 Map Size Sparks Debate: Is Bigger Always Better?

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user u/SnooCrickets9713, highlights a growing concern among some fans regarding the rumored immense scale of Grand Theft Auto 6's map. While acknowledging a love for expansive open worlds, the user questions the adage that 'bigger is better,' drawing parallels to the perceived emptiness in certain areas of GTA V. With claims suggesting the GTA 6 map could be 3.5 times larger than its predecessor, and early trailers showcasing numerous aircraft, the user expresses hope that Rockstar Games will effectively justify the vastness and ensure the world remains engaging and filled with content.
