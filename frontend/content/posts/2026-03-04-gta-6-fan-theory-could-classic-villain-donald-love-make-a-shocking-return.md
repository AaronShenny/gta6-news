# GTA 6 Fan Theory: Could Classic Villain Donald Love Make a Shocking Return?

A popular fan theory originating from Reddit speculates on the potential return of Donald Love, a prominent character from the Grand Theft Auto 3D Universe, in the highly anticipated GTA 6. Despite Rockstar Games' general practice of maintaining a clear separation between the 3D and HD Universes, the theory highlights several canonical links that could theoretically pave the way for his reappearance. These include Love's existence being acknowledged within the HD Universe through an Easter Egg and a 2009 interview, the historical carryover of other 3D Universe characters like Fernando and Moorehead, and the canonization of Lazlow's 'Chatterbox' radio show from GTA III within the HD Universe. The latter suggests that Love Media, Love's company, should also exist in the current timeline. While the original poster admits that a full return is unlikely given Rockstar's stance on universe separation, they suggest that Love's reappearance, even as a minor character or story element, would be a "cool twist" for the game.

## Key Takeaways
- A fan theory suggests Donald Love, a character from GTA III, could potentially reappear in GTA 6.
- Love is canonically acknowledged in the HD Universe through an Easter Egg and a 2009 Rockstar interview.
- Other 3D Universe characters, such as Fernando and Moorehead, have seen carryovers or mentions in the HD Universe.
- Lazlow's GTA III radio show, 'Chatterbox,' is confirmed as canon in the HD Universe, implying Love Media's existence.
- The theory acknowledges Rockstar's clear separation between 3D and HD Universes, making a return unlikely.
- Despite the low probability, the author believes Love's reappearance would be a 'cool twist' for GTA 6.
