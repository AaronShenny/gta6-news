# GTA 6: Community Hopes for Enhanced In-Car Radio Volume and Dynamic Controls

A recent discussion on the GTA 6 subreddit highlights a popular community request: improved in-car radio volume control. User /u/Iebronsigma articulated a desire for the ability to adjust radio volume on the fly while driving, specifically calling for a much louder maximum setting than was available in Grand Theft Auto V. The user pointed out that even with GTA V's music volume maxed out, it often wasn't loud enough, contrasting this with the prevalent loud car audio culture in Miami, a likely inspiration for GTA 6's setting. This feature, they suggest, would significantly enhance the immersion and experience, particularly within the game's online component.

## Key Takeaways
- Request for dynamic, on-the-fly radio volume adjustment in GTA 6.
- Desire for a significantly louder maximum volume than GTA 5 offered.
- GTA 5's radio volume was often perceived as too low, even when maxed out in settings.
- Inspired by the loud car audio culture common in Miami, a potential setting for GTA 6.
- Believed to enhance both single-player and online gaming experiences.
