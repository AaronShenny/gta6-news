# GTA 6 Fan Wishes for RDR2-Style Ecosystem, Random Encounters, and Honor System

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by user /u/OppositeSuccessful58 has garnered attention, outlining specific features they hope to see implemented in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. The user expressed a strong desire for GTA 6 to mirror the sophisticated open-world ecosystem and dynamic random encounters that defined Rockstar's critically acclaimed Red Dead Redemption 2. Furthermore, the post highlighted a wish for an honor system, similar to RDR2's moral choices, which would allow players to influence their protagonist's demeanor, offering the choice to play as either a 'rude' or 'polite' character, thereby adding depth to player agency and narrative interaction.

## Key Takeaways
- Desire for GTA 6 to feature an open-world ecosystem akin to Red Dead Redemption 2.
- Hopes for dynamic and engaging random encounters, drawing inspiration from RDR2's design.
- Suggestion for an honor system, allowing players to choose between 'rude' or 'polite' character interactions.
- Emphasis on increased player agency and moral choices within the game's world.
