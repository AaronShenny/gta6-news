# GTA 6: Could Rockstar Finally Unify Its Expansive American Universe?

A recent discussion among fans speculates that Grand Theft Auto VI could be a pivotal title for Rockstar Games, potentially unifying its distinct American game universes. The core idea revolves around whether locations from Red Dead Redemption, such as Lemoyne, New Austin, and Ambarino, will coexist or at least be referenced alongside established GTA regions like Leonida, San Andreas, and Alderney. Furthermore, the discussion delves into the long-standing question of canonizing and naming the state in which Liberty City resides, with suggestions ranging from 'Rotterdam' to 'The State Of Liberty,' solidifying its place within Rockstar's overarching lore.

## Key Takeaways
- Speculation that GTA 6 will merge Grand Theft Auto and Red Dead Redemption universes.
- Question of whether RDR locations (e.g., Lemoyne, New Austin) will exist alongside GTA locations (e.g., Leonida, San Andreas).
- Discussion on canonizing and officially naming Liberty City's state.
- Proposed names for Liberty City's state include 'Rotterdam' or 'The State Of Liberty'.
