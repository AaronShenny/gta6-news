# GTA 6's Vice City Realism Hits Close to Home for Miami Residents

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit captures the profound impact of Grand Theft Auto 6's anticipated realism on real-world residents. User u/PlayfulInvestment649, a self-identified Miami local, shared their 'scary' yet deeply immersive experience viewing early screenshots. The user highlighted how strikingly similar in-game locations appeared to their actual surroundings, leading to a mix of awe and trepidation. The post humorously, yet genuinely, conveys the feeling of contemplating in-game chaos, such as RPGs and supercars, potentially appearing 'outside my house' in the virtual Vice City.
