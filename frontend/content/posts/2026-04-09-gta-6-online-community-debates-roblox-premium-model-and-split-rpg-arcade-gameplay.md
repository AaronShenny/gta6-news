# GTA 6 Online: Community Debates 'Roblox Premium' Model and Split RPG/Arcade Gameplay

A recent discussion on the GTA 6 subreddit, initiated by user /u/Responsible_Today658, has sparked debate regarding the potential direction of GTA 6 Online. The user's post title speculated whether Rockstar might introduce a 'Roblox Premium'-like model, hinting at potential subscription services or premium content. However, the core of the user's argument focused on gameplay mechanics, expressing a strong desire for Rockstar to develop two distinct versions of GTA 6 Online. One version would incorporate deeper RPG elements, appealing to fans of role-playing, while the other would retain the more traditional, arcade-like gameplay style familiar from previous Grand Theft Auto Online iterations. The user suggested that a single, RPG-heavy GTA Online might not resonate with all players, highlighting a perceived need for choice in gameplay experiences.

## Key Takeaways
- Speculation about a 'Roblox Premium'-like model for GTA 6 Online.
- Desire for two separate GTA 6 Online game modes.
- One game mode with RPG elements for role-playing enthusiasts.
- Another game mode maintaining an arcade-like gameplay style, similar to previous GTA Online.
- Concern that a single RPG-focused GTA Online might not appeal to all players.
