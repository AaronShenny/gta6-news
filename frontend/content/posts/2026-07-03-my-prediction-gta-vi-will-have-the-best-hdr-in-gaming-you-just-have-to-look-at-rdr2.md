# My prediction: GTA VI will have the best HDR in gaming. You just have to look at RDR2.

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan and Reddit user, avidrunner84, has put forth a bold prediction: Grand Theft Auto VI is poised to deliver the finest High Dynamic Range (HDR) experience seen in any video game to date. The user bases this strong claim on Rockstar Games' previous work, specifically highlighting Red Dead Redemption 2's exceptional HDR implementation. Despite being a 2018 title developed on the Rage Engine for PlayStation 4, RDR2 received an HDR update (1.09) in 2019 that, when set to 'Game HDR' and properly calibrated for the display, offers one of the most visually stunning HDR experiences available. The user emphasizes that achieving proper HDR is a complex process, not just a simple addition, but the visual rewards are immense for those with compatible displays. Looking ahead to GTA VI's setting in Vice City, the enthusiast anticipates a truly spectacular visual fidelity. There's also a hope that HGiG (HDR Gaming Interest Group) mode will ensure players can experience GTA VI's HDR exactly as the developers intended, addressing concerns that many players might be using standard dynamic range (SDR) or uncalibrated HDR setups.
