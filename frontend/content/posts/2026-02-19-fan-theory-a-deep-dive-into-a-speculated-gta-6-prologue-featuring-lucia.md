# Fan Theory: A Deep Dive into a Speculated GTA 6 Prologue Featuring Lucia

A recent discussion on Reddit's r/GTA6 community has sparked interest with a detailed fan prediction for Grand Theft Auto VI's opening sequence. User /u/The_Honest_Owl speculates a playable prologue where players control Lucia years before the main story, introducing her as the franchise's first female protagonist. This dramatic opening would see Lucia involved in a robbery, making the difficult decision to abandon her baby before being apprehended. The prologue would conclude with Jason facilitating her release from jail, setting the stage for the game's core narrative. Following this intense introduction, the theory suggests a music-driven title sequence, potentially featuring Steve Winwood's 'Freedom Overspill,' showcasing the vast and diverse locales of Leonida.
