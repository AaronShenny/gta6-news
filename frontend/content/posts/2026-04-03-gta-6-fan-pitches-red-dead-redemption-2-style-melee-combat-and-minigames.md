# GTA 6 Fan Pitches Red Dead Redemption 2-Style Melee Combat and Minigames

As anticipation builds for Grand Theft Auto 6, a passionate Reddit user, /u/Mister_Gta_Kapitan, has outlined a wishlist of features they hope to see in the upcoming title. Foremost among these is the robust and strategic melee combat system from Red Dead Redemption 2. The user lauded RDR2's detailed approach, citing the varied weapons, blocking mechanics, grappling, and unique animations as 'chef's kiss' elements that made melee a viable and enjoyable strategy. Additionally, they expressed a longing for card or tabletop minigames, though with admittedly low expectations for their inclusion. Despite these specific hopes, the user conveyed a general optimism that GTA 6 will deliver an immense amount of content, enough to keep players engaged for the next 10-12 years.

## Key Takeaways
- Strong desire for Red Dead Redemption 2's detailed melee combat system to be implemented in GTA 6.
- Appreciation for RDR2's melee features, including varied weapons, blocking, grappling, and unique animations.
- Hope for the inclusion of card or tabletop minigames, albeit with low expectations.
- Belief that GTA 6 will provide extensive content, offering replayability for over a decade.
