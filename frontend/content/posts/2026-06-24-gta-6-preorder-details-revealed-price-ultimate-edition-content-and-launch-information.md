# GTA 6 Preorder Details Revealed: Price, Ultimate Edition Content, and Launch Information

Rockstar Games has officially detailed the pricing and preorder bonuses for Grand Theft Auto 6, with preorders set to launch on June 25 at midnight local time. The standard edition will cost $79.99, while the Ultimate Edition is priced at $99.99. A significant change is the absence of traditional physical copies; all versions, including shipped editions, will contain a download code. Preordering grants a free month of GTA+, which includes $500,000 in-game cash and access to the GTA+ Games Library. The Ultimate Edition boasts a comprehensive collection of exclusive vehicles, weapons, apparel, and activities, meticulously woven into Jason and Lucia's story. Players can also look forward to a preload starting on November 12, 2026, ensuring they're ready for the global launch on November 19.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 preorders launch on June 25 at midnight local time.
- The Standard Edition will cost $79.99, and the Ultimate Edition will be $99.99.
- There will be no traditional physical copies of GTA 6; all versions will provide a download code.
- Preorders include a free month of GTA+, granting $500,000 in-game cash and access to the GTA+ Games Library.
- The Ultimate Edition offers a vast array of in-game bonuses, including exclusive vehicles ('95 Grotti Cheetah, '67 Vapid Dominator Buggy), unique weapons (Hawk & Little Morgan Revolvers, personalized pistols), Vice City-themed outfits and tattoos (Electric Fang Tattoo), and access to special locations and customizations (Sara's Unisex Salon, Rideout Customs, One-Eyed Willie's).
- A Vintage Vice City Pack is available for preorders made before November 20, featuring a '55 Vapid Stanier sedan, a personal garage, exclusive outfits, hairstyles, and a Tommy Vercetti-inspired weapon pattern.
- A game preload for GTA 6 will commence on November 12, 2026, leading up to the November 19 launch.
