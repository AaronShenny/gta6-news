# Will Pay ‘N’ Spray make a comeback??

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked speculation among fans regarding the future of vehicle customization in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/tjackso6 raised the question of whether the iconic 'Pay 'N' Spray' mechanic, a staple in earlier GTA titles, might make a return. This comes amidst observations that 'Los Santos Customs', introduced in GTA V as a parody of popular car customization shows like *West Coast Customs* and *Pimp My Ride*, may no longer be culturally relevant. The post suggests that Rockstar Games might opt for a nostalgic return to the simpler, classic 'Pay 'N' Spray' system, or perhaps introduce an entirely new approach to vehicle repairs and modifications, moving away from the more elaborate, reality-show-inspired customization of GTA V.
