# GTA 6 Fans Campaign for May 26 Trailer to Honor Ray Liotta's Legacy

A poignant suggestion from the Grand Theft Auto community is gaining traction: fans are advocating for Rockstar Games to release a GTA 6 gameplay trailer on May 26. This date would serve as a powerful tribute to the late Ray Liotta, the beloved voice actor behind the iconic protagonist Tommy Vercetti from Grand Theft Auto: Vice City. The proposal, initially shared on Reddit, aims to honor Liotta's significant contribution to the series' legacy on the anniversary of his passing.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user on r/GTA6 proposed May 26 as the release date for a GTA 6 gameplay trailer.
- The suggested release date is intended as a tribute to the late actor Ray Liotta.
- Ray Liotta famously voiced Tommy Vercetti, the protagonist of Grand Theft Auto: Vice City.
- The community sees this as an opportunity to honor Liotta's lasting impact on the GTA franchise.
