# Fan Proposes Enhanced Drug and Alcohol Mechanics for GTA 6

A Grand Theft Auto fan has voiced their desire for significantly more immersive and detailed drug and alcohol mechanics in GTA 6. Dissatisfied with the brief, 10-second effects seen in GTA V, the user envisions a system where substances like LSD could induce "crazy visuals" in first-person, and cocaine could lead to "jittery" sensations. The proposal includes dose-dependent effects, the introduction of after-effects, and even a tolerance system for frequent use. Given the rumored Vice City setting, known for its vibrant party scene, the fan believes this mechanic could reach an unprecedented level of realism and immersion.

## Key Takeaways
- Desire for more immersive and longer-lasting drug/alcohol effects compared to GTA V.
- Suggestion of dose-dependent effects.
- Specific examples: LSD visuals in first-person, cocaine jitters.
- Inclusion of after-effects and a tolerance build-up system.
- Belief that the rumored Vice City setting is ideal for enhanced mechanics.
