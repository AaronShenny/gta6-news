# GTA 6: Will Cheat Codes Be 'Massively Nerfed' Again?

A dedicated fan has initiated a community discussion regarding the potential status of cheat codes in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. Recalling their early experiences with Vice City and San Andreas, the user, /u/Sparks_H, shared how cheat codes provided 'purest joy,' especially as a non-English speaker who used them to create sandbox fun rather than follow the story. They specifically remember the 'PANZER' code for spawning a tank. However, their experience with GTA V saw a significant reduction in cheat code functionality, leading to concern that GTA 6 will continue this trend, making cheats an 'afterthought' rather than a core part of the game's playful appeal.

## Key Takeaways
- User expresses deep nostalgia and 'purest joy' for cheat codes in classic GTA titles like Vice City and San Andreas.
- Cheat codes allowed for sandbox fun and exploration, particularly for a non-English speaking child who didn't understand missions.
- GTA V is noted for having 'heavily nerfed' cheat codes compared to earlier installments.
- The user fears that GTA 6 will similarly treat cheat codes as an 'afterthought.'
- The post serves as a community prompt for other fans to share their opinions on the future of cheats in GTA 6.
