# GTA 6 Community Calls for Robust Single-Player Content and Classic Features

In a recent Reddit post on r/GTA6, user u/Western-Stress1185 voiced a passionate appeal for Grand Theft Auto 6 to deliver a substantial single-player experience, particularly for those who don't engage with online modes or lack access to services like PS Plus. The user emphasized the critical need for a wealth of content beyond the main narrative. Specific requests included a broad selection of vehicles to purchase, an abundance of side missions available after the main story, and the reintroduction of beloved classic activities such as Vigilante, Firemen, and Paramedic missions. A significant point of contention was the desire for a lengthy story campaign, ideally comparable in scope to Red Dead Redemption 2, and the highly requested inclusion of a New Game+ mode. This sentiment highlights a community desire for Rockstar Games to balance its evident focus on GTA Online with a deep, replayable single-player offering, especially for players without access to the modding community on PC.

## Key Takeaways
- Strong demand for extensive single-player content in GTA 6.
- Desire for a wide variety of cars available for purchase.
- Request for numerous side missions to be available after the main story concludes.
- Call for the return of classic activities like Vigilante, Firemen, and Paramedic missions.
- Hope for a lengthy story campaign, ideally comparable to Red Dead Redemption 2.
- Explicit request for the inclusion of a New Game+ mode.
- Acknowledgement of Rockstar's focus on online but a plea for balanced single-player support.
- Emphasis on the importance of single-player content for players without PC mod access.
