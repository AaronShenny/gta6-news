# Will GTA 6 Face an Avalanche of Instant Criticism Upon Release?

A discussion on the GTA 6 subreddit, initiated by user /u/NYdamico13, expresses apprehension regarding the forthcoming public reception of Grand Theft Auto 6. The user speculates that despite the inherent presence of detractors for any major release, GTA 6 might encounter an unusually rapid wave of negative judgment. This concern stems from an observed cultural shift where new media, including films and video games, are frequently subjected to swift and often decisive criticism within a very short timeframe of their release. The sentiment underscores a fear that the initial public perception of GTA 6 could be significantly shaped by these quick-to-form, impactful opinions, potentially overshadowing a more measured evaluation.

## Key Takeaways
- Concerns raised about GTA 6's immediate public reception.
- Anticipation of rapid negative feedback and 'hater' sentiment upon release.
- Observation of a cultural trend: quick judgment of new media (films, games) within minutes or days.
- Fear that this trend could unfairly influence GTA 6's initial critical and public perception.
