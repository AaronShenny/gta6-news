# Fan Theory: GTA Online's Dramatic End Could Pave Way for GTA 6 Online

A compelling fan theory circulating among the Grand Theft Auto community proposes a dramatic narrative conclusion for GTA Online, directly linking it to the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. The theory suggests that players' existing online characters could be sabotaged by the elusive Mr. Faber, a figure previously warned about by Michael De Santa. This forced betrayal would necessitate players to 'go off the grid' and relocate to Leonida, the rumored setting for GTA 6, effectively initiating a fresh online experience within the new title. The theory gains traction from a recent in-game dialogue where the character Avi advises players to 'delete Snapmatic, change name, and go off the grid,' which many interpret as a direct nod to this potential storyline.
