# GTA 6 Community Humores Lack of Official Jason & Lucia Artwork Amidst Persistent Information Drought

The anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6 continues to build, but official details remain scarce, leading to a palpable 'information drought' among its dedicated fanbase. A recent Reddit post within the r/GTA6 community humorously encapsulates this frustration, asking users to identify the 'worst' artwork depicting the rumored protagonists, Jason and Lucia. The post, which features only a generic Reddit image placeholder, quickly became a focal point for fans lamenting the absence of any official visual content. One user, u/Individual_Type8874, pointed out the irony, stating that the 'worst' artwork 'honestly gotta be the first one for me because of The Great 2024 Information Drought making it the only GTA 6 artwork.' This sentiment underscores the community's collective yearning for new information, official screenshots, or character art for the highly anticipated title. The discussion serves as a lighthearted, yet poignant, reminder of the ongoing wait for Rockstar Games to unveil more about the next installment in the Grand Theft Auto series.
