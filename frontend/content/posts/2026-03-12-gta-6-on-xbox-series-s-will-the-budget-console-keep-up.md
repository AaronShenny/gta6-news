# GTA 6 on Xbox Series S: Will the Budget Console Keep Up?

As anticipation builds for Grand Theft Auto 6, a recent discussion on Reddit highlights growing concerns among Xbox Series S owners regarding the console's ability to deliver an optimal experience. A user, identifying as a casual gamer who has generally enjoyed titles on their Series S, expresses apprehension about GTA 6's performance and graphical presentation. The primary worry is that the game might suffer from significant visual compromises or performance issues, drawing an unfavorable comparison to the troubled launch of Cyberpunk 2077 on PlayStation 4. This sentiment underscores a broader community debate about the Series S's long-term viability for demanding next-generation titles.

## Key Takeaways
- An Xbox Series S owner is concerned about GTA 6's performance and visual quality on their console.
- The user fears a significant graphical downgrade or performance issues, likening it to Cyberpunk 2077's problematic PS4 launch.
- Despite being a casual gamer generally satisfied with the Series S, doubts persist for a title as demanding as GTA 6.
- The discussion reflects a broader community debate about the Xbox Series S's capability to handle upcoming AAA games compared to the PS5 and Series X.

## FAQ
**What is the main concern regarding GTA 6 on Xbox Series S?**
The primary concern is whether the Xbox Series S, being a less powerful console compared to the Series X and PS5, will be able to run Grand Theft Auto 6 without significant visual compromises or performance issues, potentially leading to a substandard experience.

**Why are gamers worried about the Series S specifically?**
The Xbox Series S has lower graphical processing power and RAM compared to its more expensive counterparts, leading some to question its ability to handle demanding next-generation titles like GTA 6 at acceptable resolutions and frame rates.

**Is there a precedent for these types of performance issues?**
Yes, the Reddit user specifically references the troubled launch of Cyberpunk 2077 on PlayStation 4, where the game suffered from severe performance and graphical issues, setting a benchmark for what gamers hope to avoid with GTA 6 on Series S.

**Has Rockstar Games commented on Series S optimization for GTA 6?**
As of now, Rockstar Games has not released detailed technical specifications or optimization plans specifically addressing the Xbox Series S version of Grand Theft Auto 6. Further details are expected closer to the game's release.
