# Is Sony Pushing a Digital-Only Future with GTA 6's Disc-Less Launch?

A recent theory circulating among gaming enthusiasts suggests that the absence of a physical disc release for the highly anticipated *Grand Theft Auto 6* might be part of a larger strategy by Sony to transition consumers towards digital-only game purchases. While initial speculation pointed to reasons like preventing leaks or curbing second-hand sales, the new hypothesis links it to PlayStation's visibly strong partnership with Rockstar Games.Evidence cited includes PlayStation's recent rebranding of app logos with GTA VI themes and Rockstar's own advertisements promoting GTA VI to 'play best on PS5,' notably omitting Xbox in some instances. Given the unprecedented hype and guaranteed massive sales for *GTA 6*, the theory posits that Sony could have struck a deal with Rockstar to make the game a digital-exclusive launch title. This move would leverage *GTA 6*'s immense popularity to gently nudge a vast player base into the digital storefront ecosystem, effectively normalizing digital-only purchases for future titles.
