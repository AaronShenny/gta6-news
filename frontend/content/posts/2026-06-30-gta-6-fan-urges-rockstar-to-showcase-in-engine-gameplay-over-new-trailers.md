# GTA 6: Fan Urges Rockstar to Showcase In-Engine Gameplay Over New Trailers

A dedicated Reddit user, u/UnchainedGoku, has voiced a strong desire for Rockstar Games to release actual in-engine gameplay footage of Grand Theft Auto VI, rather than another cinematic trailer. The user emphasizes the need to see the game running on current-gen consoles (PS5/Series X/S) to understand its look, playability, and feel. 

The core concern stems from the fact that pre-orders for GTA 6 are already open and Rockstar is "making bank," yet no substantial gameplay demonstration has been provided. With the game's release slated for 2026, and only approximately five months remaining until that year begins, the user finds the continued lack of in-motion footage "crazy." While acknowledging that Rockstar might be holding back for a grand reveal or "spectacle/black magic reasons," the user also expresses a more pessimistic worry about potential "worrisome performance reasons" as a cause for the delay in showing gameplay.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user strongly advocates for in-engine GTA 6 gameplay footage, preferring it over a third cinematic trailer.
- Concerns are raised about the absence of gameplay despite pre-orders being open and the 2026 release window approaching rapidly.
- The user speculates Rockstar is withholding footage either for maximum spectacle or due to potential performance issues.
- The ongoing lack of gameplay is deemed 'crazy' given the relatively short time remaining until the 2026 launch.
