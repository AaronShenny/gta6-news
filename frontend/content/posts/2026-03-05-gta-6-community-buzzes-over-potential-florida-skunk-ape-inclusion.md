## FAQ
**What is the Florida Skunk Ape?**
The Florida Skunk Ape is a cryptid, similar to Bigfoot, said to inhabit the swamps and wilds of Florida. It is often described as a large, hairy, ape-like creature known for its foul odor.

**Why would the Skunk Ape be relevant to GTA 6?**
GTA 6 is set in Leonida, a fictional state heavily inspired by Florida. Its inclusion would be a nod to Florida's unique local legends and could serve as an intriguing Easter egg or side mission for players to discover within the game's open world.
