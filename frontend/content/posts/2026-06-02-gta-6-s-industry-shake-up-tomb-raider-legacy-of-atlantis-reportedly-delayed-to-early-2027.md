# GTA 6's Industry Shake-Up: Tomb Raider: Legacy of Atlantis Reportedly Delayed to Early 2027

The gaming industry continues to brace for the seismic impact of Grand Theft Auto 6, with its influence reportedly causing other major titles to re-evaluate their release strategies. According to a recent post, 'Tomb Raider: Legacy of Atlantis' is the latest game to adjust its launch schedule, now slated for February 10, 2027. This marks a significant shift from its original target of Autumn 2026. The article's evocative title, 'GTA 6 spares no one,' underscores the prevailing sentiment that developers are actively seeking to avoid direct competition with Rockstar's highly anticipated blockbuster, ensuring their own titles have ample space to thrive in the market.

## Key Takeaways
- "Tomb Raider: Legacy of Atlantis" release date has been delayed.
- The new launch date is reportedly February 10, 2027.
- The game was originally planned for an Autumn 2026 release.
- The delay is attributed to the anticipated market dominance of Grand Theft Auto 6.
