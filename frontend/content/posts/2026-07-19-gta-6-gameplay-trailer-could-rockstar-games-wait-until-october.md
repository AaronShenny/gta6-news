# GTA 6 Gameplay Trailer: Could Rockstar Games Wait Until October?

A Reddit post from user /u/Tight-Opinion9932 suggests that Rockstar Games might hold off on releasing the official GTA 6 gameplay trailer until October, despite many fans anticipating a September reveal. The author argues that with the game's launch set for **November 19**, an October gameplay reveal would create peak hype just weeks before release. 

The reasoning behind this prediction highlights that two cinematic trailers have already introduced the game's world and characters. What players are truly eager to see are actual gameplay mechanics: how driving feels, the HUD design, combat systems, police AI, player interactions, menu interfaces, and character switching. 

The post emphasizes that GTA 6, unlike many other titles, doesn't require an extensive, year-long marketing campaign due to its massive built-in audience. A late October trailer would serve as one monumental final marketing push, rather than revealing key gameplay elements months in advance. The author concludes by acknowledging that Rockstar's plans are unknown but expresses no surprise if gameplay isn't shown before October.
