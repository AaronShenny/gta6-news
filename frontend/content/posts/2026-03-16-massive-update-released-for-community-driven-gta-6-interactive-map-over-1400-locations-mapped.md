# Massive Update Released for Community-Driven GTA 6 Interactive Map, Over 1400 Locations Mapped

A passionate community developer, known by the Reddit handle u/kalterapfel123, has unveiled a substantial update to their highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI interactive map project. This latest iteration introduces a revamped user interface and a wealth of new features, significantly enhancing the ability for fans to explore and speculate about the upcoming game world. The map now meticulously details over 1400 locations across the fictional states of Leonida and Vice City, incorporating precise screenshot spots with preview images directly from the first trailer, and mapping all known locations revealed in Trailer 1. The developer acknowledges the collaborative effort, crediting Yanis for the base map layer and the broader GTA mapping community and GTADB for invaluable location data, emphasizing that while the UI is a work-in-progress, its utility for pre-release exploration is undeniable.

## Key Takeaways
- Over 1400 locations meticulously mapped across Leonida and Vice City.
- Features specific screenshot spots with preview images directly from the official trailer.
- All known locations from the first GTA 6 trailer are now integrated into the map.
- Includes an improved user interface, enhanced filters, and a refined marker system.
- Supports both English and German languages for broader accessibility.
- Built on collaborative community efforts, crediting Yanis for the base map layer and GTADB for landmark data.
- Aims to assist fan exploration and speculation in anticipation of GTA 6's release.
