# Will Bigfoot Stomp Back into Grand Theft Auto 6? Community Speculates on New Myths

Following a discussion initiated by Reddit user u/Solid_Cress_4341, the Grand Theft Auto community is actively debating the potential inclusion of Bigfoot in the highly anticipated GTA 6. Given Bigfoot's evolution from an urban myth in GTA San Andreas to an actual in-game character in GTA V, fans are expressing strong desires for its return. Alternatively, if the elusive cryptid doesn't make a comeback, players are hoping Rockstar Games will introduce an entirely new set of captivating in-game myths and easter eggs to discover, keeping the series' tradition of hidden secrets alive.

## Key Takeaways
- Bigfoot was a prominent myth in Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas.
- An 'actual' Bigfoot appeared as an easter egg in Grand Theft Auto V.
- Community members hope for Bigfoot's return in Grand Theft Auto 6.
- Alternatively, there's a strong desire for new, compelling in-game myths if Bigfoot is absent.
- The discussion highlights the community's engagement with GTA's hidden secrets and lore.
