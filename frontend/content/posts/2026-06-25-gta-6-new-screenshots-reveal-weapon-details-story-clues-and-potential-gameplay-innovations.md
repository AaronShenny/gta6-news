# GTA 6: New Screenshots Reveal Weapon Details, Story Clues, and Potential Gameplay Innovations

Rockstar Games has officially dropped new details for Grand Theft Auto 6, including pricing and preorder information, accompanied by a fresh batch of screenshots and artwork. While not explicitly revealing major story beats or deep dives into the Leonida setting, these new assets have allowed journalists to uncover intriguing tidbits. From unique 'his and hers' revolvers for protagonists Lucia and Jason, hinting at their intertwined narrative, to the return of President Hardin on in-game currency, the details are piling up. Most notably, a description for the '67 Vapid Dominator Buggy suggests a new 'fencing' mechanic for stolen goods at the Paradise Garage, potentially introducing a deeper level of consequence for criminal activities beyond just cash theft. The game is set to launch on November 19 for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.

## Key Takeaways
- Rockstar announced GTA 6 pricing, preorder details, and released new screenshots/artwork.
- The $100 Ultimate Edition includes 'Hawk & Little Morgan Revolvers,' custom-etched for Lucia Caminos and Jason Duval, with a possible Red Dead Redemption 2 Easter egg.
- President Hardin, previously seen in GTA Online, reappears on in-game currency.
- Lucia and Jason are shown together on a Shitzu Squalo watercraft, suggesting a story-centric moment and potential character switching.
- A new gameplay mechanic for fencing stolen goods at the 'Paradise Garage' is hinted at, potentially expanding criminal activities beyond just cash.
- Grand Theft Auto 6 launches November 19 on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.
