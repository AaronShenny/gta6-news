# RDR2’s NPC carrying was amazing — GTA VI trunks could take it even further

A discussion on the GTA 6 subreddit highlights a popular feature from Red Dead Redemption 2: the ability to carry NPCs. User /u/Clark__Fent expresses hope that GTA 6 will adopt and expand upon this mechanic, suggesting that modern vehicles, particularly their trunks, could serve a similar role to horses in RDR2 for transporting bodies. The user envisions this leading to 'insane, modern ways' to interact with the game world, building on the reactive gameplay RDR2 offered.

## Key Takeaways
- Red Dead Redemption 2's NPC carrying mechanic was highly praised for its freedom and reactive world interactions.
- The Reddit user hopes GTA 6 will incorporate a similar system for carrying both dead and alive NPCs.
- A key suggestion is to utilize vehicle trunks in GTA 6 for transporting NPCs, mirroring the use of horses in RDR2.
- The proposed feature could lead to expanded and 'insane' modern gameplay possibilities within GTA 6.
