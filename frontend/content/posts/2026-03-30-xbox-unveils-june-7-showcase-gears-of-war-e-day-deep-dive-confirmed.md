# Xbox Unveils June 7 Showcase, Gears of War: E-Day Deep Dive Confirmed

Microsoft has officially announced its annual Xbox Games Showcase for Sunday, June 7, kicking off at 10 AM PT / 1 PM ET. Gamers can tune into the official Xbox YouTube and Twitch channels for first gameplay reveals and news from both first-party studios and key third-party partners. A significant highlight will be the immediate follow-up deep dive into Gears of War: E-Day, where developer The Coalition will unveil new details and gameplay. This highly anticipated prequel is set at the harrowing dawn of Emergence Day, featuring iconic characters Marcus Fenix and Dom in their first encounter with the Locust, aiming to recapture the original Gears' atmospheric horror. The event also marks the return of Xbox FanFest, commemorating 25 years of the brand. Under the leadership of new Microsoft Gaming CEO Asha Sharma, Xbox is committed to delivering "great games" and returning to its foundational principles, with strategic considerations for its 2026 tentpole releases, reportedly planning to avoid a direct clash with Grand Theft Auto VI.

## Key Takeaways
- **Date & Time:** Xbox Games Showcase confirmed for Sunday, June 7, at 10 AM PT / 1 PM ET.
- **Broadcast Channels:** Livestream available on official Xbox YouTube and Twitch.
- **Showcase Content:** Expect first gameplay looks and news from both first-party and third-party developers.
- **Gears of War: E-Day Deep Dive:** Immediately follows the main Showcase, offering new details and gameplay.
- **E-Day Premise:** Prequel set at the start of Emergence Day, starring Marcus Fenix and Dom, aiming to recreate the original Gears' "spooky vibes."
- **Xbox FanFest:** Returns to celebrate 25 years of the Xbox brand.
- **New Leadership Focus:** First Showcase under CEO Asha Sharma, emphasizing "great games" and a return to Xbox's roots.
- **Strategic Release Schedule:** Xbox is reportedly planning its 2026 tentpole releases to avoid direct competition with Grand Theft Auto VI.
