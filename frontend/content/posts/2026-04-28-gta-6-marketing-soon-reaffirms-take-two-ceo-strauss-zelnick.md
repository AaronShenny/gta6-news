# GTA 6 Marketing 'Soon,' Reaffirms Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick

Take-Two Interactive CEO Strauss Zelnick recently reconfirmed that marketing for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6 is on its way. Speaking with Variety's Jenny Maas, Zelnick stated that the marketing campaign would begin 'soon.' However, he refrained from providing any specific dates for when fans can expect the promotional efforts to kick off, maintaining the previously vague 'soon' timeframe.

## Key Takeaways
- Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick reconfirmed GTA 6 marketing efforts.
- Marketing for Grand Theft Auto 6 is expected to begin 'soon.'
- No specific date for the marketing launch was provided by Zelnick.
