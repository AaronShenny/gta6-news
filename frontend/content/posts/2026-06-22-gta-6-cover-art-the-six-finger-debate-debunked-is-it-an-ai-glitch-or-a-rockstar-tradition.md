# GTA 6 Cover Art: The Six-Finger Debate Debunked – Is it an AI Glitch or a Rockstar Tradition?

A recent viral discussion regarding the GTA 6 cover art has centered on an apparent anomaly: a character seemingly depicted with six fingers, leading many to speculate about the use of AI image generation. However, a dedicated Reddit user has stepped forward to decisively debunk these claims. The user points to a historical precedent within Rockstar Games' own portfolio, specifically the cover art for Grand Theft Auto IV from 2008. In GTA 4's cover, the character Lola also exhibits what appears to be six fingers. Given that advanced AI image generation was virtually non-existent in 2008, the post argues that this recurring detail is highly unlikely to be an AI artifact. Instead, it suggests the 'six-finger' motif could be either a consistent artistic oversight by Rockstar or, more intriguingly, an intentional inside joke or tradition among the development team, perhaps even a subtle nod in response to the modern prevalence of AI-generated content. The post concludes by assuring fans that the GTA 6 cover is not AI-generated.
