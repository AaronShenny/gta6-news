# GTA 6 Fans Flood Rockstar With Negative Google Reviews Amid Trailer Wait

Grand Theft Auto 6 fans are expressing significant impatience, a year after the game's last trailer. This frustration has led many to flood Rockstar North's Google Maps listing with negative reviews, specifically over the lack of fresh details. Beyond direct feedback, some fans are resorting to unusual speculative methods, such as a Reddit user analyzing foot traffic at a cafe near Rockstar's offices in an attempt to predict the next trailer drop. While these theories have yet to yield results, attention is now turning to Take-Two's upcoming investment briefing on May 21 as a potential date for new GTA 6 news. The game is currently slated for a November 19 launch on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S, and is projected to be one of the most expensive games ever developed, though Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick has suggested AI could help reduce future big-budget game costs.
