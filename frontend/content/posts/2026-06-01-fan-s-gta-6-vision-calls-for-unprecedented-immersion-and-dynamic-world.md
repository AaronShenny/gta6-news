# Fan's GTA 6 'Vision' Calls for Unprecedented Immersion and Dynamic World

A passionate Grand Theft Auto fan, u/MillionDollaDream, has shared their 'ideal vision' for GTA 6, moving beyond simple graphical upgrades and map size to focus on a deeply immersive and dynamic world. The core desire is for the game world to feel genuinely alive, providing players with compelling reasons to exist within it rather than merely traversing it. This vision includes highly advanced NPC AI, reactive social media and news systems, and a vast array of enterable buildings with actual gameplay value. The author also stresses the importance of a deep business ownership system and meaningful ways to spend money, addressing common criticisms of past GTA titles where wealth often became trivial. The post concludes by asking the community if such ambitious expectations are reasonable given Rockstar's development time and budget.

## Key Takeaways
- Hyper-realistic NPC AI, routines, and social interactions to create an 'alive' world.
- Dynamic social media and news systems that react to player actions.
- A large number of enterable buildings with gameplay value, including malls, restaurants, hotels, and offices.
- Functioning stadiums with events and crowds.
- Massive, explorable cruise ships and fully explorable airports with passenger flights (economy, business, first-class options).
- Diverse range of accommodations, from cheap motels to luxury 5-star resorts.
- Huge variety of vehicles with more realistic driving physics.
- A deep business ownership and criminal empire system.
- Meaningful things to spend money on throughout the game to maintain economic relevance.

## FAQ
**Are these ambitious expectations for GTA 6 reasonable given the development time and budget?**
The author of the 'vision' poses this question to the community, suggesting that while the demands are high, Rockstar's extensive development cycle and significant budget might make many of these immersive features achievable.
