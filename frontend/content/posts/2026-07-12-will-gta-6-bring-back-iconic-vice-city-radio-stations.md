# Will GTA 6 Bring Back Iconic Vice City Radio Stations?

A recent discussion on Reddit's r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user AssignmentBest7181, speculates on the potential return of iconic radio stations from Grand Theft Auto: Vice City in the upcoming GTA 6. The user points to the precedent set by Grand Theft Auto V, which saw the return of stations like Radio Los Santos and West Coast Talk Radio from Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. This has fueled hopes that Rockstar Games might implement a similar feature for GTA 6, which is expected to be set in a modern-day Vice City.

Specific stations mentioned by fans hoping for a comeback include Flash FM and V-Rock, both beloved for their nostalgic soundtracks. There's also a particular desire for Wildstyle to make a return, with a wish for it to feature modern hip hop and rap, adapting to the contemporary setting while retaining its classic identity. This discussion highlights the community's anticipation for how GTA 6 will blend new content with beloved elements from the series' rich history.
