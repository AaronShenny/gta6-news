# Dedicated Gamer Upgrades to PS5 Pro, Eyes 60fps for Grand Theft Auto 6

A dedicated gamer recently made the jump from a PlayStation 5 to a PS5 Pro, with the primary motivation being the highly anticipated release of Grand Theft Auto 6. The user expressed a strong desire to experience the generational title at a smooth 60 frames per second, drawing a contrast with their 30fps experience in Red Dead Redemption 2 and their current enjoyment of 60fps in modern PS5 story games like God of War Ragnarök and Horizon Forbidden West. While acknowledging the technical ambitions of Rockstar Games, the gamer stated a willingness to accept a lower resolution, such as 1440p, if it ensures a stable 60fps. They specifically hope for a performance mode on the PS5 Pro, potentially utilizing technologies like PSSR and optimized RTGI, to achieve this desired frame rate.
