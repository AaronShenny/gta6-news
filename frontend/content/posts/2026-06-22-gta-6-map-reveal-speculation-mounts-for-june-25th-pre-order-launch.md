# GTA 6 Map Reveal Speculation Mounts for June 25th Pre-Order Launch

Anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6 continues to build, with a recent Reddit discussion speculating on a potential partial map reveal as early as June 25th. The theory posits that if Rockstar Games announces special and collector's editions for GTA 6—coinciding with the start of pre-orders—an image or detail of the game's map could be included. This speculation is based on the precedent set by GTA V, where its special editions featured a blueprint map of Los Santos. While acknowledging that this is not a guarantee, the post suggests that if special editions are indeed unveiled, a glimpse of Vice City, or another section of the new map, is a strong possibility.

- GTA 6 pre-orders are anticipated to begin on June 25th.
