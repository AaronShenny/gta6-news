# Deep Dive into GTA 6 Leaks Reveals Potential Gameplay Innovations

A dedicated fan has meticulously analyzed Grand Theft Auto 6 leaks and trailers, sharing a speculative list of potential gameplay features. While acknowledging these observations are not official and may not appear in the final game, the analysis points to significant advancements in environmental physics, NPC behavior, and core mechanics. Key highlights include highly realistic water physics, a sophisticated NPC system where characters remember player interactions and maintain persistent roles, and the return of a detailed witness system potentially involving CCTV surveillance. The report also touches on personal vehicle maintenance, dynamic extreme weather events like hurricanes, and more destructible environmental elements such as trees, while noting that building destruction will likely remain minimal.

## Key Takeaways
- Enhanced water physics with realistic ripples and splashes.
- NPCs possessing unique identities who remember player interactions, potentially influencing relationships.
- Persistent NPCs occupying specific roles (e.g., lifeguard, shop owner) with simulated shift changes; replacements appear if killed.
- Requirement for personal vehicle maintenance, such as oil changes, to maintain performance.
- Dynamic and extreme weather conditions, including hurricanes and flooding, leveraging advanced water physics.
- A returning witness system, with CCTV cameras potentially playing a significant role in crime tracking and police investigations.
- Minimal building destructibility, but more realistic visual damage to structures and destructible environmental elements like trees.
