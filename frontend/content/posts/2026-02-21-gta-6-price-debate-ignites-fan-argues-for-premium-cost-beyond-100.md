# GTA 6 Price Debate Ignites: Fan Argues for Premium Cost Beyond $100

A passionate discussion has emerged on Reddit concerning the potential price of Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/FeelingSouth4837 expressed surprise and frustration at reports that some fans expect the highly anticipated title to cost under $100. The user argues that given GTA 6's unprecedented scale – which they deem the 'biggest Game Release ever' – and its rumored $2 billion development budget, a premium price is not only warranted but expected. The post reflects a sentiment of willingness to 'pay anything' for the game, challenging conventional pricing expectations in light of the massive investment and hype surrounding Rockstar's next blockbuster.

## Key Takeaways
- Some fans reportedly expect GTA 6 to be priced under $100.
- The Reddit user argues against this expectation, citing the game's massive scale.
- GTA 6 is considered by the user to be the 'biggest Game Release ever'.
