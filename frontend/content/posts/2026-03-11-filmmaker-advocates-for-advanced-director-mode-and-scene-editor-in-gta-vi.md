# Filmmaker Advocates for Advanced Director Mode and Scene Editor in GTA VI

A passionate filmmaker, currently utilizing Grand Theft Auto V's Director Mode and Rockstar Editor for significant film projects, has voiced a strong desire for an improved version of these creative tools in the upcoming GTA VI. The author lauds GTA V's existing mode as an unparalleled, accessible platform for animated filmmaking, bypassing the complexities of traditional 3D software. They envision limitless creative potential with GTA VI's next-gen graphics, expansive map, and diverse characters, provided an advanced Director Mode is integrated, ideally with fewer inherent limitations than its predecessor. While community mods currently address many of GTA V's shortcomings, the filmmaker hopes for native, enhanced functionalities in the new installment, particularly with the eventual PC release.

## Key Takeaways
- A filmmaker extensively uses GTA V's Director Mode and Rockstar Editor for creating feature-length and short films.
- The current Director Mode is praised as the best accessible tool for animated filmmaking without requiring complex 3D software knowledge.
- It leverages the game's vast resources, environments, and characters to enable diverse storytelling.
- Existing limitations in GTA V, such as character animations, are often overcome by community mods (e.g., EVE, mouth movement, camera limits, Menyoo trainer).
- The author hopes for an even better Director Mode in GTA VI, free of current limitations, to fully exploit the game's next-gen graphics, huge map, and distinct characters.
- The full potential of such a mode is anticipated to be realized primarily with the PC version of GTA VI.
- A direct plea is made to developers to include and enhance this feature.
