# GTA VI - NVMe Edition: A Satirical Look at Future Game Bundles

A striking 3D mock-up, dubbed 'GTA VI - NVMe Edition,' has captured the attention of the gaming community, showcasing a humorous take on future game bundles. Created in Blender by Reddit user u/Gerofied, this satirical concept imagines a physical package containing a 1TB NVMe SSD, perfectly 'paired for all your memory shortages,' alongside the digital version of GTA VI Ultimate Edition. The creator explicitly states the mock-up is 'completely built for satire,' prompting a discussion among fans about the practicality and desirability of such a unique, hardware-integrated game release. While not a real product, it highlights the ongoing anticipation for GTA VI and the ever-increasing storage demands of modern AAA titles.
