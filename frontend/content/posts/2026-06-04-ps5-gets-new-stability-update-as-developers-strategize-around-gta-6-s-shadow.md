# PS5 Gets New Stability Update as Developers Strategize Around GTA 6's Shadow

Sony has rolled out a new firmware update for PlayStation 5, Version: 26.04-13.40.00, which is a standard maintenance patch focused purely on improving system software performance and stability. Unlike recent updates that introduced features like PSSR for PS5 Pro or enhanced social widgets, this release offers no new functionalities. The update lands amidst a bustling period for the gaming industry, with a packed schedule of high-profile releases anticipated in late 2026. Notably, many developers are reportedly adjusting their launch windows, particularly in September, to avoid direct competition with the monumental release of GTA 6. Sony's major exclusive for the year, Marvel's Wolverine, continues to impress, alongside other demanding titles like Control Resonant and Marvel Tokon: Fighting Souls, showcasing the continued prowess of the nearly six-year-old PS5 hardware.

## Key Takeaways
- Sony released a new PS5 firmware update, Version: 26.04-13.40.00.
- The update's sole focus is on improving system software performance and stability, with no new features.
- Previous updates added significant features like PSSR for PS5 Pro and an improved Friends Activity widget.

- Marvel's Wolverine is highlighted as Sony's tentpole exclusive for 2026.
- Upcoming games like Control Resonant, Onimusha: Way of the Sword, and Marvel Tokon: Fighting Souls are pushing PS5 hardware limits.
