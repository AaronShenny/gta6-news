# GTA 6 Cover Under Scrutiny: Fan Questions Alligator Teeth Count

A keen-eyed fan on Reddit, known as /u/ITehJelleh, has ignited a debate within the Grand Theft Auto community by highlighting a potential anomaly on the official GTA 6 cover art. The user meticulously counted the teeth of the alligator featured on the cover, arriving at a total of 60. This count is notably lower than the typical range for real alligators, which usually possess between 74 and 80 teeth. The observation has led to widespread speculation, with fans questioning whether this discrepancy is a simple artistic mistake or if it holds a deeper, perhaps hidden, meaning relevant to the game's lore or future reveals.
