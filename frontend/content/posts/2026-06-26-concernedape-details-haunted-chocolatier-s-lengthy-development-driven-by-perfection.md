# ConcernedApe Details Haunted Chocolatier's Lengthy Development Driven by 'Perfection'

Eric 'ConcernedApe' Barone, the acclaimed creator of Stardew Valley, has provided an update on the long-awaited development of his next title, Haunted Chocolatier. In a new blog post, Barone addressed the game's five-year development cycle, emphasizing that the extended timeline is due to his rigorous pursuit of perfection in every aspect. He highlighted core mechanics, such as the chocolate recipe book, as needing to be 'seamless, clear, intuitive, satisfying, [and] aesthetic,' undergoing multiple iterations to achieve flawlessness. Barone also explained his cautious approach to revealing gameplay, preferring to present a 'fully baked bread' rather than risk player disappointment from early reveals that may change. Haunted Chocolatier, which promises a spooky experience centered on creating sweet treats and running a confectionery, currently has no release date, with the team remaining deep in development.

## Key Takeaways
- Haunted Chocolatier, from Stardew Valley creator Eric 'ConcernedApe' Barone, has been in development for five years since its announcement.
- Barone attributes the game's long development to a meticulous pursuit of perfection, ensuring every player interaction is 'seamless, clear, intuitive, satisfying, aesthetic'.
- The chocolate recipe book, a core gameplay element, is undergoing extensive iteration to be flawless and provide optimal player information.
- Barone is hesitant to reveal extensive gameplay footage to avoid showing 'half-baked bread' and prevent potential player disappointment if features change before launch.
- Haunted Chocolatier is envisioned as a spooky experience focused on managing a confectionery store and creating sweet treats.
- There is currently no official release date for the game, as development continues.
