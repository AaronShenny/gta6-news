# GTA 6: Will Rising PS Plus Costs and Single-Player Nostalgia Push Gamers Away From Online?

A long-time Grand Theft Auto enthusiast expresses their strong preference for the series' single-player story mode over its online component, particularly in light of recent PlayStation Plus price hikes. Citing a lack of engagement with GTA Online's repetitive missions in GTA V and fond memories of single-player rampages from earlier titles, the player states they are unlikely to subscribe to PS Plus solely for GTA 6's online features. The article then poses a question to the community: are many other players similarly prioritizing the story mode, or is the online experience the primary draw for the majority?

## Key Takeaways
- Rising PlayStation Plus prices are a significant deterrent for players considering GTA 6 Online.
- The author has a strong preference for GTA's single-player story mode, finding GTA Online repetitive and unengaging.
- Nostalgia for classic GTA single-player experiences (e.g., Vice City rampages) heavily influences player preference.
- The decision to skip GTA 6 Online is driven by both cost (not wanting to pay for PS Plus for one game) and perceived lack of quality/fun in past online modes.
- The article questions whether this sentiment is widespread, or if most players will still flock to GTA 6 Online.

## FAQ
**Will the increase in PlayStation Plus prices deter players from GTA 6 Online?**
The article suggests that rising PS Plus costs are a significant factor for some players, making them less inclined to pay for the subscription solely for GTA 6 Online, especially if they primarily play free-to-play online games.
