# Casual Gamers Misinformed on GTA 6 Release, Highlighting Need for New Trailer

A recent Reddit post by user /u/AirDifficult1 highlights a significant disconnect between the dedicated GTA 6 community and casual gamers regarding the game's release date. The user's 58-year-old father, a long-time fan of the series since Vice City, mistakenly believed Grand Theft Auto VI was launching in a matter of weeks. This confusion, likely stemming from older trailers, illustrates that many casual players are unaware of the game's actual, much later release window. The post argues that a new official trailer is crucial to update this broader audience, manage expectations, and prevent widespread disappointment among those not constantly following every development.

## Key Takeaways
- Many casual gamers are misinformed about the Grand Theft Auto VI release date.
- Some casual players believe GTA 6 is launching very soon, possibly due to outdated trailer information.
- A personal anecdote involving a 58-year-old father illustrates this widespread confusion.
- There is a clear need for a new official trailer to clarify the actual release timeline for a broader audience.
- The dedicated gaming community's awareness of delays doesn't extend to all potential players.
