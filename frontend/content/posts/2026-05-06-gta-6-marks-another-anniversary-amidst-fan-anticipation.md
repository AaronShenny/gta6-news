# GTA 6 Marks Another Anniversary Amidst Fan Anticipation

Today, May 6, marks a year since the release of the second Grand Theft Auto 6 trailer, adding to the fourth anniversary of the game's initial unveiling on February 4, 2022. Despite these milestones, fans have received little new information or a third trailer from Rockstar Games. The second trailer offered a detailed look at Vice City, protagonists Jason and Lucia, and confirmed a partnership with Sony, with the trailer captured on PS5. In May 2025, Rockstar also launched a dedicated GTA 6 website with new details. While new content has been scarce, Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick has commented on the game, stating that a significant marketing push will begin this summer, the game was "expensive" to make, and he is "terrified" of the expectations. Zelnick also hinted that GTA 6 would be priced at a "reasonable" level.

## Key Takeaways
- May 6, 2025, marked the one-year anniversary of the second GTA 6 trailer's release.
- February 4, 2022, was the fourth anniversary of the game's original unveiling.
- Fans have received almost no new information or a third trailer since the second trailer's release.
- Trailer 2 showcased more of Vice City, protagonists Jason and Lucia, and indicated a partnership with Sony (captured on PS5).
- Rockstar launched a GTA 6 website with new images and details in May 2025.
- Take-Two boss Strauss Zelnick confirmed the marketing push for GTA 6 will begin this summer.
- Zelnick described the game as "expensive" to make and expressed being "terrified" about expectations.
- He also stated that GTA 6 would be priced at a "reasonable" level, though specifics were not given.
