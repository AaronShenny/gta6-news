# GTA 6 Marketing Draws 'Weird' Reactions from Gaming Community

A recent discussion initiated on Reddit's r/GTA6 community reflects a growing sentiment of confusion and curiosity regarding Rockstar Games' promotional approach for Grand Theft Auto VI. The original poster articulates an 'honest opinion' that the marketing feels 'weird asf,' a pattern they observe to be consistent with Rockstar's historical tactics. A central point of contention revolves around the perceived availability of GTA 6 pre-orders, despite the absence of a third official trailer or any actual gameplay footage. This situation draws parallels to the marketing cycle of Grand Theft Auto V, where pre-orders reportedly opened in late 2012, sparking similar discussions and reactions within the community due to the then-limited gameplay showcases. The Reddit thread actively invites other users to contribute their honest opinions, fueling a broader conversation about the efficacy and reception of Rockstar's often enigmatic marketing strategies.
