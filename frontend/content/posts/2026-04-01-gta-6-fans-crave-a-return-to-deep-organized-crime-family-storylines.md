# GTA 6 Fans Crave a Return to Deep Organized Crime Family Storylines

A recent discussion among GTA 6 enthusiasts highlights a strong desire for the upcoming title to feature intricate organized crime family narratives, reminiscent of Grand Theft Auto IV. One Reddit user specifically expressed a wish to see mob families play a significant role, offering more engaging interactions and storyline opportunities than the cartel and triad factions seen in Grand Theft Auto V, which were primarily combat targets. Given Vice City's historical ties to real-world mob activity, many hope for a return to working for a powerful crime syndicate, echoing the narrative depth found in previous installments.

## Key Takeaways
- Fans desire the return of organized crime families in GTA 6.
- GTA 4's Pegorino family is cited as a positive example of engaging mob dynamics.
- GTA 5's Madrazo Cartel and Triads were considered 'lackluster' and primarily combat targets.
- Hope for narrative opportunities to work for a crime family, similar to Niko Bellic's experience.
- Vice City's (Miami's) real-world history with mob families makes this a fitting narrative direction for GTA 6.
