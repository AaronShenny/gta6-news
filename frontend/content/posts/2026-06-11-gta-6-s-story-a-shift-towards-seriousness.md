# GTA 6's Story: A Shift Towards Seriousness?

A discussion originating from the GTA 6 subreddit suggests that the upcoming game's story might adopt a more serious tone than its predecessor, Grand Theft Auto V. The user 'M1ikkaell' points to the seemingly grave demeanors of protagonists Jason and Lucia in the trailers as evidence. This contrasts sharply with GTA V's cast, which included numerous 'goofy meme worthy characters' such as Trevor, Michael's family, Lamar, and Lester. While acknowledging that GTA 6 will undoubtedly feature eccentric, Florida-inspired NPCs, the core narrative, as perceived from early glimpses, appears to be leaning towards a more earnest approach.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's story is speculated to be more serious than GTA 5's.
- Protagonists Jason and Lucia are perceived as serious based on trailer appearances.
- GTA 5 featured many 'goofy' characters like Trevor, Michael's family, Lamar, and Lester.
- The game is still expected to include eccentric, 'mad NPCs' inspired by real-life Florida.
