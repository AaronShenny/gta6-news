# GTA 6 Fan Concept: 'Final Concept of Leonida' Map Vision Surfaces

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan, u/Gyranos, has shared their 'Final Concept of Leonida' map, a highly speculative and wishful vision for GTA 6's setting. The concept draws inspiration from Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas's structure, featuring three distinct cities separated by rivers and numerous small towns. Emphasizing diverse biomes and plenty of activities, particularly within the Mount Kalaga National Park, the map aims to avoid barren landscapes. It details inspirations for key locations like Vice City (Miami), Port Gellhorn (Panama City/St. Petersburg), and Sundown (Daytona/Orlando/Tallahassee), even including the Bahamas (Lucayas) and unique icon explanations for various in-game activities.

## Key Takeaways
- The 'Final Concept of Leonida' is a fan-made, highly speculative map for GTA 6, not official.
- It proposes a three-city structure similar to Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas.
- Key cities include Vice City (based on Miami), Port Gellhorn (Panama City/St. Petersburg with a 'run-down' theme), and Sundown (an amalgam of Daytona, Orlando, Tallahassee with a theme park and racetrack).
- Mount Kalaga National Park is envisioned with diverse biomes and significant activities, rather than being a barren landscape.
- The concept includes the Lucayas (Bahamas, with Hessen as Nassau) and various smaller towns like Seaview (Marco Island) and Redhill (Blueridge, GA).
- Specific activities and locations are suggested, such as government missions in Sundown, Epsilon Program ties in Port Gellhorn, and burglary locations indicated by mask icons.
- The map includes a variety of icons for different store types, and even a basketball icon, hinting at diverse in-game interactions.

## FAQ
**Is this an official map for GTA 6?**
No, this is a highly speculative fan-made concept map created by Reddit user u/Gyranos, based on wishful thinking and creative outlets.

**What is 'Leonida' in this context?**
Leonida is the rumored fictional state in which Grand Theft Auto 6 is set, widely believed to be Rockstar's equivalent of Florida.

**What are the main cities proposed in this concept?**
The concept proposes three main cities: Vice City (based on Miami), Port Gellhorn (inspired by Panama City and St. Petersburg), and Sundown (a blend of Daytona, Orlando, and Tallahassee).

**Does the map include areas outside of the main Florida-inspired landmass?**
Yes, the concept explicitly includes the Lucayas, which are inspired by the Bahamas, with Hessen representing Nassau.

**What kind of activities are suggested for this map?**
The map suggests diverse activities including missions for government agencies (FIB/IAA) in Sundown, potential Epsilon Program content in Port Gellhorn, burglary locations, and general exploration in a vibrant Mount Kalaga National Park.
