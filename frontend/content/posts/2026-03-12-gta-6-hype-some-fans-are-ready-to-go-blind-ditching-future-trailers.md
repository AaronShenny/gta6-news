# GTA 6 Hype: Some Fans Are Ready to Go Blind, Ditching Future Trailers

Amidst the fervent anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6, a unique sentiment has emerged from a Reddit user on the /r/GTA6 subreddit: a hope that Rockstar *doesn't* release a third trailer. User /u/milankurchina expresses that the initial trailers have already 'sold' them on the game, and they now wish to go into GTA 6 'as blind as possible.' This preference stems from a desire to personally discover missions, locations, and random events upon the game's eventual release, maximizing the sense of exploration and surprise. The post also extends an open question to the community, asking if others share this view and how they plan to navigate the inevitable marketing blitz and spoiler culture leading up to launch.
