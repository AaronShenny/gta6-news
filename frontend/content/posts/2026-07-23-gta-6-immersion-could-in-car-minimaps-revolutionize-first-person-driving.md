# GTA 6 Immersion: Could In-Car Minimaps Revolutionize First-Person Driving?

A compelling theory circulating among GTA 6 enthusiasts suggests a groundbreaking approach to in-game navigation, especially for first-person driving. Drawing inspiration from L.A. Noire VR, where the minimap is seamlessly integrated into the car's dashboard, the idea proposes that GTA 6 could adopt a similar system. This would involve modern vehicles displaying navigation directly on their infotainment screens, while older cars might utilize a smartphone mounted on the dashboard. The primary goal of such an implementation would be to significantly enhance the immersion of the driving experience by eliminating the traditional HUD minimap. However, the proposal also raises questions about how navigation would be handled in third-person driving, where a traditional corner minimap might still be necessary, prompting further community discussion on potential solutions.
