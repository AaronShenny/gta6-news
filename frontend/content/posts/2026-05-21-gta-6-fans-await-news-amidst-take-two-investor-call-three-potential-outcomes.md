# GTA 6 Fans Await News Amidst Take-Two Investor Call: Three Potential Outcomes

With Take-Two Interactive's investor call scheduled for today, the Grand Theft Auto community is buzzing with speculation about potential updates for GTA 6. A recent Reddit post by u/Outrageous_Truck_880 outlines three realistic outcomes for fans to consider. The most optimistic, though least likely, scenario suggests a reveal of 'Trailer 3' alongside pre-order availability. Conversely, a delay announcement, similar to past events where Rockstar posted news shortly before investor calls, remains a possibility, potentially sweetened with minor news. However, the most probable outcome, according to the post, is a reaffirmation of the current November 19th release date without any new immediate announcements. This is viewed positively, indicating confidence in the schedule, with further reveals like 'Trailer 3' or pre-orders then expected in early June.

## Key Takeaways
- Take-Two's investor call is today, prompting anticipation for GTA 6 news.
- The least likely, but most desired, outcome is the release of 'Trailer 3' coupled with pre-order announcements.
- A potential scenario involves a delay announcement from Rockstar, mirroring past practices before investor calls.
- The most probable outcome is a reaffirmation of the existing November 19th release date, with no new game-related announcements during the call.
- A reaffirmation is seen as positive, suggesting confidence in the release schedule, with 'Trailer 3' or pre-orders likely to follow in early June.
