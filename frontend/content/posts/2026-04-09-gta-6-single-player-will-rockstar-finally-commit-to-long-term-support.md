# GTA 6 Single-Player: Will Rockstar Finally Commit to Long-Term Support?

A recent discussion on the GTA 6 subreddit, initiated by user /u/PapaNaj, highlights a significant concern among fans regarding the future of the game's single-player experience. The original poster expressed a strong desire for Grand Theft Auto VI to prioritize single-player depth, advocating for a wealth of new activities, businesses, side missions, and engaging post-story content. However, a prevailing sentiment of skepticism also emerged, with fears that Rockstar Games might revert to its established pattern, channeling the majority of its post-launch updates and new content exclusively into GTA Online, leaving the single-player mode largely untouched after its initial release.

## Key Takeaways
- Fan hope for increased single-player depth in GTA 6.
- Desire for more activities, businesses, side missions, and post-story content.
- Concern that updates will primarily focus on GTA Online, similar to past titles.
- Debate over Rockstar's potential strategy for GTA 6's content roadmap.

## FAQ
**What is the main concern regarding GTA 6's single-player?**
The primary concern is whether Rockstar Games will provide long-term, substantial post-launch support and content updates for GTA 6's single-player mode, or if the majority of new content will once again be directed towards GTA Online.
