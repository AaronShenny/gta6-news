# GTA 6 Online: Fan Speculation Hints at a Shift Towards Bullet-Based Combat Over Explosives

A recent Reddit post has sparked discussion among the Grand Theft Auto community, suggesting that GTA 6 Online's player-versus-player (PvP) combat could see a significant shift. The user, u/Spiritual-Vast7660, speculates that the inclusion of a minigun and a .50 caliber weapon on the Buzzard helicopter in GTA 6 indicates a move towards more bullet-centric engagements. The post criticizes GTA V Online's PvP for being 'explosive infested,' arguing that an overabundance of explosives diminished their impact and overshadowed traditional firearms. The user expresses a desire for a more 'visceral and fun' experience in GTA 6, where bullets feel powerful again and explosives are used more sparingly, thus making their deployment more impactful.
