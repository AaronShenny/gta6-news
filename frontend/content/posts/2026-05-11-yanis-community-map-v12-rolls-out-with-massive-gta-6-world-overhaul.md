# YANIS Community Map V12 Rolls Out with Massive GTA 6 World Overhaul

The dedicated GTA 6 community has received a substantial update to the YANIS fan-made map, with version V12 introducing a plethora of geographical refinements, structural additions, and speculative content. This latest iteration meticulously details various regions like Ambrosia, Key Lento, PGH, and Watson Bay, incorporating fixes for existing inaccuracies and adding new roads, buildings, and landmarks based on leaks and community speculation. The update aims to provide an even more accurate and immersive approximation of the highly anticipated game's world, showcasing the community's ongoing efforts to piece together the map of Leonida.

## Key Takeaways
- **Extensive Geographical Refinements:** Numerous locations across the map, including Ambrosia, Key Lento, Waning Sands, and Bahia Honda Key, have received updates, with new roads, underpasses, drawbridges, and adjusted terrain like hills and canals.
- **Structural Additions:** The map now features a wealth of new industrial, residential, and unidentified buildings in areas such as Crosstown, Lollipop Ridge, VCIA, PGH, Allied Crystal, and Northern Vice City, along with specific landmarks like a shed in Bahia Honda State Park and a potential skate park.
- **Road Network Overhaul:** Key Lento's roads have been fixed, highways between Peacock Bay and Hamlet rewired, and new intersections added. Speculative routes like I-97 have been extended, and Route 50 has been updated to reflect a mountain pass.
- **Accuracy and Speculation:** Many changes address existing inaccuracies, fixing building outlines, moving rail lines, and adjusting shipping lanes. The update also incorporates speculative elements, including a rail extension towards Yorktown and a Hendry County Sheriff's Office, based on community analysis.
- **Map Marker and UI Improvements:** Various markers and labels have been adjusted for better placement and numbering, including the S2/05 Marker and the 'Sundown' label. Font sizes for major regions have been increased, and the map legend and contributing users have been updated.
