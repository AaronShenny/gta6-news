# GTA 6 Gore Speculation Ignited by Leaked 'Scarface Crime Scene' Event

A recent discussion on Reddit has ignited speculation regarding the potential gore mechanics in Grand Theft Auto 6, drawing attention to a previously leaked 'Scarface crime scene' world event. The user suggests this event, reminiscent of the iconic *Scarface* movie and *GTA Vice City*'s bloody hotel bathroom with a chainsaw, could imply a significantly advanced gore system. The core argument is that if a chainsaw is a usable weapon, as it was in *Vice City*, then GTA 6's gore should surpass *Red Dead Redemption 2*'s fidelity, not be limited to *GTA 5* levels as some have suggested. The post calls for community discussion on this overlooked detail from the leaks, while also acknowledging the possibility of the scene being purely cosmetic.

## Key Takeaways
- A 'Scarface crime scene' world event was noted in early GTA 6 development leaks.
- This event is linked to the iconic *Scarface* movie scene and *GTA Vice City*'s bloody hotel bathroom with a chainsaw.

The speculation stems from a leaked 'Scarface crime scene' world event mentioned in early GTA 6 development footage, which some interpret as a bloody hotel bathroom with a chainsaw.
