# GTA VI Receives First Official Public Update Since Delay: A Discord Channel Reorganization

In a subtle yet significant move, Rockstar Games has officially acknowledged Grand Theft Auto VI publicly for the first time since the game's delay in November. The developer has reorganized its official Discord server, moving the 'grand-theft-auto-vi' channel from under the general 'Rockstar Games' category into its own distinct 'Grand Theft Auto VI' category. This marks the initial official public action regarding the highly anticipated title, sparking speculation among fans about potential upcoming announcements.

## Key Takeaways
- Rockstar Games moved the 'grand-theft-auto-vi' Discord channel.
- The channel is now in a dedicated 'Grand Theft Auto VI' category.
- Previously, the channel was located under the general 'Rockstar Games' category.
- This is the first official public action by Rockstar regarding GTA VI since its delay in November.
