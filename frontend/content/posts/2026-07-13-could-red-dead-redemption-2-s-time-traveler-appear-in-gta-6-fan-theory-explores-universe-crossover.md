# Could Red Dead Redemption 2's Time Traveler Appear in GTA 6? Fan Theory Explores Universe Crossover

A recent fan discussion has ignited speculation about a unique crossover between Rockstar Games' highly acclaimed titles, Red Dead Redemption 2 and the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. This concept is championed as a 'neat way' to explicitly tie the distinct universes of Red Dead Redemption and Grand Theft Auto together.

Francis Sinclair is known in RDR2 for his anachronistic dialogue and appearance, hinting at his ability to traverse different eras. His potential inclusion in GTA 6 would not only serve as a significant Easter egg for long-time Rockstar fans but also provide a tangible, albeit supernatural, link between the historical Wild West setting of RDR2 and the modern-day Vice City (or wherever GTA 6 is set).

Proponents of this theory suggest that such a character could appear in a minor side mission or a background detail, subtly affirming the shared multiverse concept that many fans already believe exists within Rockstar's titles. This crossover would be a bold move by Rockstar, offering a deeper narrative layer and rewarding players who have delved into the lore of both franchises.
