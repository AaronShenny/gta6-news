# Rockstar Store Only Lists PS5 for GTA VI and XBOX option is missing...

A recent observation on Reddit's r/GTA6 community has ignited discussion regarding the platform availability for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. According to user /u/HeroVax, while browsing the official Rockstar Store, they found that GTA VI was exclusively listed for PlayStation 5, with no option appearing for Xbox consoles. The user reportedly refreshed the page and checked in an incognito browser to confirm their findings, stating that the PS5 remained the sole platform visible for purchase. This discovery has led to immediate speculation among fans about a potential console exclusivity deal, which the user described as a 'crazy deal' that would be 'bad for XBOX fans.' The observation is currently unconfirmed by Rockstar Games, and the store link provided in the original post (https://store.rockstargames.com/game/buy-gta-vi) appears to be a placeholder, as GTA VI pre-orders are not yet officially live.
