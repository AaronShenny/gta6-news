# GTA 6 Chapter 1 Theory: Ankle Monitor to Act as Tutorial Restraint?

A compelling theory circulating on Reddit's r/GTA6 subreddit speculates on the structure of Grand Theft Auto 6's opening chapter. Drawing parallels to Red Dead Redemption 2's narrative-driven tutorial, the theory suggests that GTA 6's first chapter will likewise restrict player exploration while introducing core mechanics.In RDR2, the initial snowstorm served as a natural barrier, limiting Arthur Morgan's movements and guiding players through fundamental gameplay elements before opening up the vast world in Chapter 2. For GTA 6, the theory posits that Lucia's ankle monitor, a detail seen in early footage, could serve a similar purpose. This device would logically restrict her (and thus the player's) ability to roam freely, providing a believable in-game reason to funnel players through introductory missions and controls. Once Chapter 2 begins, presumably after the ankle monitor's removal or expiration, the expansive world of Vice City would then become fully accessible.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's Chapter 1 may function as a tutorial, initially restricting player exploration.
- This approach mirrors Red Dead Redemption 2's opening, which used a snowstorm to limit movement.
- Lucia's ankle monitor is theorized to be the narrative device limiting exploration in GTA 6's Chapter 1.
- Full world exploration is expected to unlock in Chapter 2, after the initial tutorial phase.
