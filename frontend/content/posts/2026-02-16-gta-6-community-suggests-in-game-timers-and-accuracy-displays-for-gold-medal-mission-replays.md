# GTA 6: Community Suggests In-Game Timers and Accuracy Displays for Gold Medal Mission Replays

A dedicated GTA fan has voiced a quality-of-life suggestion for GTA 6's mission replay system, specifically targeting the notoriously challenging gold medal requirements. The user, /u/Capital_Answer_7491, argues that Rockstar Games should implement on-screen timers for time-based objectives and real-time accuracy percentages for precision-based goals during mission replays. This proposed UI enhancement aims to alleviate the frustration players experience when attempting to achieve gold medals, a common issue highlighted in previous titles like GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2, making the replay process more transparent and enjoyable for completionists.

## Key Takeaways
- Difficulty in achieving gold medals during mission replays in GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2 is a significant point of frustration for players.
- The suggestion proposes adding an on-screen timer during replays for missions with time-based gold medal objectives.
- It also recommends displaying real-time accuracy percentages for missions requiring high precision to earn gold medals.
- The user believes these UI improvements are 'very easy things to implement' for Rockstar Games.
- The proposal aims to make the gold medal pursuit less trial-and-error and more player-friendly.
