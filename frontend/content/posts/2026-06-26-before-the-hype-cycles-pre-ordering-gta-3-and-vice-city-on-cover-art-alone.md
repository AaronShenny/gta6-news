# Before the Hype Cycles: Pre-Ordering GTA 3 and Vice City on Cover Art Alone

A nostalgic Reddit post from u/bot_comment1234 takes a trip down memory lane, reflecting on the pre-order culture of the early 2000s, specifically for Grand Theft Auto 3 and Vice City. The user highlights the stark contrast with today's gaming landscape, recalling how these seminal titles were pre-ordered based on incredibly limited information – often just cover art and a few screenshots found in magazines.
