# Community Plea: GTA 6 Needs a Robust Photo Mode from Day One

A passionate plea from the Grand Theft Auto community has emerged, urging Rockstar Games to integrate a comprehensive photo mode into GTA 6 right from its launch. The sentiment, prominently shared by Reddit user u/FEEDTHEGRINDER, emphasizes the significant player engagement such a feature can drive. Citing the success and personal time invested in Insomniac's Spider-Man 2 camera mode, the user highlights the potential for a photo mode to extend GTA 6's playtime and creative appeal beyond its core gameplay.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 community advocates for a photo mode to be included at launch.
- Reddit user u/FEEDTHEGRINDER expresses strong personal desire for photography features in the game.
- Insomniac's Spider-Man 2 camera mode is cited as a benchmark for player engagement and time investment.
- A robust photo mode is anticipated to significantly increase player playtime and foster creativity within GTA 6.
