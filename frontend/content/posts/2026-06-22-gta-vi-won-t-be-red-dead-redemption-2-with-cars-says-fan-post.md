# GTA VI Won't Be Red Dead Redemption 2 With Cars, Says Fan Post

A widely discussed fan post from Reddit's r/GTA6 community pushes back against prevalent predictions for Grand Theft Auto VI, asserting that the game will not be a hyper-realistic life simulator akin to Red Dead Redemption 2. The author emphasizes that while GTA VI is expected to be more immersive than its predecessor, it will retain the franchise's core identity of being fast, chaotic, and gameplay-focused. Predictions like permanent tattoos, mandatory tooth brushing, strict traffic law enforcement, or NPCs remembering every past crime are deemed incompatible with GTA's established design philosophy, which differentiates immersion from pure realism. Simple game mechanics for vehicle ownership and weapon storage are anticipated, rather than intricate simulations that could hinder gameplay flow, reinforcing that GTA and RDR2, despite sharing a developer, cater to very different experiences and audiences.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA VI is unlikely to be a hyper-realistic 'life simulator' despite some fan expectations.
- The game will prioritize fast, chaotic, and gameplay-focused experiences, not extreme realism.
- The author differentiates between immersion and realism; GTA VI aims for greater immersion than GTA V but not at the expense of core gameplay.
- Fan predictions like permanent tattoos, mandatory tooth brushing, or strict traffic law enforcement are viewed as detached from GTA's identity.
- Expect simple, game-friendly mechanics for features like weapon storage and personal vehicle retrieval, rather than complex simulations.
- Despite being from the same studio, GTA and Red Dead Redemption 2 are distinct franchises with different design philosophies and target audiences.

## FAQ
**Will GTA VI require players to brush their teeth or obey all traffic laws?**
No, the article suggests such hyper-realistic features are unlikely, as they would detract from GTA's core gameplay loop and transform it into a life simulator.

**Will GTA VI be as slow and immersive as Red Dead Redemption 2?**
While GTA VI is expected to be more immersive than GTA V, the article argues it will maintain GTA's characteristic fast-paced, chaotic, and gameplay-focused nature, distinguishing it from RDR2's intentionally slower pace.

**How will personal vehicle ownership or weapon storage likely work in GTA VI?**
The article anticipates simple game mechanics, such as calling back a stolen vehicle or weapon storage functioning like multiple accessible lockers, rather than a complex, realistic simulation.
