# GTA 6 Malls: A Deep Dive into Player Expectations and Design Challenges

A recent Reddit post by u/anjohas has ignited discussion within the GTA 6 community regarding the highly anticipated inclusion of malls in the game. With the first trailer already showcasing a mall sign and the game's website mentioning 'enclosed malls in Ambrosia,' players are eager to see how these retail behemoths will be implemented. The central question revolves around the feasibility of Rockstar populating these malls with hundreds of unique, interactive stores, and the sheer imagination required to bring such a complex environment to life within Vice City.

## Key Takeaways
- First GTA 6 trailer showed a mall sign, confirming their presence.
- Official website mentions 'enclosed malls in Ambrosia'.
- Players are speculating on the inclusion of a parody of Brickell City Centre in Vice City.
- Concerns raised about the practical implementation of hundreds of unique stores within the game's malls.
- Community is questioning the scale and realism Rockstar can achieve with these complex environments.
