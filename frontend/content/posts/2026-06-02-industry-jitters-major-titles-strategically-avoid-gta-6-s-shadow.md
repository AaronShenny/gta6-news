# Industry Jitters: Major Titles Strategically Avoid GTA 6's Shadow

A recent Reddit post has ignited discussion within the gaming community, pointing out a peculiar trend in upcoming game releases. The post observes that titles such as 'Silent F' and 'Control Resonant' are scheduled to launch on the same day, with 'Onimusha' following just one day later. This close proximity of releases is interpreted by the post's author as a strategic move by publishers to compete with each other rather than risk a direct confrontation with the colossal market presence anticipated for Grand Theft Auto 6. The sentiment conveyed is that GTA 6 possesses such unprecedented power that other major releases are actively avoiding its potential launch window, acknowledging it as the 'GOAT' (Greatest Of All Time) in terms of industry impact.
