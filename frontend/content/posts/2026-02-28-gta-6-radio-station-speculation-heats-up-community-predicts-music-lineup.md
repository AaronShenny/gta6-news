# GTA 6 Radio Station Speculation Heats Up: Community Predicts Music Lineup

The Grand Theft Auto VI community is buzzing with speculation about the in-game radio stations, a staple of the series' immersive experience. A popular Reddit thread, initiated by user /u/trisome21, explores potential radio lineups, with predictions ranging from the number of stations to specific genre inclusions. The discussion highlights common hopes for genre diversity and new musical experiences in the highly anticipated title.

## Key Takeaways
- A user predicts at least 18 radio stations in GTA 6: 16 music and 2 talk shows.
- Speculation includes the strong possibility of a dedicated Latin folk music station, featuring genres like Salsa and Mambo.
- The community foresees the return of a 'classic vs. modern' genre approach, with separate stations for different eras of music (e.g., classic rock and modern rock).
- There is a strong desire for an extreme metal radio station, particularly given Vice City's Florida inspiration, a state known for its influential death metal scene.
