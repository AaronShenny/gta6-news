# GTA VI Launch Date Reportedly Reaffirmed for November 19th, Marketing Kicks Off This Summer

According to a Reddit user's account of a recent Take-Two Interactive earnings call, CEO Strauss Zelnick has reportedly reaffirmed November 19th as the projected launch date for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. The same report indicates that the marketing campaign for the blockbuster title remains firmly on track to kick off this summer, signaling that promotional activities are imminent as the game's release draws closer.

## Key Takeaways
- Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick reportedly reaffirmed November 19th as the launch date for GTA VI.
- The marketing campaign for GTA VI is said to be on schedule to begin this summer.
- This information originates from a Reddit user's report of an earnings call.
