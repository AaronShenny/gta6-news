# GTA 6 Community Hopes for Iconic Vice City 80s Tracks in New Game's Soundtrack

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit, by user /u/Maximus3311, has ignited discussion among fans about the potential soundtrack for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. While an 80s-themed radio station is generally assumed to be part of the game, the central point of contention and desire revolves around the re-inclusion of specific, beloved tracks that were iconic to Grand Theft Auto: Vice City. The original poster expressed a strong wish for these nostalgic songs to make a comeback, acknowledging the intricate challenges associated with music licensing, and differentiating this hope from merely having a generic 80s station.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user /u/Maximus3311 initiated a discussion on r/GTA6 about GTA 6's soundtrack.
- Fans generally expect an 80s radio station to be included in GTA 6.
- The primary desire is for specific, iconic songs from Grand Theft Auto: Vice City to return.
- The poster acknowledged the complexities of music licensing for such a return.
- The wish is for classic Vice City tracks, not just a generic 80s station.
