# GTA 6's Premium Pricing and Monetization Strategy Echoes Take-Two's Approach

While the article primarily details a 'nightmare' Statue of Liberty cosmetic skin for *PGA Tour 2K25*'s July 4th celebration, it significantly pivots to discuss Take-Two Interactive's broader monetization strategies, particularly focusing on *Grand Theft Auto VI*. The report confirms that *GTA 6* will launch with a premium base price of $80. Furthermore, an Ultimate Edition is available for pre-order at $100, promising access to several extra in-game stores and various cosmetics. This pricing model is presented as consistent with Take-Two's established 'value extraction' approach, common across its 2K sports and wrestling titles. Despite these higher price points, the article highlights that *GTA 6* pre-orders are already performing 'very healthy,' indicating strong consumer demand and acceptance of the company's monetization strategy for its highly anticipated flagship title.
