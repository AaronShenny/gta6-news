# Concerns Mount Over Potential GTA 6 Marketing Timeline Following Tom Henderson's August Prediction

A user on Reddit's r/GTA6 subreddit has voiced strong doubts regarding Tom Henderson's prediction that the next significant news for Grand Theft Auto 6 might not surface until August. The user argues that an August announcement would leave a mere three months for the entire marketing and hype cycle, including Trailer 3, gameplay reveals, pre-orders, cover art, and the launch trailer. They contend that compressing such a crucial campaign into just three months is 'crazy' and 'unrealistic' for what is anticipated to be the 'biggest game launch of all time,' especially given that many players are planning to purchase new consoles specifically for GTA VI and would require more time to build hype and prepare.

## Key Takeaways
- Tom Henderson reportedly predicted major GTA 6 news might not arrive until August.
- The Reddit user expresses significant skepticism about this timeline.
- A three-month marketing window (August to November/December) is deemed insufficient for GTA 6's anticipated scale.
- This compressed timeline would need to include Trailer 3, gameplay trailers, pre-orders, cover art, billboard ads, and the launch trailer.
- The user highlights that many players are planning console purchases specifically for GTA 6, requiring more preparation time.
- The overall sentiment is that such a short marketing period 'doesn't feel realistic' for the biggest game launch ever.
