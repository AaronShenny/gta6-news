# GTA 6 Community Reacts to Fan-Made Nighttime Artwork Edit

The dedicated r/GTA6 subreddit recently became a hub for aesthetic debate as user /u/Glass-Sea9665 shared a fan-edited version of the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI artwork. The creator transformed the original daytime scene into a nocturnal setting, prompting community members to weigh in on which version they preferred. While the editor personally favored the 'day time' look, they acknowledged that the 'night time works too,' inviting fellow fans to share their thoughts on the visual alteration.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user, /u/Glass-Sea9665, shared an edited version of the official GTA 6 artwork.
- The edit transforms the artwork's original daytime setting into a nighttime scene.
- The creator expressed a personal preference for the daytime version but noted the night edit also 'works'.
- The post actively encouraged community discussion and feedback on the visual change.
