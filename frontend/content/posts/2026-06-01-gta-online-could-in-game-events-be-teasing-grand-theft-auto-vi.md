# GTA Online: Could In-Game Events Be Teasing Grand Theft Auto VI?

A speculative theory gaining traction among Grand Theft Auto fans proposes that Rockstar Games may begin teasing GTA VI through unique in-game events within GTA Online. The idea draws a direct parallel to the promotional strategy employed for Red Dead Redemption 2, where GTA Online players could complete special events like 'The Treasure Hunt' and 'Maude’s Bounties' to unlock exclusive items, such as the Double Action Revolver and Stone Hatchet, for use in both GTA Online and RDR2 upon its release. If this pattern holds, the next GTA Online summer DLC, typically launched in June, could serve as the initial platform for these subtle teasers, potentially aligning with broader community speculation about a June reveal for GTA VI. However, the original poster emphasizes that this theory should be taken 'with an earth-size mountain of salt,' highlighting its purely speculative nature.

## Key Takeaways
- Theory suggests GTA 6 promotion could begin within GTA Online.
- This would mirror RDR2's pre-release strategy, where GTA Online events unlocked items for both games.
- Examples include the Double Action Revolver and Stone Hatchet from RDR2 promotional events.

- The theory is highly speculative and should be treated with significant skepticism.
