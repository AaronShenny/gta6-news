# GTA 6 Hype Ignites PS5 Pro Upgrades Amidst Price Hike Fears

The intense anticipation surrounding Grand Theft Auto 6 is already influencing consumer behavior, with a Reddit user on r/GTA6 detailing their recent decision to upgrade to a PlayStation 5 Pro. The user, /u/G4MER4LYF, explained they had previously resisted upgrading but were ultimately swayed by 'GTA 6 enhancement FOMO' and an impending price hike. This pre-emptive hardware investment underscores the significant impact highly anticipated titles like GTA 6 can have on console sales, as players eagerly hope Rockstar Games will deliver bespoke 'Pro treatment' optimizations for the enhanced console experience.

The user upgraded to a PS5 Pro primarily due to 'GTA 6 enhancement FOMO' (fear of missing out on optimal performance) and an impending price hike, wanting to ensure the best possible experience for the upcoming game.

'Pro treatment' refers to specific optimizations and enhancements from Rockstar Games that would take full advantage of the PlayStation 5 Pro's increased power, potentially offering better graphics, frame rates, or unique features for GTA 6.
