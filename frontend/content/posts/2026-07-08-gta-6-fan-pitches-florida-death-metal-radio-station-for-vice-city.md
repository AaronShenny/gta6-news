# GTA 6: Fan Pitches Florida Death Metal Radio Station for Vice City

While anticipating a strong focus on Vice City's hip-hop scene in Grand Theft Auto 6, a passionate fan has taken to Reddit to propose an intriguing addition to the game's soundtrack: a dedicated Florida Death Metal radio station. The user, /u/Theydontsleep, argues that it would be a missed opportunity for Rockstar to overlook the rich and influential history of Florida's death metal scene, drawing parallels to Cyberpunk 2077's inclusion of an extreme metal station. The suggestion goes beyond just music, envisioning an in-game character who collects rare old-school Florida Death Metal, prompting players to undertake quests to find elusive items. The post meticulously lists several pioneering bands from the genre, including Morbid Angel ('Altars of Madness', 'Covenant'), Obituary ('Cause of Death'), Deicide ('Legion'), Death ('Symbolic'), Atheist ('Unquestionable Presence'), Cannibal Corpse, Six Feet Under, and Monstrosity ('Millennium'), alongside others who relocated to the state like Malevolent Creation and Angelcorpse. The fan concludes by expressing excitement at the prospect of cruising Vice City's beaches while blasting tracks like 'Chapel of Ghouls' by Morbid Angel, believing it would significantly enhance the player experience.
