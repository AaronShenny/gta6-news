# GTA 6 Trailers Hit Staggering 423 Million Combined Views, Fueling Hype

The anticipation for Grand Theft Auto VI continues to reach unprecedented levels, as the game's second official trailer recently crossed the 150 million view mark within nine months of its release. This impressive milestone, when combined with the first trailer's colossal 273 million views, pushes the total viewership for both GTA 6 trailers to an astounding 423 million. This monumental engagement underscores the global excitement and 'incredible progress' by Rockstar Games, cementing GTA 6's status as one of the most eagerly awaited titles in gaming history.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 Trailer 2 has surpassed 150 million views in approximately nine months.
- GTA 6 Trailer 1 currently holds 273 million views.
- The combined viewership for both official GTA 6 trailers now totals 423 million.
- These figures demonstrate significant community engagement and high anticipation for Rockstar Games' upcoming title.
