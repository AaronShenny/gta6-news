# GTA VI: Fans Closer Than Ever to Launch Following Delay Milestone

Dedicated Grand Theft Auto VI fans are currently closer to the game's highly anticipated launch than ever before, according to a recent community post. As of May 3, 2026, players are precisely 200 days away from release. This marks a significant milestone, as it surpasses the previous closest point of 201 days, which occurred just before Rockstar Games announced a delay on November 6, 2025. The post optimistically notes that with each passing day, the wait for GTA VI will only shorten, barring any unforeseen future postponements.

## Key Takeaways
- As of May 3, 2026 (Australian time), GTA VI is 200 days from its scheduled launch.
- This date marks the closest fans have been to the game's release since its initial delay.
- The previous closest point to launch before the delay was 201 days out, on November 6, 2025.
- Each subsequent day from May 3, 2026, will bring players even closer to the game's release.
