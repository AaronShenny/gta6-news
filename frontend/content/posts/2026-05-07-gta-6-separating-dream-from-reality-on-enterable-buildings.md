# GTA 6: Separating Dream from Reality on Enterable Buildings

The Grand Theft Auto VI community is buzzing with speculation regarding the number of enterable buildings in the highly anticipated title. While many players dream of accessing every house and establishment, a recent discussion post on Reddit offers a dose of realism. The author points out that even Red Dead Redemption 2, with a smaller map than the projected GTA 6 and fewer major cities, features numerous inaccessible structures. Given that GTA 6 is expected to boast a map twice the size of RDR2's and include five major cities, the likelihood of every building being enterable is deemed unrealistic. However, the author optimistically suggests that GTA 6 will likely offer more accessible interiors than GTA V, with specific key buildings and potentially all houses in smaller villages being fully explorable, while dismissing the idea of widespread procedural generation for every interior.

## Key Takeaways
- Player desire for fully enterable environments in GTA 6 is high.
- Realistic expectations are tempered by comparisons to Red Dead Redemption 2, which has a smaller map but still features many locked buildings.
- GTA 6's projected map size (twice RDR2's) and five major cities make universal accessibility unlikely.
- The author anticipates an increase in enterable locations compared to GTA V.
- Specific key buildings and potentially all houses in small villages are expected to be accessible.
- The use of procedural generation for all interiors is doubted, emphasizing the design challenge of manually creating every location.

## FAQ
**Will every building in GTA 6 be enterable?**
Realistically, it's highly unlikely. Even Red Dead Redemption 2, with a smaller map, had many locked buildings. GTA 6's projected size and number of cities make universal accessibility improbable.

**How will GTA 6 compare to previous Rockstar titles in terms of accessible interiors?**
The community expects more enterable buildings than in GTA V, with specific important locations and possibly all houses in smaller villages being accessible, but not on the scale of every single structure.

**Is procedural generation a solution for more enterable buildings in GTA 6?**
The author of the discussion post expresses skepticism that Rockstar will rely on widespread procedural generation for every interior, highlighting the immense design challenge of creating unique, enterable spaces for all buildings.
