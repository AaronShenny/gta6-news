# GTA 6 Prominently Featured in Sony's Latest Earnings Call

This inclusion is widely believed to be a confirmation of an existing marketing partnership between Sony and Rockstar Games. Furthermore, its presence in such a significant corporate briefing has been interpreted by many as 'further proof' that the game is on track and unlikely to experience any additional delays.
