# GTA V's Sniper Scopes Slammed by Players, Hopes High for GTA VI Overhaul

A recent discussion on Reddit's r/GTA6 community has highlighted a common point of contention among Grand Theft Auto V players: the game's sniper rifle scopes. User /u/SplitOutside9081 specifically criticized the crosshairs for the Sniper Rifle, Heavy Sniper, and Marksman Rifle, labeling them as 'horrible.' The sentiment points towards a strong desire for significant improvements in GTA VI, with players hoping for more realistic and functional scopes, ideally featuring horizontal or vertical Mil-Dots and a clean, unobtrusive design to enhance the gameplay experience.

## Key Takeaways
- Strong criticism directed at the existing sniper rifle scopes (Sniper Rifle, Heavy Sniper, Marksman Rifle) in GTA V.
- Players desire 'actual scopes' with a clean design for GTA VI.
- Specific request for scopes featuring horizontal or vertical Mil-Dots for improved realism and functionality.
