# The Inevitable Cycle of GTA Hype: Looking Beyond GTA 6 to GTA 7

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by user /u/Pajamaetchi has ignited discussion about the long-term trajectory of fan excitement within the Grand Theft Auto series. The user speculates that the current fervent anticipation for GTA 6 will, in time, evolve into a similar demand for its successor, GTA 7. - Future fan demand for GTA 7 will mirror the current hype for GTA 6.
- Highlights the cyclical nature of player expectations and desire for new content in gaming.
