# GTA 6 Hype Sparks PS5 Pro Availability Concerns Among Gamers

The gaming community is buzzing with speculation surrounding the potential impact of Grand Theft Auto 6's release on the availability of the rumored PlayStation 5 Pro console. A recent Reddit discussion highlights a common dilemma for eager fans: whether to preemptively secure a PS5 Pro pre-order due to fears of scarcity, or if Sony will adequately ramp up production to meet the expected massive demand surge that GTA 6 is sure to ignite. This reflects a broader concern about hardware readiness for one of gaming's most anticipated titles.

## Key Takeaways
- Gamers are expressing concern over potential PS5 Pro stock shortages.
- GTA 6 is expected to drive significant demand for new console hardware.
- A debate exists on whether to pre-order the PS5 Pro early or rely on Sony's production capacity.
- The discussion underscores the high anticipation for GTA 6 and its hardware implications.
