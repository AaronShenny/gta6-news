# GTA 6 Fans' Hopes for Trailer 3 Dashed as May 26th Approaches Without New Content

As May 26th approaches, a date some fans speculate might have been the original release window for Grand Theft Auto VI, the community is bracing for another quiet day from Rockstar Games. Contrary to persistent 'hopium' among some players expecting a third trailer or new screenshots, one Reddit post strongly dismisses these theories. The author highlights recent statements from Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick, who confirmed during an investor call that GTA VI's marketing campaign is slated to begin in 'summer' (late June/July). This suggests that Rockstar is unlikely to release new content now, especially given the potential for negative fan reaction by reminding players of the game's delay. The post anticipates a 'ghost town' on official social media channels, leaving fans to grapple with 'depressing memes' rather than fresh game reveals.
