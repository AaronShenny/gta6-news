# GTA 5 Player Advocates for Enhanced In-Game TV Experience in GTA 6

Revisiting Grand Theft Auto 5, one player has highlighted an often-overlooked feature they hope sees significant expansion in GTA 6: the in-game television programming. Describing GTA 5's TV as "amazing," the player expresses a fervent wish for Rockstar Games to have dedicated substantial resources, specifically animators, to developing richer GTATV content since as early as 2018. While acknowledging it as a highly unrealistic dream, their ultimate desire is for full episodes and even seasons of in-universe TV shows, a significant leap from the current 3-4 hours of content available in GTA 5. This sentiment sparks discussion on whether players can expect a similar or greater range of television entertainment in the highly anticipated sequel.

## Key Takeaways
- A GTA 5 player praises the quality and entertainment value of the in-game television content.
- The player hopes Rockstar has invested heavily in creating new GTATV content for GTA 6, potentially since 2018.
- A major, albeit unrealistic, wish is for full episodes and seasons of fictional TV shows within GTA 6.
- GTA 5 currently offers approximately 3-4 hours of unique in-game TV content.
- The article raises the question of whether GTA 6 will maintain or surpass the current volume of TV programming.
