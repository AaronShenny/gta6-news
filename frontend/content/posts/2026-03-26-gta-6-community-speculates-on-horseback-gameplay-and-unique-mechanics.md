# GTA 6 Community Speculates on Horseback Gameplay and Unique Mechanics

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited speculation among fans regarding the potential inclusion of horses in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/ScouseJimmy1990 proposed the idea, humorously suggesting scenarios like selling illicit substances from horseback. Beyond the comedic potential, the discussion delved into possible gameplay mechanics, such as players remaining off-radar while mounted until spotted or engaging in combat, similar to GTA V's deathmatch rules. However, the concept also acknowledges potential drawbacks, including the vulnerability of horses to injury or death during gameplay, adding a layer of risk to the proposed feature.

## Key Takeaways
- Fan speculation about the inclusion of horses in Grand Theft Auto 6.
- Humorous suggestion of using horses for illicit activities, like selling acid.
- Proposed gameplay mechanic: Players on horseback could remain off-radar until spotted by another player or until they fire a weapon.
- Potential drawback: Horses could be injured or killed during gameplay, adding a strategic element to their use.
