# GTA 6 Fauna Analysis: Identifying the Mysterious Pelican in the Trailer

The latest installment of 'Your daily bird in GTA VI' series, 'PART 5', focuses on a specific bird spotted at the 0:12 mark in the first Grand Theft Auto VI trailer. Characterized by a large bill, short legs, and a stout, brownish-black body, the bird has been identified as a pelican. Through a process of elimination, comparing the eight extant pelican species, the analysis concludes it is most likely a Brown Pelican. This determination is based on the bird's coloration (ruling out the white-plumed American White Pelican) and the Brown Pelican's year-round residency in Florida, the game's likely setting. The article also humorously notes Rockstar's apparent fondness for pelicans, referencing their inclusion in Red Dead Redemption 2.

## Key Takeaways
- A bird is spotted at the 0:12 mark in the first GTA VI trailer.
- The bird is described as having a big bill, short legs, and a stout, brownish-black body.
- It is identified as a pelican, consistent with water bird characteristics.
- The Brown Pelican is concluded to be the most likely species.
- The American White Pelican is ruled out due to its white plumage and seasonal Florida presence.
- Brown Pelicans are year-round residents in Florida, aligning with the game's setting.
- Rockstar Games has previously featured both American White and Brown Pelicans in Red Dead Redemption 2.
