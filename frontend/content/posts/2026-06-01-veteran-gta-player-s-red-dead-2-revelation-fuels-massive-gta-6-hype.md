# Veteran GTA Player's Red Dead 2 Revelation Fuels Massive GTA 6 Hype

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto veteran, with thousands of hours logged in GTA 5 and its FiveM role-playing community, recently ventured into the world of Red Dead Redemption 2 for the first time. Despite initially overlooking the Red Dead franchise, a significant sale prompted the player, known as /u/Sound_Aware, to pick up the acclaimed title. After only 20 hours, they report being 'completely blown away' by RDR2's intricate details, citing the 'insane amount of ambient events,' dynamic NPC interactions, realistic game physics, and visceral combat. This profound experience has dramatically re-ignited the player's anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6, leading them to speculate that Rockstar's next urban epic will incorporate and vastly expand upon the groundbreaking features seen in Red Dead Redemption 2, promising an even more immersive and detailed open world.
