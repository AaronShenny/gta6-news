# GTA 6 'Early Release' Dream Leaves Gamer Utterly Disappointed

In a peculiar account, a dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan recounts a vivid dream where they allegedly received an early console release of GTA 6 on May 26th. What began as overwhelming joy quickly devolved into a nightmare of technical woes and bizarre gameplay. The dream started with a cutscene featuring Lucia and Jason in a bank heist, mirroring the official trailer, but then presented a game world resembling a low-quality 'GTA 5 on Android' knock-off. The player observed missing LODs, sparse NPCs, and animations akin to free Unity assets. A strange character switch occurred, transporting the player to a Japanese high school with Silent Hill F-like characters, before returning to a buggy, 15-FPS version of Los Santos. The dream culminated in severe visual glitches, leading to immense frustration and a waking sense of disappointment, prompting the dreamer to declare, 'GTA 6 is going to suck; because I had a bad dream about it.'
