# GTA 6 Fan Hopes for Return of Classic Vice City Explosion Mechanics and Instant Wanted Levels

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan has taken to Reddit to share a nostalgic wish for GTA 6, recalling a specific explosive interaction from the original Grand Theft Auto: Vice City. The user, /u/RichtofenFanBoy, fondly remembers a particular in-game object that, when destroyed, would trigger a 'ridiculously big explosion' and immediately grant a three-star wanted level. They express hope that GTA 6 will feature a similar mechanic, wishing for an 'atomic explosion' and an instant three-star wanted level, suggesting a desire for the return of over-the-top, classic GTA chaos and immediate high-stakes gameplay.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user /u/RichtofenFanBoy shared a nostalgic observation regarding GTA: Vice City.
- The user recalled a specific in-game object in OG Vice City that, when blown up, caused a massive explosion and an instant three-star wanted level.
- They expressed a desire for GTA 6 to include a similar mechanic, hoping for 'atomic explosions' and immediate three-star wanted levels.
- This sentiment highlights a fan's wish for classic, exaggerated GTA gameplay elements to return in the upcoming title.
