# Red Dead Redemption 2 Fuels Sky-High Expectations for GTA 6, Says Dedicated Fan

A recent Reddit post highlights how the immersive experience of Red Dead Redemption 2 has significantly amplified a user's anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6. The gamer, who initially played RDR2 to gauge GTA 6's potential, was left 'amazed,' finding GTA V's world to 'pale in comparison.' They contend that only those who have experienced RDR2 truly understand Rockstar's prowess on modern hardware, emphasizing the game's ability to provide hours of engaging world exploration without traditional quests. The user confidently dismisses the notion that GTA 6 will merely be a graphical upgrade of its predecessor, asserting that Rockstar's storytelling, world-building, and advanced NPC simulations are a decade ahead of the industry, setting an incredibly high bar for the upcoming title.

## Key Takeaways
- Red Dead Redemption 2 is seen as a crucial benchmark for GTA 6's potential.
- The RDR2 experience makes GTA V feel 'pale in comparison' for world immersion.
- Rockstar's true capabilities on modern hardware are best showcased through RDR2.
- RDR2 offers hours of enjoyable world exploration and interaction without needing quests.
- The notion that GTA 6 will simply be 'GTA V with better graphics' is strongly refuted.
- Rockstar's storytelling, world-building, and NPC simulations are considered 10 years ahead of the industry.
