# GTA 6 Marketing & Trailer Speculation: A Deep Dive into a Fan Theory

A compelling fan theory from Reddit user /u/Complete_River_6226 posits a calculated timeline for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6 marketing launch. Drawing parallels to previous GTA Online update schedules, the theory outlines a potential scenario where a major online DLC is announced in early June and released mid-month. Following this, the community could see an announcement for GTA 6's third trailer by June 19th, with its release potentially aligning with the official start of summer around June 23rd. This approach, the theorist argues, allows Rockstar to clear its immediate commitments to GTA Online before a full-scale marketing blitz for its next flagship title.
