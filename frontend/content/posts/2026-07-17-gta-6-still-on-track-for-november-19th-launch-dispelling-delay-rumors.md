# GTA 6 Still on Track for November 19th Launch, Dispelling Delay Rumors

Despite recent speculation suggesting an internal delay, Take-Two Interactive has officially reaffirmed that *Grand Theft Auto VI* remains on track for its highly anticipated launch on November 19th. This confirmation comes directly from an official Take-Two document, which explicitly states the Grand Theft Auto series 'continued to outpace expectations significantly' and is demonstrating 'strong momentum leading up to the planned launch of Grand Theft Auto VI on November 19th.' The document also highlights the continued success of the franchise, noting that *GTA Online*'s 'A Safehouse in the Hills' update was 'one of the best-performing updates in its history,' and *GTA+* has delivered 'significant year-over-year growth.' Financially, Take-Two is optimistic, projecting over $1 billion in operating cash flow for FY2027, which will provide flexibility for future investments, acquisitions, and maximizing the commercial potential of its extensive portfolio of franchises.
