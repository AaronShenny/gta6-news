# GTA 6: A Fan's Hopes for Next-Level Racing and Car Customization

A long-time Grand Theft Auto player, revisiting GTA Online on PS5 after a decade-long hiatus, shares their specific hopes for GTA 6. While generally maintaining 'down-to-earth' expectations for the game's overall release, their passion ignites when it comes to cars and racing. The player expresses a strong desire for Rockstar Games to significantly expand upon current GTA Online's racing and tuning options, pushing them to an unprecedented level. They envision GTA 6's online component becoming a superior alternative to traditional racing games like Need for Speed, with drift and various race types integrated from launch. Furthermore, the player hopes for a robust and active online racing community and a vastly improved matchmaking system, reflecting a common sentiment among online gamers.

## Key Takeaways
- The author, a returning GTA Online player, expresses excitement for GTA 6's release.
- Primary expectation focuses intensely on Rockstar innovating and expanding car customization and racing mechanics.
- Hopes for GTA 6 Online to feature diverse race types, including drift, right from launch.
- Envisions GTA 6 becoming a leading platform for online racing, potentially surpassing dedicated racing titles.
- Calls for a significant improvement in the lobby and matchmaking system for the online mode.
- Desires the resurgence of a strong, active GTA racing community.
