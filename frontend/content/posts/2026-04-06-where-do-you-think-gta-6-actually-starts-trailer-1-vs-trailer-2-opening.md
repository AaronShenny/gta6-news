# Where do you think GTA 6 actually starts? Trailer 1 vs Trailer 2 opening

A recent discussion on Reddit's r/GTA6 community delves into the potential starting point of Grand Theft Auto VI, analyzing hints from the game's official trailers. The original poster suggests that beginning in the Keys, as potentially hinted by Trailer 2's opening shot, would offer a 'low level,' laid-back introduction to the game world, allowing players to build up resources and connections (like Jason's apparent early-game base and small jobs for Brian) before transitioning into the chaos of Vice City. The discussion also explores various alternative or combined prologues, such as starting with Lucia in prison, a flashback sequence, or separate introductions for Jason and Lucia before they eventually link up. Ultimately, the community ponders whether Rockstar will indeed start players in the Keys or if the true beginning remains a closely guarded secret.

## Key Takeaways
- The starting point of GTA VI is a topic of speculation, contrasting the openings of Trailer 1 and Trailer 2.
- A strong argument is made for starting in the Keys (Trailer 2 opening) due to its quieter, 'low level' vibe.
- The Keys setting could facilitate early-game progression, featuring Jason's house as a base and small jobs.
- Alternative starting scenarios include Lucia in prison, a flashback, or separate introductions for Jason and Lucia.
- The community questions whether Rockstar has revealed the true beginning or if it's still hidden.

## FAQ
**What is the main theory for GTA 6's starting point?**
The main theory discussed is that GTA 6 will start in the Keys, as suggested by the opening of Trailer 2. This would offer a more laid-back, 'low level' beginning before the action escalates into Vice City.

**Why do some believe the game will start in the Keys?**
Reasons include the quieter, more laid-back vibe, the potential for a 'low level' beginning, Jason's house appearing to be an early-game base, and the possibility of working small jobs before moving to Vice City.

**Are there other theories about how GTA 6 might begin?**
Yes, other theories include starting with Lucia in prison, a flashback sequence, separate introductions for Jason and Lucia before they meet, or a combination of these elements (e.g., Jason in the Keys, then Lucia in prison, then they meet).

**Has Rockstar officially confirmed the starting location or sequence?**
No, Rockstar has not officially confirmed the game's starting location or sequence. The theories are based on interpretations of the released trailers and speculation from the gaming community.
