# Grand Theft Auto VI: Ultimate Edition Unleashed – Exclusive Content Detailed for Leonida

Rockstar Games has officially announced the Grand Theft Auto VI: Ultimate Edition, a comprehensive premium package set to elevate the player experience in the sprawling state of Leonida. Slated for release on November 19, 2026, for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, this $99.99 edition offers an exclusive array of vehicles, weapons, customization options, and story-integrated content for protagonists Jason and Lucia. Players can anticipate a deeper immersion into Vice City's vibrant yet dangerous world with bespoke rides, personalized armaments, unique style choices, and new activities, all unlocking progressively throughout their criminal journey. All pre-orders will also receive the 'Vintage Vice City Pack,' while digital pre-orders further benefit from a complimentary month of GTA+.

## Key Takeaways
- **Release Date & Platforms:** Grand Theft Auto VI, including the Ultimate Edition, will launch on November 19, 2026, for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.
- **Ultimate Edition Price:** The GTAVI Ultimate Edition will be available for $99.99, while the Standard Edition is priced at $79.99.
- **Ultimate Edition Content Highlights:** Includes exclusive vehicles ('67 Vapid Dominator Buggy, '95 Grotti Cheetah, Dinka Enduro, Crest Kayak, Vapid Ganado Retro Build, Shitzu Squalo, 4 UE-exclusive classic cars), personalized weapons (Hawk & Little Morgan Revolver, engraved pistols), unique vehicle customization shops (Rideout Customs, One-Eyed Willie’s), a raid mission (PTT Youngin$ Illegal Goods Store), and extensive cosmetic options (Vice City Styles, Goodtime Gear, Stock 305 Clothing, Sara’s Unisex Salon, Electric Fang Tattoo).
- **Progressive Unlocks:** All Ultimate Edition bonuses will become available to Jason and Lucia as their story unfolds.
- **Upgrade Option:** Owners of the Standard Edition can purchase the Ultimate Edition Upgrade separately at any time.
- **Pre-Order Bonus: Vintage Vice City Pack:** All pre-orders (digital before Nov 20, 2026, or physical while supplies last) receive this pack, featuring the '55 Vapid Stanier sedan, Shore Court Garage, luxury outfits and hairstyles for Jason and Lucia, and a tropical weapon pattern.
- **Digital Pre-Order Bonus: Free Month of GTA+:** Digital pre-orders on PlayStation Store or Microsoft Store also include a free month of GTA+, offering monthly GTA$500,000, bonus Shark Card cash, free/discounted vehicles, and access to a rotating library of games in GTA Online.
- **Physical Edition Note:** Physical copies of GTAVI will contain a digital download code; no disc will be included in the box.
