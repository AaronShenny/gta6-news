# GTA 6: Players Anticipate Prioritizing Map Exploration Over Story Missions

A recent discussion on Reddit's /r/GTA6 community highlights a common sentiment among fans: the irresistible allure of Grand Theft Auto 6's expansive open world might overshadow its narrative at launch. User /u/LucheG shared their plan to spend the initial hours in GTA 6 purely on map exploration, drawing parallels to the hours spent simply driving around GTA 5's Los Santos. This sentiment suggests a significant portion of the player base is more eager to discover the intricate details and hidden gems of Vice City and its surrounding areas than to immediately delve into the main storyline, emphasizing the series' strong reputation for immersive and engaging environments.

## Key Takeaways
- Many players anticipate prioritizing open-world exploration over main story missions in GTA 6.
- The desire to explore stems from previous GTA titles, like GTA 5, where map traversal was a significant draw.
- Players are excited to discover the new map's details and atmosphere before engaging with the narrative.
- The community discussion points to the strong appeal of GTA's immersive environments.
