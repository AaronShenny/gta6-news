# GTA 6 Cover Art: Hidden Details Emerge from 'Shards' Extracted by Fan

A user on Reddit, /u/Clean-Extension1094, has undertaken the task of extracting and compiling high-quality 'shards' from the official Grand Theft Auto VI website's cover art. These digital segments, originally designed to create a unique visual effect on the site, contain portions of the full cover that are typically obscured by the prominent 'GTA VI' logo. The fan has resized and converted these native-quality images to PNG format, making them available for the community to meticulously analyze for any potential hidden details or Easter eggs from Rockstar Games.

## Key Takeaways
- Official GTA 6 website's cover art is digitally divided into 'shards'.
- These shards contain details hidden behind the 'GTA VI' logo.
- A Reddit user (/u/Clean-Extension1094) extracted high-quality versions of these shards.
- The extracted images are native quality, resized, and converted to PNG format.
- The purpose is to allow community analysis of the previously unseen details within the cover art.
