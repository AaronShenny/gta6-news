# GTA 6 on Nintendo Switch 2? Community Speculates on Potential Port

Following the highly anticipated announcement of Grand Theft Auto 6, and its confirmed release for platforms including the Xbox Series S, discussions have erupted within the gaming community regarding its potential availability on Nintendo's next-generation console. A popular Reddit thread on r/GTA6, initiated by user u/oversizedeclipse, has become a focal point for this debate. The original post highlights the perceived similarities in raw computing power between the Xbox Series S and the anticipated specifications of the Nintendo Switch 2, prompting a widespread conversation among fans about the feasibility and likelihood of Grand Theft Auto 6 eventually making its way to Nintendo's upcoming hardware.

## Key Takeaways
- Gamers are discussing a potential GTA 6 port for the Nintendo Switch 2.
- The discussion was sparked by GTA 6's confirmed release on the Xbox Series S.
- Comparisons are being drawn between the rumored hardware capabilities of the Switch 2 and the Xbox Series S.
- The debate originated from a Reddit post by user u/oversizedeclipse.
