# GTA 6: Vice City Map Size Visualized Against Los Santos

A recent community effort, highlighted through a Reddit post by /u/RedDad1, presents a visual comparison of Grand Theft Auto VI's anticipated Vice City map size against Grand Theft Auto V's Los Santos. Sourced from the dedicated GTA VI Mapping Discord, this visualization provides fans with an early perspective on the potential scale of the upcoming game's primary setting. It is noted, however, that the comparison currently lacks data for the rumored Port Gellhorn area.

## Key Takeaways
- Visual comparison of GTA 6's Vice City map size relative to GTA 5's Los Santos.
- The visualization was sourced from the active GTA VI Mapping Discord community.
- Aims to provide an early understanding of Vice City's potential scale.
- The comparison does not include data for the Port Gellhorn area at this time.
