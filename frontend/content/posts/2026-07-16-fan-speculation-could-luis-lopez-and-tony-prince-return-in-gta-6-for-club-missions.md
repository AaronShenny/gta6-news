# Fan Speculation: Could Luis Lopez and Tony Prince Return in GTA 6 for Club Missions?

A recent post on the dedicated r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked discussion among fans regarding potential character returns in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. User _balrose shared their strong desire to see Luis Lopez and 'Gay' Tony Prince, fan-favorite characters from Grand Theft Auto IV's expansion, *The Ballad of Gay Tony*, make an appearance. The user specifically envisioned club-related missions involving the duo, believing their established personalities and narrative arc would seamlessly 'match the vibe' of the upcoming title. This speculation highlights the community's engagement with past GTA lore and their hopes for how it might intertwine with the new setting and story of GTA 6.

## FAQ
**Who are Luis Lopez and Tony Prince?**
Luis Lopez and 'Gay' Tony Prince are prominent characters from Grand Theft Auto IV's expansion, *The Ballad of Gay Tony*. Luis serves as Tony's bodyguard and business partner, managing nightclubs in Liberty City.

**Why do fans want them in GTA 6?**
Fans appreciate their dynamic and involvement in the nightclub scene, believing their established roles could fit well with the rumored Vice City-inspired setting of GTA 6, potentially bringing back club-related missions.

**Is their return confirmed for GTA 6?**
No, their return is purely fan speculation and a wish expressed by a Reddit user. Rockstar Games has not made any official announcements regarding character appearances from past titles in GTA 6.
