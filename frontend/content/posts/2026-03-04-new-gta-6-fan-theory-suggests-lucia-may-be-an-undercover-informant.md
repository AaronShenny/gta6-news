# New GTA 6 Fan Theory Suggests Lucia May Be an Undercover Informant

A compelling new fan theory from Reddit user /u/Sea_Philosopher1080 suggests that Lucia, one of GTA 6's protagonists, might secretly be an informant. This theory challenges previous speculation about Jason or Brian and draws evidence from the game's trailers and official Rockstar website details, particularly focusing on Lucia's ankle monitor and her past with Jason. The premise is that Lucia may have been involved with Jason prior to her incarceration, possibly working with authorities to bring down bigger targets.

## Key Takeaways
- Evidence from the Raul Bautista section of Rockstar's website reportedly shows Lucia, Jason, and Raul after a bank robbery where Lucia does not appear to have an ankle monitor.
- The first two trailers are said to only cover the initial three chapters of a five-chapter game, leaving significant room for plot twists.
- During the bank robbery scene in the trailer where Lucia says 'hands above your heads now,' her feet are not shown, potentially masking the absence of an ankle monitor at that point.
- This pre-incarceration connection could explain Jason's familiarity with Lucia, evident in his 'hey' upon her release and his 'we're doing it right' comment, implying a shared history.
- The theory suggests the game might begin in Vice City with Lucia already connected to Jason, before her prison stint and subsequent ankle monitor.

## FAQ
**Why would authorities allow Lucia, an alleged informant, to engage in violent crimes like bank robbery?**
The theory suggests Lucia could be part of a larger sting operation, aiming to catch a 'big fish' like Raul Bautista, making her actions justifiable within that context.

**If Lucia is an informant, why would the game start in Vice City with a geo-blocking ankle monitor?**
This could be explained through narrative choices, serving the game's overarching storytelling and potentially being a plot device for her undercover activities.
