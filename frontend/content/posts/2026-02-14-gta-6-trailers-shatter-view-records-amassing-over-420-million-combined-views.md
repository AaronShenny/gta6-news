# GTA 6 Trailers Shatter View Records, Amassing Over 420 Million Combined Views

Rockstar Games has achieved an impressive milestone with its Grand Theft Auto 6 trailers, which have collectively garnered over 420 million views. Trailer 2 recently hit 150 million views within nine months, adding to the substantial 273 million views already accumulated by Trailer 1. This combined viewership underscores the massive global anticipation and excitement surrounding the next installment in the iconic GTA series.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 Trailer 2 has reached 150 million views.
- This milestone for Trailer 2 was achieved within nine months of its release.
- GTA 6 Trailer 1 currently holds 273 million views.
- The combined total views for both GTA 6 trailers now exceed 423 million.
- The high viewership reflects significant global anticipation for Rockstar Games' Grand Theft Auto 6.
