# GTA 6: The Community's Hopes for Enhanced Cycling Experience

A Reddit post from user /u/gonegirly444 titled "Anyone else excited for cycling in VI?" has initiated a discussion among the GTA 6 community regarding expectations for cycling mechanics. The user expresses a desire for significant improvements over GTA V's cycling, noting that despite its shortcomings, players still created impressive content like stationary bike mods and BMX tricks. Hopes for GTA VI include new features, diverse environments for both road and mountain biking, and even bike destruction. The post highlights a general lack of in-depth biking experiences in video games outside of extreme sports titles, prompting a call for community input on desired features.

## Key Takeaways
- Desire for improved cycling mechanics in GTA VI compared to GTA V.
- Acknowledgement of community-created content (mods, tricks) despite GTA V's limitations.
- Hopes for new cycling features in GTA VI.
- Expectation of diverse environments suitable for road and mountain biking.
- Interest in bike destruction mechanics.
- Observation that few video games capture the 'fun of biking' beyond extreme jumps.
- Call for community input on desired cycling features.

## FAQ
**What are the main hopes for cycling in GTA VI?**
