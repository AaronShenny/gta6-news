# GTA 6 Online: A Community Plea for Grounded Realism in Vehicle and Weapon Tech

A compelling argument from a dedicated GTA 6 fan on Reddit suggests a fundamental design philosophy for GTA 6 Online: a strict adherence to real-world vehicle and weapon technology. The proposal posits that by excluding futuristic or non-existent tech, Rockstar could foster a more balanced, grounded, and deeply immersive online experience. The only concession proposed is the cautious inclusion of real-life prototype technologies, provided they remain rare and retain their inherent limitations, thus ensuring variety without veering into unrealistic "la la land" scenarios.

## Key Takeaways
- Advocates for the exclusion of all vehicle and gun technology that does not exist in real life within GTA 6 Online.
- Aims to achieve better game balance and a more visceral, grounded player experience.
- Emphasizes enhancing player immersion by making the game feel like a "real place."
- Suggests that real-life prototype technologies could be included, but only if rare and with realistic limitations.
- Seeks to maintain variety and experimentation without resorting to futurism or unrealistic elements.

## FAQ
**What is the central argument regarding technology in GTA 6 Online?**
The primary argument advocates for restricting GTA 6 Online's vehicles and weapons to only those that exist in real life.

**Why is a real-world tech approach suggested for GTA 6 Online?**
This approach is proposed to create a more balanced, grounded, and immersive gameplay experience, making players feel more connected to a realistic world.

**Are any exceptions made for advanced technology?**
The proposal allows for the inclusion of real-life prototype technologies, but only if they are rare and come with all their real-world limitations.
