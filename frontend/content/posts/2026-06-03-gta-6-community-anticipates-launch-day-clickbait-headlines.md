# GTA 6 Community Anticipates Launch Day Clickbait Headlines

The r/GTA6 subreddit is buzzing with a lighthearted discussion where users are brainstorming potential clickbait headlines for Grand Theft Auto VI's launch day. The thread, initiated by u/parkwayy, highlights the community's anticipation and humor regarding the inevitable sensationalist content that will accompany the game's release. One popular example cited is the classic 'rage bait' headline: 'IS GTA 6 OVERRATED?!' often paired with an exaggerated facial expression in the thumbnail.
