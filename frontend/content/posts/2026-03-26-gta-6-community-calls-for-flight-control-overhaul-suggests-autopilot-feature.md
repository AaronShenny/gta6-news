# GTA 6: Community Calls for Flight Control Overhaul, Suggests Autopilot Feature

The persistent issue of challenging flight controls in Grand Theft Auto titles has resurfaced in community discussions surrounding GTA 6. Reddit user /u/The-Bite_of_87 recently voiced frustrations, specifically targeting the 'god awful' helicopter controls in GTA V. The user proposed a quality-of-life improvement for GTA 6: an optional autopilot mode, aimed at streamlining flying missions and making aerial navigation more accessible for all players. This sentiment reflects a broader desire within the fanbase for more intuitive and less frustrating aerial gameplay.

## Key Takeaways
- Community concern regarding flight controls in previous Grand Theft Auto games.
- Specific criticism directed at GTA V's helicopter controls, described as 'god awful'.
- A key suggestion for GTA 6 is the inclusion of an optional autopilot mode.
- The proposed autopilot aims to simplify flying missions and improve accessibility for players.
