# GTA 6 Hype Builds: Why May Could Be a Key Month for New Information

Speculation is mounting among fans regarding a potential Grand Theft Auto VI news drop in May. These factors combine to create a compelling argument for those eagerly awaiting fresh details on Rockstar's highly anticipated title.

- May 19th represents the six-month proximity mark from the game's release, a period during which Rockstar often provides updates.
- May 26, 2026, was the first proposed release date for GTA 6 before its delay.
