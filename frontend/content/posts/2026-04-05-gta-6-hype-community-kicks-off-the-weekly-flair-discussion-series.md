# GTA 6 Hype: Community Kicks Off 'The Weekly Flair' Discussion Series

A dedicated fan on Reddit, u/Valuable-Bag1024, has initiated a new weekly discussion series titled 'The Weekly Flair,' designed to engage the community in anticipation of Grand Theft Auto VI. The series, which will run every week until the game's launch, encourages users to suggest discussion topics and participate in weekly questions. The inaugural post asks fans to share their 'niche/very specific thing' they hope to see in GTA 6, with favorite answers to be highlighted in subsequent posts, fostering ongoing community engagement and speculation.

## Key Takeaways
- New weekly discussion series 'The Weekly Flair' launched on Reddit by u/Valuable-Bag1024.
- Series aims to maintain community engagement and discussion leading up to GTA VI's release.
- Users are encouraged to suggest topics and participate in weekly questions.
- The first week's discussion prompt is: 'What niche/very specific thing would you like to see in the game?'
- Favorite community answers will be highlighted in subsequent weekly posts.
