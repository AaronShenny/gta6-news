# GTA 6: Fan Speculation Points to a Potential 6-Star Wanted System for Enhanced Challenge

A discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited speculation about a potential 6-star wanted system in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/Helpful-Photo9408 expressed the desire for a more challenging experience, noting that GTA 5's current 5-star wanted level often falls short in terms of difficulty. The user believes a 6-star system in GTA 6 could introduce significantly tougher gameplay scenarios and unlock a wealth of new mission possibilities, particularly for the online component of the game.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user proposes a 6-star wanted system for GTA 6.
- The current 5-star wanted system in GTA 5 is perceived as not challenging enough.
- A 6-star system could offer a greater difficulty curve for players.
- It could lead to more complex and engaging missions, especially in GTA Online.
