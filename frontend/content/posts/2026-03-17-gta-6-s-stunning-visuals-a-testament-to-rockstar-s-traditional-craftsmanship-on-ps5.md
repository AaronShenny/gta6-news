# GTA 6's Stunning Visuals: A Testament to Rockstar's Traditional Craftsmanship on PS5

Furthermore, it highlights that these impressive visuals are achieved even on a base PlayStation 5 console, leading many to conclude that Rockstar Games remains significantly ahead of its competitors in terms of game development and graphical innovation, relying on traditional artistic and technical prowess.
