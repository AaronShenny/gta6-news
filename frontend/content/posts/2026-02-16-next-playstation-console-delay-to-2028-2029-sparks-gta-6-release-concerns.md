# Next PlayStation Console Delay to 2028/2029 Sparks GTA 6 Release Concerns

A recent Bloomberg report indicates that Sony is reportedly considering pushing back the release of its next PlayStation console to as late as 2028 or 2029. This potential delay, described as a 'major upset' to Sony's strategic plans, has fueled speculation within the gaming community regarding the release timeline of highly anticipated titles like Grand Theft Auto 6. The article connects this potential hardware shift with existing, unconfirmed rumors suggesting that Rockstar Games is encountering difficulties optimizing GTA 6 to run smoothly on the current PlayStation 5 hardware, raising questions about a possible further delay for the game.

## Key Takeaways
- Bloomberg reports Sony is considering delaying its next PlayStation console's debut to 2028 or 2029.
- This potential hardware delay would be a significant strategic shift for Sony.
- Existing rumors suggest Rockstar Games is facing challenges optimizing GTA 6 for the PlayStation 5.
- The article speculates whether these factors combined could lead to a further delay for GTA 6's release.
