# GTA 6 Online: Fan Proposes Realistic Fighter Jet Overhaul for Enhanced Gameplay

A passionate Grand Theft Auto fan has taken to Reddit to share a comprehensive vision for overhauling fighter jet mechanics in GTA 6 Online. Citing concerns over the unrealistic and overpowered nature of weaponized vehicles in GTA 5 Online, the user, /u/AlarmingInterview374, proposes several changes aimed at introducing more realism and strategic depth. Key suggestions include implementing a fuel consumption system, limiting explosive weaponry, and making military jets exclusive to high-risk heists rather than easily purchasable assets, hoping to curb the 'futuristic and weaponised' vehicle problem.

## Key Takeaways
- Introduce a realistic fuel system for jets where full throttle depletes fuel faster, leading to crashes if fuel runs out.
- Remove explosive rounds from jets entirely or limit them to a maximum of 100, prioritizing machine turrets.
- Limit powerful heat-seeking missiles on military jets to four per aircraft.
- Implement a slight delay (approximately 1.5 seconds) for ejecting from a jet.
- Assign a 4-star wanted level if an ejected jet crashes into civilians or vehicles.
- Make military jets unpurchasable, requiring players to steal them from heavily fortified military bases with difficult break-ins.
