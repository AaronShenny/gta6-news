# GTA 6 Trailer 3: Fan Speculation Hints at Major Unrevealed Content Beyond Jason & Lucia

A recent fan theory from Reddit's GTA 6 community speculates that Rockstar Games has been intentionally holding back significant new information for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto VI, particularly concerning its third trailer. The theory posits that the 2022 leaks, which inadvertently revealed details about Vice City, Jason, and Lucia, have shaped Rockstar's marketing strategy thus far. Instead of immediately showcasing entirely new content, the developer may have strategically focused earlier marketing on elements already exposed by the leaks.

The core of the speculation suggests that the decision to delay Trailer 3 is a calculated move to unleash a flood of entirely fresh information. The Redditor believes that this trailer will 'completely steer away from the Jason and Lucia focused storylines,' potentially introducing new playable characters, additional cities or islands beyond the known Leonida, and a much broader scope for the game than previously understood. This theory implies that Rockstar has been using the initial leaks to its advantage, maintaining a veil over a potentially much larger and more diverse world for GTA VI, which Trailer 3 is expected to finally begin revealing.
