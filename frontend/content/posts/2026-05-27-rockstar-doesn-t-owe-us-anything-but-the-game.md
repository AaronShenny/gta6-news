# Rockstar doesn’t owe us anything but the game.

A Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit, titled 'Rockstar doesn’t owe us anything but the game,' addresses the escalating impatience and frustration within the community over Rockstar Games' perceived lack of communication regarding Grand Theft Auto VI. The user, u/Imaginary-Plate5600, advocates for fans to 'ease up,' 'stop obsessing,' and 'go outside,' suggesting they enjoy the present rather than fixating on unreleased details. The post reminds the community that pre-order links and the game itself (with a speculative release date of November 19th mentioned) will eventually materialize, reinforcing the idea that Rockstar's primary obligation is to deliver the game, not constant social media updates.

## Key Takeaways
- Fans are reportedly upset about Rockstar's lack of tweets regarding GTA VI.
- The post encourages the community to relax, stop obsessing, and enjoy other activities.
- It suggests that pre-order links and the game will eventually be released (with a speculative date of 11/19).
- The core message is that Rockstar's primary duty is to deliver the game, not continuous social media engagement.
