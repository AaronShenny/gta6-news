# Will the 'New Zealand Trick' Work for GTA 6? Fans Debate Rockstar's Global Launch Strategy

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked debate among fans regarding Rockstar Games' potential launch strategy for Grand Theft Auto 6. User u/DaVoiceOfTruth suggests that Rockstar may opt for a global simultaneous launch, similar to the recent release of Crimson Desert, which rendered the 'New Zealand trick' ineffective. The primary concern is the immense spoiler potential surrounding GTA 6, with fears that early access streamers could ruin the game's narrative for a significant portion of the global player base hours before their official launch.

## Key Takeaways
- The 'New Zealand trick' is a common method for players to access games early by changing their console's region settings.
- There is speculation that Rockstar Games may block this method for GTA 6 to control the launch experience.
- Crimson Desert recently utilized a global simultaneous launch, preventing early access via region changes.
- The main concern is the prevention of widespread spoilers, given the highly anticipated story and content of GTA 6.
- Fans worry that early streamers could spoil key plot points up to 18 hours before others can play.

## FAQ
**What is the 'New Zealand trick'?**
The 'New Zealand trick' involves changing a console's region settings to New Zealand, allowing players in other time zones to access digitally released games earlier due to New Zealand being one of the first regions to hit midnight on a release date.

**Why might Rockstar block early access for GTA 6?**
The main speculation is to combat severe spoilers. Given GTA 6's highly anticipated narrative, Rockstar may want to ensure a more equitable experience for fans globally, preventing early plot revelations from spoiling the game for others.

**Have other games implemented a global simultaneous launch?**
Yes, the Reddit discussion specifically mentions 'Crimson Desert' as a recent example where a global sync launch prevented the New Zealand trick from working.
