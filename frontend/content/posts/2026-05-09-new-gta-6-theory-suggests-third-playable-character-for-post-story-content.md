# New GTA 6 Theory Suggests Third Playable Character for Post-Story Content

A compelling new theory originating from the GTA 6 subreddit suggests that Rockstar's highly anticipated title could feature a third playable protagonist, distinct from the confirmed duo Jason and Lucia. The theory posits this character might emerge in an epilogue, following a potential tragic end for the main couple, drawing inspiration from the Bonnie and Clyde narrative and Rockstar's own storytelling patterns in previous games. Evidence cited includes a character resembling the 'guy on the right' from a recent screenshot reportedly appearing in 2022 leaked gameplay for aiming tests, and the significant effort Rockstar seems to have put into redesigning these background characters from Trailer 1, hinting at a larger narrative role.

## Key Takeaways
- The theory suggests GTA 6 may feature a third playable protagonist beyond Jason and Lucia.
- This character could be introduced in an epilogue, potentially after Jason and Lucia meet a tragic end, mirroring the Bonnie and Clyde story and Rockstar's past narrative trends (e.g., Red Dead Redemption 2).
- A character similar to the 'guy on the right' in a recent screenshot was reportedly used in 2022 leaked gameplay for aiming mechanics testing.
- Rockstar's apparent effort in redesigning these background characters, who were also present in Trailer 1, and featuring them in new screenshots, suggests a significant role beyond mere NPCs.
- The 'guy on the right' is specifically highlighted as a potential candidate, described as 'cool and nonchalant,' making him a fitting epilogue protagonist akin to John Marston.
