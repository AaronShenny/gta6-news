# GTA 6 Fan Wishlist: Community Dreams Big for Vice City Activities

While official details for Grand Theft Auto VI remain scarce, the gaming community is already buzzing with anticipation and creative ideas for what the next installment could offer. One dedicated fan on Reddit, u/SunnySideYupX, has compiled an extensive wishlist of activities they hope to see, blending nostalgic throwbacks with ambitious new concepts. From returning classic services like taxi and bus routes to more immersive experiences like accessible theme parks and RDR2-style hunting, the list provides a fascinating glimpse into what players are dreaming of for GTA 6's open world.

## Key Takeaways
- Return of classic services: Taxi, Police, Fire Truck, and Bus.
- Nostalgic activities: Basketball, Bowling, Tennis, and underground street racing.
- Expanded leisure: Fully accessible theme parks with playable rides.
- New gameplay mechanics: Drone/RC missions, RDR2-style hunting, and interactive zoos where animals can be released.
- Immersive world features: Enterable restaurants (Cluckin' Bell, Burger Shot) and home invasions.
- Unique side missions: Scary mythical creature hunts.
- Vehicle variety: Speed boat racing and the return of the Tug boat.
- Athletic challenges: Triathlon.
