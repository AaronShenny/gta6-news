# Will the Ocelot Stromberg Dive into GTA 6? Community Weighs In

A passionate Grand Theft Auto fan, known as /u/JacksonBuck888 on Reddit, has ignited a lively discussion within the GTA 6 community regarding the potential return of the Ocelot Stromberg. The user expressed profound admiration for the Stromberg's design, engine sound, and driving dynamics in GTA Online, particularly when equipped with low grip tires. They posited that the vehicle's distinct 80s wedge-shaped design could make it a fitting inclusion for GTA 6, especially given the strong rumors of the game being set in a Vice City-like locale. However, the user also acknowledged a significant hurdle: the Stromberg's status as a 'special vehicle' with clear James Bond inspirations, which might position it as too fantastical for a mainline story or perhaps an exclusive feature to GTA Online's more outlandish gameplay. The post serves as an open invitation for fellow fans to speculate on the likelihood of this fan-favorite submersible car making its way into the next installment of the Grand Theft Auto series.

## Key Takeaways
