# GTA 6: Fan Suggests Ultra Music Festival-Style Event for Vice City

A user on the GTA 6 subreddit, u/PenExotic2859, has sparked discussion by suggesting that Grand Theft Auto VI could feature a large-scale music festival, drawing parallels to Miami's iconic Ultra Music Festival. The fan envisions an event spanning three days with multiple stages, hosting famous DJs and bands, potentially serving as a dynamic backdrop for in-game missions. The suggestion highlights the potential for Rockstar Games to leverage such an event to enhance the immersive open-world experience and mission variety in the highly anticipated title.
