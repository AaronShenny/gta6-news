# GTA 6 Community Buzz: Anticipating Enhanced Gameplay Features

With the marketing campaign for Grand Theft Auto 6 officially underway, marked by the recent reveal of its cover art and pre-order dates, the gaming community is eagerly discussing the new gameplay features they are most excited to experience. A popular Reddit thread on r/GTA6 captures this sentiment, with one user, Sylentdream, specifically highlighting their anticipation for an improved NPC dialogue system, drawing parallels to the acclaimed system in Red Dead Redemption 2, alongside a significant increase in overall NPC density within the game world. The community also notes that 'Trailer 3' is expected soon, further fueling speculation and excitement.
