# GTA 6 Online: Fan Fears Repeat of Red Dead Redemption 2's Missteps

A passionate Grand Theft Auto fan, known on Reddit as u/Spirited-Ad9699, has articulated anxieties regarding the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6 Online experience. Drawing parallels from Rockstar's previous titles, the user expresses deep concern that GTA 6's online component could mirror the perceived shortcomings of Red Dead Redemption 2 Online, which they found unenjoyable even after updates. In contrast, the fan lauded GTA 5's online mode, alongside its story, as a significant time investment and hopes GTA 6 Online will emulate that successful formula.

## Key Takeaways
- A dedicated GTA fan (u/Spirited-Ad9699) expresses significant concern over the future of GTA 6 Online.
- The user thoroughly enjoyed GTA 5's story and online mode, accumulating extensive playtime and expecting similar engagement with GTA 6.
- Strong disapproval for Red Dead Redemption 2 Online, despite loving RDR2's story, citing it as 'bad' even after updates.
- The primary fear is that Rockstar might 'mess up' GTA 6 Online in a way reminiscent of RDR2 Online's perceived failures.
- The fan hopes for GTA 6 Online to emulate the beloved and successful structure of GTA 5 Online instead.

## FAQ
**What is the primary concern voiced about GTA 6 Online?**
The main concern is that Rockstar Games might develop GTA 6 Online in a similar vein to the less-received Red Dead Redemption 2 Online, rather than the highly successful and popular Grand Theft Auto V Online.
