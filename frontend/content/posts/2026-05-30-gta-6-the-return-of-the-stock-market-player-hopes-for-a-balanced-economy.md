# GTA 6: The Return of the Stock Market – Player Hopes for a Balanced Economy

A dedicated fan on Reddit has shared their thoughts on the potential return of the stock market in Grand Theft Auto 6, expressing a desire for its inclusion but with significant improvements over its previous iteration in GTA V. The user highlights that in GTA V, the stock market, alongside heists, was virtually the only effective way to accumulate substantial wealth, with other activities, side missions, and properties offering laughably poor returns. They hope GTA 6 will follow the example of Red Dead Redemption 2, which provided a variety of engaging and profitable systems, ensuring the stock market is a fun, optional avenue for money-making rather than an essential grind.

## Key Takeaways
- Player hopes for the stock market to return in GTA 6.
- Criticism of GTA V's economy, where the stock market was the primary non-heist method for earning significant money.
- GTA V's other activities and properties were deemed ineffective for wealth generation.
- Desire for the GTA 6 stock market to be an optional, fun system, not the sole viable money-making option.
- Cites Red Dead Redemption 2 as a positive example with diverse and effective money-making systems.
