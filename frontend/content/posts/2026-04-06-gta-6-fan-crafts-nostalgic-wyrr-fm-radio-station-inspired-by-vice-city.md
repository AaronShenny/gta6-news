# GTA 6 Fan Crafts Nostalgic 'WYRR-FM' Radio Station Inspired by Vice City

A passionate member of the GTA 6 community, operating under the username /u/inspectahmemes, has unveiled a meticulously curated fan-made radio station dubbed '95.7 WYRR-FM, "The Wave of Vice City."' While drawing heavily on the iconic late 60s to early 80s rock and yacht rock soundscape reminiscent of the classic Vice City era, this project, shared within the GTA 6 subreddit, exemplifies the creative spirit of the fanbase. Available as a collaborative Spotify playlist, the creator emphasizes that this is a non-promotional, purely fan-driven endeavor and is actively inviting fellow enthusiasts to suggest additions to the tracklist, fostering a shared musical experience for the community.

## Key Takeaways
- A fan-made radio station, '95.7 WYRR-FM, "The Wave of Vice City,"' has been created and shared within the GTA 6 community.
- The station's theme focuses on late 1960s to early 1980s rock and yacht rock genres.
- It is available as a collaborative Spotify playlist.
- The creator is openly inviting community members to contribute song suggestions to the playlist.
- The project is purely a fan endeavor, not self-promotion, and aims to provide a shared nostalgic experience.
