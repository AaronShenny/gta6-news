# Fan Suggests Iconic Grumman LLV Mail Truck for GTA VI's Vehicle Roster

A recent discussion on the popular GTA 6 subreddit has highlighted an unexpected but highly recognizable vehicle for potential inclusion in Grand Theft Auto VI: the Grumman LLV mail truck. Initiated by user u/sllsfhm, the thread argues that this delivery vehicle, which has been a common sight across America for nearly 40 years, is a surprisingly overlooked candidate for the game's vehicle roster. The submission points out its pervasive presence in urban and suburban settings, suggesting it would be a fitting and authentic addition to GTA VI's anticipated open world, especially given its curious absence from prior Grand Theft Auto installments.

## Key Takeaways
- The Grumman LLV mail truck is a highly recognizable vehicle in America, present for nearly 40 years.
- The vehicle has been notably absent from previous Grand Theft Auto titles.
- A Reddit user suggests its inclusion in GTA VI, arguing it's an 'obvious choice' for a city setting.
- The suggestion highlights the vehicle's ubiquity and its potential to enhance the game's realism.
