# GTA 6: Community Debates Realistic Vehicle Exits – Will Windshields Be an Escape Route?

A popular discussion within the Grand Theft Auto VI community has ignited speculation about the realism of vehicle exits, particularly when a player's car is completely obstructed. Unlike the 'teleportation' mechanic employed in GTA 4 and 5, where characters would instantly appear outside a blocked vehicle, fans are advocating for a more immersive and gritty solution in the upcoming GTA VI. A prominent suggestion gaining traction is the implementation of a feature similar to Sleeping Dogs, where characters can dramatically smash through a windshield to escape a confined situation, promising a more visceral and believable player experience.

## Key Takeaways
- In GTA 4 and 5, characters 'teleport' out of vehicles when both sides are blocked.
- The GTA VI community desires a more realistic and immersive vehicle exit mechanic.
- A popular suggestion is to allow players to smash through windshields to exit, similar to Sleeping Dogs.

## FAQ
**How do characters currently exit blocked vehicles in GTA 4 and 5?**
Characters in GTA 4 and 5 will 'teleport' out of a vehicle if both exit sides are blocked.

**What kind of change are fans hoping for in GTA 6 regarding vehicle exits?**
Fans are hoping for a more realistic and immersive method of exiting blocked vehicles, rather than the instant teleportation seen in previous titles.

**Which game is cited as an example for a more realistic exit mechanic?**
Sleeping Dogs is mentioned as an example, where characters can smash through the windshield to escape a vehicle.
