# More Major Enterable Places in GTA 6: A Call for Enhanced Immersion

The gaming community is expressing a strong desire for Grand Theft Auto VI to vastly improve upon the number and quality of enterable interiors compared to its predecessors. While GTA V offered a sprawling map, many iconic structures like airports, malls, and large buildings remained inaccessible, often feeling like mere set dressing. Fans are hoping for fully interactive environments such as multi-floor malls, detailed airports with security and shops, casinos, hospitals, and multiple police stations, all accessible during free roam, not just for specific missions. The appearance of a club interior in Trailer 2 has fueled hopes that these locations will be freely explorable. Additionally, the community wishes for dynamic crowds within these interiors, changing based on the time of day to further enhance realism and immersion. The sentiment is that even if not every building is enterable, major landmarks should be, making the world feel significantly more alive.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA V's map, despite its size, felt limited by a lack of enterable major interiors.
- Players desire fully enterable airports (terminals, security, shops), multi-floor malls, casinos, hospitals, and more police stations in GTA 6.
- The emphasis is on free-roam access to these interiors, not just mission-specific entry.
- Hope for dynamic crowd changes within interiors based on time of day (e.g., quiet daytime, packed nighttime).
- The goal is to enhance immersion and make the world feel '10x more alive' by reducing 'locked doors everywhere'.
- Trailer 2's glimpse of a club interior has raised expectations for more accessible social spaces.

## FAQ
**Why is the community so focused on enterable interiors for GTA 6?**
Players feel that while previous GTA games offered vast open worlds, the lack of accessible major buildings like malls or airports broke immersion. Being able to enter and interact with these spaces during free roam would make the world feel more dynamic and alive, deepening the gameplay experience beyond just missions.

**What specific types of interiors are players hoping to see in GTA 6?**
The most requested interiors include fully explorable airports (with terminals, security, shops), multi-floor malls, casinos, hospitals, multiple police stations, and large buildings with actual interiors, all accessible outside of mission parameters.

**Will Rockstar Games likely implement these changes?**
While Rockstar has not confirmed the extent of enterable interiors, the increasing detail and player immersion focus in modern games, coupled with community feedback, suggests they are aware of this desire. The appearance of a club interior in Trailer 2 offers a glimpse of potential improvements, though the scope of free-roam access remains to be seen.
