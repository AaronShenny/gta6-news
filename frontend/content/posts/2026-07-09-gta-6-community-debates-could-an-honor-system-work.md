# GTA 6 Community Debates: Could an Honor System Work?

The Reddit community dedicated to Grand Theft Auto 6 is actively engaged in a discussion regarding the potential inclusion of an 'honor system' in the highly anticipated game. While some players express enthusiasm for such a mechanic, believing it could add depth, a significant portion of the community voices strong opposition. The primary argument against an honor system centers on the established nature of GTA protagonists, who are canonically depicted as 'horrible ass criminals with bad honors.' Critics question the feasibility of implementing a 'high honor' path for characters akin to past figures like Trevor or Toni, suggesting it would fundamentally contradict the core identity and narrative of the series. This ongoing debate highlights a clear divergence in player expectations regarding character morality and gameplay mechanics for GTA 6.

## Key Takeaways
