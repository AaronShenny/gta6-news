# GTA 6 Fan Proposes Unexpected Cooking Mechanic for Vice City

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has brought forth an intriguing and somewhat unexpected fan request for Grand Theft Auto 6: a fully implemented cooking system. User /u/Fun_Philosopher_2535 outlined a concept where players could visit in-game grocery stores to purchase ingredients like meat and vegetables, then prepare meals themselves. The proposal includes gameplay consequences, suggesting that perfectly cooked dishes could earn a 'Best Cook' achievement and positive feedback from Lucia, while culinary failures would elicit fitting negative reactions. The user's inspiration for this unique feature stems from a personal passion for cooking in real life, expressing a desire to see such a detailed activity integrated into the highly anticipated open-world title.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user suggests a comprehensive cooking mechanic for GTA 6.

- Perfectly cooked meals could unlock a 'Best Cook' achievement and positive character interactions (e.g., Lucia's compliments).
- Poorly prepared food would result in negative reactions from in-game characters.
