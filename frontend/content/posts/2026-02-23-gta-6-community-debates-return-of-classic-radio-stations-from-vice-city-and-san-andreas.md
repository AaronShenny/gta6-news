# GTA 6 Community Debates Return of Classic Radio Stations from Vice City and San Andreas

A recent discussion initiated by Reddit user /u/A_Wolf_Named_Foxxy on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited fan speculation regarding the potential for 'retro' radio stations in Grand Theft Auto 6. The proposal suggests that alongside new and contemporary music channels, the upcoming title could feature dedicated stations playing a curated selection of popular hits from iconic predecessors, specifically mentioning Grand Theft Auto: Vice City and Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. While acknowledging that not every song from the original games would be included, the idea centers on evoking nostalgia through the most memorable tracks.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user /u/A_Wolf_Named_Foxxy proposed the idea of 'retro' radio stations for GTA 6.
- The suggestion includes having both new stations and nostalgic channels.
- Specific examples cited are stations featuring popular songs from GTA Vice City and San Andreas.
- The proposal clarifies that only the most popular songs would be included, not the full original tracklists.
- The concept aims to appeal to long-time fans with a dose of nostalgia.
