# Fable Reportedly Delays Release to 2027 to Evade GTA 6 Collision Course

According to recent reports circulating online, Playground Games' highly anticipated Fable reboot may face a significant internal delay, potentially pushing its release window back to 2027. The speculated reason behind this strategic move is to distance the fantasy RPG from the inevitable market dominance of Rockstar Games' Grand Theft Auto 6. Developers are reportedly keen to avoid a head-to-head battle with one of the biggest entertainment launches in history, opting instead for a clearer release window to maximize Fable's impact and sales.

## Key Takeaways
- The Fable reboot is reportedly experiencing an internal delay.
- The potential new release target for Fable is now 2027.
- The primary motivation for this delay is to avoid direct competition with Grand Theft Auto 6.
- This suggests a strategic decision by developers to secure a more favorable market launch for Fable.

## FAQ
**Is Fable's 2027 release date officially confirmed?**
No, these are currently unconfirmed internal reports, and no official announcement regarding a delay or a 2027 release has been made by Playground Games or Xbox.

**Why would Fable delay specifically to avoid GTA 6?**
Grand Theft Auto titles are known for their immense cultural and commercial impact, often overshadowing other major releases. Delaying Fable could be a strategic move to ensure it receives ample attention and doesn't get lost in GTA 6's launch window.

**What does this mean for Fable's development?**
An internal delay, if true, suggests the development team is either taking more time to polish the game or strategically choosing a less competitive release slot. It doesn't necessarily indicate development troubles.
