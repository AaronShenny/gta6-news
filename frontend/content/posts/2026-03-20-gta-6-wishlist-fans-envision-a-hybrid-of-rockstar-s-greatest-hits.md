# GTA 6 Wishlist: Fans Envision a Hybrid of Rockstar's Greatest Hits

A recent Reddit post by user /u/Ultimate_Kratos11723 has sparked discussion among fans, outlining a compelling vision for what Grand Theft Auto 6 should aspire to be. The ambitious wishlist proposes an experience that synthesizes the best elements from Rockstar Games' extensive catalog. Key suggestions include the expansive character customization options akin to *Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas*, a refined driving mechanic that balances the realism of *GTA IV* with the accessibility of *GTA V*, and the distinct, vibrant atmosphere that defined *Grand Theft Auto: Vice City*. Furthermore, the user emphasized the desire for the unparalleled environmental detail seen in *Red Dead Redemption 2* and a deeply engrossing, well-crafted narrative reminiscent of *GTA IV*. This curated selection reflects a strong community desire for GTA 6 to be a culmination of Rockstar's greatest achievements.

## Key Takeaways
- Deep character customization on par with GTA: San Andreas.
- Driving mechanics that blend the realism of GTA IV with the feel of GTA V.
- The iconic atmosphere and vibe of Grand Theft Auto: Vice City.
- Intricate world details and environmental fidelity similar to Red Dead Redemption 2.
- A well-crafted and engaging story reminiscent of GTA IV.
