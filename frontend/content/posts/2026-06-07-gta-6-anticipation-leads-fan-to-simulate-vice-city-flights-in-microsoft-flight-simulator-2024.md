# GTA 6 Anticipation Leads Fan to Simulate Vice City Flights in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024

With the highly anticipated release of Grand Theft Auto 6 still some time away, one dedicated fan has found a creative way to bridge the wait. Reddit user /u/Certain-Quarter-3280 shared a video showcasing a remarkably realistic landing at Miami International Airport (RW26L) in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024, complete with real-time weather conditions. Recorded on a PS5 Slim, the user humorously captioned their post, stating they would 'pretend this is (flying in) GTA 6 until the game is actually out.' This playful approach highlights the gaming community's immense eagerness for GTA 6, particularly given its expected Vice City-inspired setting, which shares geographical parallels with Miami.
