# GTA 6 Console Debate: PS5 Pro vs. Xbox Series X, and the PC Factor

A recent Reddit discussion in the r/GTA6 community highlights the ongoing debate among players regarding the optimal platform for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. The original poster questioned why the Xbox Series X seems to be largely absent from console discussions, which often focus heavily on the PlayStation 5 and the rumored PS5 Pro. An updated note revealed a significant trend: many players are considering or have already purchased a PS5 Pro specifically for GTA 6, despite primarily gaming on PC. This raises the question of whether these players would return their new console if a PC version of GTA 6 were to launch simultaneously with the console versions. The original poster personally expressed reluctance to buy a PS5 Pro, believing their PC would offer superior performance and preferring to purchase the game twice rather than invest in new console hardware.

## Key Takeaways
- The Xbox Series X is notably absent from many GTA 6 console discussions, which tend to focus on PS5 and PS5 Pro.
- A significant number of PC gamers are reportedly buying PS5 Pro consoles specifically for GTA 6.
- Concerns exist about the potential for PS5 Pro purchases to be rendered redundant if GTA 6 launches on PC day one.
- Some players prioritize PC performance over console exclusivity or early access, opting to buy the game across multiple platforms if necessary.

## FAQ
**Why is there less discussion about the Xbox Series X regarding GTA 6?**
The article suggests potential reasons could be fewer Xbox players or the perception that PS5/PS5 Pro versions might offer a superior experience.

**Are many people buying a PS5 Pro just for GTA 6?**
Yes, the article indicates that a number of players, particularly those who primarily game on PC, have purchased or are considering purchasing a PS5 Pro specifically for GTA 6.

**Would players return their PS5 Pro if GTA 6 launched on PC day one?**
This is a key question posed in the discussion, highlighting the dilemma for those investing in a console for a game that might eventually come to their preferred platform.

**Is it worth buying a PS5 Pro if you have a high-end PC?**
The original poster believes their PC will outperform consoles and would rather buy the game twice than invest in a new console, suggesting it might not be worth it for those with powerful PCs.
