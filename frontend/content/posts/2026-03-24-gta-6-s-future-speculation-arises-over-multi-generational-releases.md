# GTA 6's Future: Speculation Arises Over Multi-Generational Releases

A recent Reddit post by user /u/nickd0g has sparked discussion regarding Rockstar Games' potential release strategy for Grand Theft Auto 6. Drawing parallels to the highly successful, multi-generational release of GTA V, the user speculates that GTA 6 will similarly launch on current consoles before being remastered and re-released for future platforms like the anticipated Xbox (expected around 2027) and PlayStation 6. This strategy, which saw GTA V released across three console generations plus PC, is expected to generate 'absurd' profits for Rockstar by encouraging multiple purchases from dedicated fans.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user speculates on GTA 6's multi-generational release strategy.
- The prediction is based on GTA V's history of releasing across PS3/Xbox 360, PS4/Xbox One, and PS5/Series X|S, plus PC.
- Future consoles like the next Xbox (around 2027) and PS6 are expected to be part of GTA 6's lifecycle.
- The user believes Rockstar will re-release GTA 6 multiple times to maximize revenue.
- This strategy is anticipated to generate 'an absurd amount of money' for Rockstar Games.
