# GTA 6's Subtle Visual Upgrade: Modern LED Lighting Illuminates Vice City

While easily overlooked, a significant visual upgrade in Grand Theft Auto VI will be the transition from the older sodium streetlights, prevalent in GTA V, to modern white LED lighting. This detail reflects real-world urban development, as sodium lights were largely phased out by the end of the 2010s. As glimpsed in the official trailer, Vice City's streets will be illuminated with contemporary LEDs, enhancing the game's realism and visual fidelity to align with current-day aesthetics.

- GTA 6 will feature modern white LED streetlights in Vice City.

- The new LED lighting is visible in the Grand Theft Auto VI trailer.
