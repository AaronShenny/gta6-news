# GTA 6 Ultimate Edition Reveals New Klose K17 Pistol, Heavily Inspired by Glock

The highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6 has revealed a new in-game firearm, the Klose K17 pistol, which will be included as a bonus for players who purchase the Ultimate Edition. The K17's design is a clear homage to real-world weaponry, drawing its primary inspiration from the iconic Glock 17, while also incorporating stylistic elements from other popular firearms such as the SIG Sauer P320. This new addition promises a familiar yet distinct weapon experience for players diving into the next iteration of the Grand Theft Auto universe.
