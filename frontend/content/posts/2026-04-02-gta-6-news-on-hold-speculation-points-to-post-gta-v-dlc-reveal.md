# GTA 6 News on Hold? Speculation Points to Post-GTA V DLC Reveal

A recent post on the dedicated GTA 6 subreddit has sparked discussion regarding the timing of future Grand Theft Auto 6 news. According to Reddit user /u/Neither_Chart_5737, significant revelations, official pre-order windows, or console bundle announcements for GTA 6 are unlikely to surface before the release of the upcoming Grand Theft Auto V DLC. The theory suggests that Rockstar Games will prioritize its current content roadmap for GTA V. The only potential exceptions cited are in-game collaborations, narrative bridges, or subtle advertisements within GTA V itself, designed to gradually transition players towards the highly anticipated sequel. Otherwise, any major 'paid' promotions, such as PlayStation 5 bundles, are not expected until late May at the earliest, potentially mirroring past release strategies for titles like Red Dead Redemption.

## Key Takeaways
- Major GTA 6 news, including official reveals and pre-order launches, is anticipated *after* the new GTA V DLC.
- Potential in-game teasers within GTA V, such as collaborations or ads, could precede major announcements.
- Paid promotional activities, like PS5 console bundles, are speculated to push pre-orders to late May or beyond.
- This outlook originates from community speculation on Reddit, not an official Rockstar statement.
