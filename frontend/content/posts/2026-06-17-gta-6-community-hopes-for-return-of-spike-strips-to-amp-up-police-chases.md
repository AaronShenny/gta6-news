# GTA 6 Community Hopes for Return of Spike Strips to Amp Up Police Chases

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan has taken to Reddit to voice a popular community desire: the reintroduction of spike strips in GTA VI. User 'Powerful_Slide3291' specifically highlighted their presence in previous titles like Grand Theft Auto: Vice City and Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories, arguing that these tactical police tools significantly enhanced the complexity and excitement of in-game pursuits. The sentiment suggests players are eager for deeper, more challenging chase mechanics in the upcoming installment.
