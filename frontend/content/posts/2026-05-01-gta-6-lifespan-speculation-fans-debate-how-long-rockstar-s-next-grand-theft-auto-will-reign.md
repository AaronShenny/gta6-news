# GTA 6 Lifespan Speculation: Fans Debate How Long Rockstar's Next Grand Theft Auto Will Reign

A recent discussion initiated by Reddit user u/OGAnimeGokuSolos on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked fan speculation regarding the potential lifespan of the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. The user posited that GTA 6 could remain relevant for approximately 13 years, citing Grand Theft Auto V's remarkable run from its 2013 launch, extended significantly by the continuous success and support for GTA Online, which they project to last until 2026. The core of this prediction rests on the assumption that Rockstar Games may not release another main series Grand Theft Auto title for an extended period following GTA 6's eventual release.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user u/OGAnimeGokuSolos speculated on GTA 6's potential lifespan.
- The prediction suggests GTA 6 could last around 13 years.
- Comparison drawn to GTA V's 2013-2026 estimated run, largely due to GTA Online.
- The speculation implies a lengthy gap before the next main GTA title after GTA 6.
