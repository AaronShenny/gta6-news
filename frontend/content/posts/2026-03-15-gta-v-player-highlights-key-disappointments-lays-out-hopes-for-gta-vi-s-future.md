# GTA V Player Highlights Key Disappointments, Lays Out Hopes for GTA VI's Future

A passionate Grand Theft Auto V player has voiced common frustrations with the game, particularly lamenting the one-time nature of spectacular missions like robbing trains or hijacking military aircraft, wishing for their repeatable availability akin to armored car robberies. Another significant point of contention raised is the persistent issue of character outfits resetting when switching between protagonists. Looking ahead to Grand Theft Auto VI, the player expresses a strong desire for a 'New Game Plus' feature, aiming to provide added incentives or streamline replays, alongside ensuring character customization choices remain persistent across character switches.

## Key Takeaways
- Disappointment over high-impact missions (e.g., train robberies, military aircraft hijacking) being one-time only.
- Desire for repeatable, engaging side activities similar to armored car robberies in GTA V.
- Frustration with character outfits resetting to defaults when switching between protagonists.
- Hope for a 'New Game Plus' feature in GTA VI to enhance replayability and offer bonuses.
- Expectation for persistent character customization choices in GTA VI.
