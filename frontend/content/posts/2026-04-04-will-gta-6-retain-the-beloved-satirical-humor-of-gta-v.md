# Will GTA 6 Retain the Beloved Satirical Humor of GTA V?

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited a conversation among fans concerning the potential comedic tone of the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/zuliiiiii, reflecting on a second playthrough of GTA V, emphasized how crucial the game's distinctive humor, over-the-top events, and memorable characters are to its appeal. This appreciation for GTA V's comedic essence led to a direct question about its successor, with the user noting that current GTA 6 trailers appear to convey a 'much more serious' overall tone. The discussion highlights a growing curiosity about whether Rockstar Games will maintain the series' signature satirical and humorous edge in its next major installment.
