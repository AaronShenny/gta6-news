The core of this analysis rests on the observation that both GTA V and RDR2 dropped their official gameplay reveals approximately 70-78 days before their respective launch dates. Applying this pattern to GTA 6, which is rumored for a late 2024 or early 2025 release, suggests a potential window for the highly anticipated gameplay trailer between September 2nd and September 10th. While purely speculative, this timeline offers an intriguing look at what Rockstar's pre-launch campaign might entail, providing fans with a potential roadmap for upcoming announcements.
