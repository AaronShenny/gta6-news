# GTA 6 Physique Mechanic Sparks Fan Debate: Immersion or Chore?

Following a brief glimpse of protagonist Jason bench-pressing in the official GTA 6 trailer, a Reddit user has sparked a discussion within the community regarding the potential return of character physique maintenance, reminiscent of Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. The original poster questioned whether such a mechanic would enhance the game's immersion or become an 'annoying chore' for players, inviting community speculation.

## Key Takeaways
- The GTA 6 trailer features protagonist Jason engaging in bench-pressing.
- This observation has led to speculation about the return of character physique maintenance mechanics.
- Comparisons are drawn to CJ's muscle stat leveling system in GTA: San Andreas.
- The community is debating whether this potential feature would be an immersive addition or a repetitive chore.
