# GTA 6 Online: What Could the New Rank System Look Like?

A recent discussion has emerged within the Grand Theft Auto community, focusing on the potential design of the rank and leveling system for **GTA 6 Online**. The conversation highlights the current system in GTA 5 Online, which features a notoriously high-level cap of 8000, with most unlocks ceasing at level 135. This led to a situation where very few players, perhaps only one legitimately, ever reached the maximum rank.

The core of the discussion revolves around whether Rockstar will opt to retain a similar progression model or introduce a more "in-depth and rewarding" system for the upcoming iteration. Players are keen to see if **GTA 6 Online** will offer a more engaging and continuous reward structure for leveling up, addressing the perceived stagnation of the current system once key unlocks are achieved.

## Key Takeaways
- The community is speculating on the ranking system for GTA 6 Online.
- GTA 5 Online's rank system had a cap of 8000, but unlocks stopped at level 135.
- Only a handful of players legitimately reached the maximum rank in GTA 5 Online.
- Players hope for a more in-depth and rewarding progression system in GTA 6 Online.
- The discussion questions if Rockstar will innovate or stick to the previous model.

## FAQ
**What is the main topic of discussion regarding GTA 6 Online's progression?**
The main topic is how the rank and leveling system will work in GTA 6 Online, and whether it will differ significantly from GTA 5 Online's model.

In GTA 5 Online, the level cap was 8000, but most in-game unlocks concluded at level 135, leaving a vast portion of the progression without new rewards.

The discussion suggests a desire for a more 'in-depth and rewarding' system, implying some dissatisfaction with the current model's lack of continued incentives past a certain point.
