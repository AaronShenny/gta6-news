# GTA 6's 13-Year Wait: How Players Have Grown Up in the Gap Between Mainline Titles

The upcoming release of Grand Theft Auto 6 on November 19, 2026, will mark an extraordinary 13-year gap since Grand Theft Auto 5 launched in September 2013. This extended wait has become a poignant point of reflection for a generation of gamers who have literally grown up during the interim. Many players, who were kids sinking hundreds of hours into GTA 5, are now university students or navigating adult life, noting that even some friends they played with have since moved on from gaming entirely. Despite these profound personal transformations and evolving lifestyles, the anticipation for the next mainline GTA entry has remained a surprising constant for many, highlighting the unique impact of Rockstar's lengthy development cycles.

## Key Takeaways
- Grand Theft Auto 5 was released in September 2013.
- Grand Theft Auto 6 is slated for release on November 19, 2026, creating a gap of over 13 years between mainline games.
- The long wait has seen players transition from childhood/teenage years to adulthood, with many now in university or pursuing careers.
- A significant portion of original GTA 5 players have reportedly stopped gaming during this extended period.
- Despite major life changes, the anticipation for GTA 6 has remained a consistent element for many players.
