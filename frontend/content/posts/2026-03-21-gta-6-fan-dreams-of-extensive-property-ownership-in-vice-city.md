# GTA 6 Fan Dreams of Extensive Property Ownership in Vice City

A fervent Grand Theft Auto 6 fan has taken to Reddit to share their ultimate wishlist feature: the ability to purchase and extensively customize mansions and penthouses within the game's rendition of Vice City. The user, /u/bigfoot_manhunter, specifically hopes for large, enterable properties, dreaming of owning a waterfront mansion on Starfish Island complete with a private dock, a swimming pool, a driveway filled with supercars, and a yacht. The core of their excitement lies in the potential for Rockstar Games to deliver richly detailed and immersive interiors for these high-end properties, suggesting that such a feature would make the game incredibly captivating.
