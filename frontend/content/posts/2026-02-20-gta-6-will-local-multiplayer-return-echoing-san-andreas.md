# GTA 6: Will Local Multiplayer Return, Echoing San Andreas?

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited discussions among the Grand Theft Auto community concerning the potential inclusion of local multiplayer in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6.
