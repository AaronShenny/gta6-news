# GTA VI Fans Demand Truly Menacing Antagonists

A recent Reddit post by u/Fazersion has ignited discussion within the GTA 6 community, expressing a strong desire for the upcoming title to feature a truly menacing and formidable antagonist. The user critiques past GTA villains as often being 'pathetic morons' or easily overcome, leading to protagonists who rarely felt vulnerable. Drawing parallels to chilling characters like Lalo Salamanca from *Better Call Saul* and Anton Chigurh from *No Country for Old Men*, the post advocates for a 'cold calculated psychopath' who instills a constant sense of dread. This type of antagonist, the author suggests, would be a perfect foil for protagonists Jason and Lucia, especially if they are depicted as relatively new to the criminal underworld and more susceptible to the dangers posed by powerful, ruthless figures.

## Key Takeaways
- Previous GTA antagonists are often perceived as 'pathetic' or lacking genuine menace.
- Past GTA protagonists rarely convey a sense of vulnerability to the player.
- A strong desire for a 'cold calculated psychopath' antagonist in GTA 6.
- Inspiration for such a villain comes from characters like Lalo Salamanca (Better Call Saul) and Anton Chigurh (No Country for Old Men).
- The author hopes Jason and Lucia will be portrayed as vulnerable, rising criminals to heighten the impact of a menacing antagonist.
