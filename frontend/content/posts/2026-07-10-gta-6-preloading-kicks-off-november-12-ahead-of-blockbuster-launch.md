# GTA 6 Preloading Kicks Off November 12 Ahead of Blockbuster Launch

Rockstar Games has officially announced the preloading schedule for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. Players who pre-order a digital copy of GTA 6 will be able to begin preloading the game on **November 12**, a full week before its official launch on **November 19**. This early access to downloads is designed to ensure players can dive into the game the moment it unlocks.

Interestingly, GTA 6 will **not be released on a physical disc**. Instead, physical copies sold at retailers will contain a download code within the box. These physical editions will also be available for purchase starting **November 12**, allowing those who prefer a boxed product to pick it up and preload the game before the official launch day.

While the exact file size for GTA 6 remains undisclosed, it is widely expected to be substantial, likely exceeding 100 GB, given its predecessor, GTA 5, often surpassed this mark. Rockstar has yet to announce the precise launch times on November 19, advising players to stay tuned for further updates.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 digital preloading starts November 12.
- The game officially launches on November 19.
- Physical copies will not include a disc but a download code, available November 12.
- The game's file size is expected to be over 100 GB, though not yet confirmed.
- Specific launch times for November 19 are still unannounced by Rockstar.
