# GTA 6 Fans Demand Next-Level Car Customization: Wide Body Kits and NOS Inspired by Need for Speed

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan has voiced their desires for an overhaul in vehicle customization for the upcoming GTA 6. The Reddit user, /u/Sukaran09, emphasized a strong focus on car upgrades, specifically requesting a diverse range of wide body kits and the inclusion of Nitrous Oxide Systems (NOS). They expressed a hope that the level of customization in GTA 6 would at least rival that found in the popular Need for Speed series, suggesting a deeper and more engaging modification experience for players. While the post acknowledged the possibility of customizing other vehicle types like bikes, boats, and planes, the core sentiment clearly revolved around enhancing the car modification aspect of the game.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 fans are eager for advanced vehicle customization options.
- Specific requests include wide body kits and NOS for cars.
- The desired customization depth is compared to the Need for Speed series.
- While other vehicle types could benefit, the primary focus is on car upgrades.
