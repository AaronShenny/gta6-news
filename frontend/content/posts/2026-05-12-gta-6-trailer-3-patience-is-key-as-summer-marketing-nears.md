# GTA 6 Trailer 3: Patience is Key as Summer Marketing Nears

A recent Reddit post reassures disappointed fans eagerly awaiting the third Grand Theft Auto VI trailer, emphasizing that while May didn't deliver, a significant marketing push is imminent this summer. The author reminds the community that 'May is NOT summer,' and Rockstar's rapid marketing pickup is slated for June 21st onwards. Key indicators like CEO Strauss Zelnick's confirmation of a November 2026 release, emails to PS4 players, and leaked ratings suggest the game is on track without delays. Drawing parallels to Red Dead Redemption 2's marketing timeline, the post suggests that GTA 6 is still ahead of schedule for its third trailer. Fans are advised to relax and anticipate a June/July filled with new trailers, screenshots, pre-order announcements, and TV commercials.

## Key Takeaways
- Disappointment over the absence of a third GTA 6 trailer in May is acknowledged.
- Summer (starting June 21st, or commonly June 1st) is the confirmed period for rapid marketing, not May.
- Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick has affirmed GTA 6's November 2026 release target.
- Recent activities like PS4 upgrade emails and leaked ratings indicate no delays.
- RDR2's third trailer was released closer to its launch date, suggesting GTA 6 is still on schedule.
- June/July is expected to bring extensive marketing, including trailers, screenshots, pre-orders, and commercials.
