# GTA 6 Fans Demand a 'More Alive' and Engaging Map Experience

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user Historical_Buyer5248, highlights a significant community desire for Grand Theft Auto VI: a vastly improved and more engaging map. The user specifically points to Grand Theft Auto V's map, criticizing its 'dead' feel and the large portions of 'empty land with nothing to do.' The hope is that GTA VI's trailers accurately represent a city that will be 'much more alive and real,' offering a richer, more dynamic player experience compared to its predecessor.

## Key Takeaways
- The primary desired improvement for GTA 6 is the game's map.
- Criticism of GTA V's map for feeling 'dead' and containing large, empty areas with little to do.
- Hope that GTA 6 trailers accurately depict a 'much more alive and real' city.
- A strong desire for a more engaging and dynamic open world experience in GTA 6.
