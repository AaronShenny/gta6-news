# GTA 6 Map Speculation: Mount Kalaga Discovery Fuels Three-State Theory

A dedicated GTA 6 enthusiast recently ignited a discussion by highlighting a curious detail on a popular fan-made map. While exploring a speculative rendition of the game's rumored Leonida state, they identified the inclusion of "Mount Kalaga." A quick cross-reference with real-world geography revealed that the actual Mount Kalaga is situated in North Carolina, a notable distance from the typical Florida representation associated with "Leonida." This geographical observation has prompted intriguing speculation among fans about Rockstar Games' potential design choices, with the original poster pondering if the developer's renowned commitment to realism might suggest players could traverse not just one or two, but potentially three distinct states within the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI.

## Key Takeaways

- The real-life Mount Kalaga is located in North Carolina.
