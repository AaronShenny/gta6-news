# GTA 6 Hype Surges for August 7 as Take-Two Alters Earnings Call Schedule

Grand Theft Auto 6 preorders are now live, and new screenshots have recently been released, with the game's highly anticipated launch date locked in for November 19 on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S. Despite these updates, Rockstar Games has remained notably quiet regarding specific gameplay details or a third cinematic trailer. However, fan excitement is now fixated on **August 7**, the date Take-Two Interactive, Rockstar's parent company, is scheduled to release its next quarterly sales report. Intriguingly, Take-Two has changed its usual reporting schedule for this date, opting to release the report and hold a conference call before the market opens at 8 AM ET, a departure from its historical practice of reporting after market close. While Take-Two has offered no official explanation for this shift, the change, coupled with past precedents of Rockstar revealing information around earnings reports and Take-Two's promise of a 'summer marketing campaign' for GTA 6, is fueling intense speculation among fans for a potential new reveal or trailer. It has also been confirmed that GTA 6 will be a digital-only release.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 is set to launch on November 19 for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, with preorders reportedly live.
- The game will be a digital-only release, with no disc version available.
- Fans are eagerly anticipating a third trailer or new gameplay details, as Rockstar has been quiet on specifics.
- August 7 is a key date for fans, as Take-Two Interactive will release its quarterly earnings report.
- Take-Two is deviating from its usual schedule, releasing the report and holding a conference call before the market opens on August 7, sparking speculation about a potential GTA 6 announcement.
- There is precedent for Rockstar to reveal new information around Take-Two's earnings reports, and a 'summer marketing campaign' is expected.
