# Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick Surprises Players as Secret Playable Character in WWE 2K26 DLC

The latest downloadable content for WWE 2K26 has delivered an unexpected surprise to players: Strauss Zelnick, CEO of Take-Two Interactive (parent company of 2K Sports), is now a playable character. Unannounced in the official press release, Zelnick appears in a blue suit and dress shoes, boasting a 77 overall rating. His in-game persona features an exclusive entrance to Frank Sinatra's 'My Way,' complete with unique commentary and a custom Titantron video, as highlighted by YouTube users and discussions on ResetEra. While a novel addition, this isn't the first instance of a real-world gaming executive appearing in a video game, echoing similar inclusions like Yosuke Matsuda and Kenichi Sato in Nier: Automata.

## Key Takeaways
- Strauss Zelnick, CEO of Take-Two Interactive, is now a secret playable character in WWE 2K26's latest DLC.
- Zelnick is depicted in a blue suit, has a 77 overall rating, and enters to an exclusive rendition of Frank Sinatra's 'My Way'.
- His inclusion was not mentioned in the official DLC press release, surprising many players.
- Unique commentary and Titantron video accompany Zelnick's entrance.
- This marks another instance of a gaming executive appearing as an in-game character, following examples like Nier: Automata.
