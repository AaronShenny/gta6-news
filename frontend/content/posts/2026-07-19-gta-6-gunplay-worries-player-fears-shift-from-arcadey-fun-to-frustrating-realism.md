# GTA 6 Gunplay Worries: Player Fears Shift from Arcadey Fun to Frustrating Realism

A concerned Reddit user, /u/No_Factor_7501, has voiced significant apprehension regarding the potential direction of gunplay in Grand Theft Auto 6, drawing comparisons to the established, arcadey combat of GTA Online and the more realistic, yet often criticized, mechanics of Red Dead Redemption 2. The user's primary fear centers on rumors suggesting GTA 6 will feature more pronounced weapon recoil and bloom. While acknowledging a potential limit of 2-4 weapons isn't a major issue, the user argues that recoil and bloom could render firearms unreliable and unsatisfying, contrasting sharply with the "all about shooting" philosophy they associate with GTA 5's successful gunfights. The post specifically highlights the "horrible bloom" in RDR2, expressing a strong desire for GTA 6 to avoid a similar system that demands overly thoughtful shot placement. The user concludes by hoping that if challenging bloom is implemented, enemy AI won't retain the "aimbot" precision often seen in current GTA titles.

## Key Takeaways
- Player expresses fear over potential changes to GTA 6 gunplay.
- Concerns include increased recoil and weapon bloom.

- Hopes enemy AI won't have aimbot if bloom is implemented.
- Weapon carry limit (2-4 guns) is a minor concern.

## FAQ
**What are the main concerns about GTA 6 gunplay?**
The main concerns revolve around potential increased weapon recoil and the introduction of bloom, which some fear could make gunplay unreliable and less satisfying compared to previous GTA titles.

The user prefers the arcadey, reliable combat of GTA Online and praises GTA 5's "all about shooting" gunfights. They express strong concern about GTA 6 adopting bloom mechanics similar to the "horrible" system found in Red Dead Redemption 2.

**Is the weapon limit a major issue for the user?**
While a weapon carry limit of 2-4 guns is mentioned, the user states they are "not very against" it, indicating it's a minor concern compared to recoil and bloom.

**What is the user's hope regarding enemy AI if bloom is present?**
The user hopes that if GTA 6 implements challenging bloom, the enemies will not retain the "aimbot" precision often seen in current GTA games, as this combination would be overly frustrating.
