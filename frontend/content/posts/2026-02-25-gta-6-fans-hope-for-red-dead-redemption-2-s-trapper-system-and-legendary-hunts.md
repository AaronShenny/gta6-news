# GTA 6 Fans Hope for Red Dead Redemption 2's Trapper System and Legendary Hunts

Enthusiasts on the GTA 6 subreddit are actively discussing the potential for deeper wilderness mechanics in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. A prominent thread highlights fan hopes for a 'trapper' system, reminiscent of the popular feature in Red Dead Redemption 2, which would be adapted for GTA 6's modern setting. Beyond crafting and trading, the discussion also delves into desires for camping functionalities and the thrilling prospect of hunting legendary animals within the game world, suggesting players are eager for expansive, immersive activities beyond traditional urban crime.
