# Beyond Graphics: GTA 6 Online Fans Demand Overhauled Gunplay and Dynamic AI

A recent discussion on the GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user /u/kushingtonsteele, delves into the community's desires for *Grand Theft Auto 6 Online* beyond just graphical enhancements. The primary demand highlighted is a significant overhaul of the gunplay mechanics, with players citing *Red Dead Redemption 2* and *Mafia 3* as prime examples of engaging shooting experiences they hope to see in GTA 6.

Beyond combat, the user speculates on the potential for a groundbreaking, proprietary A.I. system from Rockstar. This advanced A.I., theorized to be a reason for the delayed gameplay reveal, could offer unique experiences for each player based on various in-game circumstances. The challenge, however, lies in integrating such a complex system into a massive online environment with numerous players.

Further speculation suggests the introduction of a sophisticated reputation system, possibly tied to the rumored social media elements within the game. This system could potentially influence lobby assignments, leading 'grifters' to 'shitty lobbies' where the A.I. becomes inherently more hostile. Additionally, the article anticipates a much steeper and more realistic police and resistance system, designed to heavily penalize players for chaotic in-game actions, aligning with the expected realism of the game's world.

## Key Takeaways
- Players are strongly advocating for a significant overhaul of GTA 6 Online's gunplay, referencing RDR2 and Mafia 3.
- Speculation exists about a proprietary A.I. system from Rockstar that could create unique player experiences.
- Concerns are raised about how such an A.I. system would function in a large-scale online environment.
- A reputation system, possibly linked to in-game social media, might influence player lobbies and A.I. hostility.
- Expectations are for a much stricter and more realistic police and resistance system in GTA 6 Online.
