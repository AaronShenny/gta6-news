# GTA 6 Community Debates Returning Vehicles: Classics vs. Controversial Favorites

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit sparked a lively discussion among fans regarding which vehicles from Grand Theft Auto V and previous installments they wish to see make a comeback in the highly anticipated GTA 6. The original poster, u/Soggy-Garlic-4011, initiated the conversation by sharing their personal wishlist, highlighting a desire for classic cars and powerful utility vehicles.

Among the vehicles the poster hopes to see are the **Jubilee**, **FMJ**, and **Coquette Blackfin**, suggesting a preference for stylish, high-performance cars. For more robust options, the **Rhino Tank**, **Menacer**, and the iconic **Titan** aircraft were also mentioned as desired returns.

Crucially, the post also expressed a strong sentiment against certain 'unskillful' weaponized vehicles, specifically calling out the **Oppressor Mk2** as one they definitely do not want to see in GTA 6. This reflects a common community desire for a more balanced and less disruptive online experience, moving away from vehicles that can dominate gameplay with minimal skill.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 fans are actively discussing desired returning vehicles on Reddit.
- The community expresses a wish for classic cars like the Jubilee, FMJ, and Coquette Blackfin.
- Powerful vehicles such as the Rhino Tank, Menacer, and Titan are also requested.
- There is strong opposition to 'unskillful' weaponized vehicles like the Oppressor Mk2 returning to GTA 6.
