# GTA 6 Collector's Edition: Fans Debate £299+ Price Tag and Dream Merch

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited a lively debate among Grand Theft Auto fans regarding the potential cost and contents of a highly anticipated GTA 6 collector's edition. User /u/AbzPrime initiated the discussion by posing the question, 'Would you buy a collectors edition if it costs over £299?', while also detailing their own ambitious wishlist. AbzPrime indicated a willingness to purchase such a premium bundle if it included extensive merchandise like a glass plaque featuring the 'VI' sign alongside Vice City branding, a themed cap and mug, a GTA 6-branded controller, and a console skin. The post served as a catalyst for other fans to share their own wishlists for what they would expect from a high-value collector's edition for the upcoming title.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user initiated a discussion about a hypothetical GTA 6 collector's edition.
- The proposed price point for this collector's edition was over £299.
- The user /u/AbzPrime expressed willingness to buy if specific merchandise was included.
- Desired merchandise included a glass plaque (VI sign, Vice City), a cap, a mug, a GTA 6-themed controller, and a console skin.
- The post invited other fans to share their ideal collector's edition contents.
