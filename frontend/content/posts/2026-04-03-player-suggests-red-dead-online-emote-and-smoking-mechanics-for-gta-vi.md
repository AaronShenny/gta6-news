# Player Suggests Red Dead Online Emote and Smoking Mechanics for GTA VI

A dedicated fan has taken to Reddit to suggest that Grand Theft Auto VI could benefit greatly from re-imagining certain features found in Red Dead Online. The primary focus is on the emote system, specifically noting how NPCs in RDO sometimes react to player emotes, which could add a layer of dynamic interaction in GTA VI. More notably, the user highlights the detailed smoking animations from RDO, where players can control the duration of a cigarette smoke and when their character takes a puff. This level of detail is preferred over the typical 'one puff and discard' animation seen in many games. The suggestion extends to incorporating the ability to smoke weed, with a desire for more pronounced visual effects that scale with increased consumption, further enhancing immersion.

## Key Takeaways
- Reimagining Red Dead Online's emote system for GTA VI, noting NPC reactions.
- Detailed smoking animations from RDO, allowing player control over puff timing and animation length.
- Desire to move beyond 'one puff and discard' animations for consumables.
- Suggestion to include smoking weed with escalating visual effects based on consumption.
- Focus on enhancing player immersion through refined character interactions and animations.
