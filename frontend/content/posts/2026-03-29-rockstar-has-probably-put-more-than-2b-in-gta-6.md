# Rockstar has probably put more than $2B in GTA 6

A recent deep dive into public financial documents filed with the UK government has uncovered a staggering potential investment in Grand Theft Auto 6. An analysis of Rockstar Games UK Limited (Rockstar North)'s employee wages and salaries from March 2019 to March 2025 indicates a spend of at least £1.6 billion, which translates to approximately $2.1 billion USD. This significant sum, derived from financial statements assuming GTA 6 entered full production in 2019, represents only the costs associated with Rockstar North. With other Rockstar studios undoubtedly contributing to the highly anticipated title, the total development cost for GTA 6 is projected to potentially reach or even exceed the $3 billion mark, solidifying its status as one of the most expensive games ever made.

## Key Takeaways
- Analysis of public UK government financial documents for Rockstar Games UK Limited (Rockstar North) reveals development costs.
- Employee wages and salaries from March 2019 to March 2025 were compiled, assuming GTA 6 entered full production in 2019.
- Total estimated spend for Rockstar North during this period is £1.6 billion, roughly $2.1 billion USD.
- This figure exclusively accounts for Rockstar North; costs from other contributing Rockstar studios are not included.
- The total development cost for GTA 6 is projected to potentially reach or exceed $3 billion when all studios are considered.
