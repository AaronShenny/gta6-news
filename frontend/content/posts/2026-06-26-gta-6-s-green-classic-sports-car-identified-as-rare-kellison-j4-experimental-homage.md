# GTA 6's Green Classic Sports Car Identified as Rare Kellison J4 Experimental Homage

Eagle-eyed fans have identified a prominent green classic sports car featured in recent Grand Theft Auto 6 screenshots as being based on the exceptionally rare 1959 Kellison J4 Coupe 'Experimental'. This custom-built vehicle appears to be represented by a new in-game brand, sporting an 'R' badge on its bonnet, yet interestingly features Declasse-branded rims. This detail serves as a clever Easter egg, referencing how real-life Kellison cars were historically constructed using Chevrolet parts, a brand often parodied by Declasse in the GTA universe.
