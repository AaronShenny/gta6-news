# The ocean may be a massive and active expanse in the game, here's why

This article explores the compelling theory that Grand Theft Auto 6's ocean will be a far more integral and active part of the gameplay experience than ever before. Drawing connections between South Florida's real-world geopolitical ties to the Caribbean, the emphasis on modern-day smuggling in the leaks, and Rockstar's recent experiments in GTA Online, the author suggests a robust maritime environment. This could include advanced boat handling, a unique naval wanted system featuring the Coast Guard and Navy, and a wealth of dynamic events across a chain of islands, all potentially aligning with the rumored 'Project Americas' vision for the game.

## Key Takeaways
- The ocean's significance is tied to South Florida's geopolitical connection with Cuba and the Caribbean, serving as a real-world hub for modern smuggling and contraband trafficking.
- Rockstar has been experimenting with modern trafficking elements like encrypted cargo, stealth submarines, and high-tech sonar in GTA Online, hinting at their inclusion in GTA 6.
- The real-life presence of the Navy and Coast Guard in the region could translate into a unique, deep-ocean wanted system in the game, featuring specialized combat ships and attack drones.
- Boat handling is expected to be more sophisticated than GTA 5, with customization, different model advantages, and skill-based navigation influenced by dynamic waves and weather effects.
- The deep ocean and island chain have the potential to form a unique map biome, offering random events such as smuggling jobs, underwater wrecks, salvaging operations, and encounters with rival NPCs or law enforcement.
- Confirmed elements like yachts, cargo ships to steal, islands with airstrips, boat racing, and both overwater and underwater fishing will integrate into the maritime experience.
- The ocean at night is described as a terrifying place, with hints of unusual encounters and the Bermuda Triangle being mentioned in leaks.
- Boats and seaplanes are expected to be quintessential tools for exploration, akin to cars on land.
- The ability to switch between Jason and Lucia could enable dynamic attack and defense gameplay during naval engagements.
- Recent GTA Online updates (Cayo Perico, smuggling, money laundering, shipwrecks, HEAT systems) suggest Rockstar is already 'dabbling' with these concepts, reinforcing the 'Project Americas' vision.
