# GTA 6 Hype Soars: Trailer 2 Hits 150 Million Views, Total Showcase Nears Half a Billion

The excitement surrounding Grand Theft Auto 6 continues to escalate, as its second official trailer recently surpassed the impressive milestone of 150 million views within nine months of its release. This achievement, when combined with the first trailer's substantial 273 million views, brings the cumulative viewership for the GTA 6 trailer showcase to an astonishing 423 million. This exceptional engagement underscores the 'incredible progress' made by Rockstar Games and the immense global anticipation for their next blockbuster title.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 Trailer 2 has accumulated 150 million views.
- This milestone was achieved within nine months of its release.
- GTA 6 Trailer 1 currently stands at 273 million views.
- The combined viewership for both GTA 6 trailers totals 423 million views.
- The high view counts signify immense global anticipation for Rockstar Games' upcoming title.
