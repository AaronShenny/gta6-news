# The Search for a True 'GTA Clone': Is GTA 6 Destined for Unrivaled Dominance?

A recent discussion thread on Reddit's r/GTA6 community, initiated by user u/LeifEriksson187, highlights a perceived drought of quality 'GTA clones' or direct competitors within the open-world genre. The original poster argues that many recent attempts to rival the Grand Theft Auto series have fallen short. Titles like Watch Dogs Legion and the Saints Row Reboot are dismissed as 'mid,' while Mindseye is labeled 'trash' and the 'Samson game' as 'pretty disappointing.' Although Mafia Definitive Edition is acknowledged for its quality, it's noted as being primarily story-driven with limited open-world activities. Cyberpunk 2077, despite its scale, is not considered sufficiently similar to GTA. The prevailing sentiment is that GTA 6 is unlikely to encounter any truly formidable alternatives or competitors in its genre anytime soon.

## Key Takeaways
- The current decade has not produced a truly good 'GTA clone' or direct competitor.
- Watch Dogs Legion and Saints Row Reboot were deemed 'mid' by the community member.
- Mindseye was labeled 'trash' and the 'Samson game' was 'disappointing.'
- Mafia Definitive Edition, while good, is seen as too story-driven to be a true open-world alternative.
- Cyberpunk 2077 is not considered similar enough to GTA to be a direct competitor.
- GTA 6 is predicted to face little to no significant competition in its genre upon release.
