# GTA 6: Could Liberty City's Presence in Vice City Imply a 'New York' Equivalent?

A recent Reddit discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user /u/BenfordAbrahams, explores an intriguing speculative question regarding city names within the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. The core of the discussion posits that if GTA 6's Vice City, a clear parody of Miami, were to feature a location named 'Liberty City' (referencing a real Miami neighborhood rather than the GTA equivalent of New York City), this could open the door for a 'New York' equivalent to exist within the game's world. The user acknowledges Rockstar's established pattern of parodying real-world locations but also raises a valid concern about potential player confusion if a neighborhood shares a name with an entire prior game's primary setting.
