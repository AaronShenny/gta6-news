# Fan Uses AI to Reimagine GTA 6 Night Screenshots in Daylight

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto 6 fan on Reddit, known as /u/AFKInACutscene, has applied artificial intelligence to transform official or leaked GTA 6 night screenshots into daytime scenes. The creator explicitly states that these conversions are solely for demonstration purposes, aimed at helping the community visualize how the game's environments might appear under daylight conditions. While acknowledging that the AI-generated results are not perfectly accurate, the project provides a compelling rough idea of Vice City's potential daytime ambiance, sparking further community discussion and speculation.
