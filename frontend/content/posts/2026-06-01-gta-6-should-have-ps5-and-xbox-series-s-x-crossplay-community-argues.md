# GTA 6 Should Have PS5 and Xbox Series S/X Crossplay, Community Argues

A fervent discussion within the Grand Theft Auto community has emerged, advocating for the introduction of crossplay functionality exclusively between PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series S/X in the highly anticipated GTA 6. While acknowledging the challenges of PC crossplay due to prevalent cheating, the sentiment suggests that console-only cross-platform play would significantly enrich the online experience. However, concerns have been voiced regarding Rockstar's potential adherence to older, peer-to-peer online hosting methods, a system dating back to GTA 4. This perceived reluctance by Take-Two, Rockstar's parent company, to modernize online infrastructure sparks speculation that crossplay might not be prioritized, potentially leading to the familiar platform separation seen in previous installments.

## Key Takeaways
- A strong desire exists for crossplay in GTA 6, specifically between PS5 and Xbox Series S/X.
- PC players are suggested to be excluded from console crossplay due to widespread cheating concerns.
- Concerns are raised that Rockstar might not implement crossplay due to a continued reliance on outdated peer-to-peer online hosting methods.
- This approach is seen by some as a cost-saving measure by Take-Two, rather than an investment in modernizing online services.
- The community fears GTA 6 could launch without crossplay, resulting in complete platform separation similar to past Grand Theft Auto titles.
