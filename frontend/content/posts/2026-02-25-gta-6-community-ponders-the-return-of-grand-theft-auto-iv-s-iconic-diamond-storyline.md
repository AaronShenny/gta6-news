# GTA 6 Community Ponders the Return of Grand Theft Auto IV's Iconic Diamond Storyline

The Grand Theft Auto community is buzzing with speculation regarding the narrative direction of the highly anticipated GTA 6. A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user u/OGAnimeGokuSolos, has sparked a lively debate about the potential return of Grand Theft Auto IV's memorable diamond storyline. The original poster highlighted the diamond narrative as a standout feature that made GTA IV's story particularly special, suggesting its reintroduction in GTA 6, perhaps as an engaging side story. This prompt has opened the floor for fans to share their thoughts on bringing back this fan-favorite plot element.
