# GTA Online DLC Cycle Fuels GTA 6 Trailer Release Speculation

A recent discussion thread on Reddit's r/GTA6 community has sparked debate regarding Rockstar Games' potential strategy for releasing new content and trailers. User u/sasukeamongus observes that GTA+ benefit cycles typically reset around the 16th of each month, often preceding new GTA Online DLCs. The user highlights a past instance where Rockstar dropped a DLC trailer a week before its release, a move believed to have effectively boosted player engagement. This pattern has led to speculation that Rockstar might adopt a similar 'plus or minus one week' approach for future DLCs, and potentially even for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI trailer. However, the post also acknowledges the counter-argument that Rockstar might choose to isolate GTA 6 announcements from DLC news, especially given the game's nearing release.

- GTA+ benefits usually reset around the 16th, often signaling new GTA Online DLC releases.
- Rockstar Games previously released a GTA Online DLC trailer approximately one week prior to the content's launch.
