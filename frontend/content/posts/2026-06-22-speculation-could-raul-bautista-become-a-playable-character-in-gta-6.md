# Speculation: Could Raul Bautista Become a Playable Character in GTA 6?

A recent discussion on Reddit's GTA 6 community suggests that the character Raul Bautista could potentially emerge as a playable protagonist, particularly in a post-story scenario. The speculation posits that Raul's physical build, being reportedly similar to Jason's, would allow Rockstar Games to efficiently reuse existing animations, assets, and interactions. Citing precedents like the protagonist switch in Red Dead Redemption 2, the theory proposes Raul as a practical choice for free-roam if GTA VI features a darker ending where a primary protagonist like Jason, or even both Jason and Lucia, might not survive. The original poster acknowledges this idea is 'probably a reach' and not a confirmed possibility.

## Key Takeaways
- Speculation suggests Raul Bautista as a potential post-story playable character in GTA 6.
- His similar physical build to Jason could allow Rockstar to efficiently reuse animations, clothing, and vehicle interactions.
- Rockstar Games has a history of protagonist switches, as seen with Arthur and John in Red Dead Redemption 2.
- Raul could serve as a practical free-roam character if a darker ending leads to the death of primary protagonists like Jason or Lucia.
- The theory is presented as speculative, with the original author admitting it's 'probably a reach'.
