# GTA 6 Collector's Edition Scalping Fears Emerge Ahead of Pre-Orders

Anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6 is high, but so is the anxiety among fans regarding the availability of special physical and Collector's Editions. A Reddit post by u/PapaNaj highlights widespread concern that scalpers and automated bots will instantly deplete pre-order stock, forcing legitimate collectors to pay exorbitant prices on secondary markets. The community is calling for Rockstar Games and retailers to implement strict purchasing limitations, such as one per customer or account verification, to ensure fair access for dedicated fans.

## Key Takeaways
- Fans are highly concerned about scalpers and bots instantly buying up GTA 6 Collector's Editions.
- There's a strong desire among collectors to secure legitimate physical copies without inflated aftermarket prices.
- The community is advocating for Rockstar Games and retailers to implement pre-order restrictions (e.g., limit 1 per customer, account verification).
- Discussion includes whether players will opt for digital or attempt to secure a physical/collector's edition.

## FAQ
**What is the main concern regarding GTA 6 Collector's Editions?**
Fans are worried that scalpers and bots will instantly buy up all limited-edition physical copies, making them unavailable at retail price for legitimate collectors.

**What solutions are fans suggesting to combat scalping?**
Suggestions include implementing strict pre-order limitations like one per customer, account verification, and other restrictions to deter scalpers.
