# GTA 6 Fans Debate RDR2-Style 'Honor Bar' System for Story Outcomes

A recent discussion originating from the GTA 6 subreddit has ignited debate among fans regarding the potential inclusion of an 'Honor bar' system in the upcoming title, drawing parallels to Red Dead Redemption 2's acclaimed morality mechanics. The concept, proposed by Reddit user u/Front-Stable2612, suggests that player choices and actions throughout GTA 6 could directly influence the ultimate fate of its dual protagonists, Jason and Lucia. Under this system, a 'good' honor rating might lead to a happier conclusion for the duo, while a 'bad' honor rating could result in a darker ending, potentially involving betrayal or even death for the characters. This speculative feature has sparked considerable interest, as it would represent a significant evolution in player agency and narrative branching for the Grand Theft Auto series.
