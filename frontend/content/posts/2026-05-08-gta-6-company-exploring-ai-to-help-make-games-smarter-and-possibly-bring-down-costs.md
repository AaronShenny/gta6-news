# GTA 6 Company "Exploring" AI To Help Make Games "Smarter" And Possibly Bring Down Costs

Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick has expressed concerns over the escalating production costs in the gaming industry, stating that such massive investments are only sustainable for "massive blockbusters" like GTA 6. He warns that exponential or even linear growth in development expenses is untenable and could prevent certain titles from being made. To combat this, Take-Two is "exploring" the use of AI and other technologies to create efficiencies and potentially reduce costs, emphasizing that the goal is to make games "smarter" and not "lighter, shorter, worse." While AI-driven cost savings haven't been observed yet, the company remains hopeful. The article also touches upon the possibility of GTA 6 costing $80, as suggested by an analyst, and confirms its November 19 launch on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S, with PC not being a launch platform.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 is anticipated to be one of the most expensive games ever made.
- Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick states that massive investments are only justifiable for "massive blockbusters."
- Zelnick warns that current production cost growth (exponential or linear) is unsustainable for the industry.
- Take-Two is "exploring" AI and technology to drive efficiencies and potentially reduce development costs.
- The company's aim is to make games "smarter" and more efficient, not to compromise on quality or content.
- AI-driven cost savings have not yet been observed by Take-Two, and it's uncertain if they ever will be.
- A Bank of America analyst suggested Rockstar should charge $80 for GTA 6 to help normalize higher game prices.
- GTA 6 launches on November 19 for PS5 and Xbox Series X|S, with no PC launch at this time.

## FAQ
**Why is Take-Two exploring AI for game development?**
Take-Two is exploring AI and other technologies to create efficiencies in game development and potentially bring down rising production costs, without compromising on game quality or content.

**Will GTA 6 cost more than $70?**
While Take-Two has not confirmed pricing, a Bank of America analyst suggested Rockstar should charge $80 for GTA 6 to help establish higher pricing standards across the industry. The final price remains to be seen.

**When and on what platforms will GTA 6 launch?**
Grand Theft Auto 6 is scheduled to launch on November 19 for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. It will not be available on PC at launch.
