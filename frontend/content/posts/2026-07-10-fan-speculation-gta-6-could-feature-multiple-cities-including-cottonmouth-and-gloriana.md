# Fan Speculation: GTA 6 Could Feature Multiple Cities, Including Cottonmouth and Gloriana

A dedicated fan on Reddit has shared a 'what if' scenario for Grand Theft Auto 6, proposing a multi-city map reminiscent of GTA: San Andreas. Disappointed by GTA V's single major city, the author envisions GTA 6 featuring more than just Vice City.

The concept introduces **Cottonmouth**, a city originating from Rockstar's Manhunt 2, drawing inspiration from real-world Georgian cities like Atlanta, Charleston, and New Orleans. The author acknowledges that Rockstar might have forgotten this lesser-known city, known only by name from manhole covers.

Further speculation centers on **Gloriana**, a potential new city that the author believes Rockstar is subtly teasing through 'purposefully placed' license plates in screenshots. This city is hoped to provide a significant northern counterbalance to Vice City, preventing a map imbalance similar to what the author felt occurred in GTA V. The fan-made map, based on a community design, visually incorporates these additions, expressing a strong desire for a diverse and expansive urban landscape in the upcoming title.
