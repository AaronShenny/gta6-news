# Revisiting a Decade-Old Rumor: FNAC Portugal's Alleged GTA VI Prices from 2013 Resurface

An old Reddit post from 2013, shared by /u/TriggoNz, has resurfaced, purporting to show leaked prices for Grand Theft Auto VI from FNAC Portugal. This alleged leak, predating GTA VI's official confirmation by nearly a decade, suggested a base version priced at 90 euros, a second version at 120 euros, and a premium edition at 200 euros. While intriguing for historical context, it is crucial to note that this information originated long before Rockstar Games officially acknowledged GTA VI's development. As such, these prices are highly speculative, unconfirmed, and almost certainly not reflective of the final product given the significant time elapsed and the game's evolving development cycle.
