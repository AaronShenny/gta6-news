# GTA 6 Trailer Timeline Prediction: Could Rockstar Follow RDR2's Marketing Playbook?

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan on Reddit, u/Acoldite, has put forth a compelling prediction for the upcoming GTA 6 trailer and release timeline, meticulously drawing parallels to Rockstar Games' marketing strategy for Red Dead Redemption 2. The analysis suggests that while the initial GTA 6 Trailer 1 landed in December 2023, the subsequent marketing beats will likely mirror RDR2's staggered approach, albeit with significant gaps attributed to game production delays. According to the user's forecast, following the December 2023 reveal, GTA 6 Trailer 2 is anticipated around March 2025, setting the stage for characters, lifestyle, and location details. A crucial 'Trailer 3' is then predicted for July-early August 2025. The rationale behind a third cinematic trailer is threefold: it aligns with Rockstar's historical patterns, serves as an optimal sweet spot for generating pre-order waves without divulging excessive information, and prevents an abrupt leap into gameplay details after a prolonged information drought, maintaining cinematic hype. The timeline continues with Gameplay Video Part 1 in September 2025, followed by Gameplay Video Part 2 in late October to the first week of November. The grand finale, a Launch Trailer, is projected for the second week of November, culminating in an estimated game launch on November 19, 2025. This speculative timeline offers an intriguing look at how Rockstar might pace its marketing for one of the most anticipated games in history.
