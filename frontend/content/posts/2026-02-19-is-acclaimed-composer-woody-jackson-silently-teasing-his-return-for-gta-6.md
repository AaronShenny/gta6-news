# Is Acclaimed Composer Woody Jackson Silently Teasing His Return for GTA 6?

A recent discovery by a vigilant fan has ignited speculation that acclaimed composer Woody Jackson, celebrated for his iconic scores in *Grand Theft Auto V*, *Red Dead Redemption 2*, and *L.A. Noire*, may be subtly hinting at his involvement in the highly anticipated *Grand Theft Auto 6*.
