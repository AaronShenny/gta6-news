# GTA 6 Fans Speculate on Potential 'Dexter' Easter Eggs in Vice City

The Grand Theft Auto 6 community is actively discussing the potential for 'Dexter' themed easter eggs within the upcoming game. Initiated by a Reddit user, the speculation centers on Vice City's undeniable inspiration from Miami, the real-world setting of the popular show 'Dexter,' which features a serial killer working as a blood spatter analyst. Fans are suggesting various ways Rockstar Games could subtly or overtly nod to the series, ranging from environmental details like black trash bags at the bottom of the ocean or an apartment resembling Dexter's, to more elaborate inclusions such as a 'Strangers & Freaks' mission involving tracking down a similar character.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's Vice City setting is heavily inspired by real-world Miami.
- The popular TV show 'Dexter,' featuring a serial killer, is also set in Miami.
- Fans are speculating that Rockstar Games could include 'Dexter' themed easter eggs or references.
- Proposed easter egg ideas include black trash bags in the ocean, a replica of Dexter's apartment, or a dedicated mission.
- The discussion highlights the community's excitement and desire for pop culture references in GTA 6.
