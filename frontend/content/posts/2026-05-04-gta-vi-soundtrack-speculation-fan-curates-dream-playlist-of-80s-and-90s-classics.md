# GTA VI Soundtrack Speculation: Fan Curates Dream Playlist of 80s and 90s Classics

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto VI fan has compiled an extensive wishlist of songs they hope will feature on the game's highly anticipated soundtrack. The speculative playlist leans heavily into iconic 80s pop, rock, and R&B anthems, suggesting tracks like Tears For Fears' 'Everybody Wants to Rule the World,' Whitney Houston's 'I Wanna Dance with Somebody,' and Van Halen's 'Jump.' The author believes these selections perfectly encapsulate the vibrant, retro 'vibe' expected from the game's Vice City-inspired setting of Leonida, envisioning them as ideal companions for high-speed cruising. The list also notes a couple of potential repeats from GTA V, such as 'What a Fool Believes' and 'Higher Love,' and encourages fellow fans to contribute their own soundtrack predictions.
