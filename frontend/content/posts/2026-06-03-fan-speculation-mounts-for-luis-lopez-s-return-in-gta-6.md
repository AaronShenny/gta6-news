# Fan Speculation Mounts for Luis Lopez's Return in GTA 6

Dedicated fans are increasingly convinced that Luis Lopez, a beloved character from Grand Theft Auto: The Ballad of Gay Tony, could be making a return in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. Further fueling these rumors, GTA 6 protagonist Lucia's actor, Manni L Perez, was briefly observed following Luis Lopez's actor, Mario D Leon, on Instagram before swiftly unfollowing, suggesting a potential connection and setting the stage for Luis's possible reappearance in Vice City.
