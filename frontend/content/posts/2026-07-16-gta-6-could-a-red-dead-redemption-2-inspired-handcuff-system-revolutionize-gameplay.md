# GTA 6: Could a Red Dead Redemption 2-Inspired Handcuff System Revolutionize Gameplay?

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked considerable interest, with user /u/No-Speed7715 proposing an intriguing gameplay mechanic for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. Drawing inspiration from Red Dead Redemption 2's beloved hogtie system, the user envisions a similar handcuff or cable tie mechanic in GTA 6. This system, they suggest, could significantly expand player interactions beyond traditional combat. The speculation includes the ability to antagonize non-player characters (NPCs), kidnap them, and extort valuables. Furthermore, the user highlights the potential for a robust in-game economy, where players could sell these stolen goods at a 'fence or pawn shop type system,' adding a new layer of depth to criminal activities and resource management within the game world.
