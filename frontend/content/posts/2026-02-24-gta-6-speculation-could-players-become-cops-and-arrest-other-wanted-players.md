# GTA 6 Speculation: Could Players Become Cops and Arrest Other Wanted Players?

A recent Reddit post has sparked discussion among the GTA 6 community, speculating on a revolutionary new gameplay mechanic: the ability for players to serve as police officers and detain other wanted players. This intriguing idea stems from Rockstar Games' acquisition of Cfx.re, the development team behind the popular FiveM role-playing platform, in August 2023. With long-standing rumors suggesting Rockstar aims to integrate more extensive RP elements directly into the main game, the user envisions a scenario where players could moonlight as law enforcement in Vice City, actively pursuing and apprehending not just scripted NPCs, but also other players with bounties or criminal records, adding a dynamic new layer to multiplayer interaction.

## Key Takeaways
- Rockstar Games acquired Cfx.re, the developers of FiveM, in August 2023.
- The acquisition fuels speculation about Rockstar integrating more dedicated role-playing (RP) elements into GTA 6.
- A Reddit user proposed the idea of players being able to work as police officers within GTA 6.
- This player-controlled police role would include the functionality to detain both NPCs and other wanted players.
- The concept suggests a significant shift in multiplayer interaction and law enforcement dynamics within the game's open world, potentially set in Vice City.
