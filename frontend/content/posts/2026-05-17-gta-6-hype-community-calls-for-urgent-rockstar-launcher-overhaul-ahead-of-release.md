# GTA 6 Hype: Community Calls for Urgent Rockstar Launcher Overhaul Ahead of Release

As anticipation for Grand Theft Auto VI continues to build, PC players are increasingly vocal about the need for significant improvements to the Rockstar Games Launcher. A recent Reddit discussion highlights major user experience issues, primarily focusing on the launcher's requirement for users to log in 'EVERY time' it's opened, with the 'Auto Sign In' feature reportedly failing to function. While some users express a desire for a complete user interface refresh, there's a prevailing skepticism that Rockstar will deliver more than just adding GTA VI to the 'Now Available' section, rather than addressing the core usability problems plaguing the platform.

## Key Takeaways
- Users are frustrated by the Rockstar Games Launcher's persistent demand for re-logging on every launch.
- The 'Auto Sign In' feature is reported as non-functional, failing to save user credentials.
- There's a desire for a visual and functional update to the launcher's user interface.
- Skepticism exists regarding the likelihood of a comprehensive overhaul, with many expecting only minimal changes for GTA VI's release.
