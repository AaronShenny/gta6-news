# GTA 6 Trailer 2 Reaches 150 Million Views, Combined Trailers Exceed 420 Million

Rockstar Games continues to demonstrate its immense pulling power as the second trailer for Grand Theft Auto 6 has officially reached 150 million views within nine months of its release. This significant milestone, combined with the first trailer's impressive 273 million views, brings the total viewership for both GTA 6 trailers to an astounding 423 million. This performance highlights the extraordinary anticipation and excitement surrounding the upcoming installment in the iconic series.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 Trailer 2 achieved 150 million views within nine months.
- GTA 6 Trailer 1 currently stands at 273 million views.
- The combined viewership for both GTA 6 trailers totals 423 million views.
- The high view counts underscore the massive anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6.
