# GTA Online Debuts 'The Kortz Center Heist,' First Major Heist Since 2020

Rockstar Games is set to launch a significant new update for *Grand Theft Auto Online* on July 14, introducing 'The Kortz Center Heist.' This marks the game's first major brand-new heist since the Cayo Perico Heist in 2020. Players will team up with Mr. Faber and Raf De Angelis to orchestrate the theft of valuable art. A new in-game feature allows players to acquire an Art Studio for their home, which will then be occupied by a 'gifted counterfeiter' who creates forgeries to replace the stolen originals. The Kortz Center Heist can be tackled solo or with a team of up to three other players, offering a multi-stage experience. This involves initial reconnaissance to identify entry points, gearing up, and finally executing the art theft, with an emphasis on leaving no witnesses and destroying CCTV footage. Designed for replayability, the heist will feature three new paintings to steal each week, coupled with 'tons of variability' in mission approaches. Beyond the heist itself, the update also brings new vehicles to *GTA Online* and additional upgrade options from Hao's Special Works. *GTA+* subscribers will receive the new Grotti Veleno GT car for free starting July 14. While the focus is on *GTA Online*, the article also briefly reiterates the confirmed release date for *Grand Theft Auto 6*, set for November 19 on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.
