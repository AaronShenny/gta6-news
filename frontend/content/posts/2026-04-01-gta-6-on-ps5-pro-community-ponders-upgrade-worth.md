## FAQ
**Will GTA 6 run better on PS5 Pro than on the base PS5?**
While not officially confirmed by Rockstar Games, the PS5 Pro is expected to offer enhanced performance, potentially including higher resolutions, improved frame rates, or advanced graphical features for games like GTA 6, compared to the standard PS5.

**Is a PS5 Pro upgrade essential for enjoying GTA 6?**
The base PS5 is expected to run GTA 6 perfectly well, as it is a primary target platform. A PS5 Pro upgrade would likely offer a premium experience rather than being a necessity for enjoyment.

**What are the expected benefits of playing GTA 6 on PS5 Pro?**
Potential benefits could include more stable frame rates, support for higher resolutions (e.g., 4K with ray tracing), faster loading times, or enhanced visual fidelity, depending on how Rockstar optimizes the game for the Pro model and the specific capabilities of the console.
