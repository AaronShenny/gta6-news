# GTA 6: Strauss Zelnick Interviews Reveal Marketing Timeline, Billion-Dollar Budget, and Development Insights

Based on a series of recent interviews with Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick, significant details regarding Grand Theft Auto 6 have emerged. Zelnick confirmed that marketing for the highly anticipated title is slated to commence this summer, with pricing information to follow shortly. He also revealed that he has personally viewed gameplay, expressing a mix of excitement and apprehension about the game's eventual release. The development process for GTA 6 has reportedly spanned over eight years, involving thousands of developers—a considerably larger team than those behind GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2. The project's budget has already surpassed $1 billion, underscoring Rockstar's 'unlimited financial/resources.' Regarding internal conditions, Zelnick suggested that 'crunch' culture is not as pervasive as rumored, possibly being an issue localized to specific regions like India. Historically, Rockstar has released major announcements 9-14 days before earnings calls, leading to speculation that an official reveal or significant update is imminent, particularly before the upcoming Q4 call. The article's author anticipates news within three weeks, with a hopeful projection of playing the game in approximately six months, allowing for additional polish after two and a half years since the first trailer.
