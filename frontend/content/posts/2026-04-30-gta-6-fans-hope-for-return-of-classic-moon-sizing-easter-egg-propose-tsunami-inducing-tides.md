# GTA 6 Fans Hope for Return of Classic Moon-Sizing Easter Egg, Propose Tsunami-Inducing Tides

A recent Reddit post by user /u/Some_Breadfruit235 on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked discussion among fans, calling for the return of a beloved easter egg from classic Grand Theft Auto titles. The feature, which allowed players to change the moon's size by shooting it with a sniper rifle in GTA III, Vice City, and San Andreas, is a nostalgic element many hope to see revived in GTA 6. The user further proposes an intriguing new layer to this mechanic: linking the enlarged moon to in-game physics, specifically suggesting that it could alter ocean tides, creating stronger waves and potentially even tsunami-like events, adding a dynamic and impactful environmental element to the game.

## Key Takeaways
- Classic GTA games (III, Vice City, San Andreas) featured an easter egg where shooting the moon changed its size.
- Reddit user /u/Some_Breadfruit235 advocates for the return of this feature in GTA 6.
- A new suggestion proposes that an enlarged moon in GTA 6 could influence ocean tides.
- This influence could lead to stronger waves and potentially tsunami-like events within the game world.

## FAQ
**What is the 'enlarging the moon' feature?**
It's a classic Grand Theft Auto easter egg where players could shoot the moon with a sniper rifle, causing its size to change.

**Which GTA games included this feature?**
This feature was present in GTA III, Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, and Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas.

**What new mechanic is proposed for GTA 6?**
The suggestion is that if the moon-enlarging feature returns, an enlarged moon could dynamically alter ocean tides, potentially causing stronger waves or tsunami-like events.
