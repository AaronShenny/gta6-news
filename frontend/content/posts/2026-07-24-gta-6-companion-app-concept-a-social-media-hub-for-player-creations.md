# GTA 6 Companion App Concept: A Social Media Hub for Player Creations

A Reddit user has proposed an intriguing concept for a **Grand Theft Auto VI companion app**, envisioning it as a significant evolution of GTA V's iFruit. Instead of a simple utility, this app would function as a central hub for **in-universe social media platforms**, dedicated entirely to player-created content.

The core idea is to provide players with a mobile launcher containing GTA VI's fictional social networks. One key feature suggested is a **TikTok-style app**, allowing players to upload short video clips created using the game's Director Mode. These videos could be published to the in-universe platform via Social Club, with popular content potentially appearing on a global trending feed.

Another exciting proposition is an **Instagram-style platform** for sharing screenshots taken with Photo Mode. This would encourage players to showcase their customized cars, outfits, scenic views, or bizarre in-game encounters. The concept highlights the potential for Rockstar to host weekly photography challenges, rewarding players whose posts are featured.

Crucially, the concept differentiates between player-created content on the companion app and **NPC-driven social media within the game itself**. The companion app would focus solely on community content, while the in-game social networks would remain tied to the story, world events, and NPC reactions, enhancing immersion. This modern approach aims to make GTA VI's social media culture feel like a tangible part of the experience, both inside and outside the game.
