# GTA 6 Community Hopes for Immersive Side Activities to Become Major Time Sinks

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user u/gamersecret2, highlights a shared sentiment among fans: the hope for compelling side activities in Grand Theft Auto 6. The original post emphasizes Rockstar's track record of creating engaging optional content, with the user specifically wishing for activities fun enough to momentarily sideline the main narrative. Key examples cited include spending hours on car customization, leisurely cruising through the streets of Vice City, and discovering new areas without the pressure of story progression. This sentiment underscores a desire for a richly detailed open world where exploration and personalized experiences can become significant time sinks.

## Key Takeaways
- Players are hoping for highly engaging side activities in GTA 6.
- Rockstar Games is recognized for its ability to create compelling optional content.
- Specific desired activities include in-depth car customization.
- Leisurely cruising through Vice City and discovering new locations are also hoped-for time sinks.
- The goal is for side content to be fun enough to encourage players to temporarily ignore the main story.
