# GTA 6 Marks a Digital Milestone for Long-Time Fan

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan, who has consistently collected physical copies of every main series title since GTA 3 on the PS2, including GTA IV and V on Xbox 360, shares a personal milestone: GTA 6 will be their first digital-only purchase. This shift is primarily driven by their ownership of an Xbox Series S, a console without a disc drive, and a general move away from physical game purchases that began some time ago. The user opens the floor for discussion, asking fellow gamers if they plan to buy a physical copy of GTA 6 or have also transitioned to digital-only.

## Key Takeaways
- A long-time GTA player is transitioning to digital-only for GTA 6.
- The user previously owned physical copies of GTA 3, Vice City, San Andreas, GTA IV, and GTA V.
- The primary reasons for the switch are owning an Xbox Series S (a digital-only console) and a personal preference shift away from physical media.
- The post invites community discussion on physical vs. digital game ownership for the upcoming title.
