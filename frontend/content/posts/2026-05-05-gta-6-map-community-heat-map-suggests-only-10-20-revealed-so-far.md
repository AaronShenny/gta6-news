# GTA 6 Map: Community 'Heat Map' Suggests Only 10-20% Revealed So Far

A recent 'heat map' compiled from Grand Theft Auto 6 trailers and screenshots is generating buzz within the community, providing a visual representation of the currently known areas of the game world. According to the map's creator, shared by /u/someent420 and attributed to @xlazefps, only an estimated 10-20% of the full map, excluding Vice City itself, has been publicly revealed. The sentiment among fans is a mix of speculation and excitement, with hopes that the northern regions of the map could be significantly larger than current estimates suggest, promising a vast amount of undiscovered content for players.

## Key Takeaways
- A community-made 'heat map' visualizes the known areas of the GTA 6 map based on official trailers and screenshots.
- Estimates suggest that only 10-20% of the total map, beyond Vice City, has been revealed to date.
- There is speculation and hope that the northern parts of the map will be much larger than currently perceived.
- The community expresses enthusiasm for the extensive amount of undiscovered content in the upcoming game.
- The map concept was sourced from Twitter user @xlazefps and shared on Reddit by /u/someent420.
