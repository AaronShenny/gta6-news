# GTA 6 Pre-Orders: Could They Drop as Early as Tomorrow?

The gaming community is abuzz with speculation that pre-orders for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI could open much sooner than expected. This surge in discussion follows reports of GTA 6 IDs appearing on the PlayStation Store. Drawing parallels to previous Rockstar releases, Grand Theft Auto V pre-orders opened approximately 10.5 months before its launch, while Red Dead Redemption 2's pre-orders began around 4.5 months out. Given an estimated 8.5 months until GTA VI's release, some analysts suggest a March pre-order window is plausible, perhaps coinciding with a third trailer. A crucial detail noted is that both GTA V and RDR2 pre-orders were announced on a Monday, leading to fervent speculation about a potential announcement as early as 'tomorrow' or 'next Monday' for the upcoming installment.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 IDs have reportedly been added to the PlayStation Store, fueling pre-order speculation.
- GTA V pre-orders opened approximately 10 months and 12 days (310 days) before its release.
- Red Dead Redemption 2 pre-orders opened 4 months and 22 days (144 days) before its release.
- With an estimated 8.5 months until GTA 6's release, a March pre-order window is considered realistic.
- Both GTA V and RDR2 pre-orders were announced on a Monday, suggesting a potential upcoming Monday announcement for GTA 6 pre-orders.
