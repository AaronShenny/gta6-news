# GTA VI: Community Hopes for Enhanced Boat Loading Mechanics

A Reddit user, /u/tnasstyy, has voiced a common frustration among Grand Theft Auto V players regarding the game's boat mechanics, specifically the inability to properly load a boat back onto its trailer. While launching boats was a straightforward process, retrieving them was not a true game mechanic, often requiring cumbersome and unofficial methods. The user expresses a strong hope that Grand Theft Auto VI will address this by introducing a fully functional and immersive feature allowing players to seamlessly load boats onto trailers and transport them, enhancing realism and player agency within the game world.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA V lacked a true boat loading mechanic onto trailers.
- Players could only reliably launch boats from trailers in GTA V.
- Attempting to load boats back onto trailers was difficult and not an intended feature.
- The community member hopes GTA VI will implement a full boat loading and transport system.
- Such a feature would enhance immersion and gameplay realism.
