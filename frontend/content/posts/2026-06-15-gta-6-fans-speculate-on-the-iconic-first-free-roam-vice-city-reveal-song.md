# GTA 6: Fans Speculate on the Iconic 'First Free Roam' Vice City Reveal Song

A recent discussion within the Grand Theft Auto community highlights a pivotal moment in every Rockstar game: the initial 'free roam' experience where the game world truly opens up. For the upcoming GTA 6, fans are particularly focused on the musical accompaniment for what's envisioned as a long drive revealing the Vice City skyline for the first time. The original post ponders whether Rockstar will choose a licensed, high-energy radio track, a bespoke cinematic score, or a more atmospheric piece to underscore this crucial transition, inviting players to share their personal song preferences for this iconic scene.

## Key Takeaways
- The 'first free roam' moment is a significant, highly anticipated transition in every Rockstar game.
- For GTA 6, this moment is imagined as a drive where the Vice City skyline gradually comes into view.
- Speculation centers on the type of music Rockstar will use: a high-energy licensed song, a cinematic scripted track, or a slower, atmospheric piece.
- Community members are encouraged to share their ideal song choices for this Vice City reveal.
- The original poster humorously suggested Bruno Mars' 'Runaway Baby' (from a SpongeBob teaser trailer) as a personal vibe.
