# Fan Recreates GTA 6 Trailer Scenes in Real-Life Miami for 'Vice City' Short

Inspired by the cinematic style of GTA 6 and the vibrant backdrop of Miami, a fan filmmaker has produced a 5-minute short film titled 'Vice City.' The project meticulously recreates specific shots from the official GTA 6 trailers, capturing the game's aesthetic in real-life. The creator focused on matching elements like low sun angles, warm color palettes, and shallow depth of field for character-focused shots, even casting actors to resemble Lucia and Jason. Drone footage was used to replicate iconic Miami Beach scenes, with the filmmaker noting new architectural developments. The entire production was achieved on a modest $400 budget with a crew of four, utilizing a Sony FX6 camera and filming at sunrise to capture the perfect golden hour.

## Key Takeaways
- Fan-made 5-minute short film 'Vice City' inspired by GTA 6 trailers and Miami.
- Recreated specific trailer shots, focusing on cinematic style and atmosphere.
- Character scenes (resembling Lucia and Jason) used low sun, warm colors, and shallow depth of field.
- Drone shots replicated Miami Beach scenes, including identifying exact buildings.
- Technical details: Shot on Sony FX6 with 35mm f/1.8 lens, filmed at sunrise in Miami.
- Project completed with a crew of 4 and a budget of $400.
