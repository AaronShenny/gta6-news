# GTA 6 Fan Dials Numbers from Trailer 2, Finds Florida Connection

A curious Grand Theft Auto 6 fan, u/Speedygameplayz on Reddit, took the initiative to call all discernible phone numbers spotted in the highly anticipated second GTA 6 trailer. The numbers identified were 405-555-0178, 305-555-0145, and 786-555-0109. Upon dialing each number, the fan was met with a generic 'Sorry, we're unable to complete your call' message. However, a significant discovery was made: all three numbers correspond to actual Florida area codes, aligning with the game's Vice City (Miami-inspired) setting. While currently inactive, this finding has sparked speculation among the community that these numbers could become active for in-game Easter eggs or interactive content closer to the game's launch, though for now, they yield no special response.
