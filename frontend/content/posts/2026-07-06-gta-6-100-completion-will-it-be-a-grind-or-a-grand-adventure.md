# GTA 6 100% Completion: Will it Be a Grind or a Grand Adventure?

A recent Reddit post by user u/One-Look-8408 sparked a discussion on the potential nature of 100% completion in the upcoming *Grand Theft Auto VI*. The user reflected on their recent experience achieving 100% in *Grand Theft Auto V*, noting that despite the time investment, it was largely enjoyable, with only minor complaints about collecting spaceship parts and letter scraps. This positive experience was contrasted sharply with the 'absolutely tedious' 100% checklist found in *Red Dead Redemption 2*, and implicitly, *Grand Theft Auto IV*.

The core question posed to the community is whether *GTA VI*'s completion requirements will lean towards the more engaging, albeit lengthy, style of *GTA V*, or if it will adopt the more demanding and potentially 'dreaded' approach seen in some of Rockstar's other titles. The discussion highlights a common player concern regarding the balance between challenging content and enjoyable gameplay when striving for full game completion.
