# Beyond iFruit: GTA 6 Players Envision a Watch Dogs-Style Smartphone Experience

In anticipation of Grand Theft Auto VI, a passionate fan has outlined an ambitious vision for the game's in-hand device, advocating for a modern smartphone experience akin to what's seen in the 'Watch Dogs' series. Moving past GTA V's more traditional phone, the proposal suggests a fully functional, customizable phone with an in-game app store, designed to deepen immersion and offer unique gameplay mechanics. The concept includes a detailed list of parody apps, each mirroring real-world services, aiming to integrate contemporary digital life into the Vice City criminal underworld.

## Key Takeaways
- Desire for a modern, Watch Dogs-style smartphone in GTA 6, offering more depth than GTA V's phone.
- Proposed features include customizable wallpapers and a functional in-game app store.
- Specific app ideas include 'Zit' (a fake Spotify for in-game music and custom playlists, potentially with premium options).
- 'What-Up!' (a WhatsApp parody for character communication, including calling for backup).
- 'Bleeter' (a social media app for viewing NPC and player content, fostering a 'modern vibe').
- 'LonelyFans' (a unique, potentially adult-themed app for in-game entertainment).
- 'Ryde Me' (an Uber/Lyft parody for calling cabs, possibly leading to dynamic missions).
- 'Maze Bank' (a banking app for managing finances, sending money, and checking debt).
- 'Grub Guzzler' (a DoorDash/Uber Eats parody for ordering food from various locations).
- 'Hooker Galore' (a fishing app with a suggestive double meaning).
- 'LomBike' (an app for renting bikes and other vehicles across Vice City).
- 'Love Meet' (a dating app for protagonists to interact with bots and criminals).
- 'SecuroServ' (an app for managing criminal organizations and businesses to earn extra cash).
