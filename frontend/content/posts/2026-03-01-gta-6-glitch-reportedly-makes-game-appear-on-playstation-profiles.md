# GTA 6 Glitch Reportedly Makes Game Appear on PlayStation Profiles

Reports from GTAforums indicate a new glitch has surfaced, enabling 'Grand Theft Auto 6' to appear on users' PlayStation profiles as a recently played game. This phenomenon is similar to a previously observed glitch on Xbox. The method for activating this on PlayStation is described as 'complicated' and involves GitHub, suggesting it's not a straightforward process for the average user.
