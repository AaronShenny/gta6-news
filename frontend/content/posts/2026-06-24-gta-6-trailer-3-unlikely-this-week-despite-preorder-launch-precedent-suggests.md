# GTA 6 Trailer 3 Unlikely This Week Despite Preorder Launch, Precedent Suggests

Despite widespread fan anticipation for a third Grand Theft Auto 6 trailer to coincide with preorders launching on June 25, expert analysis of Rockstar's historical marketing strategies suggests this may not be the case. Past titles like GTA 5 and GTA 4 saw significant gaps between preorder announcements and subsequent trailer releases. While Rockstar's approach isn't always consistent, a third GTA 6 trailer is more likely to appear in early July rather than immediately alongside preorders this week.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 preorders are set to go live on June 25.
- Many fans anticipate a third trailer release alongside the preorder launch.
- Historically, Rockstar's GTA 5 and GTA 4 marketing saw trailers released several days after preorder announcements (9 days for GTA 5's character trailers, 8 days for GTA 4's third trailer).
- GTA 6's release is approximately five months post-preorder launch, a shorter window than GTA 5's ten months.
- Significant gaps have already occurred between GTA 6's first and second trailers (519 days), and it has been 414 days since the second trailer.
- Rockstar's marketing isn't entirely predictable, as Red Dead Redemption 2's third trailer dropped a month before preorders.
- Based on precedent, the third GTA 6 trailer might be expected in the first week of July.

No, Red Dead Redemption 2's third trailer was released a month before preorders, indicating Rockstar doesn't stick to one specific marketing approach.
