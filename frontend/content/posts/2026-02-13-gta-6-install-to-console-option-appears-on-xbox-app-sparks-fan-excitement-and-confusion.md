# GTA 6 'Install to Console' Option Appears on Xbox App, Sparks Fan Excitement and Confusion

A Reddit user, u/GroundbreakingRing42, reported a peculiar incident where they encountered an 'install to console' option for Grand Theft Auto 6 on their Xbox app. Despite the game being months away from release, they proceeded with the option, which seemingly turned on their Xbox and displayed a GTA 6 tile on the home screen. This momentarily led to a surge of excitement. However, upon checking, the 'installation' was revealed to be a mere placeholder, showing 0.0MB and no actual game files. The user expressed surprise and frustration, questioning why the console system allows such steps to be followed for a game that is not yet released.
