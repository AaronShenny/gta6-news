# Why are people so pessimistic about the 700 interiors?

A recent Reddit post challenges the skepticism surrounding the rumored 700 enterable interiors in Grand Theft Auto VI, arguing that such a number is entirely realistic. The author points to leaked screenshots and observations, suggesting a strong focus on accessible hotel pools and bars, which would necessitate enterable lobbies. Examples include the detailed Vice City concert hall with visible interiors and the intricate design of buildings along Vice Beach. The post posits that with numerous hotel and condo lobbies, along with various stores and museums, reaching 700+ unique interiors is a plausible target for Rockstar Games.
