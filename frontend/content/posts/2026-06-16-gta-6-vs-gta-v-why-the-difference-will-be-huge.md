# GTA 6 vs. GTA V: Why the Difference Will Be 'Huge'

Drawing comparisons between the development cycles of Grand Theft Auto V and the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI, a recent Reddit discussion highlights the potential for an unprecedented shock. The original post notes that GTA V, developed by approximately '1,000 developers' with a budget of '$265 million' over five years, still featured an unfinished Los Santos in its 2011 trailer. In stark contrast, the 2023 trailer for GTA VI showcased a 'massive and fully realized' Vice City. Projecting GTA VI's scale based on rumored figures of '5,000 developers' and a staggering '$2 billion' budget over '6 years of development or more', the journalist anticipates a colossal difference in scope and detail, promising a 'real shock' for players.
