# GTA 6 Trailer 2: Is the Prison Pickup Scene a Story Reveal or Dynamic Gameplay?

A compelling new theory has emerged from the Grand Theft Auto 6 community, suggesting that the much-discussed prison scene from Trailer 2, where Jason collects Lucia, might be a dynamic gameplay cutscene rather than a pivotal story moment. Drawing parallels to Red Dead Redemption 2's system where characters could be bailed out or released following an arrest, the theory posits that this scene could play out whenever Lucia is apprehended during gameplay. The guard's line, "Have I seen you here before?", further supports the idea of it being a recurring event. The author believes Rockstar Games might be intentionally misleading fans, implying an established relationship between Jason and Lucia from the outset, only to surprise players with the actual narrative progression upon release. This interpretation also addresses Lucia's ankle monitor, suggesting the gameplay event could occur at an earlier stage in the game's timeline, post-initial release but still under supervision.
