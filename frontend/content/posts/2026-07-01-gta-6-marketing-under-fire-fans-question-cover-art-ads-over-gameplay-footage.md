# GTA 6 Marketing Under Fire: Fans Question Cover Art Ads Over Gameplay Footage

A concerned Reddit user, u/colbysnumberonefan, has voiced strong criticism regarding Rockstar Games' marketing strategy for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. The user claims that despite being allegedly "4 months away from release," Rockstar is primarily running advertisements showcasing only the game's cover art reveal, with "absolutely zero footage of the actual game." The post argues that this approach is ineffective, suggesting that a brief 30-second ad featuring new gameplay would generate significantly more excitement and maximize sales compared to what is described as a "boring cover art reveal." The author implies that while GTA 6's inherent hype is substantial, a suboptimal marketing campaign could still hinder its full sales potential, even going as far as to suggest the marketing team responsible should be 'sacked'.
