# GTA 6's Staggering Budget Could Eclipse Years of GOTY Nominees and Winners

Recent discussions highlight the potentially colossal budget of Grand Theft Auto VI, with estimates ranging from $1 billion to $1.5 billion. This staggering figure could mean GTA 6's development cost alone might exceed the cumulative budgets of all Game of the Year nominees over the last three years, or even all GOTY winners from the past eleven years. While some projections reach as high as $2 billion, further expanding these comparisons, the original post prudently notes that budget size doesn't necessarily guarantee quality, yet it undeniably underscores the immense scale and ambition Rockstar Games is pouring into the highly anticipated title.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's estimated budget is between $1 billion and $1.5 billion.
- This budget could be equal to or greater than the combined budgets of all Game of the Year nominees from the last three years.
- Alternatively, it could match or exceed the combined budgets of all Game of the Year winners from the last eleven years.
- A higher estimate of $2 billion would allow for even more extensive comparisons.
- The article emphasizes that budget size does not directly correlate with game quality.
