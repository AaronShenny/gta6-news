## FAQ
**Should PC gamers buy a console just for GTA 6?**
This is a personal decision based on your desire to play at launch versus waiting for a potential PC release. If immediate access is crucial, a console purchase might be necessary.

**Is the Xbox Series S good enough to play GTA 6?**
While the Xbox Series S is less powerful than the Series X or PS5, it is designed to run all current-gen games. It is expected to run GTA 6, though potentially at lower resolutions or frame rates compared to its more powerful counterparts.

**Xbox Series S vs. PS5 for GTA 6, which is better?**
The PlayStation 5 generally offers superior raw performance (higher resolution, potentially better graphical fidelity). The Xbox Series S is a more budget-friendly option. Your choice depends on your budget and priorities for visual quality and performance.

**When is GTA 6 expected to release on PC?**
Rockstar Games typically releases their Grand Theft Auto titles on consoles first, followed by a PC version months, or even a year or more, later. An official PC release date for GTA 6 has not yet been announced.
