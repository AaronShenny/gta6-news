# GTA 6: The Console Seller? Retailers Report Increased Demand for PS5 & Xbox

For years, the gaming industry has observed a trend where individual game titles struggled to become standalone hardware sellers. This phenomenon has led to concerns among some retailers about potential stock shortages later in the year, as a considerable number of players who skipped the current console generation appear to be reconsidering their stance specifically for GTA 6. The article highlights the rarity of a single game having such a profound impact on hardware sales, prompting a discussion about what other titles, if any, have previously compelled players to purchase a new console or upgrade their PC.
