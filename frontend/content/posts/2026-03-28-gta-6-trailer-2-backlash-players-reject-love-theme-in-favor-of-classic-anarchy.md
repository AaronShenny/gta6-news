# GTA 6 Trailer 2 Backlash: Players Reject 'Love Theme' in Favor of Classic Anarchy

A viral Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has surfaced, pointing out a notable player sentiment regarding Grand Theft Auto 6's second trailer. The post claims that over 93,000 dislikes on the trailer are attributed to its perceived main theme of 'love,' suggesting a disconnect with a segment of the fanbase. According to the original poster, many players are primarily anticipating GTA 6 for its iconic chaotic sandbox gameplay, including actions like vehicular mayhem, rather than a narrative focus on romantic relationships.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 Trailer 2 reportedly accumulated 93,000 dislikes.
- The dislikes are linked to the trailer's perceived 'love theme' by some viewers.
- A portion of the fanbase expresses a strong desire for the series' characteristic chaotic and violent gameplay.
- The sentiment highlights a potential divergence between player expectations and the perceived narrative direction shown in the trailer.
