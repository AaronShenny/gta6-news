# GTA 6 Pre-Orders: Fans Question Absence on Consoles Compared to Fable

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited discussion among the fanbase regarding the absence of pre-order options for Grand Theft Auto VI on consoles. User /u/Snoo_10177 expressed confusion and humor at the situation, noting that Fable (2027), a game they perceive as 247 days away, is already available for pre-order. In contrast, GTA 6, which the user believes is slated for release in 156 days, still lacks pre-order links for Xbox and PlayStation, prompting questions about Rockstar Games' strategy.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user inquired about the lack of GTA 6 pre-order availability for consoles (Xbox/PlayStation).
- The user highlighted that Fable (2027), a game they state is 247 days away, is already open for pre-orders.
- They contrasted this with GTA 6, which they believe is 'supposed to be released in 156 days,' yet has no pre-order options.
