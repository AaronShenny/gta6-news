# Fan Speculates on Hyper-Realistic Injury Reactions in GTA 6

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by user /u/PineappleFluffy7766 has sparked discussion among fans, proposing a significant leap in realism for Grand Theft Auto 6's injury mechanics. Moving beyond the 'bullet-tanking' or simple fleeing seen in previous Rockstar titles like GTA V and Red Dead Redemption, the user envisions a system where NPCs react dynamically to being shot. For instance, a shot to the leg or ribs wouldn't just result in a quick escape, but rather a more detailed sequence: the injured NPC might seek cover in an alleyway, attempt to bandage their wounds, and then call for assistance. This concept suggests a more immersive and consequence-driven combat experience, adding a layer of tactical depth and realism that could redefine player interactions within the game world.
