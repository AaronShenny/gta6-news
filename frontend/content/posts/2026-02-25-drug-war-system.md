# Drug War System

A user on the GTA 6 subreddit, /u/No_Tomorrow_719, proposes that Rockstar Games implement a drug-selling mechanic similar to the one found in Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars for the upcoming GTA 6. They recall the Chinatown Wars system, where different 'plugs' offered varying buy and sell prices for various drugs, as a distinctive and enjoyable feature. The user believes this dynamic economic system would significantly enhance GTA 6 and invites the community to share their thoughts and other desired features for the game.

## Key Takeaways
- Suggestion for a drug-selling system in GTA 6.
- Inspired by the mechanics in Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars.
- Key feature: dynamic pricing where different NPCs ('plugs') offer high or low prices for various drugs.
- System is highlighted as a unique and engaging feature that set Chinatown Wars apart.
- Community discussion initiated to gather thoughts and other feature requests for GTA 6.

**What game inspired the suggested drug system for GTA 6?**
The suggested drug-selling system for GTA 6 is inspired by Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars.

The dynamic buying and selling prices for different drugs with various 'plugs' or NPCs, which created an engaging economic mini-game.
