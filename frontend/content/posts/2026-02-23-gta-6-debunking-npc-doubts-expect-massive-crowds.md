# GTA 6: Debunking NPC Doubts – Expect Massive Crowds

Contrary to popular skepticism regarding potential performance issues, Grand Theft Auto 6 is highly anticipated to deliver the dense NPC crowds showcased in its trailers, and potentially even more in certain areas. This assertion is supported by several key factors. Firstly, Rockstar Games has a consistent track record of using actual in-game footage for its promotional materials, suggesting the trailer's visuals are representative of the final product. Secondly, modern gaming consoles possess ample power to render such large populations, especially when compared to examples like Hitman WOA running on less capable mobile hardware, even accounting for simpler NPC interactions in the latter. Finally, Rockstar's immense financial and human resources, significantly dwarfing those of studios like IO Interactive, indicate a strong capacity for optimizing the game to handle exceptionally large and interactive crowds.
