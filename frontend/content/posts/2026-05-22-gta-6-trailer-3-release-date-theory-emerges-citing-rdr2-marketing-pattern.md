# GTA 6 Trailer 3 Release Date Theory Emerges, Citing RDR2 Marketing Pattern

A speculative post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has proposed a potential release date for Grand Theft Auto VI's third trailer, basing its theory on Rockstar Games' historical marketing patterns for Red Dead Redemption 2. The user, /u/Great-Juice-9894, notes that RDR2's third trailer dropped exactly 177 days before its official launch. Applying this logic to a presumed GTA VI launch date of November 19, 2026, the theory suggests that GTA VI Trailer 3 could arrive on May 26, 2026. The post highlights this timing as an opportune moment for Rockstar to 'soft launch' its marketing campaign ahead of the summer period, sparking discussion among fans about the plausibility of this prediction.

## Key Takeaways
- The theory posits GTA VI Trailer 3 could release on May 26, 2026.
- This date is derived from the observation that Red Dead Redemption 2's Trailer 3 was released 177 days before its launch.
- Applying the 177-day gap to a hypothetical GTA VI launch date of November 19, 2026, yields May 26, 2026.
- The timing is suggested as ideal for a 'soft launch' of marketing efforts before the summer season.
