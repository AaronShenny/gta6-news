# Spider-Man: Brand New Day Trailer Smashes GTA 6's Debut View Record

In a surprising turn of events, the recently released trailer for 'Spider-Man: Brand New Day' has eclipsed the record previously held by Grand Theft Auto 6's May 2025 trailer for debut views. While GTA 6's trailer impressively garnered 475 million views in under a day, the 'Brand New Day' trailer soared past 500 million views within its first 24 hours. This feat takes the crown from what Rockstar Games had touted as the biggest trailer debut ever, which had itself surpassed 'Deadpool & Wolverine.' It's important to note that these colossal view figures account for aggregated viewership across various platforms, not just single YouTube channels. Both 'Brand New Day,' launching this July, and GTA 6, slated for November, are poised for monumental success, with the latter projected to sell over 40 million copies and generate $3 billion in revenue in its inaugural year.

## Key Takeaways
- The 'Spider-Man: Brand New Day' trailer achieved over 500 million views in its first 24 hours.
- This new record surpasses the 475 million views garnered by GTA 6's May 2025 trailer in less than a day.
- GTA 6's May 2025 trailer was previously declared the biggest debut ever, exceeding 'Deadpool & Wolverine's' 365 million views.
- Total view counts reported by studios aggregate viewership across multiple hosting platforms, not just individual YouTube channels.
- 'Spider-Man: Brand New Day' is scheduled for release in July, while GTA 6 is expected in November.
- Both titles are highly anticipated blockbusters, with GTA 6 projected to sell over 40 million units and generate $3 billion in revenue in its first year.
