# GTA 6 Pre-Order Warning: Beware of Unofficial Retailer Scams

A recent post on the dedicated r/GTA6 subreddit has alerted the gaming community to a growing concern: unofficial Grand Theft Auto 6 pre-order listings from unverified retailers. The post, submitted by user /u/Charon_06, underscores the simplicity with which unscrupulous entities can create fraudulent pre-order pages, collect payments from eager fans, and potentially withhold funds or delay delivery until the game's actual release, if not outright scamming buyers. This serves as a critical reminder for gamers to exercise extreme vigilance and await official announcements from Rockstar Games and authorized distributors before committing to any purchases.

## Key Takeaways
- Unofficial GTA 6 pre-order offers are circulating online.
- Unverified retailers can easily create fake pre-order listings.
- These offers pose a risk of financial loss or significantly delayed product delivery.
- Gamers are strongly advised to await official pre-order announcements from Rockstar Games and trusted, authorized sources.
