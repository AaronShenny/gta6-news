# GTA 6 Trailer vs. Reality: Will Optimization Lead to Cut Content and Visual Downgrades?

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked discussion among fans regarding the potential for content and visual elements showcased in the GTA 6 trailer to be absent from the final game. Drawing parallels to Grand Theft Auto V, the original poster points out instances where GTA V's trailers featured elements—like a plane dropping gas in a mission that never appeared, or significantly higher crowd density—that were ultimately cut or reduced in the released game, even in its enhanced versions. The core concern revolves around optimization challenges inherent in developing vast open-world titles. The discussion is further fueled by a comparison between the bustling, crowded beach scene in the first official GTA 6 trailer and a more recent screenshot reportedly showing a beach with '0 NPC,' raising questions about whether similar concessions might be made for GTA 6. This speculation underscores a common apprehension within the gaming community about the fidelity of pre-release footage compared to the final product.
