# World Cup Star Erling Haaland: Xbox Fan, Gaming Setup Revealed, and Hyped for GTA 6

In a recent 'day in the life' video on his YouTube channel, Norwegian football star Erling Haaland offered fans a glimpse into his gaming world. The striker openly declared himself a huge Xbox enthusiast, even showcasing his Xbox minifridge, which he keeps stocked with MTN Dew and Coke. He expressed a clear desire for Xbox to sponsor him, stating, "One day, Xbox, I want them to sponsor me."

Despite his strong affinity for Xbox, a DualSense controller was also spotted on his gaming table, suggesting he enjoys games on PlayStation as well. Haaland's gaming space, designed for LAN parties with friends, was also featured. He highlighted 2009's *Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2* as one of his top three games of all time. Furthermore, he named *Minecraft* and *Grand Theft Auto* as his ultimate 'desert island' games, emphasizing his excitement for the highly anticipated *GTA 6* by saying, "I can't wait for the new one."

A humorous moment in the video showed Haaland playing *FC 24*, a game that famously features him on its cover. The article also briefly mentioned his upcoming World Cup quarterfinal match against England on July 11.
