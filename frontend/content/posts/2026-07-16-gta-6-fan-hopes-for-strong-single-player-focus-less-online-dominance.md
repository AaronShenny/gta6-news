# GTA 6: Fan Hopes for Strong Single-Player Focus, Less Online Dominance

A Reddit user, u/gamersecret2, ignited a discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit by posing a crucial question: What single element from GTA 5 should Rockstar *not* carry over into the highly anticipated GTA 6? The user's personal hope is that Rockstar avoids repeating the 'heavy focus on online features at the cost of the single player experience.' They laud GTA 5's story mode as 'amazing' and advocate for GTA 6 to receive the same meticulous 'attention, detail, and unforgettable moments' dedicated to its narrative campaign, rather than letting online components overshadow the core single-player offering.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user hopes GTA 6 will not prioritize online features over single-player content.
- The user praises GTA 5's story mode and wishes for similar attention to detail in GTA 6's single-player.
- The core sentiment is a desire for a rich, dedicated single-player experience in the upcoming title.

## FAQ
**What is the main concern a fan has about GTA 6 repeating elements from GTA 5?**
The fan's primary concern is that Rockstar might again heavily focus on online features at the expense of the single-player experience, as they felt happened with GTA 5.

**What does the Reddit user hope for in GTA 6?**
The user hopes that GTA 6's single-player mode receives the same level of attention, detail, and unforgettable moments that they believe GTA 5's story mode initially offered.

**Who submitted this opinion on Reddit?**
The opinion was submitted by Reddit user u/gamersecret2.
