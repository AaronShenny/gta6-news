# GTA 6 Map Theory: Is Gloriana Connected Beyond Vice City's Border?

Building upon previous theories, the user posits that the upcoming title might break from the traditional island-based map design, suggesting a land connection to the neighboring state of Gloriana. The theory is primarily fueled by the observation of numerous 'Gloriana' license plates within leaked or teased content, alongside a specific phrasing in an unconfirmed game text mentioning a 'northern state's border' rather than simpler terms like 'northern end of the state' or 'north shores.' This distinction, combined with the 'visit Gloriana' hints, leads to the speculation that Rockstar might be purposefully hinting at a contiguous landmass rather than an isolated island for Vice City. While acknowledging the speculative nature and potential for 'reaching,' the post suggests a 'small chance' that players could eventually explore beyond Vice City's immediate northern confines into Gloriana, offering a more expansive and less insular world than previous titles.
