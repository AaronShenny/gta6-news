# GTA VI Ending Theory: Jason's Tattoo May Foreshadow a Tragic Finale for Protagonists

A compelling new fan theory circulating among the Grand Theft Auto VI community suggests that a custom tattoo prominently displayed on protagonist Jason's back in recent screenshots may hold a significant clue to the game's ending. The tattoo depicts two winged skeletons, interpreted by many as symbolic representations of Jason and his co-protagonist, Lucia. Crucially, the Latin word 'Finis,' meaning 'the end,' is also visible. This imagery has led to speculation that GTA VI could conclude with the deaths of both Jason and Lucia, a narrative choice that would align with the Bonnie and Clyde-esque criminal romance theme expected from the game. Furthermore, some theorists propose that if this tragic ending comes to pass, players might transition to a third, as-yet-unrevealed protagonist for an epilogue segment.
