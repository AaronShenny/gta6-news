# GTA 6: Will Progression Mimic Vice City's Mid-Game Business Model?

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit sparks a discussion about Grand Theft Auto 6's potential mission structure, drawing parallels to Grand Theft Auto: Vice City. The original poster highlights how GTA: Vice City, at a certain point, left players to independently discover and complete a sufficient number of business setups to trigger the game's final mission, without explicit guidance. The core question posed to the community is whether GTA 6 will replicate this 'find-your-own-path' business acquisition model in Vice City and its surrounding areas, or if Rockstar Games will implement a different approach to mid-game progression this time.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA: Vice City's mid-game progression involved players independently finding and completing business setups.
- These business completions were necessary to unlock the game's final mission.
- The game did not explicitly guide players on how to find or complete these businesses.
- The discussion queries if GTA 6 will follow this same 'business acquisition' pattern.
- Alternatively, GTA 6 might introduce a different mission progression approach.

Yes, at a certain point in the mid-game, GTA: Vice City mandated that players independently find and complete a sufficient number of business setups to unlock the final mission, often without clear in-game direction.
