# GTA 6 Online: Community Hopes for Cross-Platform Play as Rockstar's Stance Remains Unclear

Discussions within the r/GTA6 subreddit are heating up regarding a highly anticipated feature for Grand Theft Auto VI Online: cross-platform play. A user, u/JoacoMexican07, recently voiced strong dissatisfaction with Rockstar Games' historical approach, noting the absence of console cross-play in titles like GTA V and Red Dead Online. The user highlighted that such functionality, particularly between different console ecosystems, has become an industry standard and its continued omission feels 'ridiculous.' While the post directly queries for any news regarding this feature for GTA VI, there has been no official confirmation or denial from Rockstar Games to date, leaving the community to hope for a more interconnected online experience in the upcoming title.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user expressed frustration regarding Rockstar's lack of cross-platform play in previous online titles like GTA V and Red Dead Online.
- The user believes that console cross-platform play is now an industry standard that should be included.
- The post specifically asks if there is any news regarding cross-platform play for GTA VI Online.
- As of the discussion, there is no official news or confirmation from Rockstar Games about cross-platform play for GTA VI Online.
