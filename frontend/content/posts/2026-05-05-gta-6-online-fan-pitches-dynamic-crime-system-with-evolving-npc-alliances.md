# GTA 6 Online: Fan Pitches Dynamic Crime System with Evolving NPC Alliances

A compelling concept for GTA 6 Online's dynamic crime system has emerged from the community, envisioning a central, wealthy NPC—akin to El Rubio—who offers players a significant choice. Instead of repetitive heists, players could opt to either partner with this drug distributor, selling their illicit business goods to him, or continue to rob him. The system's innovative twist lies in its dynamic nature: consistent partnership could lead to the NPC accumulating more valuable assets, making future heists against him even more lucrative, thus creating a strategic interplay between cooperation and confrontation.

## Key Takeaways
- Introduces a powerful, wealthy NPC (e.g., drug distributor) in GTA 6 Online, similar to El Rubio.
- Players would have the choice to either partner with or rob this NPC.
- Partnering would involve selling business goods to the NPC.
- Robbing would involve conducting heists against the NPC.
- Player actions could dynamically influence future opportunities; for example, selling to the NPC could increase the value of items available for subsequent heists.
- The system aims to provide more player agency and evolving interactions beyond repetitive missions.
