# Subtle Visual Flair: GTA 6's RGB Skyscraper Windows Praised for Enhancing City Life

A recent discussion within the Grand Theft Auto 6 community has highlighted a subtle yet highly appreciated visual detail: the presence of RGB lighting within some skyscraper windows. This small artistic choice, moving beyond typical uniform yellow or greenish glows, is lauded for significantly contributing to the feeling of an active, living city, adding a layer of authenticity and vibrancy to the urban environment.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 features RGB lighting in select skyscraper windows.
- This is noted as a subtle yet impactful visual detail.
- The RGB lights make the game's buildings feel more 'alive' and vibrant.
- It's a departure from generic, uniformly lit window facades.
