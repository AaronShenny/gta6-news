# GTA VI's Digital Shift Signals the End of Midnight Launch Parties, Fans Lament Loss of Tradition

The highly anticipated release of Grand Theft Auto VI appears to be marking the end of an era for traditional gaming launch events. A recent Reddit post by user /u/hushpolocaps69 expresses profound disappointment over the news that physical copies of GTA VI will not contain a disc. Instead, pre-ordered physical editions will be mailed to consumers prior to release, containing only a redemption code. This move effectively eliminates the need for store pickups and, consequently, the vibrant midnight launch parties that have long been a cherished part of major game releases. The author, who was particularly excited for GTA VI to be their first-ever video game launch party experience, laments the loss of atmosphere, unity, and the opportunity to interact with fellow fans, ultimately opting for a digital pre-order on the PlayStation Store due to the perceived futility of a physical purchase.

## Key Takeaways
- Traditional midnight launch parties for GTA VI are unlikely due to new physical copy distribution methods.
- Physical pre-orders will reportedly not contain a disc, instead shipping early with a redemption code.
- This change eliminates store pickups and the communal atmosphere associated with launch events.
- A fan expresses significant disappointment, having looked forward to GTA VI as their first potential launch party.
- The shift is seen as a loss of fan interaction and shared excitement within the gaming community.
- The disappointed user ultimately pre-ordered the game digitally on the PS Store.
