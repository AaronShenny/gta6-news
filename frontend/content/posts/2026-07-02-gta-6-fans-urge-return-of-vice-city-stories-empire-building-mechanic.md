# GTA 6 Fans Urge Return of Vice City Stories' 'Empire Building' Mechanic

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited discussion among fans, with user RogerRoger63358 advocating for the return of the 'Empire Building' mechanic, a beloved feature from *Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories*, in the upcoming *Grand Theft Auto 6*. The user highlighted that this system offers players 'so much to do outside of the main story narrative' and could serve as 'an engaging endgame gameplay loop.' The sentiment suggests a community desire for more expansive and long-term activities beyond the primary storyline, reminiscent of the empire management and business acquisition mechanics that provided significant replayability in past GTA titles.
