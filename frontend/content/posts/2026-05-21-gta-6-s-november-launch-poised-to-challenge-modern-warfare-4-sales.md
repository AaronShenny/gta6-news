# GTA 6's November Launch Poised to Challenge Modern Warfare 4 Sales

While Infinity Ward builds hype for its next Call of Duty title, presumed to be Modern Warfare 4, promising a 'bold' and 'relentless' experience, the article highlights a significant competitive factor: the launch of Grand Theft Auto 6. Rockstar's highly anticipated sequel is also slated for a November release, setting up a direct 'head-to-head' battle with Modern Warfare 4. This clash is expected to be substantial, with predictions that GTA 6 could 'eat significantly into' Modern Warfare 4's sales volumes, especially given Call of Duty's recent sales struggles and its new launch strategy of not being available on Game Pass or PS4 at release.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 is scheduled for a November launch.
- It will go 'head-to-head' with Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 4.
- The competition is predicted to 'eat significantly into' Modern Warfare 4's sales volumes.
- This comes as Call of Duty has seen recent sales declines and is shifting its launch strategy (no Game Pass or PS4 at launch for MW4).
