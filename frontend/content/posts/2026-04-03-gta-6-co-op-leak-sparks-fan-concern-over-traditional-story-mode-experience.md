# GTA 6 Co-op Leak Sparks Fan Concern Over Traditional Story Mode Experience

A recent leak hinting at co-operative gameplay being integrated into Grand Theft Auto 6's story mode has generated apprehension among fans. One Reddit user, u/agee_slick9, voiced their worry that such an integration might be permanent, potentially diluting the traditional single-player experience that defines the GTA series. The user expressed a strong desire to experience GTA 6 primarily as a solitary narrative, hoping that if co-op is indeed implemented, it will remain an optional feature rather than a mandatory component that could alter the game's 'golden experience' by mixing it with an online dynamic.

## Key Takeaways
- A recent leak suggests co-op functionality might be included in GTA 6's story mode.
- A Reddit user expressed concern that this co-op could be permanently integrated, impacting the traditional single-player experience.
- The user hopes that any co-op feature will be optional rather than mandatory.
- There is a strong preference for a classic single-player GTA experience over an online-mixed story mode.
