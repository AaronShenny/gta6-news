# Strauss Zelnick Jokes Again About GTA 6 Causing Mass Sick Days on November 19th

During a recent executive panel, Take-Two Interactive CEO Strauss Zelnick once again humorously predicted a significant societal impact from the launch of Grand Theft Auto 6. Zelnick joked that 'a lot of people will be calling in sick on November 19th,' implying the game's release is expected to cause a widespread phenomenon. This sentiment, widely echoed by the gaming community, suggests that Rockstar's highly anticipated title could indeed lead to a temporary employee shortage as players immerse themselves in the game.

## Key Takeaways
- Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick reiterated a joke about GTA 6's launch impact.
- He predicted many people would call in sick on November 19th, the implied launch date.
- The joke highlights the immense anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6.
- The gaming community shares Zelnick's view, expecting a significant disruption and potential 'employee shortage'.
