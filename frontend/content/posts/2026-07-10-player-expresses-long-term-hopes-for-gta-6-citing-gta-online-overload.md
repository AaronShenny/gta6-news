# Player Expresses Long-Term Hopes for GTA 6, Citing GTA Online Overload

A Reddit user, u/SharkByte1993, has shared their perspective on the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6, expressing a desire for long-term engagement with the title. The user reflects on their past experience with GTA Online, noting that despite attempts to re-engage and acquire in-game assets, the sheer volume of content made it difficult to jump back in and know where to start. This sentiment highlights a common challenge for live-service games with years of accumulated updates.

Looking forward, u/SharkByte1993 anticipates playing GTA 6 for "the next few years," indicating a hope for sustained interest through regular content releases and weekly updates. This suggests a desire for a more manageable and consistently engaging live-service experience compared to their past encounters with GTA Online.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user anticipates playing GTA 6 for many years.
- The user found GTA Online to have too much content, making it difficult to re-engage.
- They hope for a sustained and manageable content stream in GTA 6.
- The post reflects a player's desire for long-term engagement with the next GTA title.
