# Fan Suggestion: GTA Online Radio Could Bridge the Gap to GTA 6

A recent Reddit discussion post by u/Silent_Butterscotch5 explores a unique approach for Rockstar Games to transition players from Grand Theft Auto V's online component to the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. The user suggests leveraging GTA Online's in-game radio stations to deliver subtle teasers, such as news bulletins from Vice City, intercepted transmissions, or even snippets of the new protagonists' voices, gradually building organic hype and creating a sense of a continuously evolving world.

## Key Takeaways
- The core idea proposes using GTA Online's in-game radio stations as a bridge to GTA 6.
- Teasers could include news bulletins or intercepted transmissions originating from Vice City.
- Suggestions for content include introducing new protagonists' voices or mentioning escalating chaos in the new setting.
- The goal is to build organic hype and make the transition between games feel like one continuous, evolving world.
- The author questions if this 'environmental storytelling' would be more impactful than a standard trailer.

## FAQ
**What is the main idea behind bridging GTA Online to GTA 6?**
The main idea is to use GTA Online's existing in-game radio stations to subtly introduce teasers and lore for the upcoming GTA 6, making the transition between games feel more continuous.

**What kind of teasers are suggested for the radio stations?**
Proposed teasers include brief news bulletins, intercepted transmissions from Vice City, glimpses of new protagonists' voices, and mentions of escalating events in the new game's setting.

**What is the expected benefit of this approach?**
This method aims to build organic hype among players and create a sense of an evolving, continuous world between GTA V/Online and GTA VI, potentially being more engaging than traditional trailer reveals.
