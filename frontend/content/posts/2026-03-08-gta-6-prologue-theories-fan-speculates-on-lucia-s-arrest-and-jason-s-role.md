# GTA 6 Prologue Theories: Fan Speculates on Lucia's Arrest and Jason's Role

A Grand Theft Auto 6 enthusiast has shared intriguing theories regarding the game's potential prologue or first mission, both centered around Lucia's arrest and Jason's subsequent involvement. One scenario envisions Lucia leading a major store robbery that ends in a chaotic shootout, car chase, and her crew abandoning her after a crash. The other suggests a deal gone wrong, where Lucia and Jason are ambushed by undercover police, leading to a desperate escape attempt and Lucia's capture. Both theories incorporate dramatic elements, including betrayals, intense action sequences, and a setup for Jason to retrieve Lucia later in the game, drawing parallels to previous GTA titles.

## Key Takeaways
- Two distinct theories for GTA 6's prologue, both leading to Lucia's arrest.
- **Theory 1 (Store Robbery):** Lucia leads a crew in a high-stakes store heist involving explosives, a vault, and a secret escape route.
- The heist culminates in a chaotic shootout and car chase, ending with a crash where Lucia is abandoned by her crew and arrested, likened to GTA V's North Yankton prologue.
- **Theory 2 (Deal Gone Wrong):** Lucia and Jason are ambushed by undercover police during a negotiation with a rich dealer.
- A shootout ensues, separating the protagonists, with Lucia attempting a car escape that ends in a crash and her arrest, noted as a callback to Vice City.
- Both theories establish Lucia's imprisonment and set up Jason's eventual mission to free her.
