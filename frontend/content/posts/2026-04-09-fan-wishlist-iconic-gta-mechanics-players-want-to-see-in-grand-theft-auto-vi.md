# Fan Wishlist: Iconic GTA Mechanics Players Want to See in Grand Theft Auto VI

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan has compiled a comprehensive wishlist of mechanics from previous GTA titles they hope to see integrated into Grand Theft Auto VI. The list spans from the foundational GTA III to the more recent GTA V, highlighting elements like diverse vehicle sub-missions, asset management with deeper involvement, RPG elements such as character weight and relationship meters, robust friend activities, and intricate heist setups. The author expresses optimism that Rockstar will incorporate and expand upon many of these beloved features, potentially blending them with the immersive elements seen in Red Dead Redemption 2, to create an unparalleled experience in GTA VI.

## Key Takeaways
- **GTA III:** Vehicle Sub-Missions (paramedic, firefighter, vigilante, taxi), Off-Road challenges, Import/Export vehicle lists, Hitman-style missions.
- **GTA Vice City:** Asset management (property income with missions), Racing (stadium, closed circuit, demolition derbies).
- **GTA San Andreas:** Dual wield weapons, RPG elements (weight mechanic), Gang war system (Cuban/Haitian), Dating/gambling/relationship meters, School trials for vehicle types, Courier/delivery runs, Burglaries/enhanced theft mechanics.
- **GTA IV (and DLCs):** Weightier vehicle mechanics, Better NPC reactions, Engaging friend activities (comedy shows, bars, bowling, pool, darts), Clubhouse style games (Lost and Damned), Drug wars (Ballad of Gay Tony), Clubbing and club management activities.
- **GTA V:** In-depth heist set-ups (with more variety and focus on smaller jobs), Underwater exploration, Sports.
- **Overall & RDR2 Influence:** Desire for more immersive elements similar to Red Dead Redemption 2 (without being obtrusive), alongside expected leaps in graphics and NPC AI.
