# Fan Suggests GeoCache-Inspired Collectibles for GTA 6

A dedicated fan on Reddit's r/GTA6 subreddit, u/Frosty-Confection546, has put forth an intriguing concept for Grand Theft Auto VI's collectible system: a parody GeoCache app for the in-game phone. The idea aims to move players away from relying on online guides and instead foster organic exploration of the map. The proposed app would feature collectible 'branches' unlocked through story progression or freeroam discovery, providing clues and approximate locations, possibly even riddles, to guide players. Additionally, the concept includes 'Legendary Collectibles,' akin to Red Dead Redemption 2's Legendary Animals, which would function as unique treasure hunts offering higher rewards. The proponent believes this system would significantly enhance player interactivity, game depth, and overall realism.

## Key Takeaways
- Proposes a GeoCache parody app within GTA 6 for managing collectibles.

## FAQ
**What is the core idea for GTA 6 collectibles?**
The core idea is to integrate a parody GeoCache app into the player's in-game phone, which would serve as the primary tool for discovering and tracking collectibles across the map.

**How would the in-game GeoCache app work?**
Players would open the app to find various collectible 'branches' that are unlocked through story progression or free-roam discovery. The app would then provide clues, approximate locations, or even riddles to guide players to the collectibles, promoting exploration.

**What are 'Legendary Collectibles' in this concept?**
'Legendary Collectibles' are envisioned as unique, high-value items, similar to the Legendary Animals in Red Dead Redemption 2. They would not belong to a specific branch but would offer a distinct treasure hunt experience with greater rewards upon discovery.
