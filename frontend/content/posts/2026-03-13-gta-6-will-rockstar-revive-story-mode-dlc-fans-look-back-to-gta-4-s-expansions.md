# GTA 6: Will Rockstar Revive Story Mode DLC? Fans Look Back to GTA 4's Expansions

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited a fervent discussion among fans regarding the potential return of substantial story mode DLC for Grand Theft Auto 6. The original poster articulated a strong desire for single-player expansions akin to GTA 4's critically acclaimed 'The Ballad of Gay Tony' and 'The Lost and Damned,' expressing disappointment over the absence of similar narrative-driven content for Grand Theft Auto V. This sentiment underscores a widespread community hope that Rockstar Games will re-embrace its earlier model of enriching the single-player experience with significant post-launch story additions.

## Key Takeaways
- Fans are actively discussing the possibility of story mode DLC for GTA 6.
- There is a strong desire for expansions similar to GTA 4's 'The Ballad of Gay Tony' and 'The Lost and Damned'.
- Community members express regret over the lack of comparable single-player DLC for GTA 5.
