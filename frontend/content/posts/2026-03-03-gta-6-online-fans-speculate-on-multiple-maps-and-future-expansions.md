# GTA 6 Online: Fans Speculate on Multiple Maps and Future Expansions

The Grand Theft Auto community is buzzing with speculation regarding the potential for multiple playable maps within GTA 6 Online. A Reddit post by u/HarvWhanDon on the r/GTA6 subreddit posits that Rockstar Games might introduce additional geographical areas throughout the game's lifecycle, much like the Cayo Perico island update for GTA 5 Online. The discussion highlights Cayo Perico as a possible 'testing ground' for such future expansions and recalls 'Project Americas' leaks that once suggested a Latin American or Caribbean region as a potential playable area. Fans are now debating whether these future maps should be entirely new locales or incorporate beloved returning cities like Liberty City or Los Santos.

## Key Takeaways
- Speculation among fans about multiple maps being added to GTA 6 Online throughout its lifecycle.
- GTA 5 Online's Cayo Perico update is cited as a potential precedent or 'testing ground' for future multi-map strategies.
- 'Project Americas' leaks previously hinted at Rockstar considering a Latin American/Caribbean playable region.
- Community discussion revolves around preferred types of additional maps: entirely new areas or returning classic cities (e.g., Liberty City, Los Santos).

## FAQ
**Could GTA 6 Online feature multiple playable maps?**
Community speculation, fueled by past updates and leaks, suggests this is a strong possibility, with fans hoping for new areas to explore beyond the initial launch map.

**What indicates Rockstar might add more maps to GTA 6 Online?**
The successful Cayo Perico update in GTA V Online is seen by many as a precedent, potentially testing the waters for similar expansions in GTA 6. Additionally, 'Project Americas' leaks once pointed towards consideration of a Latin American/Caribbean region.

**What kind of additional maps are fans hoping for in GTA 6 Online?**
Players are debating between entirely new geographical regions based on other states or countries, or the return of iconic locations from previous GTA titles like Liberty City or Los Santos.
