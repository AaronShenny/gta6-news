# GTA Online's Success May Have Delayed GTA 6, Prompting Speculation on GTA 7

A recent Reddit post by user /u/Eastern_Syrup_2362 speculates that the overwhelming success of Grand Theft Auto Online may have significantly altered Rockstar Games' release schedule for the main series. The theory suggests that had GTA Online not achieved such massive and sustained popularity, Grand Theft Auto 6 might have already been released as early as 2021, and the gaming community could currently be discussing Grand Theft Auto 7. This speculation references a past fan theory involving 'airport doors' that once hinted at a 2021 launch for GTA 6, implying that Rockstar's priorities likely shifted to leverage GTA Online's ongoing profitability.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA Online's immense success is hypothesized as a major factor in GTA 6's delayed release.
- The author suggests GTA 6 could have launched in 2021 if not for GTA Online's impact.
- It's speculated that discussions about GTA 7 would be happening now under that scenario.
- A past 'airport doors' fan theory for a 2021 GTA 6 release is referenced as the original plan.
