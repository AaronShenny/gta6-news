# GTA 6 Release Date Confirmed for November 19, Official Marketing Kicks Off This Summer

During a recent earnings call, Take-Two Interactive provided eagerly anticipated updates on Grand Theft Auto 6. The game's release date remains locked in for November 19, quashing any recent speculation of delays. Crucially, official marketing efforts for GTA 6 are slated to begin this summer, with executives advising fans not to expect significant news until late June or early July. This marketing push will reportedly include the highly anticipated Trailer 3 and the commencement of pre-orders. Additionally, Take-Two addressed the mysterious Best Buy emails, with Strauss Zelnick clarifying he had no knowledge of their origin, suggesting they were not official communications. The company projects a substantial revenue of $8.0-8.2 billion for Fiscal Year 2027, largely driven by GTA 6's launch.
