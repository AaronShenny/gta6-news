# GTA 6 Fans Express Desire for Enhanced Fire Hydrant Explosions

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has surfaced a niche, yet indicative, fan expectation for Grand Theft Auto VI. User /u/Ok_Net_4196 voiced a hope for significantly more realistic and impactful fire hydrant explosion effects in the upcoming title. The user specifically referenced Grand Theft Auto IV and V, where they found these environmental interactions to be 'toned down and unrealistic,' suggesting a desire for heightened environmental detail and physics fidelity in GTA 6.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user hopes for improved fire hydrant explosion effects in GTA 6.
- The user found these effects 'toned down and unrealistic' in GTA 4 and 5.
- This points to a player desire for enhanced environmental detail and physics in GTA 6.
