# Eagle-Eyed Player Uncovers Potential GTA 6 'VIN Tracking' Feature in GTA Online

An observant Grand Theft Auto Online player has reportedly uncovered a peculiar detail within the game that could hint at a new feature in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. While customizing a specific Coquette D10 acquired from the chop shop, the player noticed an unusually long sunstrip description referencing police tracking a vehicle's VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) – a mechanic currently absent from GTA V and Online. The player's subsequent investigation suggests this anomaly is isolated to that particular vehicle and customization location, leading to speculation that a Rockstar developer may have inadvertently ported code from GTA 6, where a 'suspect profiling system' including vehicle information was hinted at in earlier leaks. The Coquette D10 itself has been featured in GTA 6 trailers, adding weight to the theory, though the player notes that no one else has reported or replicated this unique finding.
