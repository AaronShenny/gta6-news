# Take-Two CEO Hints at GTA 6 Price Point: $70-$80 Expected, No In-Game Ads Confirmed

Take-Two Interactive CEO Strauss Zelnick has recently provided a glimpse into the potential pricing strategy for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. Zelnick indicated that the title is likely to debut at a standard premium price, falling within the $70 to $80 range. Furthermore, in a move that will likely appease fans, the CEO explicitly confirmed that GTA VI will not feature any in-game advertisements.

## Key Takeaways
- Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick suggests GTA VI will launch at a premium price of $70-$80.
- Zelnick confirmed that Grand Theft Auto VI will not include any in-game advertisements.
