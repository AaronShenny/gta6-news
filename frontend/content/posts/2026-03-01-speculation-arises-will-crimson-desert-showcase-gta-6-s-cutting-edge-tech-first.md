# Speculation Arises: Will Crimson Desert Showcase GTA 6's Cutting-Edge Tech First?

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited discussion among fans, proposing that some of the cutting-edge technology anticipated for Grand Theft Auto 6 might make its public debut in Pearl Abyss's upcoming action RPG, Crimson Desert, before Rockstar's highly awaited title. The original post, titled 'I think a lot of the new technology present in GTA 6 will be in Crimson Desert first,' offers a speculative take on game development cycles and shared technological advancements. While the post itself is brief, an accompanying comment reflects the immense hype surrounding GTA 6, stating, 'From what we have seen, this game looks absolutely insane,' underscoring the high expectations for its visual and technical fidelity.

## Key Takeaways
- Speculation on Reddit suggests GTA 6's advanced technology could first appear in Crimson Desert.
- The theory posits that game development might see shared or earlier showcases of tech.
- Fans express high anticipation for GTA 6's technological and visual advancements.
