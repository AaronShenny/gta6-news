# GTA 6 Timeline Mystery: Is Rockstar Teasing a Prequel or a Modern Setting?

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan has ignited a vibrant discussion within the community by highlighting a potential timeline paradox in the eagerly anticipated GTA 6. Analyzing footage from the official trailers, a Reddit user points out a significant contradiction concerning the game's setting relative to the iconic GTA: Vice City. The appearance of veteran character Phil Cassidy with both arms intact in one trailer suggests a prequel scenario, as he famously loses an arm in the 1986-set Vice City. However, this theory is directly challenged by the undeniable presence of modern social media elements in another trailer, strongly indicating a contemporary setting. This observation leaves fans speculating whether Rockstar Games is deliberately crafting a temporal puzzle for players to solve or if these are simply early-development inconsistencies that will be clarified closer to launch.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 Trailer 2 features Phil Cassidy with both arms intact.
- In GTA: Vice City (set in 1986), Phil Cassidy loses an arm, suggesting GTA 6 could be a prequel.
- GTA 6 Trailer 1 displays modern social media platforms, which did not exist in 1986.
- The presence of modern social media contradicts a prequel setting and points to a contemporary timeline.
- The observations create a timeline paradox based on current trailer footage, sparking community debate.
