# GTA 6: Could Player Actions Influence In-Game TV News?

A user on the r/GTA6 subreddit, /u/NoPear1301, has shared an intriguing idea regarding potential game mechanics in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. Drawing inspiration from Red Dead Redemption 2, where major player actions like robberies or violent encounters would be reported in subsequent newspaper editions, the user proposes a similar system for GTA 6. However, instead of newspapers, the suggestion is that these significant in-game events could be broadcast on TV news channels, offering a more dynamic and immersive way for the game world to react to the player's choices and actions. This speculative feature could significantly enhance the feeling of consequence and a living, breathing world within GTA 6.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user suggests GTA 6 could feature in-game news reports reflecting player actions.
- The idea is inspired by Red Dead Redemption 2's newspaper system, which reported on player-initiated events.
- Instead of newspapers, the speculation points towards TV news channels as the medium for these reports in GTA 6.
