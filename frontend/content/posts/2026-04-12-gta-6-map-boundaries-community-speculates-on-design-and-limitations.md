# GTA 6 Map Boundaries: Community Speculates on Design and Limitations

A recent community discussion initiated by Reddit user /u/AshamedWolverine1684 delves into the anticipated map boundaries for Grand Theft Auto VI. The user notes a general understanding of the southern water boundary, speculating it might mirror GTA V's system where vehicles are destroyed upon exiting the playable area. A primary point of curiosity revolves around the northern boundary: will Rockstar employ 'infinitely high mountains' to prevent players from venturing too far, or will there be another method? The user also expresses a desire to avoid an 'island-style map' while acknowledging its possibility, opening the floor for community input and ideas on how Rockstar might design the game's geographical limits.

## Key Takeaways
- Speculation on southern water boundary mechanics, possibly mirroring GTA V's vehicle destruction.
- Questioning the design of the northern map boundary, specifically if 'infinitely high mountains' will be used.
- Expressed hope to avoid an 'island style map,' while acknowledging its possibility.
- Call for community ideas and thoughts on map boundary implementation.
