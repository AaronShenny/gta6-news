# GTA 6 Could Punish 'GTA 5 Mentality,' Proposing Slower, More Punishing Combat

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user /u/FanaticBanatic, posits that Grand Theft Auto 6 will fundamentally shift its gameplay experience, particularly concerning combat and pacing. The post suggests that the 'Rambo-type gameplay' prevalent in GTA 5 might not be viable in the upcoming title. Instead, the author anticipates a slower, more deliberate, and punishing combat system, where high-star Wanted levels evoke a feeling of being 'hunted' and necessitate 'extreme measures for desperation,' contrasting sharply with the more arcade-like feel of previous installments. The post invites community discussion on desired combat pacing and potential game inspirations for Rockstar.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 is expected to feature significant changes in combat and overall pacing.
- The 'Rambo-type gameplay' style of GTA 5 may not be supported or rewarded in GTA 6.
- Anticipation for a slower, more challenging, and punishing combat experience.
- High Wanted levels (e.g., 6 stars) should feel desperate, forcing players into extreme measures.
- The post encourages community discussion on ideal combat pacing and potential game inspirations for Rockstar.
