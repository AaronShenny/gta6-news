# GTA 6 Car Interiors: How Interactive Will They Be?

A recent discussion among the dedicated GTA 6 community, initiated by Reddit user u/aggam84, delves into the anticipated level of interactivity within the game's vehicle interiors. While Grand Theft Auto V significantly advanced first-person driving with detailed dashboards, actual interactive elements were largely confined to the radio. Fans are now expressing considerable excitement for enhanced realism and intricate detail in GTA 6's car interiors, citing observations from official trailers and recent pre-order screenshots that showcase remarkably detailed vehicle models. A pivotal point of discussion revolves around the 'legitimate 2022 leaks,' which reportedly unveiled several advanced interior features. These included highly sought-after elements such as steering wheel height adjustment, seat adjustment, realistic seat fabrics, and fully functional side mirrors. The community is eager to discover how many of these detailed features will be integrated into the final game, with a general desire for immersive, small interactive details that enrich the driving experience without veering into full driving-simulator mechanics. The post concludes by inviting further speculation on what other realistic features players hope to see.
