## FAQ
**What is the main concern regarding GTA 6 pre-orders?**
The primary concern among fans is whether the immense volume of day-one pre-orders for GTA 6 will overload and crash the digital store servers of platforms like Xbox and PlayStation.

**Why is there such high anticipation for GTA 6?**
Grand Theft Auto is one of the most popular and commercially successful gaming franchises globally. Each new installment generates monumental excitement, sales, and cultural impact, leading to unprecedented demand.

**Have game pre-orders crashed servers before?**
While full system crashes are rare, high demand for major game launches has historically led to slow downloads, queueing systems, or temporary outages on digital storefronts and game servers, indicating the strain placed by massive user influx.
