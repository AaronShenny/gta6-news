# GTA 6 Community Hopes for Equal Launch Access, Excluding Streamers

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit, submitted by /u/Gainfulz, highlights a significant sentiment within the community regarding early access for Grand Theft Auto VI. The user expresses a strong hope that prominent content creators, such as YouTubers and streamers, will not receive early access to the highly anticipated title. Instead, the poster believes that a simultaneous launch for the general public would be ideal, with early access strictly limited to traditional journalists and reviewers for critical assessment. The core concern revolves around the perceived unfairness and potential spoiler fatigue if popular content creators were to extensively stream or upload playthroughs immediately upon gaining early access, diminishing the shared discovery experience for the wider player base.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user advocates against early access for content creators (YouTubers, streamers) for GTA 6.
- The user believes early access should be exclusive to traditional journalists and reviewers.
- The desire is for the general player base to experience the game simultaneously.
- Concern is raised about content creators potentially spoiling the game for others.
