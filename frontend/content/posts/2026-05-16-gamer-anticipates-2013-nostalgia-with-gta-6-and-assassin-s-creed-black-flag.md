# Gamer Anticipates 2013 Nostalgia with GTA 6 and Assassin's Creed Black Flag

A Reddit user expresses profound excitement for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6, anticipating a nostalgic return to what they consider the 'best year' for video games: 2013. The user plans to 'resync' their gaming experience by playing GTA 6 alongside a 'resynced' Assassin's Creed Black Flag, a title originally released in 2013. While acknowledging that Black Flag won't likely compare to GTA 6, they note that the latter is exceeding their expectations. The user intends to play both titles, primarily focusing on GTA 6 (95%), possibly in November.

## Key Takeaways
- Gamer anticipates GTA 6 release as a return to 2013's 'best year' for gaming.
- Plans to play GTA 6 alongside a 'resynced' Assassin's Creed Black Flag.
- Acknowledges GTA 6 will likely dominate playtime, despite Black Flag looking 'A LOT better' than imagined.
- Suggests a potential November playtime window for both titles.
