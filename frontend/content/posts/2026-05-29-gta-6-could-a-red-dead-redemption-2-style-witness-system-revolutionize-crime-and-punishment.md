# GTA 6: Could a Red Dead Redemption 2-Style Witness System Revolutionize Crime and Punishment?

A discussion has emerged within the GTA 6 community regarding the potential implementation of a more sophisticated witness system, taking inspiration from Red Dead Redemption 2. A Reddit user expressed dissatisfaction with GTA V's instant police awareness, even in isolated areas. They propose that GTA 6 could feature NPCs who actively call 911 upon witnessing crimes, provide descriptions rather than immediate identification, and offer players opportunities to intimidate witnesses or de-escalate situations before law enforcement is fully alerted. This system aims to create a more realistic and engaging crime and consequence dynamic.

## Key Takeaways
- Criticism of GTA V's instant police awareness regardless of location.
- Suggestion for GTA 6 to adopt a witness system similar to Red Dead Redemption 2.
- Proposed features include NPCs calling 911 and providing descriptions.
- Opportunity for players to intimidate witnesses or talk their way out of suspicion.
- Goal is to prevent instant police knowledge and add depth to crime mechanics.
