# GTA 6 Online: Will Rockstar Dial Back the Absurdity?

A recent Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited a discussion among fans, questioning whether the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6 Online will adopt a more 'grounded' approach compared to its predecessor. User /u/current-seven expressed a desire for GTA 6 Online to forgo the 'dumb outlandish stuff' that became prevalent in GTA 5 Online, specifically citing examples like 'flying rocket bikes' and 'flying Mandalorian-looking cars with wings.' This sentiment reflects a portion of the community hoping for a return to a more realistic and less fantastical online experience.

## Key Takeaways
- Community speculation regarding GTA 6 Online's gameplay direction.
- User expresses hope for a more 'grounded' online experience.
- Critiques specific 'outlandish' elements from GTA 5 Online, such as flying vehicles (rocket bikes, winged cars).
