# GTA 6 Fans Hope for More Diverse and Damaged Vehicles

A recent discussion initiated by Reddit user /u/Any-Recipe9970 on the r/GTA6 subreddit highlights a desire among fans for a more varied and realistic vehicle experience in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. The user points out that GTA 5 often features identical, clean cars in the same areas, which they find immersion-breaking. They propose that GTA 6 should introduce a system where common car models have multiple variations, including different configurations and varying degrees of cosmetic wear and tear such as scratches, scuffs, or broken bumpers, acknowledging the development challenges this would entail. The post invites community feedback on this vision for enhanced vehicular realism.

## Key Takeaways
- Criticism of GTA 5's repetitive and uniformly pristine cars in game environments.
- Desire for greater car variety and realism in GTA 6.
- Suggestion for different configurations and pre-existing cosmetic damage (scratches, scuffs, broken bumpers) on vehicle variants.
- Acknowledgement that implementing such a system would be a time-consuming development process.
