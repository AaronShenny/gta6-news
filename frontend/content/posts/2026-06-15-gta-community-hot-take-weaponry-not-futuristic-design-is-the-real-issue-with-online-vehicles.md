# GTA Community Hot Take: Weaponry, Not Futuristic Design, Is the Real Issue with Online Vehicles

A recent 'hot take' from the GTA community suggests that the long-standing complaints about futuristic vehicles in GTA Online, such as the Oppressor Mk2, are primarily due to their absurdly strong weaponry rather than their futuristic nature. The user argues that if these vehicles lacked their devastating missile launchers, player discontent would be significantly reduced. Drawing parallels to classic 'wacky' vehicles like the San Andreas jetpack, the opinion piece proposes that military-grade tech vehicles could have a place in GTA 6 if implemented in a more 'tasteful' manner, specifically citing the regular Oppressor without its missile capabilities as an example of a balanced approach.

## Key Takeaways
- The core issue with futuristic vehicles in GTA Online is their powerful weaponry, not their futuristic design.
- Player complaints about vehicles like the Oppressor Mk2 would likely decrease without their missile capabilities.
- The Grand Theft Auto series has a history of 'wacky' vehicles, predating current futuristic designs.
- Military-grade tech vehicles could be included in GTA 6 if implemented 'tastefully' and without excessive weaponry.
- An example of tasteful integration is suggested as an Oppressor-type vehicle without missile launchers.
