# GTA 6 Fan Proposes Customizable '18+ Filter' for Family-Friendly Gameplay

A user on the r/GTA6 subreddit, u/aTwerkingPikachu, has proposed an intriguing idea for Grand Theft Auto 6: an optional '18+ filter' setting. This feature, if implemented, would allow players to modify or disable certain mature aspects of the game. For instance, enabling the filter could result in strippers wearing clothes, the inability to solicit prostitutes, blurred-out cuss words from NPCs, and the removal of blood effects during violent encounters.

The core of the idea emphasizes player customization, allowing users to toggle specific elements of the filter independently. This means a player could choose to censor nudity but keep the profanity, or vice versa. The filter would not be a default setting; players would have to actively enable it. Crucially, once activated, the filter would be password-protected, preventing unauthorized deactivation. The primary motivation behind this concept is to provide parents with a tool to feel more comfortable allowing younger children to play the game.
