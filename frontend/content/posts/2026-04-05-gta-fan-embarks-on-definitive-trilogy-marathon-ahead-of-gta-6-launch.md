# GTA Fan Embarks on Definitive Trilogy Marathon Ahead of GTA 6 Launch

In a recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit, user u/Far-Series1829 announced their intention to replay the Grand Theft Auto Definitive Trilogy, and possibly Grand Theft Auto 4, before November. This move highlights a common trend among fans gearing up for the highly anticipated release of Grand Theft Auto 6, as many look to refresh their memories of the series' rich history and iconic titles.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user plans to replay the GTA Definitive Trilogy.
- They are also considering replaying Grand Theft Auto 4.
- The replay initiative is in preparation for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6.
- The user aims to complete their playthroughs before November.

## FAQ
**Why are fans replaying older GTA games now?**
Many fans are replaying older Grand Theft Auto titles to relive nostalgia, refresh their understanding of the series' lore and mechanics, and build excitement in anticipation of the highly awaited Grand Theft Auto 6.

**Which games are typically included in these 'pre-GTA 6' replays?**
Commonly, fans revisit the Grand Theft Auto Definitive Trilogy (comprising GTA III, Vice City, and San Andreas), and often Grand Theft Auto IV and Grand Theft Auto V, to experience the progression of the series.
