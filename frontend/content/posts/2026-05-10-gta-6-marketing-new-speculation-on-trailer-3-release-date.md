# GTA 6 Marketing: New Speculation on Trailer 3 Release Date

Amidst ongoing anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6, a Reddit user has put forth a theory regarding the release date of the game's third official trailer. While some fans have circled May 13th, the user, /u/BasketEquivalent5462, suggests May 17th holds a stronger possibility. This speculation is rooted in the idea that Rockstar might release the trailer as a 'thank you' gesture on Norway's National Day, May 17th, following a significant promotional effort for GTA 6 by a Norwegian retailer earlier this year.

- Speculation is circulating regarding the release date of GTA 6's third trailer.
