# GTA 6: Fans Speculate on Direct Dexter References in Vice City

Building on subtle Easter eggs found in GTA V, a recent Reddit discussion highlights the strong possibility of more direct references to the popular TV show 'Dexter' in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto VI. With Vice City heavily inspired by Miami, fans are eager to see if Rockstar Games will capitalize on the setting to include explicit nods to the serial killer protagonist. The discussion proposes several specific in-game additions, from iconic locations to environmental storytelling, and questions whether Rockstar will integrate a full mission or stick to more discreet references.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA V featured subtle Dexter references, such as character names 'Dexter Fisher' and 'Morgan Price'.
- Vice City's Miami-inspired setting presents a unique opportunity for more overt 'Dexter' Easter eggs.
- Suggested direct references include Dexter's Bay Harbor apartment building.
- The 'Slice of Life' boat could be featured in Vice City marinas.
- Submerged trash bags, reminiscent of Dexter's dumping grounds, are proposed as environmental details.
- References to the Miami Metro Homicide department or a blood spatter analyst NPC within the VCPD headquarters are also suggested.
- Fans are debating whether Rockstar will implement a full 'Stranger and Freaks' mission involving a serial killer or opt for subtle environmental storytelling.
