# GTA 6 Community Hopes for Social Club Revival, Speculates on In-Game Social App

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan on Reddit, u/bigfoot_manhunter, has voiced their significant disappointment over the perceived loss and discontinuation of the Rockstar Social Club. The user fondly recalls the Social Club's functionality as a robust social network, akin to platforms like Twitter and Facebook, for publishing game analytics, invested stocks, and overall player progress. Looking ahead to GTA VI, the fan confidently speculates that the upcoming title will undoubtedly feature its own in-game social media application, humorously suggesting the name 'i'm Hooked,' where players can 'waste time.' The post concludes with a plea for the return of the original Social Club functionalities.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user u/bigfoot_manhunter expresses deep regret over the loss of Rockstar Social Club's social network features.
