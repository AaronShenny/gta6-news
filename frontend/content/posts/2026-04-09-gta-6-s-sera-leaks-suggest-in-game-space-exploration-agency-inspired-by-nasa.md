# GTA 6's SERA: Leaks Suggest In-Game Space Exploration Agency Inspired by NASA

Recent leaks surrounding Grand Theft Auto 6 have unveiled intriguing details about 'SERA,' a potential in-game space exploration and research agency. First discovered in the 2022 data breach, SERA references included 'Failed Space Launch Engine/Capsule/Fairing/Debris' and a 'SERA Minivan.' Speculated to stand for 'Space Exploration and Research Agency,' SERA is widely believed to draw inspiration from real-world facilities like NASA's Kennedy Space Center in Florida. This suggests players might be able to witness spectacular rocket launches and engage with space-related events within the highly anticipated game, adding a unique layer of immersion.
