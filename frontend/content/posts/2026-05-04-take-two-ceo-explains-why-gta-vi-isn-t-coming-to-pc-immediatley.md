# Take-Two CEO explains why GTA VI isn't coming to PC immediatley

Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick has addressed the anticipated console-first launch of Grand Theft Auto VI, explaining why PC players will need to wait. In an interview with Bloomberg News' Jason Schreier at the Interactive Innovation Conference, Zelnick stated that Rockstar Games historically prioritizes console releases to serve its core audience first. He also dismissed claims that this strategy is a marketing deal with Sony, reinforcing Rockstar's long-standing practice of launching on consoles initially.

## Key Takeaways
- Grand Theft Auto VI will not launch on PC immediately, prioritizing consoles.
- Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick attributes this to Rockstar's historical practice of serving its core console audience first.
- Zelnick denies that console exclusivity is a marketing deal, emphasizing Rockstar's consistent console-first approach.
- The explanation came from an interview with Bloomberg News' Jason Schreier at an industry conference in Las Vegas.
