# Insomniac's Marvel's Wolverine PS5 Release Date Set for September 2026, Preceding GTA 6

Insomniac Games, the acclaimed studio behind the Marvel's Spider-Man series, has officially announced that its upcoming Marvel's Wolverine title will release on PlayStation 5 on September 15, 2026. This highly anticipated game, continuing Insomniac's successful partnership with Sony and Marvel, will bring Logan's brutal combat and intense quest for his past to players a full two months before the colossal launch of Grand Theft Auto 6, which is slated for November 19, 2026. The article notes that the next Call of Duty game is also expected in October or November of the same year, making late 2026 a packed schedule for gamers. Positioned as a spiritual successor to 2009's X-Men Origins: Wolverine, Insomniac's take promises a return to the character's visceral action and relentless determination.
