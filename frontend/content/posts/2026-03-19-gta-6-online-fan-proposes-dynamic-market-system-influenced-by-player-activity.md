# GTA 6 Online: Fan Proposes Dynamic Market System Influenced by Player Activity

A dedicated fan on Reddit, u/Skywarsmaster63, has proposed an intriguing concept for GTA 6 Online's economy: dynamic business sell prices. This idea suggests that the profitability of in-game businesses, such as car washes, would be directly influenced by the collective actions of the player base. If a large percentage of online players are engaging in a particular business, its sell prices would decrease due to market oversaturation. Conversely, less popular or 'opposite' businesses could see their prices rise. The goal is to prevent players from solely focusing on one 'meta' business, encouraging diversification and adding a layer of economic realism and immersion to the game world.

## Key Takeaways
- Proposal for dynamic business sell prices in GTA 6 Online.
- Sell prices would fluctuate based on the percentage of players engaging in specific businesses.
- High player engagement in one business would lead to price decreases due to market oversaturation.
- Aims to encourage players to diversify their in-game economic activities.
- Seeks to enhance immersion by simulating real-world market dynamics.
