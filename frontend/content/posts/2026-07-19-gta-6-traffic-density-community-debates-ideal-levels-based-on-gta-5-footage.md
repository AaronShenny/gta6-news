# GTA 6 Traffic Density: Community Debates Ideal Levels Based on GTA 5 Footage

A recent Reddit post has ignited a community discussion among Grand Theft Auto fans regarding the optimal traffic density for the highly anticipated GTA 6. It directly asks the community whether GTA 6 should aim for higher traffic density or maintain similar levels.

This discussion underscores players' strong desire for an immersive and realistic open-world experience in the upcoming title. The conversation highlights the ongoing anticipation and speculation surrounding GTA 6's world design and how Rockstar Games might evolve the series' signature open-world elements.
