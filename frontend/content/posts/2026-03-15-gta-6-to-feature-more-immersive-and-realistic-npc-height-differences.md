# GTA 6 to Feature More Immersive and Realistic NPC Height Differences

According to a Reddit post, Grand Theft Auto 6 will significantly enhance NPC realism through more varied and consistent height differences among characters. Unlike Red Dead Redemption 2, where noticeable height variations were often confined to cutscenes, GTA 6 aims to display a broader spectrum of heights in general gameplay. This includes realistic scenarios where males and females can share similar heights, and clear, distinct height disparities between various characters, contributing to a more immersive and believable in-game world.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 will feature clear and consistent differences in NPC heights.
- This is a notable improvement over Red Dead Redemption 2, where height variations were primarily visible in cutscenes.
- The game will show greater variety, allowing males and females to have similar heights.
- Characters will exhibit more distinct and noticeable height differences from each other.
- The change aims to enhance the overall immersion and realism within the game world.
