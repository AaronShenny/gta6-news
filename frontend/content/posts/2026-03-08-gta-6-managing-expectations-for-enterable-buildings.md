# GTA 6: Managing Expectations for Enterable Buildings

A recent discussion on Reddit highlights community expectations regarding enterable buildings in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/Dry-Historian-8896 suggests that while GTA 6 will undoubtedly feature more accessible interiors than previous installments, players might be overhyping the sheer volume. The user points to Rockstar's previous title, Red Dead Redemption 2, as an example where, despite its immense detail, many buildings remained inaccessible, urging fans to manage their expectations for GTA 6's interior access.

## Key Takeaways
- Community may be overhyping the number of enterable buildings in GTA 6.
- GTA 6 is expected to feature more accessible interiors compared to previous titles.
- Reference to Red Dead Redemption 2's limited interior access as a cautionary example.
- A call to manage expectations regarding the extent of interior accessibility in the new game.
