# GTA Wiki Ditches Fandom for Independent Home Ahead of GTA VI Release

In a significant move coinciding with its 20-year anniversary and the highly anticipated release of GTA VI (reportedly in November), the official Grand Theft Auto Wiki has announced its permanent relocation from Fandom to a new, independent domain: gta.wiki. This strategic shift aims to provide users with a dramatically improved experience, featuring significantly fewer intrusive advertisements, reduced content censorship (including the removal of word filters), and the convenience of editing articles without the need for an account. The new platform also boasts a redesigned front page and enhanced mobile compatibility.

Despite Fandom's deliberate refusal to close the old wiki, which means its links will unfortunately persist in search engine results, the GTA Wiki team is actively urging its community to support the transition. Users are encouraged to spread the word, prioritize gta.wiki links in search results (potentially utilizing the Indie Wiki Buddy browser extension), update existing bookmarks, and contribute to the new site's content to ensure its continued success as the definitive encyclopedia for the Grand Theft Auto series.
