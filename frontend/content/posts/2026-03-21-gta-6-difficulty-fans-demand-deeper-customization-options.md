# GTA 6 Difficulty: Fans Demand Deeper Customization Options

The highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI is already generating significant community discussion, with a recent Reddit post from u/Additional-Remove46 highlighting a popular desire for more comprehensive difficulty customization. The user's 'hot take' suggests that Rockstar Games should implement granular options, allowing players to tailor their experience. This includes the ability to disable death penalties, adjust the overall difficulty from 'easy' to 'hard,' and cater to a wider range of player preferences. The sentiment arises from a common perception that previous titles like GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2 were often too easy for some veteran players, while others might struggle with specific mechanics.

## Key Takeaways
- Players desire more extensive difficulty customization in GTA 6.
- Specific suggestions include the option to disable death penalties.
- The ability to adjust overall difficulty (low, hard) is a key request.
- The proposal stems from the belief that GTA V and RDR2 lacked sufficient difficulty options for diverse player skill levels.

## FAQ
**What kind of difficulty customization are players requesting for GTA 6?**
Players are primarily asking for more granular control over difficulty settings, including the ability to disable specific penalties like death consequences, and to fine-tune the overall challenge level from very easy to very hard.

**Why is this a 'hot take' or a significant discussion point?**
It's considered a 'hot take' because while many enjoy Rockstar's standard difficulty, a vocal segment of the community feels previous games like GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2 were too easy. Adding extensive customization would be a departure from Rockstar's traditional approach but could significantly broaden the game's appeal.
