# GTA 6 Fan Theories Explore Potential for Expanded Cults and Conspiracies

A recent Reddit discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited speculation among fans regarding the potential for new and expanded cults and conspiracy theories in Grand Theft Auto VI. Drawing parallels to GTA V's prominent Epsilon Program and Altruist cults, users are debating whether Rockstar will introduce more such factions, potentially integrate the Epsilon Program further into the main narrative, or even create entirely new, elaborate conspiracy storylines for the upcoming title, reflecting a desire for richer, more mysterious in-game lore.

## Key Takeaways
- Fans are actively discussing the possibility of new cults and conspiracy theories in GTA 6.
- The Epsilon Program and Altruists from GTA V are cited as precedents for in-game factions.
- Speculation includes the potential for the Epsilon Program to be expanded or integrated into GTA 6's main story.
- The community expresses interest in more fleshed-out mysterious organizations and storylines.

## FAQ
**What cults were featured in GTA V?**
Grand Theft Auto V featured the Epsilon Program and the Altruists as prominent in-game cults.

**Will the Epsilon Program return in GTA 6?**
While unconfirmed, fan discussions suggest a strong desire for the Epsilon Program's return, potentially with an expanded role or integration into the main storyline.

**Are fans expecting new conspiracy theories in GTA 6?**
Yes, the community is speculating about both the expansion of existing cults and the introduction of entirely new conspiracy theories and mysterious factions in GTA 6.
