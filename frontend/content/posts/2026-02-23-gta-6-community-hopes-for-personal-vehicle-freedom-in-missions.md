# GTA 6: Community Hopes for Personal Vehicle Freedom in Missions

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by user u/Riplay10 has resonated with the Grand Theft Auto community, bringing to light a long-standing point of frustration. The user expresses a strong desire to utilize their own customized and beloved personal vehicles during missions, rather than being compelled to use often 'crappy' mission-specific cars. This sentiment reflects a common player experience where time and in-game resources are invested in vehicle modification, only for that choice to be overridden during crucial gameplay. The community is hopeful that Rockstar Games will address this in GTA 6, offering greater flexibility and player agency regarding vehicle selection in missions, thus allowing for a more immersive and personalized experience.

## Key Takeaways
- Players desire to use their customized personal vehicles in missions.
- Frustration stems from being forced into specific, often inferior, mission-provided cars.
- Community hopes for greater vehicle freedom and player choice in GTA 6 missions.
- The sentiment is a common point of discussion among GTA series fans.
