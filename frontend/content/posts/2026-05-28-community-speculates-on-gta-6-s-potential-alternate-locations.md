# Community Speculates on GTA 6's Potential Alternate Locations

Inspired by memorable, temporary excursions like Grand Theft Auto V's snowy North Yankton and Red Dead Redemption 2's exotic Guarma, the gaming community is actively engaging in speculation regarding potential alternate locations for Grand Theft Auto VI. A popular Reddit discussion prompt invites players to brainstorm unique settings that could offer story-driven diversions, expanding on Rockstar Games' history of including distinct, limited-access areas outside the primary game world.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA V featured North Yankton as a notable temporary alternate location.
- Red Dead Redemption 2 included Guarma, an isolated tropical island, as a story-specific alternate area.
- Fans are discussing and suggesting what kind of alternate, temporary locations GTA VI might introduce.
- These locations often provide unique gameplay experiences and story beats distinct from the main map.
