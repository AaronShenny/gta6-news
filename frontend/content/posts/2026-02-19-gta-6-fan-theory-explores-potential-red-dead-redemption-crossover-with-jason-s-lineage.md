# GTA 6 Fan Theory Explores Potential Red Dead Redemption Crossover with Jason's Lineage

A recent discussion on Reddit has ignited an intriguing fan theory, positing a surprising familial connection for one of Grand Theft Auto VI's protagonists, Jason. The theory suggests that Jason's father could be none other than Jack Marston, a prominent character from the Red Dead Redemption series. This speculative link has captivated fans, eager to explore the potential narrative implications and the 'controversy' such a cross-franchise revelation might generate within the gaming community.

## Key Takeaways
- The fan theory proposes that Jack Marston from the Red Dead Redemption series could be the father of GTA 6 protagonist, Jason.
- One piece of 'evidence' cited is the option to select John Marston as a father in GTA Online's character creator, suggesting a distant thematic link.
- A more direct in-game lore point mentioned is the presence of a book authored by Jack Marston within Franklin Clinton's house in Grand Theft Auto V.
- Proponents of the theory believe that such a cross-franchise familial tie would create significant discussion and excitement among the gaming fanbase.

## FAQ
**What is the core of this GTA 6 fan theory?**
The theory suggests that Jack Marston from the Red Dead Redemption series could be the father of Jason, one of the protagonists in Grand Theft Auto VI.

A direct familial link between the Red Dead Redemption and Grand Theft Auto universes would be a monumental lore reveal, potentially sparking widespread discussion and 'controversy' among fans due to the different eras and settings of the games.

No, this is purely a fan theory originating from a Reddit discussion and has no official confirmation from Rockstar Games.
