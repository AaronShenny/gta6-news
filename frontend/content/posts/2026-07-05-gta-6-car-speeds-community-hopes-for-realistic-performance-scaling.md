# GTA 6 Car Speeds: Community Hopes for Realistic Performance Scaling

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan has voiced their aspirations for significant improvements in how vehicles are handled in the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. The user, /u/NinjaHamm, highlighted a key discrepancy in GTA Online where even the fastest cars struggle to break 200mph, a stark contrast to their real-world hypercar inspirations that can exceed 300mph. While acknowledging potential gameplay reasons for these limitations, the player expressed confusion over why fictional cars like the FMJ Mark 5 and Banshee GTS outperform vehicles based on real-world counterparts like Koenigseggs or Bugattis.

The core hope for GTA 6 is for a more realistic and consistent scaling of vehicles, encompassing aspects like top speed, overall performance, and even price, to better reflect their real-life inspirations. This sentiment reflects a desire for a more authentic and immersive driving experience within the next installment of the Grand Theft Auto series.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA Online car top speeds are capped significantly below real-world counterparts (under 200mph vs. 300+mph).
- There's a perceived inconsistency where fictional in-game cars outperform vehicles inspired by real-world hypercars.
- Players hope for more realistic scaling of vehicle performance, speed, and price in GTA 6 to match real-life inspirations.
- The community desires a more authentic and immersive driving experience in GTA 6.

## FAQ
**What is the main concern about car performance in GTA Online?**
The main concern is that even the fastest cars in GTA Online don't exceed 200mph, which is significantly slower than their real-world hypercar inspirations that can reach over 300mph. This creates an unrealistic experience.

**What improvements are players hoping for regarding vehicles in GTA 6?**
Players are hoping for more realistic scaling of vehicle performance, including top speeds, acceleration, and even pricing, to better align with their real-world counterparts. They also want consistency in performance between fictional and real-world inspired vehicles.

**Why do some players believe car speeds are limited in GTA Online?**
While not explicitly stated, the community understands there might be gameplay reasons for limiting car speeds, such as map size, server stability, or ensuring a balanced multiplayer experience.
