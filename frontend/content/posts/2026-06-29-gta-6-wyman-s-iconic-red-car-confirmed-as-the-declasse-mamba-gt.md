# GTA 6: Wyman's Iconic Red Car Confirmed as the Declasse Mamba GT

The distinctive red car driven by the character Wyman in Grand Theft Auto 6 has been officially identified as the **Declasse Mamba GT**. While its name first surfaced during the extensive **2022 GTA 6 leaks** within a world events list tied to Wyman, the vehicle has now received concrete visual confirmation. It was prominently showcased in **official GTA 6 Ultimate Edition screenshots**, specifically highlighted as part of a 'Wyman's Car Collection' side mission. This high-performance vehicle draws its inspiration from the legendary **Shelby Daytona Coupe**, a real-world racing car famously engineered to challenge and defeat Ferrari in the highly competitive FIA GT class, hinting at its prestige and speed within the game.
