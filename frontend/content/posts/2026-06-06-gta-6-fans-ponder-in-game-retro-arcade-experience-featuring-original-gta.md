# GTA 6: Fans Ponder In-Game Retro Arcade Experience, Featuring Original GTA

A recent Reddit discussion has ignited speculation among Grand Theft Auto fans regarding potential in-game entertainment for the highly anticipated GTA 6. Drawing inspiration from Minecraft's ability to host a playable version of Doom, user /u/parzival_thegreat proposed the intriguing idea of an arcade cabinet within GTA 6's Vice City that would allow players to experience the original top-down Grand Theft Auto. The concept was likened to 'Inception but with the GTA universe,' suggesting a layered gaming experience that could add a nostalgic and meta-narrative element to the new title.
