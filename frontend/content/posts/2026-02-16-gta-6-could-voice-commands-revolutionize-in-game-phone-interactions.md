# GTA 6: Could Voice Commands Revolutionize In-Game Phone Interactions?

A recent Reddit discussion among Grand Theft Auto fans has proposed an intriguing concept for GTA 6: integrating voice command technology into the in-game phone system. The idea suggests players could use natural language commands, akin to 'Hey Siri' or 'Alexa,' to execute common phone functions. For instance, instead of navigating menus, players might simply say 'Yo Angel, call me a taxi' or 'Boost this business' to instantly perform actions. The original poster questioned whether this innovative feature would be a welcome addition, streamlining gameplay, or a potentially problematic inclusion for the highly anticipated title.

## Key Takeaways
- Proposal for 'Hey Siri/Alexa' style voice commands in GTA 6's in-game phone.
- Aims to replace manual scrolling and clicking for phone actions.
- Examples include calling taxis, boosting businesses, or contacting specific characters like Lester.
- Discussion ponders whether this feature would be beneficial or detrimental to gameplay.
- Seeks to enhance immersion and convenience for players.
