# GTA 6 Fans Speculate on Ideal Outro Song

A recent discussion initiated by Reddit user u/wes3214 on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked community speculation regarding the ideal outro song for Grand Theft Auto 6. The original poster references Grand Theft Auto V's 'The Set Up' by Favored Nations (from choosing option C) as a prime example of a song that perfectly captures the vibe and concludes the story, prompting fans to share their own musical predictions for the upcoming title.
