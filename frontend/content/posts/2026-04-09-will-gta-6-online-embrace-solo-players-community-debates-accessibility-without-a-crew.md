# Will GTA 6 Online Embrace Solo Players? Community Debates Accessibility Without a Crew

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by user /u/gabrielripa has ignited a significant conversation within the Grand Theft Auto community regarding the solo playability of the upcoming GTA 6 Online. The user, who has limited experience with GTA 5 Online and notes a lack of friends available to play GTA 6 on PS5, expresses a strong desire to engage with the online component but questions if it will be possible to complete '100% solo.' This sentiment highlights a growing concern among players who prefer or are necessitated to play online games without a dedicated crew, prompting speculation on how Rockstar Games might design GTA 6 Online to be more accommodating to individual players compared to its predecessor.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user initiated a discussion about the possibility of playing GTA 6 Online entirely solo.
- The user's primary concern stems from not having friends who own a PS5 or plan to play GTA 6.
- Limited experience with GTA 5 Online leaves the user unsure about its solo play capabilities.
- The post reflects a broader community interest in GTA 6 Online's accessibility for players without a consistent social group.
- The debate questions whether Rockstar will design GTA 6 Online with more solo-friendly content and missions.

## FAQ
**Can GTA 5 Online be played entirely solo?**
While many missions and activities in GTA 5 Online are designed for multiple players or are significantly easier with a crew, a substantial amount of content, including some businesses, freemode events, and specific missions, can be undertaken solo, though it may be more challenging or time-consuming.

**Why are players concerned about solo play in GTA 6 Online?**
Players often seek solo options due to personal preference, a lack of friends who play, scheduling conflicts, or a desire to avoid the competitive and sometimes 'griefing' aspects of public lobbies. Ensuring a robust solo experience would broaden the game's appeal.

**Has Rockstar Games commented on solo play for GTA 6 Online?**
As of now, Rockstar Games has not released detailed information regarding the specific features or design philosophy for GTA 6 Online, including its solo play capabilities. Details are expected closer to the game's release.
