# GTA 6 Businesses: Players Hope for More Than Just Passive Income

The Grand Theft Auto 6 community is actively discussing the potential evolution of business mechanics in the highly anticipated title. A recent Reddit post highlights a common sentiment among players: a desire for businesses to offer a more engaging and in-depth experience than the largely passive income generation found in Grand Theft Auto Online. While players appreciate the concept of running criminal enterprises, many feel the current model often boils down to repetitive setup missions and waiting for a timer, detracting from the immersion. The ideal scenario, as articulated by the community, is a balance where businesses feel like genuine criminal operations without becoming overly realistic or a cumbersome management simulator that feels like a second job.

## Key Takeaways
- Players are eager for more depth in GTA 6's business mechanics.
- Current GTA Online businesses are often criticized for being repetitive passive income sources.
- The community desires businesses to feel more like active criminal operations rather than simple timers.
- There's a clear preference to avoid businesses becoming overly complex or a 'management simulator'.
- The goal is a balanced system that offers engagement without being tedious or overly time-consuming.

## FAQ
**What is the main concern regarding businesses in GTA 6?**
The main concern is that businesses might solely function as passive income generators, similar to GTA Online, rather than offering engaging, in-depth gameplay that truly simulates running a criminal enterprise. Players want more interaction without the mechanics becoming overly complex or tedious.
