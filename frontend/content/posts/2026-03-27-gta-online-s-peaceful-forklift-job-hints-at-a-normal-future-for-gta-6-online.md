# GTA Online's Peaceful Forklift Job Hints at a 'Normal' Future for GTA 6 Online

A dedicated GTA Online player recently observed a surprising new 'Forklift Operator' job, noting its peaceful and low-risk nature – a stark contrast to the typical chaotic missions. This unique experience led the player to speculate on a significant shift for 'GTA 6 Online.' Drawing parallels to games like Shenmue and acknowledging Rockstar's acquisition of FiveM developers (known for GTA RP), the author suggests that 'GTA 6 Online' might introduce more 'regular, legal jobs.' This could signal a move away from the 'everyone's exploding' lobbies dominated by vehicles like the Oppressor, towards a more 'normal, simulation' style of gameplay, akin to the calmer Red Dead Online lobbies, albeit with a touch of the series' signature craziness. The author expressed enjoyment for these low-risk, decent-paying jobs and hopes for future additions like co-worker interactions and career progression within such roles.
