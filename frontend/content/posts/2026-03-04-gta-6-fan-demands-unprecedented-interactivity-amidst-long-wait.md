# GTA 6 Fan Demands Unprecedented Interactivity Amidst Long Wait

A recent Reddit post from user u/kvibes7 on the r/GTA6 subreddit encapsulates the soaring expectations of the Grand Theft Auto community for the upcoming installment. Citing the lengthy development cycle, the user emphatically demands a level of environmental interactivity unprecedented in the series, specifically wishing for the ability to enter 'every store in the game' and even 'a random person's house,' along with a general plea for the freedom to 'do everything.' This sentiment highlights a growing desire among fans for a truly immersive and open world that goes beyond previous iterations in terms of player agency and exploration, reflecting the immense anticipation built up over the years.

## Key Takeaways
