# GTA 6 Development Hit by Major Snag? Rumors Point to Broken Save/Load Feature

Unverified rumors originating from UK gossip site PopBitch, and circulating from pubs in Dundee (the birthplace of the original GTA), suggest that the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI is facing significant development delays due to a critical and "completely broken" in-game feature. The game, reportedly eight years in development with costs nearing $3 billion, is rumored to have been built without considering this essential functionality, making its late implementation an extremely complex task requiring specialized expertise. Compounding the issue, the individual previously overseeing this feature was allegedly among those laid off by Rockstar following unionization attempts in late 2025. The most shocking claim is that the missing feature preventing the game's release is the fundamental ability to save and load progress. Readers are advised to take these claims with a substantial "pinch of salt" due to their unverified nature.
