# Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick Reconfirms GTA 6 November 19th Launch Date

Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick recently reconfirmed the highly anticipated launch date for Grand Theft Auto 6 during an impromptu street interview. Speaking with TikToker 'The School of Hard Knocks,' Zelnick reiterated that GTA 6 is indeed scheduled to release on November 19th. The Take-Two chief also took the opportunity to share some business advice during the discussion.
