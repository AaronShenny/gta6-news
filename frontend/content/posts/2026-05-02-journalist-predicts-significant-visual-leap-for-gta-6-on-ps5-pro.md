# Journalist Predicts Significant Visual Leap for GTA 6 on PS5 Pro

Contrary to some community skepticism, a gaming journalist believes that Grand Theft Auto 6 running on the PlayStation 5 Pro will deliver a distinctly superior visual experience compared to the standard PS5. The journalist highlights Rockstar Games' extensive development resources and the likelihood of them having significant lead time with PS5 Pro development kits, allowing for deep optimization. With the game's fidelity mode likely targeting 30 frames per second, the PS5 Pro's enhanced GPU power can be fully leveraged for graphical fidelity without being bottlenecked by the CPU, potentially leading to higher internal resolutions, improved image quality via PSSR, and substantial advancements in ray tracing and reflections.

## Key Takeaways
- Rockstar Games likely had at least two years with PS5 Pro devkits for extensive optimization.
- Rockstar's vast resources enable them to maximize the console's potential.
- Targeting 30 FPS in fidelity mode on the PS5 Pro means extra GPU power can be dedicated to visual enhancements, avoiding CPU bottlenecks.
- Expectations include slightly higher internal resolution or improved image quality through PSSR (PlayStation Spectral Super Resolution).
- Anticipated noticeable improvements in ray tracing and reflections.
