# PlayStation Store's Unusual Silence Fuels GTA 6 Trailer 3 & Pre-Order Speculation

A recent Reddit post has ignited fervent speculation within the GTA 6 community, highlighting an unusual and unprecedented lack of deals on the PlayStation Store's 'deals' tab. The author notes that sales are conspicuously absent, with a message indicating their resumption on the 13th of the month. This timing has drawn attention due to intense rumors suggesting a potential reveal for GTA 6's third trailer and pre-order launch on the 12th – a Tuesday, a day historically favored by Rockstar Games for major announcements. The speculation further ties into an upcoming Take-Two Interactive earnings call, where strong GTA 6 pre-order figures could significantly bolster investor confidence. The post posits that Sony's decision to halt sales, a move that would typically result in lost revenue, could be a strategic 'clearing of the runway' for an exceptionally massive title like Grand Theft Auto VI, where initial full-price sales would easily offset any temporary losses.

## Key Takeaways
- The PlayStation Store currently displays an unprecedented absence of deals on its 'deals' tab.
- PS Store sales are scheduled to resume on the 13th of the month.
- This aligns with speculation of a GTA 6 Trailer 3 reveal and pre-order launch on the 12th, which is a Tuesday – a common day for Rockstar announcements.
- An upcoming Take-Two Interactive earnings call could be positively impacted by strong GTA 6 pre-order numbers.
- Sony's unusual move to halt sales, potentially losing revenue, suggests they might be preparing for a massive game launch to offset losses.
