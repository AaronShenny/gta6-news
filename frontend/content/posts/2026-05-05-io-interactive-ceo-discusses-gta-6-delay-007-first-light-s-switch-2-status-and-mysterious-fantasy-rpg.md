# IO Interactive CEO Discusses GTA 6 Delay, 007 First Light's Switch 2 Status, and Mysterious Fantasy RPG

In a recent interview, IO Interactive CEO Hakan Abrak addressed several key topics concerning the studio's projects. He acknowledged that the delay of GTA 6 to November 2026 provides 'breathing room' for their upcoming title, 007 First Light, which launches May 27 on other platforms. Abrak also confirmed that First Light's Switch 2 version is still in development, albeit delayed to late summer to ensure quality, dispelling fears of cancellation. Furthermore, he provided a positive update on their mysterious 'Project Fantasy' RPG, stating it's 'very far' into development and 'healthy,' despite recent upheavals at its rumored backer, Xbox. Abrak also reaffirmed IO Interactive's commitment to future Hitman titles, promising continued innovation for the franchise.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's delay to November 2026 gives IO Interactive's 007 First Light 'breathing room' for its May 27 launch.
- The Nintendo Switch 2 version of 007 First Light is delayed to 'late summer' but is confirmed not to be canceled, with IO Interactive aiming for a high-quality release.
- IO Interactive's 'Project Fantasy,' a 'bold new online fantasy RPG,' is 'very far' into development, 'healthy,' and 'still financed,' despite recent Xbox cancellations.
- IO Interactive remains committed to developing new Hitman games in the future, promising to 'upgrade, invent and innovate' with the series.
- Early previews suggest 007 First Light could be a 2026 Game of the Year contender and the best Bond game of all time.

## FAQ
**How does the GTA 6 delay affect IO Interactive?**
IO Interactive CEO Hakan Abrak stated the delay of GTA 6 from May to November 2026 provides 'a bit more breathing room' for their game, 007 First Light, which launches on May 27.

**Is the Switch 2 version of 007 First Light canceled?**
No, IO Interactive CEO Hakan Abrak confirmed the Switch 2 version is still in development and 'up and running,' though it is delayed to 'late summer' to ensure the highest quality.

**What is the status of IO Interactive's fantasy game, Project Fantasy?**
Project Fantasy is 'very far' into development, 'in a very healthy place,' 'still financed,' and 'amazing,' according to Abrak, despite recent industry uncertainties at its rumored backer.
