# GTA 6 Fan Map Reaches V11, Incorporating Latest Leaks and Trailers

The highly anticipated world of Grand Theft Auto 6 continues to take shape through the diligent efforts of its dedicated community. The 'Yanis GTA 6 community map' has just seen its V11 update released, a significant step forward in fan-driven geographical reconstruction. This latest iteration meticulously incorporates new evidence gleaned from recent leaks, official screenshots, and game trailers, providing fans with an even more refined and detailed visualization of what could be Vice City and its surrounding areas. The update, originally shared by /u/Saukko505, showcases the community's ongoing commitment to piecing together the upcoming game's expansive environment.

- The 'Yanis GTA 6 community map' has been updated to Version 11.
