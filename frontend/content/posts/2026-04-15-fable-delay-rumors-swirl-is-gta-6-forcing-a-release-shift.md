# Fable Delay Rumors Swirl: Is GTA 6 Forcing a Release Shift?

Speculation is mounting that Playground Games' Fable reboot, originally slated for Fall 2026, might face an internal delay. This comes after the official Fable X (formerly Twitter) account stopped mentioning the 2026 release date in its posts after April 13, following a report from journalist Jeff Grubb. Grubb suggested a delay could be aimed at avoiding the overwhelming launch impact of GTA 6, which is reportedly set for November 19. While there's no official confirmation from Microsoft, the social media shift has fueled the rumors. Many industry figures, including the CEO of Arrowhead and veteran Glen Schofield, have voiced concerns about launching games too close to GTA 6, anticipating it will 'blot out the sun.' However, some, like Ubisoft CEO Yves Guillemot, believe GTA 6 could be beneficial for the overall gaming market by attracting more players. Fable, which marks Playground's first non-Forza Horizon title and will also launch on PS5, remains officially on track for 2026, with its pinned X post still reflecting that date.

## Key Takeaways
- Rumors suggest the Fable reboot may be internally delayed beyond its Fall 2026 release window.
- The official Fable X account stopped mentioning 'Autumn 2026' in its posts after April 13, fueling delay speculation.
- Journalist Jeff Grubb previously reported an internal delay, citing a desire to avoid the launch 'blast radius' of GTA 6.
- Many industry leaders express concern about launching games near GTA 6 due to its anticipated market dominance.
- Some executives, like Ubisoft's CEO, believe GTA 6 could positively impact the gaming market by drawing in more players.
- Microsoft has not officially confirmed any delay for Fable, and the game's pinned X post still shows a Fall 2026 date.
- Fable is Playground Games' first non-Forza Horizon title and is confirmed for Xbox, PC, and PS5.
