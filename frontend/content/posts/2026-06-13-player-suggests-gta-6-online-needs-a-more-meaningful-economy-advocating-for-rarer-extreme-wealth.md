# Player Suggests GTA 6 Online Needs a More Meaningful Economy, Advocating for Rarer Extreme Wealth

A passionate post from a GTA fan has sparked discussion regarding the potential economy of GTA 6 Online. The user, /u/deeburns, highlights how GTA Online's progression often leads to nearly every player accumulating hundreds of millions, or even billions, in in-game currency. This abundance, they argue, diminishes the significance of wealth. The suggestion for GTA 6 Online is to introduce a more balanced pricing structure and slower progression, making extreme wealth a rare and genuine accomplishment. The core idea is that money and financial choices should truly matter, reflecting real-world economic rarity and enhancing the overall sense of achievement and interest in the game's economy.

## Key Takeaways
- Many GTA Online players accumulate excessive wealth (hundreds of millions to billions), making money feel inconsequential.
- The current pricing model in GTA Online, with items costing millions, contributes to the feeling of inflated wealth.
- The author advocates for GTA 6 Online to make extreme wealth much rarer and more meaningful.
- A call for more balanced pricing and slower progression to ensure financial decisions have greater impact.
- Reaching millionaire or billionaire status in GTA 6 should feel like a major accomplishment, not a common outcome.
- A more challenging and significant economy would enhance player progression and overall game interest.
