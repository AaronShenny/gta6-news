# GTA 6 and Red dead Redemption 2 are in the same universe

A dedicated gaming journalist delves into the intricate web of connections between Rockstar Games' Grand Theft Auto 6, Red Dead Redemption 2, and GTA Online, presenting a strong case for their shared universe. The article meticulously compiles evidence, distinguishing between the separate 2D, 3D, and HD eras of GTA games, and focusing on official media from GTA 6 trailers, RDR2 lore, and recent GTA Online updates. Key connections include the recurring 'Duke Fire arms' brand, identical U.S. Presidents, character mugshots, and the enigmatic time-traveler Francis Sinclair, who aligns with the Epsilon Program's deity, Kraff. Furthermore, GTA Online provides numerous direct links, such as paintings depicting RDR2 locations and characters, and references to historical figures. The author addresses common contradictions, particularly regarding the coexistence of fictional cities like Liberty City with real-world counterparts like New York, arguing that both exist within the expansive Rockstar universe as distinct entities.
