# GTA 6: Could RDR2's Immersive NPC Interaction System Make a Return?

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user u/Crad2, speculates whether Grand Theft Auto 6 will incorporate or evolve the highly immersive 'Greet/Antagonize' NPC interaction system popularized in Red Dead Redemption 2. The system, lauded for allowing conversations with nearly every non-player character, significantly boosted RDR2's world immersion. The community is pondering if Rockstar will bring this level of dynamic social interaction to the bustling streets of Vice City, and if so, how it might build upon the existing framework.
