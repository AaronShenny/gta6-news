# GTA VI Launch Nears: Release Date Now Closer Than Second Trailer's Debut

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has brought a fresh wave of excitement to the Grand Theft Auto VI community, highlighting a notable countdown milestone. User u/nzorigolo pointed out that the highly anticipated game's release date is now closer than the date its second official trailer was released. With approximately 287 days having passed since Trailer 2 dropped, and roughly 275 days remaining until launch, this observation underscores the rapidly approaching release of GTA VI, intensifying the already high anticipation among fans.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA VI's release is approximately 275 days away.
- Approximately 287 days have passed since the release of GTA VI's second trailer.
- The time remaining until launch is now less than the time since Trailer 2's debut.
- This milestone is fueling increased excitement among the GTA VI fanbase.
