# GTA 6 Shatters Records on PlayStation Store, Ultimate Edition Driving Massive Sales

Grand Theft Auto 6 has officially become the top-selling game on the PlayStation Store, surpassing established titles like NBA, Modern Warfare 4, Wolverine, and FC. A particularly striking detail is that the vast majority of these sales are attributed to the Ultimate Edition, priced at a premium $100. This phenomenal performance highlights Rockstar Games' remarkable ability to not only generate immense anticipation but also successfully market and sell a high-priced version of their latest blockbuster to a widespread audience.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 is the current top-selling game on the PlayStation Store.
- It has surpassed major titles including NBA, MW4, Wolverine, and FC.
- The Ultimate Edition, priced at $100, accounts for the vast majority of sales.
- Rockstar Games has successfully sold a premium-priced game to a mass market.
