# Former Rockstar Dev's GTA IV Delay Insights Offer Hope for GTA 6's Schedule

Vermeij previously disclosed that the decision to delay GTA IV was made approximately four months prior to its planned release, noting that assessing the necessity of a delay becomes significantly more challenging beyond that specific timeframe. The article highlights that for a project of GTA 6's unprecedented scale and complexity, being within a six-month window of a potential release without any public indication of a delay is a remarkably positive sign, hinting that development is progressing smoothly and potentially on schedule.
