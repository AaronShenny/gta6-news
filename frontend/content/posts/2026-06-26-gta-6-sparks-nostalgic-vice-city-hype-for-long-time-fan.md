# GTA 6 Sparks Nostalgic Vice City Hype for Long-Time Fan

A Reddit user, u/stupod_101, expresses overwhelming excitement for GTA 6, admitting they were previously a 'doubter' of Rockstar Games. The catalyst for this newfound hype is an unspecified image from the upcoming game that instantly transported them back to their childhood. Vice City, their very first GTA game, holds profound core memories, particularly centered around a specific car. They recount playing on a Core i3 laptop as a 10 or 11-year-old, using arrow keys and lock-on aim, and their favorite activity was simply driving this car into the ocean with 'sea ways' and 'come fly with me' cheats enabled, all while Billie Jean played on the radio. This nostalgic experience, focused on 'fun for the sake of fun,' is what the user hopes GTA 6 will reignite, with a strong plea for the return of classic cheats.

## Key Takeaways
- User (u/stupod_101) was initially skeptical of Rockstar but is now extremely hyped for GTA 6.
- An image (not provided in the article) from GTA 6 triggered intense nostalgia and excitement.
- Vice City was the user's first GTA game and holds significant childhood memories.
- A particular car from Vice City is deeply ingrained in their psyche.
- Childhood gameplay involved using cheats ('sea ways', 'come fly with me') to drive this car into the ocean while listening to Billie Jean.
- The user hopes GTA 6 will recapture the simple joy of playing 'for the sake of fun'.
- There is a strong desire for cheats to return in GTA 6.
