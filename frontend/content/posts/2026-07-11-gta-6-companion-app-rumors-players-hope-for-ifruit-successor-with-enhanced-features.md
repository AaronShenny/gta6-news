# GTA 6 Companion App Rumors: Players Hope for iFruit Successor with Enhanced Features

The gaming community is buzzing with speculation regarding a potential companion application for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. A recent Reddit post by u/Skulltrooper674 on the r/GTA6 subreddit ignited discussions, pondering whether Rockstar Games will introduce an app similar to the iFruit app from GTA 5's era.

The original post highlights unconfirmed rumors suggesting Rockstar is actively hiring mobile app developers, fueling the belief that a new companion app might be in the works. The sentiment among players, as reflected in the post, is a strong desire for an app that is significantly more polished and less prone to crashes compared to its predecessor.

Community suggestions for potential features in a new GTA 6 app include the ability to train pets for the rumored protagonists, Jason or Lucia, or an enhanced 'Los Santos Customs' type application, allowing for deeper vehicle customization on the go. While these remain speculative, they indicate a clear appetite for integrated mobile functionality that genuinely enhances the gameplay experience.

## Key Takeaways
- Community speculation points to the potential return of a companion app for GTA 6.
- Rumors suggest Rockstar Games is hiring mobile app developers, possibly for such an app.
- Players express a strong desire for a more stable and polished app than GTA 5's iFruit.
- Feature suggestions include pet training for protagonists (Jason/Lucia) and advanced vehicle customization ('Los Santos Customs' type app).

## FAQ
**Will GTA 6 have a companion app?**
Currently, it's a community rumor and speculation, largely fueled by unconfirmed reports of Rockstar Games hiring mobile app developers. There has been no official confirmation from Rockstar.

**What features are players hoping for in a potential GTA 6 app?**
Players are hoping for a more polished and stable app than the iFruit app. Specific suggestions include pet training capabilities for protagonists Jason or Lucia, and an improved 'Los Santos Customs' style app for vehicle customization.

**What was the iFruit app for GTA 5?**
The iFruit app was a companion application for Grand Theft Auto V that allowed players to customize vehicles, train Chop the dog, and access other in-game features remotely from their mobile devices.
