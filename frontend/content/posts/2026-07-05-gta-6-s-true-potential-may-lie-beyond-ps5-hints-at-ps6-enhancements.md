# GTA 6's True Potential May Lie Beyond PS5, Hints at PS6 Enhancements

A recent discussion among fans suggests that *Grand Theft Auto 6* might not reach its ultimate potential on the PlayStation 5, but rather on the subsequent console generation, potentially the PlayStation 6. This speculation is rooted in Rockstar Games' well-documented pattern of releasing titles late in a console's lifecycle and then significantly enhancing them for newer hardware.The article highlights *Grand Theft Auto V* as a prime example, having spanned three PlayStation generations with successive improvements. Similarly, *Red Dead Redemption 2*, despite its graphical prowess, is perceived by some as still awaiting more powerful hardware to truly shine.While the PS5 version of *GTA 6* is expected to be visually stunning, the argument posits that a future PS6 release could unlock a 'definitive experience'. This would likely include substantial upgrades such as higher frame rates, more advanced ray tracing, a denser population of NPCs, extended draw distances, dramatically faster loading times, and even gameplay mechanics that current hardware simply cannot fully support. Though past performance doesn't guarantee future actions, Rockstar's consistent approach to maximizing new console generations lends credence to this theory.
