# I think that GTA 6 marketing will look like nothing we've ever seen before

A passionate Grand Theft Auto fan and Reddit user, /u/im_strak, has shared their compelling vision for Rockstar Games' Grand Theft Auto VI marketing strategy, suggesting it will be unlike anything the video game industry has witnessed before. The user argues that Rockstar operates outside the norms of typical game studios, which explains their unconventional approach. Citing previous statements from Strauss Zelnick about less TV advertising, the theory posits a significant shift towards social media engagement, real-life activations (like a potential World Cup tie-in), and unique community participation. The author speculates that upcoming marketing efforts this summer will dwarf previous hype, potentially involving major content creators—such as French YouTuber Squeezie, who reportedly filmed a GTA 6-related video in Miami—and directly engaging players in innovative ways beyond traditional trailer drops.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 marketing is expected to be unprecedented and unconventional compared to previous titles and industry norms.

- The third trailer might be part of a larger, non-traditional marketing event rather than a standalone release.
