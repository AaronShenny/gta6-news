# GTA 6 vs. Modded GTA 5: A Visual Showdown

A recent Reddit post initiated a fascinating discussion by comparing the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6's visuals against a modded Grand Theft Auto 5, specifically using the 'Natural Vision Enhanced' mod. While acknowledging GTA 6's inherent superiority over a default GTA 5, the user suggests that a heavily modded GTA 5 can achieve comparable visual fidelity in specific areas such as shaders, lighting, and volumetric clouds. However, the post concludes that GTA 6 ultimately triumphs in crucial aspects like textures, models, materials, human character rendering, overall photorealism, and other core technical features, cementing its position as a significant graphical leap.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 is considered 'far better' than GTA 5 in its default state.
- Heavily modded GTA 5, particularly with Natural Vision Enhanced, can approach GTA 6's visual quality in shaders, lighting, and volumetric clouds.
- GTA 6 holds a distinct advantage in textures, models, materials, human character detail, photorealism, and other technical features.
- The comparison acknowledges GTA 6's overall graphical superiority despite modded GTA 5's impressive capabilities.
