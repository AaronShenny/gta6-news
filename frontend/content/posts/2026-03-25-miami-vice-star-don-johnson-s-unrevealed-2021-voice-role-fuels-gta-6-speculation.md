# Miami Vice Star Don Johnson's Unrevealed 2021 Voice Role Fuels GTA 6 Speculation

A Reddit post has ignited fresh speculation among Grand Theft Auto fans, suggesting that actor Don Johnson, iconic for his role as Sonny Crockett in Miami Vice, may be involved in GTA VI. Johnson reportedly recorded voice lines for an undisclosed video game in 2021, a project that remains unrevealed. The theory gains traction due to several compelling connections: Rockstar founders Sam and Dan Houser are known Miami Vice enthusiasts; Philip Michael Thomas, Johnson's co-star in Miami Vice, previously voiced Lance Vance in GTA Vice City; and Johnson's hit song 'Heartbeat' was featured in GTA V. While acknowledging the highly speculative nature, the unannounced status of the game and the plausible 2021 recording timeline for a potential 2025 release add fuel to the fire. Fans ponder whether Johnson could portray a side character or a radio host, drawing parallels to previous GTA installments. This unconfirmed rumor, while needing to be taken 'with a grain of salt,' offers an exciting new angle for discussion amidst the ongoing anticipation for Grand Theft Auto VI.
