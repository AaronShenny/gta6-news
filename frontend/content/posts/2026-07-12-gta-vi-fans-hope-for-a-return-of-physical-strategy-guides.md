# GTA VI Fans Hope for a Return of Physical Strategy Guides

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit highlights a nostalgic sentiment among fans regarding the potential release of a physical strategy guide for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto VI. User 'kmart25888' shared an image of a past GTA strategy guide, expressing their love for collecting and using them to enhance their gameplay experience. The user lamented the closure of BradyGames, a prominent publisher of such guides, and questioned if any other company currently produces official strategy guides for major game releases. This post taps into a broader desire within the gaming community for tangible collectibles and comprehensive resources to accompany highly anticipated titles like GTA VI.
