# GTA 6: The Call for Mapless Navigation and Enhanced Immersion

A thought-provoking discussion has emerged from the r/GTA6 subreddit, initiated by user u/No_Edge_341, questioning the viability and desirability of playing Grand Theft Auto titles without an interactive map or GPS. While acknowledging that most games in the franchise offer options to disable navigation aids, the user posits that the games might not be 'intended' for such a playstyle, citing the lack of in-game directional cues like passenger instructions or blinker indications. The post invites the community to share experiences of playing GTA V or Red Dead Redemption 2 without the map, inquiring whether it enhances immersion and success. The user expresses a keen interest in potential design choices for GTA VI that could facilitate a more organic, mapless navigation, drawing parallels to the minimal hand-holding in Red Dead Redemption 2's treasure hunts and the memorability of GTA III's map due to its lack of route suggestions. This highlights a desire for features that keep players' eyes on the road rather than the screen's corner, fostering a deeper connection with the game world.
