# Take-Two Confirms No New GTA 6 Information Until Summer, Details Trailer Schedule

The company explicitly stated, "We will share more information during the summer," while referencing the release dates of previous trailers: Trailer 1 in December 2023 and Trailer 2 in May 2025. Despite this clear communication, the article highlights an ongoing trend of fan speculation, with some still expecting earlier announcements, even as soon as 'Tuesday,' despite the official reconfirmation.
