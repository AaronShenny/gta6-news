# GTA VI: Will Rockstar Incorporate RDR2-Style Character Visions?

A recent discussion among fans suggests that Grand Theft Auto VI could benefit from incorporating 'visions' similar to those experienced by Arthur Morgan in Red Dead Redemption 2. These RDR2 visions, symbolized by the deer and coyote, were lauded for their profound emotional impact and ability to reflect Arthur's moral standing and internal struggles. The community is pondering if Rockstar Games will implement a similar narrative device in GTA VI for its dual protagonists, Jason and Lucia. The argument posits that such visions could serve to humanize the characters beyond their criminal activities, allowing players to connect with their deeper emotional and psychological states, thereby enriching the storytelling and player immersion. This would mark a significant evolution in character development for the GTA series, moving beyond traditional crime narratives to explore more nuanced character arcs.
