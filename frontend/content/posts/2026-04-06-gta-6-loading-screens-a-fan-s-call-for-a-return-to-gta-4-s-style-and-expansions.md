# GTA 6 Loading Screens: A Fan's Call for a Return to GTA 4's Style and Expansions

In a recent Reddit post, user /u/rodawg88 shared their personal take on the evolution of Grand Theft Auto loading screens, specifically contrasting their experiences with GTA 4 and GTA 5. The user described GTA 5's loading screens as 'long and somewhat mundane,' despite acknowledging the soothing music. Their strong preference lies with the style of GTA 4's loading screens, and they expressed a fervent hope that GTA 6 will revert to this aesthetic. Beyond just visuals, the user also wishes for the return of substantial story expansions, reminiscent of those offered with GTA 4, indicating a desire for a more robust post-launch content strategy from Rockstar Games.
