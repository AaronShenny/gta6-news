# GTA 6 Shatters Price Barrier: Premium Edition Dominates Early Pre-Orders

Early indications suggest that *Grand Theft Auto 6* is on track to become a monumental launch, potentially redefining the relationship between game pricing and consumer purchasing habits. While official pre-order data remains unconfirmed, industry analysts are reporting exceptionally strong demand, with a surprising trend towards the higher-priced **$99.99 Ultimate Edition** over the standard **$79.99 version**.

French retailer Cdiscount reportedly experienced a staggering **six times more GTA 6 pre-orders** compared to a typical major franchise release. Further reinforcing this trend, BTIG analyst Clark Lampen, cited by Investor's Business Daily, estimates initial pre-orders could reach **15 to 22 million units**, with a significant portion opting for the premium offering.

This data sparks a crucial discussion: despite vocal gamer complaints about increasing game prices, GTA 6's immense hype appears to be driving unprecedented sales, even for its most expensive editions. It raises the question of whether the game will prove that strong enough demand and anticipation can overcome price sensitivity.
