# GTA 6 Community Ponders Desired Unrealistic Features Amidst Realism Expectations

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked considerable interest, as fans were prompted to share one 'unrealistic' feature they would want to see in Grand Theft Auto VI. The thread, initiated by user /u/Ok_Net_4196, invites the community to diverge from the game's expected realistic approach and imagine fantastical or over-the-top elements that could add unique and unexpected twists to the highly anticipated title, fostering creative and often humorous suggestions from the player base.

## Key Takeaways
- The r/GTA6 community initiated a discussion focusing on desired unrealistic features for Grand Theft Auto VI.
- Fans are encouraged to propose non-realistic elements, contrasting with the game's expected highly realistic design.
- The prompt aims to gather imaginative and creative ideas from the player community for the upcoming GTA title.
