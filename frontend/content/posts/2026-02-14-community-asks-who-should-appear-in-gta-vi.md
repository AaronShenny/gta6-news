# Community Asks: Who Should Appear in GTA VI?

A Reddit post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited a lively community discussion, inviting users to brainstorm celebrity appearances for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI. Titled "Who do you want to make an appearance in GTA VI?", the original poster references a rich history of celebrity involvement in past GTA titles, citing examples such as actors Samuel L. Jackson and Ray Liotta, comedians Ricky Gervais and Bill Burr, musicians Kenny Loggins, and models like Cara Delevingne. The discussion encourages participants to think beyond mainstream celebrities, suggesting niche TV actors, internet personalities, streamers, YouTubers, and TikTokers as potential additions. Key prompts guide the conversation, asking users to consider who they'd like to see as radio hosts, in-game characters (either playing themselves or new roles), and which returning voice actors they'd like to bring back.

## FAQ
**Who do fans want to feature as a radio host in GTA VI?**
The community is encouraged to suggest various personalities, from musicians to internet celebrities, to host radio stations in GTA VI.

**Which celebrities do players want to appear as characters in GTA VI?**
Users are asked to consider actors and other public figures who could portray new or existing characters in the game, rather than just appearing as themselves.

**Who do players want to feature as themselves in GTA VI?**
The discussion invites suggestions for real-world personalities, including comedians, internet celebrities, and other public figures, to make cameos as themselves within the game world.

**Are fans interested in returning actors for GTA VI?**
Yes, the community is keen to discuss which voice actors from previous GTA titles they would like to see return, either reprising their original roles or taking on new characters.
