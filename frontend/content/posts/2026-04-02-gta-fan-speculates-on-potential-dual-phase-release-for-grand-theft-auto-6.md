# GTA Fan Speculates on Potential Dual-Phase Release for Grand Theft Auto 6

A passionate fan on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited discussion by speculating on Rockstar Games' potential release strategy for the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. Drawing parallels to the staggered launch of Grand Theft Auto V, which saw an initial release on Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3 followed by significantly enhanced versions for PC, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One, the user proposes that GTA 6 might follow a similar two-phase approach. They suggest an initial "downgraded" version could cater to current-generation consoles (PS5/Xbox Series X|S), with a subsequent "real version" arriving later for PC and future "next-gen" platforms, boasting substantial improvements like native 4K resolution and a dedicated first-person mode. The user expresses immense excitement for what Rockstar might deliver with this potential strategy.
