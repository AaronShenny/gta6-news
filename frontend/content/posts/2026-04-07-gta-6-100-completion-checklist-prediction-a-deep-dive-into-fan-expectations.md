# GTA 6 100% Completion Checklist Prediction: A Deep Dive into Fan Expectations

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto fan has meticulously outlined their extensive predictions for what a 100% completion checklist might entail in the highly anticipated GTA 6. Shared on Reddit by u/Holiday-Fix2882, the speculative list allocates percentages across various gameplay categories, suggesting a monumental grind for completionists. The breakdown includes a significant portion for main story missions, an expanded 'Strangers and Freaks' section with an estimated 60 missions, and an incredibly diverse 'Hobbies and Pastimes' category featuring 66 distinct activities from sports and racing to gambling and hunting. The prediction also covers random events, a wide array of collectibles, open-world challenges, and property management, painting a picture of a truly massive and immersive open world experience.

## Key Takeaways
- Main Story Missions are predicted to account for 55% of 100% completion.
- An expanded 'Strangers and Freaks' category is expected, with an estimated 60 missions, significantly more than GTA 5.
- 'Hobbies and Pastimes' could be a major component (15%), encompassing approximately 66 activities like various sports (tennis, basketball, MMA, golf, pool, darts, bowling), fishing, hunting, all types of races (street, off-road, sea), shooting range challenges, kayaking, scuba diving, casino gambling, amusement park visits, and more.
- Random Events are projected to contribute 5%, requiring completion of 20 out of a potential 100.
- Collectibles and Miscellaneous tasks are allotted 5%, including finding packages, underwater items, hidden cash briefcases, spaceship parts, letter scraps, discovering special locations, weapon modding, character hangouts, pet interaction, safehouse acquisition, stock market investment, robbing stores, and receiving prostitute services.
- Open World and Vehicle Challenges make up 3%, featuring stunt jumps (50/100), vehicle modding, knife flights (10/20), under the bridge challenges (25/50), vehicle/garage purchases, and epic journeys like kayaking from Vice City to Leonida Keys or driving across the entire map.
- Properties and Businesses are expected to contribute 2%, requiring players to buy all properties and complete associated management missions.
- The fan's prediction emphasizes a 'grind' for completion, suggesting a game with immense depth and replayability for dedicated players.
