# A Visual Retrospective: The Evolution of Women on Grand Theft Auto Cover Art

A recent Reddit post by user /u/Revolutionary_Ad9468 sparked discussion by compiling a visual history of Grand Theft Auto cover art, specifically focusing on the inclusion of female characters. The retrospective showcases covers from iconic titles such as GTA 3, Vice City, Liberty City Stories, San Andreas, Grand Theft Auto IV, Grand Theft Auto V, and even an anticipated look at Grand Theft Auto VI. The post notably points out that the franchise's earliest installments, GTA 1 and GTA 2, did not feature women on their box art, marking a stylistic evolution in the series' visual branding.

## Key Takeaways
- Grand Theft Auto 1 and 2 did not feature female characters on their cover art.
- Female characters have been a recurring element on GTA game covers since GTA 3.
- The compilation highlights covers from GTA 3, Vice City, Liberty City Stories, San Andreas, GTA IV, GTA V, and GTA VI.
- The content originates from a Reddit post titled 'Gta cover art girls over the years'.
