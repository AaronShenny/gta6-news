# Will GTA 6 Feature Mission Checkpoints? Community Debates Franchise History

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited speculation among fans regarding the potential inclusion of mission checkpoints in the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. The original post by 'Independent-Sir-721' directly asked the community if they believe GTA 6 will adopt this feature. The discussion highlighted a significant shift in mission design throughout the series' history. Grand Theft Auto V and Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas are noted for their implementation of checkpoints, allowing players to restart missions from specific points after failing. This quality-of-life improvement stands in contrast to earlier titles, where the original releases of Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, Grand Theft Auto III, and Grand Theft Auto IV did not feature checkpoints, forcing players to restart missions from the very beginning upon death. This historical context fuels the debate on whether Rockstar Games will continue with the modern checkpoint system or revert to a more challenging, old-school approach for GTA 6.

## Key Takeaways
- The GTA 6 community is actively debating whether the game will include mission checkpoints.
- Grand Theft Auto V and Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas featured mission checkpoints.
- Original releases of Grand Theft Auto III, Vice City, and Grand Theft Auto IV did not have checkpoints, requiring full mission restarts upon failure.
- Players are curious about Rockstar's approach to mission design and difficulty in the upcoming title.
