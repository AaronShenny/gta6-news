# GTA 6 Online: Will It Be a Separate Game with Its Own Trophies?

A recent Reddit discussion on r/GTA6, initiated by user /u/SacredCharizard09, has sparked debate regarding the potential structure of Grand Theft Auto 6's online component. The post speculates that GTA 6 Online might be released as a completely separate game from the main single-player experience, a significant departure from Rockstar's previous approach with GTA 5 and Red Dead Redemption 2, where online modes were integrated. The user suggests that GTA 6 Online could launch as late as next year, potentially carrying its own price tag and featuring a distinct trophy list, implying a standalone product rather than an add-on.
