# GTA 6 Trailers: Fans Hype the Immersion-Boosting Small Details Over Big Moments

While major highlights like Vice City, protagonists Jason and Lucia, and the overall atmosphere are undoubtedly exciting, a recent discussion highlights that it's the subtle, background details in the Grand Theft Auto VI trailers that are truly captivating fans. Many feel the world of Leonida looks significantly more alive, denser, and believable compared to GTA V, with even the smallest moments suggesting unprecedented depth. Specific elements like improved animal behavior, dynamic NPC interactions, vibrant street life, and fluid animations are cited as key contributors to an anticipated immersive experience, making the world feel incredibly rich and full of personality.

## Key Takeaways
- Excitement for GTA VI trailers is driven significantly by small, background details, not just cinematic highlights.
- The world of Leonida is perceived as 'more alive,' 'denser,' and 'more believable' than GTA V.
- Subtle elements like animals, NPC behavior, street life, and animations are expected to greatly enhance immersion.
- The overall environment appears to have more personality and depth.
