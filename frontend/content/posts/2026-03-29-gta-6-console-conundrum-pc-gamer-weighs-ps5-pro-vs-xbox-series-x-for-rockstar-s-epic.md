# GTA 6 Console Conundrum: PC Gamer Weighs PS5 Pro vs. Xbox Series X for Rockstar's Epic

A dedicated PC gamer, who previously sold their Xbox Series X, is facing a significant decision regarding which console to purchase specifically for the highly anticipated release of GTA 6. Despite being comfortable with keyboard and mouse and Xbox controllers, the user recalls an uncomfortable experience with a PlayStation controller years ago. The core of their dilemma stems from rumors suggesting GTA 6 might perform best on the unreleased PS5 Pro, contrasting with the comfort and existing game library offered by an Xbox Series X. The gamer seeks advice on whether the potential performance gains on a PS5 Pro are worth overcoming a past negative experience with PlayStation controllers for just one game, emphasizing a desire for a relaxed gaming experience.

## FAQ
**Should a PC gamer prioritize the rumored best performance of GTA 6 on PS5 Pro or the familiarity of Xbox Series X and its controller?**
The decision hinges on individual priorities. If maximizing GTA 6's performance is paramount and you're willing to adapt to a new controller layout, the PS5 Pro might be appealing. However, if comfort, familiarity with the Xbox ecosystem, and access to an existing game library are more important for a relaxed gaming experience, the Xbox Series X remains a strong contender. While controller adaptation is generally achievable, past negative experiences can influence the learning curve.
