# GTA 6's Original Soundtrack: A Future Banger Destined for Underappreciation?

A recent discussion on Reddit's r/GTA6 subreddit posits that *Grand Theft Auto VI*'s original soundtrack (OST) is set to be a 'banger,' yet may ultimately go underappreciated, much like its predecessor, *Grand Theft Auto V*. User /u/Double_Zv1 initiated the conversation, reflecting on their recent playthroughs of *GTA V*'s story mode. They expressed newfound admiration for the game's mission-specific scores, citing tracks like 'North Yankton Memories' and 'Rich Man's Plaything' as personal favorites that often get overshadowed by the popular licensed radio stations like Non-Stop Pop and LS Rock Radio. The user anticipates a similar high-quality, memorable, and unknowingly hummable OST for *GTA VI*, predicting its potential to be overlooked by the wider player base who typically gravitate towards the in-game radio. This sentiment highlights a common trend where bespoke game scores, despite their integral role in narrative and atmosphere, often receive less public attention than curated licensed music.
