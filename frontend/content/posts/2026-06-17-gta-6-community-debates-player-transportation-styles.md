# GTA 6 Community Debates Player Transportation Styles

The Grand Theft Auto 6 community on Reddit is buzzing with speculation about player interaction with the game's transportation systems. A popular post by u/Next-Scale-9137 titled 'What kind of transportation player are YOU gotta be in GTA 6?' categorized various archetypes, prompting players to identify their likely in-game behavior. The discussion highlights the diverse ways players might engage with vehicles and public transit in the upcoming title set in Leonida.

## Key Takeaways
- Normal Player: Rides transportation as intended.
- Explorer: Seeks out every transportation service available.
- Exploder: Destroys any destructible transportation.
- Hijacker: Takes control of vehicles by force.
- Crasher: Purposely crashes vehicles after taking them over.
- Urban Explorer: Investigates abandoned or disused transportation infrastructure.
- Hater: Avoids using any in-game transportation.
- Community engagement: The post generated discussion on player behavior in GTA 6.
