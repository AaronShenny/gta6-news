# GTA VI Leaks Spark Community Concern Over Missing Features

Recent alleged leaks surrounding Grand Theft Auto VI, attributed to a source known as 'GameRoll,' have generated mixed reactions among the gaming community. One Reddit user, /u/Emmi18, expressed deep disappointment over several potential omissions detailed in these leaks. While acknowledging some positive aspects, the user specifically highlighted concerns over the absence of a panhandle region in the game's map, the lack of a prone stance for characters, no burglary side activities, and the inability to dual-wield weapons. The user also questioned the legitimacy of 'GameRoll' as a reliable leaker, expressing hope that the information is either entirely false or that Rockstar Games has utilized additional development time to re-implement these desired features before the game's rumored November release.
