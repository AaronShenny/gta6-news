# GTA 6 Reveal Nears 200-Day Mark, Community Anticipation and Anxiety Mounts

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by user /u/Brayj09200 captures the growing tension and excitement within the Grand Theft Auto community. The user highlights that tomorrow will mark exactly 200 days until the anticipated reveal of GTA 6.
