# Will GTA 6 Embrace Red Dead Redemption 2's Dynamic NPC Interactions?

The sentiment is that bringing such a nuanced interaction model to GTA 6, allowing for deeper engagement with the citizens of Vice City, would be a pivotal evolution for the series. Many feel that reverting to the more static NPC interactions seen in GTA V would represent a step backward for Rockstar's eagerly anticipated title.
