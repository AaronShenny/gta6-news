# GTA 6 Hype Dwindles for Some Amid PS5 Price Hike, Release Date Uncertainty

The anticipation for Grand Theft Auto 6, arguably one of the most awaited titles in gaming history, appears to be waning for some prospective players. A recent post by Reddit user /u/Stevecat032 on the r/GTA6 subreddit highlights a common sentiment among those who need to upgrade their console to experience the game. The user expressed a significant drop in excitement, attributing it primarily to the recent price increase of the PlayStation 5 console, the platform they expect to purchase for the game. This, combined with the continued lack of a concrete release date and new trailers, has led to a 'bleh' feeling, exacerbated by current economic pressures. For players like /u/Stevecat032, who are facing an additional financial barrier before even buying the game, the prolonged wait and rising costs are dampening the once-fervent hype.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user /u/Stevecat032 has lost excitement for GTA 6.
- The recent PS5 price increase is a primary reason for diminished hype.
- The lack of a firm GTA 6 release date contributes to player disinterest.
- Absence of new trailers also impacts player enthusiasm.
- Economic concerns amplify the user's reluctance to invest in a new console.
- The user needs to purchase a new console (PS5) specifically to play GTA 6.
