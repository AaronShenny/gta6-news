# Casual Confusion: A Girlfriend's $100 Surprise Highlights GTA 6 Release Date Misconceptions

In a recent Reddit post by u/Rsodyyy, an amusing anecdote shed light on the widespread confusion surrounding the highly anticipated release date of Grand Theft Auto 6. The user shared that his girlfriend, described as 'not very tech savvy,' mistakenly believed GTA 6 was launching on May 26, 2026, a date she apparently derived from trailers. In a touching gesture, she surprised him with $100 to purchase the game. The user then had to clarify that, according to his understanding and as stated in his post, the game is actually slated for release in November. This incident, he observed, underscores a common issue: many casual gamers and non-enthusiasts often don't keep pace with the latest gaming news and can be easily misled by early promotional material, such as specific dates shown in trailers that may not represent the final launch date or could even be misinterpretations of copyright information.
