# GTA 6: Will Rockstar Repeat the Double-Dip Strategy with a PS6 Enhanced Version?

A recent discussion within the GTA 6 community highlights growing speculation that Rockstar Games might replicate the successful staggered release strategy previously employed for Grand Theft Auto V. This model would involve an initial launch of GTA 6 on the PlayStation 5, followed by a subsequent, enhanced version for the PlayStation 6. The core of the debate centers on what compelling new features, beyond mere graphical upgrades, Rockstar could introduce to this theoretical PS6 version of GTA 6 to incentivize players to purchase the game a second time. Drawing parallels to GTA V's transition from PS3/Xbox 360 to PS4/Xbox One, where the latter introduced a significant first-person mode, players are pondering what innovative gameplay additions or expansive content could make a PS6 iteration of GTA 6 a worthwhile repurchase.

## Key Takeaways
- Speculation suggests GTA 6 could launch first on PS5, then later on PS6.
- This proposed strategy mirrors GTA V's release across console generations.
- The aim is potentially to encourage players to buy the game twice.
- GTA V's PS4/Xbox One version notably added a first-person mode as a key new feature beyond graphics.
- The community is discussing what non-graphical, significant features could entice a second purchase for GTA 6 on PS6.

## FAQ
**Is GTA 6 confirmed for a PS6 release?**
No, this article discusses community speculation and a hypothetical scenario based on Rockstar's past release strategies for GTA V, not confirmed news.

**Why would Rockstar potentially release GTA 6 on two console generations?**
The speculation suggests it's a strategy to maximize sales, encouraging players to purchase the game twice, similar to how GTA V was released across multiple generations.

**What new features did GTA V's next-gen version add?**
Beyond significant graphical improvements, the most notable gameplay addition for GTA V's PS4 and Xbox One versions was a full first-person mode.

**What kind of features are players speculating for a potential GTA 6 PS6 version?**
Players are discussing significant gameplay additions or expansive content beyond just visual upgrades, seeking a compelling reason to repurchase, akin to the impact of first-person mode in GTA V's enhanced edition.
