# The Forgotten GTA 6 Leak: Did Adin Ross's Stream Reveal Key Details Before Trailer 1?

According to the user, an alleged Rockstar employee briefly appeared on the stream and divulged several key details about GTA 6, predating the release of its first official trailer.The alleged leak included the confirmation of Lucia as the game's main character, a specific plot point detailing her leaving her baby for a robbery which leads to her imprisonment, the 2025 release date, and the scrapping of environmental features like hurricanes and tornados. The Reddit user highlighted that both the 2025 release date and Lucia's initial backstory were unknown to the public until the official trailer dropped, suggesting the leak held legitimate, previously unreleased information. The post questions why this particular leak has not been widely discussed within the GTA 6 community, especially given its apparent accuracy, and concludes that it 'kinda confirms' the absence of hurricanes in the final game.
