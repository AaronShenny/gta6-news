# GTA 6 Fans Nostalgic for Old-School Radio Station Reveals

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit by u/Fickle_Skill6038 has sparked discussion among fans, expressing a deep nostalgia for the marketing strategies of Grand Theft Auto: Vice City and Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. The user recalls how Rockstar Games would gradually reveal new radio stations and a couple of songs on the official websites, creating anticipation and excitement. They specifically wish for a similar 'drip-feed' approach for GTA 6, where new radio stations and soundtrack snippets are unveiled every few weeks. This sentiment is driven by a desire for 'new soundtrack leaks' and a general feeling of nostalgia, rather than a critique of current marketing tactics. The discussion highlights how past marketing methods fostered a unique build-up to game releases, particularly around the iconic in-game radio experience.
