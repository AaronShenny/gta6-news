# GTA 6: Fans Hope for Red Dead Redemption 2-Level Side Content Depth

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit highlights a strong community desire for Grand Theft Auto 6 to incorporate the rich, rewarding side content mechanics that made Red Dead Redemption 2 so memorable. User /u/SeniorMoonlight21 initiated the conversation, praising RDR2's unique gear, unlockable holsters, recurring stranger missions, and meaningful random encounters. They argued that RDR2's world exploration and challenges felt genuinely rewarding, a quality they felt was largely absent from GTA V's side activities, despite their fun nature. The sentiment suggests players are eager for GTA 6 to move beyond 'something to do' and embrace a more integrated, impactful approach to its non-story content.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user /u/SeniorMoonlight21 sparked a discussion on GTA 6's side content.
- The user expressed a strong preference for Red Dead Redemption 2's side content model over GTA V's.
- RDR2's side content was praised for being rewarding, offering unique gear, unlockable items, and recurring character interactions.
- Random encounters and hunting in RDR2 were highlighted as meaningful, not just filler.
- The challenges system in RDR2 and its rewards were also cited as positive examples.
- GTA V's side content, while fun, was perceived as lacking the depth and meaningful impact of RDR2's.
- The community hopes GTA 6 will adopt a more immersive and rewarding approach to its optional activities.
