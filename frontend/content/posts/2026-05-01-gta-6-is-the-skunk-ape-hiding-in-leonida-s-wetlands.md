# GTA 6: Is the 'Skunk Ape' Hiding in Leonida's Wetlands?

Gaming enthusiasts are buzzing with speculation about a potential 'Skunk Ape' Easter egg in Grand Theft Auto VI, drawing parallels to Rockstar's past inclusion of cryptids like the Sasquatch. The theory stems from a Red Bull Racing video featuring Isack Hadjar exploring the real-world Everglades, where a guide casually mentioned the Skunk Ape – a creature described as the 'sasquatch of the Everglades' and known in Florida folklore. This aligns perfectly with an official GTA 6 screenshot description of Leonida's 'Wetlands,' which teases 'far deadlier predators and weirder discoveries among the mangroves,' fueling the belief that Rockstar is preparing another memorable, mysterious encounter for players.

## FAQ
**What is the Skunk Ape?**
The Skunk Ape is a cryptid, a legendary creature similar to Bigfoot or Sasquatch, purported to inhabit the forests and swamps of the southeastern United States, most notably Florida and Georgia.

**Why do players think the Skunk Ape might be in GTA 6?**
The theory emerged after a Red Bull Racing video showed a guide mentioning the Skunk Ape in the real Everglades. Combined with Rockstar's history of including cryptids and an official GTA 6 description teasing 'weirder discoveries' in the game's wetlands, fans believe it could be an Easter egg.

Yes, Rockstar Games is known for its detailed open worlds and hidden secrets, including cryptids. A famous example is the Sasquatch Easter egg found in Red Dead Redemption 1.
