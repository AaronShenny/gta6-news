# Community Speculation: Will GTA 6 Graphics Be Just 'GTA 5 Enhanced'?

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has ignited discussion within the gaming community regarding the anticipated graphical fidelity of Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/Tight-Opinion9932 controversially claims that GTA 6's visuals will be 'no different from GTA 5 enhanced,' expressing surprise that many fans expect a significant generational leap. The post suggests that despite the long development cycle and new console generation, the graphical improvements might be more iterative than revolutionary, akin to an enhanced version of the previous title rather than a complete overhaul.
