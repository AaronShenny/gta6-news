# Cyberpunk 2077's PS5 Pro Update Fuels Hopes for GTA 6 60fps Performance

Optimism is brewing within the gaming community regarding Grand Theft Auto VI's potential performance on the PlayStation 5 Pro, largely inspired by the recent Cyberpunk 2077 update. The update successfully integrated ray tracing while maintaining a smooth 60 frames per second on the PS5 Pro, leading to speculation that Rockstar Games could achieve similar feats with GTA 6. Gamers anticipate Rockstar might offer a trio of graphical modes for GTA 6 on the PS5 Pro: a 'Quality' mode prioritizing full ray tracing at 30fps, a 'Balanced' mode targeting 40fps for those with VRR-compatible displays, and a 'Performance' mode delivering a consistent 60fps, likely with some graphical compromises.

## Key Takeaways
- Cyberpunk 2077's recent PS5 Pro update achieved 60fps while incorporating ray tracing.
- This achievement has generated hope that GTA 6 could also run at 60fps on the PS5 Pro.
- Speculation suggests Rockstar Games might implement three distinct graphical modes for GTA 6 on the PS5 Pro.
- Proposed modes include a 'Quality' mode (full ray tracing at 30fps), a 'Balanced' mode (40fps for VRR displays), and a 'Performance' mode (solid 60fps with reduced graphical fidelity).
