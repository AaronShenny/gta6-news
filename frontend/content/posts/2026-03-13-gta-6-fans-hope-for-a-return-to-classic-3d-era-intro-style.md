# GTA 6 Fans Hope for a Return to Classic 3D Era Intro Style

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has highlighted a nostalgic sentiment among fans regarding Grand Theft Auto VI's potential intro sequence. User u/Bingoboyop specifically voiced a wish for Rockstar Games to bring back the distinctive '3D era style intro' seen in older GTA titles. This style, characterized by its cool segmented clips, scrolling credits, and memorable musical scores, was a beloved element that many fans, including the Redditor, would often sit through just to appreciate the atmosphere and music. The sentiment suggests that this particular introduction format contributed significantly to the overall experience and immersion of the classic games.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user wishes GTA VI would adopt the 3D era style intro.
- The user appreciates the segmented clips, credits, and music of older GTA intros.
- Nostalgia for the classic intro sequences is a strong factor in the desire.
