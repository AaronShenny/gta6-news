# GTA 6 Community Divided: Story Mode Enthusiasts Prioritize Single-Player Over Online

A recent Reddit post titled 'I don’t care about Online, I’m only buying GTA 6 for the story mode.' has ignited a discussion within the Grand Theft Auto community, highlighting a prevalent sentiment among players who prioritize the immersive single-player narrative experience over the multiplayer component. Many long-time fans express a desire for Rockstar Games to focus resources on a rich, expansive story mode, reminiscent of previous titles, rather than the continuous live-service model seen with GTA Online. This perspective often stems from a longing for a curated, character-driven journey without the distractions or monetization structures typically associated with online multiplayer. While GTA Online has been incredibly successful for Rockstar, this vocal segment of the fanbase signals that for them, the core appeal of Grand Theft Auto lies firmly in its compelling solo adventures.

## Key Takeaways
- Many GTA 6 fans prioritize the single-player story mode experience.
- A significant portion of the community expresses disinterest in the online component for GTA 6.
- The sentiment sparks a debate about Rockstar's resource allocation between story and online development.
- Players value the narrative, character development, and curated open-world experience of single-player gameplay.
- The discussion suggests a desire for a return to focus on traditional, robust solo campaigns in Grand Theft Auto.

## FAQ
**Why are some players only interested in GTA 6's story mode?**
Players who prioritize the story mode often seek a curated narrative experience, deep character development, and a single-player journey over the persistent, often monetized, online world and its competitive aspects.

**Does this sentiment mean GTA 6 won't have an online component?**
No, while some players express disinterest, it's widely expected that GTA 6 will feature a robust online mode, following the immense success of GTA V's online counterpart, GTA Online.

**Has Rockstar historically focused more on story or online?**
Rockstar Games built its reputation on deep, engaging single-player narratives. However, with the monumental success of GTA Online, there has been a noticeable shift in focus towards live-service content and ongoing multiplayer support in recent years.
