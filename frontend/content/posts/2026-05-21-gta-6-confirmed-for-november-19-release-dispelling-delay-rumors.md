# GTA 6 Confirmed for November 19 Release, Dispelling Delay Rumors

Despite recent speculation and a history of past delays, Take-Two Interactive has officially reconfirmed Grand Theft Auto VI is set to launch on November 19. During the company's fiscal 2026 results briefing, CEO Strauss Zelnick reiterated the commitment, emphasizing the highly anticipated title's expected impact on future operating performance. While no pricing details were revealed at this time, marketing efforts for GTA VI are slated to begin in earnest this summer, potentially coinciding with events like the FIFA World Cup. The game will initially be available on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, with a PC version not launching concurrently.

## Key Takeaways
- Grand Theft Auto VI is officially set to launch on November 19.
- Take-Two Interactive CEO Strauss Zelnick reconfirmed the release date during the company's fiscal 2026 results briefing.
- This confirmation addresses and dispels recent rumors of another potential delay for the game.
- Marketing for GTA VI is expected to begin in earnest this summer, starting June 21.
- The game will be available on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S at launch.
- A PC version of Grand Theft Auto VI will not be released simultaneously with the console versions.
- No official pricing details were announced, though analysts have previously suggested an $80 price point.
