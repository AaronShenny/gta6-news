# GTA 6's Tone: Will it Blend GTA 5's Absurdity with RDR2's Grounded Feel?

A recent Reddit discussion, initiated by user u/jnverted, has sparked community speculation regarding the overarching tone of the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6. The conversation specifically steers clear of gameplay mechanics or setting differences between GTA and Red Dead Redemption, focusing instead on the 'vibe' or narrative style. Participants are pondering if GTA 6 will attempt to strike a balance between the famously over-the-top, chaotic, and often absurd nature of Grand Theft Auto V and the more grounded, albeit still humorous and violent, storytelling approach that defined Red Dead Redemption 2. Given the monumental success of both previous titles, the community is keenly debating whether Rockstar will fuse these distinct narrative styles for their next flagship release.
