# GTA 6 Fan Recreates Lucia in Stunning LEGO Style, Clarifies No AI Used

A dedicated Grand Theft Auto VI fan, known on Reddit as u/Unique_Tomato8137, has garnered attention for their impressive 3D render depicting one of GTA 6's main protagonists, often referred to as 'the GTA VI Lady' (widely believed to be Lucia), in a distinct LEGO aesthetic. The artist utilized professional 3D software Blender for modeling and Nuke for compositing to achieve the stylized look. Notably, the creator emphasized that the artwork was entirely human-made, explicitly stating 'No AI used whatsoever.' This clarification comes after the original post was reportedly removed by moderators for being mistakenly identified as AI-generated content, highlighting ongoing discussions within online communities regarding the use and detection of artificial intelligence in fan creations.
