# Fan Theory: GTA 6's Jason and Lucia Echo Red Dead Redemption 2's Arthur Morgan and Mary Linton

A compelling fan theory circulating among the Grand Theft Auto 6 community draws a striking parallel between the game's protagonists, Jason and Lucia, and Red Dead Redemption 2's star-crossed lovers, Arthur Morgan and Mary Linton. The theory posits that the unfulfilled 'deep love that never was' between Arthur and Mary could have inspired GTA 6's dual-protagonist mechanics and story direction. The author, while replaying RDR2 and observing GTA 6 trailers, noted a visual and thematic resemblance, suggesting Jason and Lucia might embody the life Arthur and Mary could have lived, perhaps evolving into a modern-day 'Bonnie and Clyde' duo. The theory even playfully suggests Jason and Lucia could be direct descendants or even reincarnations of the RDR2 characters, highlighting a potential narrative depth rooted in Rockstar's previous work.

## Key Takeaways
- The theory originates from a Reddit user replaying Red Dead Redemption 2 and observing GTA 6 content.
- It highlights a visual and thematic resemblance between Arthur Morgan/Mary Linton (RDR2) and Jason/Lucia (GTA 6).
- The core idea is that GTA 6's two-player mechanics and story might be inspired by Arthur and Mary's 'deep love that never was' side story.
- The theory suggests Jason and Lucia could be living the life Arthur and Mary might have had, potentially as a 'Bonnie and Clyde' type duo.
- It also playfully considers the possibility of Jason and Lucia being descendants or reincarnations of the RDR2 characters.
