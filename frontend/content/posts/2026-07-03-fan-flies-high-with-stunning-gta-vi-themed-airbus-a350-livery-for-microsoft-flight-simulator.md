# Fan Flies High with Stunning GTA VI-Themed Airbus A350 Livery for Microsoft Flight Simulator

A passionate fan, known as /u/simtom, has brought the vibrant world of Grand Theft Auto VI into the skies of Microsoft Flight Simulator (MSFS24) by creating a unique, fictional Airbus A350 livery. Noticing a significant absence of GTA-inspired designs within the flight simulation community, /u/simtom took the initiative to craft a custom paint scheme for the popular iniBuilds A350. This special edition livery pack is available for both the -900 and -1000 variants of the aircraft, allowing virtual pilots to fly with a distinct GTA VI aesthetic. The livery can be downloaded from flightsim.to by searching for 'A350 GTA VI Special Edition Livery Pack', offering a creative crossover for enthusiasts of both gaming franchises.
