# GTA Fan Questions Rockstar's HD Universe Location Strategy: Why San Andreas for GTA V Over Vice City?

A Reddit user, /u/No_Underscore12, has ignited a discussion within the Grand Theft Auto community by questioning Rockstar Games' strategic decisions regarding the settings of their HD universe titles. The user specifically wonders why Grand Theft Auto V, released on then-current hardware, returned to San Andreas (specifically Los Santos) instead of Vice City. Their core argument suggests that GTA V could have focused on a modernized Vice City, potentially including the Florida Keys and surrounding countryside. This approach, they contend, would have allowed a future Grand Theft Auto VI to fully leverage advanced hardware for a truly expansive San Andreas, encompassing updated versions of Los Santos, Las Fierro, and Las Venturas. While personally preferring the confirmed new state of Leonida (Vice City) for GTA VI over a modernized San Andreas, the user remains curious about Rockstar's specific rationale for the chosen sequence of these iconic locations in the HD era.

## Key Takeaways
- User questions Rockstar's decision to set GTA V in San Andreas over Vice City.
- Proposes GTA V could have been Vice City (with Keys/countryside) on older hardware.
- Suggests GTA VI could then have delivered a complete San Andreas (Los Santos, Las Fierro, Las Venturas) on newer hardware.
- User prefers a new state like Leonida (Vice City) for GTA VI over a modernized San Andreas.
- Seeks to understand Rockstar's specific reasoning for the HD universe location order.
