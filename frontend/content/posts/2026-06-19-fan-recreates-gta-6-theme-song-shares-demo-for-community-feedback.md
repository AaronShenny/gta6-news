# Fan Recreates GTA 6 Theme Song, Shares Demo for Community Feedback

Reddit user u/No-Performance979 has shared an ambitious fan-made recreation of the Grand Theft Auto 6 theme song. The creator acknowledges that their rendition, while aiming to be as close as possible to the original track from the GTA 6 cover art theme, is currently a demo version, unmixed and unmastered. The post invites the community to listen and provide their thoughts on the early-stage musical effort.

## Key Takeaways
- Reddit user u/No-Performance979 created a fan-made GTA 6 theme song.
- The track is presented as a demo, unmixed and unmastered.
- The creator aimed to replicate the original theme as closely as possible.
- The community is encouraged to provide feedback on the rendition.
