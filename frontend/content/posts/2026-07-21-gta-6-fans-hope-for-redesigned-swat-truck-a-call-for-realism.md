# GTA 6 Fans Hope for Redesigned SWAT Truck: A Call for Realism

A Grand Theft Auto 6 enthusiast has voiced their strong opinion regarding the design of the 'Riot Van' from GTA 5, expressing a desire for a significant overhaul in the upcoming installment. The user, /u/dope-uh-mine, finds the current Riot Van to be excessively 'box-y' and less realistic when compared to other vehicle designs within the game. 

The post highlights Rockstar Games' recent trend of recreating vehicles with greater fidelity to their real-world counterparts. This observation fuels the hope that GTA 6 will feature a redesigned SWAT truck that aligns more closely with contemporary vehicle aesthetics and realism. The user even suggests that an 'Insurgent' type vehicle would be a superior alternative to the existing Riot Van design.
