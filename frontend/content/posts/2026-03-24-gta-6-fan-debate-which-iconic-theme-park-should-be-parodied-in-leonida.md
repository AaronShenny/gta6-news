# GTA 6 Fan Debate: Which Iconic Theme Park Should Be Parodied in Leonida?

The GTA 6 community is buzzing with speculation about potential in-game theme park parodies. A recent Reddit post sparked a debate, asking fans to choose which hypothetical theme park should appear in the upcoming game. Options include a return of InterGlobal (a Universal parody, previously seen in the 3D universe's Vice City and San Andreas) or Fred's Pictures (a Disney parody, already established in the HD universe via GTA V and a strong fit for a Florida-inspired setting). The discussion also acknowledges a fan-made conceptual map for GTA VI that impressively incorporates both theme park concepts, leaving the door open for other suggestions or even no theme parks at all.

## Key Takeaways
- Fans are discussing which theme park should be featured in GTA VI's hypothetical Leonida map.
- InterGlobal (a Universal parody) has appeared in 3D universe titles (Vice City, San Andreas) but its inclusion in the HD universe is uncertain.
- Fred's Pictures (a Disney parody) is already part of the HD universe (GTA V) and aligns well with Florida's real-world Disney presence.
- The community is encouraged to suggest other theme park parodies or argue against their inclusion.
- A conceptual fan-made map for GTA VI by 'avatarsd' has previously included both InterGlobal and Fred's Pictures.
