# Unpacking GTA 6 Fan Theories: How Lucia and Jason Could Meet and the Story Might Unfold

A recent community discussion on Reddit delves deep into speculative theories surrounding the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto VI, particularly focusing on how its dual protagonists, Lucia and Jason, might meet and how the narrative could unfold. The prevailing theory suggests that Lucia and Jason begin the game as strangers, with their paths crossing when Jason is tasked with picking Lucia up from prison, possibly facilitated by a mutual acquaintance like 'Cal' or the 'woman from the bar.' It's speculated that players might initially control Jason, working with Cal for a character named Brian, before Lucia becomes a playable character a few missions in, similar to Michael's introduction in GTA V. The discussion also touches upon potential story beats, such as Lucia introducing a character named Raul Bautista to orchestrate heists, and the expectation that while the game may start in the Florida Keys, the iconic Vice City might not be accessible immediately. A key point of debate is how leaked footage showing Lucia and Jason cohabiting in Port Gellhorn fits into this proposed timeline, alongside the possibility of a prologue mission detailing Lucia's incarceration.
