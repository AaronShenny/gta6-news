# GTA 6 Rumor Mill: Ultimate Edition Shops Clarified, Physical Release Status Addressed

Speculation surrounding Grand Theft Auto VI's Ultimate Edition shops and the game's physical release strategy has been addressed by a Reddit user, offering clarity on several key points. Contrary to some interpretations, the exclusive Ultimate Edition shops are not intended to replace the standard array of stores players expect in a GTA title. Traditional clothing stores, barbershops, and car customization outlets will still be readily available throughout Vice City, likely offering customization options more aligned with Jason and Lucia's core aesthetic and the game's narrative. The Ultimate Edition shops, conversely, are expected to provide a wider, more 'wild' selection of customization items, catering to players seeking unique looks. Furthermore, the post touches upon the absence of discs, suggesting that Rockstar's primary motivation is to prevent pre-launch leaks. It's anticipated that physical copies with discs will become available to consumers post-launch, once initial digital sales and leak concerns have been managed.

## Key Takeaways
- Ultimate Edition shops are exclusive, not replacements for standard in-game stores.
- Standard stores (clothing, barbers, car shops) will be present in Vice City.
- Standard stores will offer customization more aligned with Jason and Lucia's base appearance.
- Ultimate Edition shops will provide a broader, more "wild" selection of customization.
- The initial absence of physical discs is likely a measure to prevent leaks.
- Physical disc versions are expected to be released post-launch.
