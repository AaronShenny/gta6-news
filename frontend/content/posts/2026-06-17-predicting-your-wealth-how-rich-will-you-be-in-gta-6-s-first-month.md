# Predicting Your Wealth: How Rich Will You Be in GTA 6's First Month?

A Reddit thought experiment, grounded in GTA Online's historical economic patterns, offers a realistic look at how players might accumulate wealth in Grand Theft Auto 6's first month. The article posits that GTA 6 will launch with a deliberately tight economy, similar to its predecessor. Players will likely start broke, with early missions offering modest payouts and major heists locked. The initial significant income is expected to come from a cheap starter business, with optimal money-making loops quickly discovered by the community. Securing a passive income property early on is highlighted as crucial for compounding wealth throughout the month. Ultimately, most players are predicted to end the first month comfortable but not excessively rich, with those who master the optimal loops gaining a significant advantage. The core advice emphasizes prioritizing in-game businesses and acquiring passive income sources over single missions.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6's economy is expected to start tight and gradually become more generous, mirroring GTA Online's launch arc.
- Players will likely begin with minimal funds, as high-payout heists and activities won't be immediately available.
- The first significant income stream is predicted to come from a cheap, starter business (e.g., an 'Acid Lab' equivalent).
- Optimal money-making strategies and loops will be discovered and shared by the community within days of launch.
- Acquiring a passive income property (e.g., a 'Nightclub' equivalent) early will be key for sustained wealth accumulation.
- Most players will likely be comfortable but not 'loaded' by the end of the first month; optimizers will pull ahead.
- Focus on establishing businesses and securing passive income sources rather than relying solely on single missions.
