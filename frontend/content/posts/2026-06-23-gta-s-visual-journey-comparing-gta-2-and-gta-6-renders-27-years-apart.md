# GTA's Visual Journey: Comparing GTA 2 and GTA 6 Renders 27 Years Apart

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit, shared by user /u/RogueBlackshift, has drawn significant attention by presenting a striking visual comparison between renders of Grand Theft Auto 2 and the highly anticipated Grand Theft Auto 6.
