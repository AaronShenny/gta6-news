# GTA 6: Fan Hopes for Director Mode, Hidden Unlockables, and Peyote Plant Return

A passionate Grand Theft Auto fan has taken to social media to express their fervent hopes for specific features in the upcoming GTA 6. Drawing inspiration from the unique experiences offered by GTA 5, the player particularly highlights the fun of 'playing as the beast' in Director Mode and the memorable transformations induced by peyote plants. Their wishlist for GTA 6 centers on the desire for similar hidden unlockables, the return of the versatile Director Mode, and the reintroduction of the beloved peyote plant collectibles, all aimed at enhancing replayability and delivering distinct gameplay moments.

## FAQ
**What is Director Mode in GTA 5?**
Director Mode allows players to take on the role of various characters, including unique NPCs and animals, and direct their own scenes within the game world, offering creative freedom and unique gameplay perspectives.

**What are peyote plants in GTA 5?**
Peyote plants are hidden collectibles in GTA 5 that, when consumed, temporarily transform the player into an animal, offering a surreal and often humorous gameplay experience with different abilities.

**Why are fans hoping for these features in GTA 6?**
Fans remember these features from GTA 5 as contributing significantly to the game's replayability, humor, and overall unique charm, believing they would add substantial value and distinct experiences to GTA 6.
