# GTA 6: Community Hopes for 'More Powerful' Gun Sounds

A recent discussion on the r/GTA6 subreddit has brought attention to community hopes for enhanced audio design in Grand Theft Auto 6, specifically regarding weapon sound effects. User /u/PositiveMusicVibes initiated the conversation, stating that gun sounds in GTA 5 often felt 'muffled' and conveyed a strong desire for 'much better' and 'more powerful' gun audio in the highly anticipated sequel. This post highlights a segment of the fanbase keen on immersive soundscapes for the upcoming title.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user criticized GTA 5's gun sounds as 'muffled'.
- There is a strong desire for 'much better' and 'more powerful' gun sounds in GTA 6.
- The discussion originated on the r/GTA6 subreddit, indicating community interest in audio fidelity.
