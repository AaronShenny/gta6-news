# Speculating on GTA VI's Potential Body Transformation System

A recent discussion on Reddit's r/GTA6 community speculates on the potential for an advanced body transformation system in Grand Theft Auto VI. Inspired by hints in the second trailer and a simpler version in Red Dead Redemption 2, the community wonders how far Rockstar might push such a feature. The discussion envisions a system where player physique – ranging from skinny to muscular to overweight – could dynamically affect various gameplay elements, including stamina, combat style, movement, intimidation, character animations, and even NPC reactions within the game world.

## Key Takeaways
- Body transformation is speculated to be a feature in GTA VI, hinted at by the second trailer.
- A simpler body transformation system previously existed in Red Dead Redemption 2.
- Players could potentially change their physique (skinny, muscular, overweight) over time.
- Physical changes might impact gameplay aspects like stamina, combat style, and movement.
- Player physique could also influence intimidation, animations, and NPC reactions.
