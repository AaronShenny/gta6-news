# What kind of realistic detail would ruin GTA VI?

The Grand Theft Auto VI community is actively debating the line between immersive realism and tedious gameplay. A popular Reddit thread on r/GTA6, titled 'What kind of realistic detail would ruin GTA VI?', sparked a discussion about potential realistic features that fans hope Rockstar avoids. While realism is often lauded, players expressed concerns that certain elements, if implemented, could detract from the core fun and freedom of the GTA experience, turning it into a chore rather than an escapade.

## Key Takeaways
- Concerns about excessive realism making gameplay less enjoyable.
- Examples of potentially 'ruinous' realism include guns jamming and overly strict police persistence (e.g., knocking on doors, tracking plates).
- Players hope to avoid 'life simulator' elements like mandatory eating, drinking, sleeping, and managing bills (phone, groceries, rent, IRS).
- Suggestions were made against implementing minor traffic infringements leading to wanted levels or overuse injuries (e.g., limping from running too far).
