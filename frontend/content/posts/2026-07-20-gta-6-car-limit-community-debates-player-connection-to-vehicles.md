# GTA 6 Car Limit: Community Debates Player Connection to Vehicles

A thought-provoking discussion has emerged within the Grand Theft Auto 6 community, initiated by Reddit user u/Full_Ad_5219. The user poses a hypothetical scenario for GTA 6: the implementation of a strict car limit, suggesting around 10 vehicles. The premise behind this idea is to foster a deeper sense of connection between players and their in-game vehicles. Citing experiences from GTA V, where players often accumulate vast collections of cars only to forget about many of them, the user questions whether a more curated garage would lead to greater player attachment and engagement with their chosen rides. The post invites the community to weigh in on the concept, asking whether they would support such a limit and, if so, what an ideal number of vehicles might be.

## Key Takeaways
- A Reddit user proposed a car limit for players in GTA 6.
- The primary goal of a car limit would be to enhance player connection and attachment to their vehicles.
- The idea stems from the experience in GTA V, where players often forget about many cars due to large collections.
- The community is asked to discuss the concept and suggest an ideal vehicle limit, if any.
- This is a community-driven discussion and not an official announcement or leak from Rockstar Games.

## FAQ
**What is the proposed car limit for GTA 6?**
The original Reddit post suggests a limit of around 10 cars, but the user is primarily seeking community input on whether a limit is desired at all and what an ideal number might be.

**Why is a car limit being discussed for GTA 6?**
The discussion arises from the observation that in GTA V, players often accumulate so many vehicles that they lose a sense of connection or forget about many of them. A limit aims to foster a stronger bond with a curated selection of cars, making each vehicle feel more significant.
