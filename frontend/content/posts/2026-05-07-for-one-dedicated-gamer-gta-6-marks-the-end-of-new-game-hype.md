# For One Dedicated Gamer, GTA 6 Marks the End of New Game Hype

A 34-year-old gamer, juggling fatherhood and a demanding schedule, shares a poignant reflection on his declining enthusiasm for new game releases. Despite his love for gaming as personal 'me-time,' he admits to being largely burnt out, often returning to a backlog of classic titles for nostalgia. He singles out Grand Theft Auto 6 as the sole remaining new game he anticipates with fervent excitement, expecting it to be a decade-long engagement and a benchmark no other title, outside of Rockstar's own, will match in hype or impact. This sentiment leads him to view GTA 6 as his 'last hurrah' for day-one anticipation, suggesting future installments like GTA 7 would arrive when he's too old to care as deeply.
