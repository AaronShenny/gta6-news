# Debate Ignites: Is 30 FPS Truly 'Fine' for GTA VI's Cinematic Vision?

A recent Reddit post in the r/GTA6 community has stirred discussion by asserting that a 30 frames per second (FPS) experience would be 'honestly fine' for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto VI. The user, /u/LA_Rym, defends this stance by highlighting the game's cinematic nature and the presence of aim assist, suggesting that prioritizing superior graphical quality over a higher frame rate would enhance the overall visual experience. Despite being accustomed to 240 FPS in other titles, the Redditor expressed willingness to adapt to 30 FPS, provided input lag remains minimal, anticipating potential backlash for their perspective.
