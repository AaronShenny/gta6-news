# GTA 6 Fans Hope for More Dynamic Gang vs. Cops Encounters, Drawing Inspiration from Watch Dogs 2

A recent post on the r/GTA6 subreddit has sparked discussion among fans regarding potential gameplay enhancements for Grand Theft Auto 6. User /u/Legal-Start9483 shared their hope for more immersive and dynamic encounters between gangs and police within the game's open world. The user specifically highlighted Watch Dogs 2, praising its "full shout-out between gangs and cops" as being more developed and engaging than similar events observed in Grand Theft Auto V. The post, accompanied by a visual example, suggests a community desire for more organic, large-scale, and chaotic street battles that could significantly deepen the immersion and emergent gameplay experiences in GTA 6. This sentiment reflects a broader wish for Rockstar Games to evolve the environmental dynamism and interaction systems to create a more vibrant and unpredictable game world.
