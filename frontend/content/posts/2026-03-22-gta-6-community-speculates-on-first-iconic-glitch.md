# GTA 6 Community Speculates on First Iconic Glitch

Even before its release, the Grand Theft Auto 6 community is engaging in a lively discussion, speculating on what the game's first iconic, community-discovered glitch might be. Drawing parallels to legendary bugs from past Rockstar titles like GTA 5's 'gate launch' and Red Dead Redemption 2's 'bouncing bridge,' players are sharing their most creative and specific predictions. Given the anticipated chaotic setting of Leonida (Rockstar's take on Florida), many foresee humorous and outlandish scenarios, such as NPCs getting stuck in bizarre situations or wildlife behaving unexpectedly. The conversation highlights the community's anticipation not just for the game itself, but for the unique, often hilarious, emergent gameplay experiences that glitches can provide.

## Key Takeaways
- GTA 6 community is actively predicting the game's first major glitch.
