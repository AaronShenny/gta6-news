# Fan Speculates on GTA 6 Plot: Lucia's Release, Jason's Rise, and a Wanted Escape

A dedicated fan on Reddit, u/EvandoYT, has shared elaborate plot predictions for the upcoming Grand Theft Auto 6. Their theory posits an epilogue where Lucia is arrested, leading to a time jump where Jason is established in Vice City. The initial gameplay would focus on Jason performing small jobs. Lucia's release from prison would then see her reunite with Jason, revealing her ankle bracelet and restricting her actions. This would lead to Jason continuing his criminal activities, eventually unlocking Lucia as a playable character. The prediction culminates in Jason dramatically removing Lucia's ankle bracelet, triggering a high-star wanted level, forcing the duo to flee Vice City for the countryside.

## Key Takeaways
- The plot begins with an epilogue where Lucia is arrested after a major job.
- Years later, Jason is established in Vice City, where Lucia is also imprisoned.
- Early missions will focus on playing as Jason, doing small favors and jobs.
- Jason reunites with Lucia upon her discharge from prison.
- Lucia is under strict supervision with an ankle bracelet, limiting her involvement in crimes.
- Jason continues to engage in minor criminal activities, meeting new contacts.
- Lucia becomes a playable character after a few missions.
- Jason eventually removes Lucia's ankle bracelet, triggering a 3-4 star wanted level.
- Jason and Lucia are forced to escape Vice City and hide in the countryside with a friend.
